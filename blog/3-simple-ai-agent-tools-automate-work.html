AI Agents Made Simple

AI agents sound complex, but they're simply automated systems that can perform tasks for you. The biggest misconception? That you need programming skills to use them. In reality, modern tools have made AI automation accessible to everyone.

The three tools we'll cover solve different automation needs: ChatGPT Custom GPTs for specialized assistants, Zapier AI for connecting apps, and Relevance AI for multi-step workflows. Each requires no coding and can be set up in minutes.

AI agents aren't about complexity—they're about systems. Start by automating small, repetitive tasks. As you see results, you can scale to more sophisticated automation.

Tool 1: ChatGPT Custom GPTs

The simplest way to create an AI agent is inside ChatGPT itself using Custom GPTs. These are specialized versions of ChatGPT that follow your specific instructions automatically.

You can create agents for tasks like resume reviewing, research assistance, or content planning. Just define the role, upload relevant documents, and set goals. The agent then operates within those parameters without needing constant prompting.

Example: A real estate agent created a Custom GPT that analyzes property listings and generates comparative market analyses automatically—saving 3+ hours per property.

Tool 2: Zapier AI

Zapier has added AI capabilities to its automation platform, creating powerful agent-like workflows between apps. When an event occurs (like a new form submission), Zapier can trigger AI to process information and take action.

Common use cases include automatically summarizing emails, categorizing support tickets, or generating responses based on incoming data. The AI handles the thinking while Zapier manages the connections between your tools.

Time saver: A marketing team automated their lead qualification process using Zapier AI, reducing manual review time by 80% while improving response speed.

Tool 3: Relevance AI

Relevance AI provides a middle ground between simple prompting and complex coding. It lets you build multi-step AI workflows that connect data sources, analyze information, and take automated actions—all through a visual interface.

While slightly more advanced than the other tools, Relevance AI remains beginner-friendly. It's ideal for processes that require multiple AI steps, like gathering data from different sources, analyzing trends, and generating reports automatically.

Power user tip: At the 2:15 mark in the video, you'll see how to chain multiple AI steps in Relevance AI to create a complete research automation system.

Choosing the Right Tool

Each of these AI agent tools serves different needs. ChatGPT Custom GPTs excel at specialized knowledge work. Zapier AI shines at connecting apps with AI in between. Relevance AI handles complex, multi-step processes.

Start by identifying your most time-consuming repetitive tasks. Then match them to the tool that best fits:

  • ChatGPT Custom GPTs: When you need a specialized assistant for content, analysis, or decision support
  • Zapier AI: When you need to connect apps with AI processing in between
  • Relevance AI: When you need multi-step AI workflows with data integration

Implementation Tips

The key to successful AI automation is starting small. Choose one repetitive task that wastes your time, automate it with one of these tools, and measure the time saved.

Common first automation projects include:

  1. Email triage and response drafting
  2. Data entry and form processing
  3. Content research and summarization
  4. Social media content planning

Pro tip: Document your manual process step-by-step before automating. This makes it easier to translate into an AI workflow.

The breakthrough comes when we realize AI can not only gather information but understand context, prioritize relevance, and format content professionally. This n8n workflow demonstrates how automation transforms this tedious process into a seamless daily delivery.

RSS to Newsletter Transformation

Raw RSS feeds contain too much noise for professional consumption. The AI agent acts as an intelligent filter, identifying the 5-7 most relevant stories based on your specified criteria and rewriting them in a consistent brand voice.

The transformation process includes:

  1. Fetching all new items from configured RSS feeds
  2. Applying relevance scoring based on keywords and recency
  3. Generating concise summaries of selected stories
  4. Formatting content with consistent headings and styling
  5. Adding appropriate images and metadata

Pro Tip: The workflow can be extended to include sentiment analysis, automatically highlighting positive news about your company or negative trends about competitors.

Anatomy of a Perfect AI Agent Prompt

Think of prompts as your agent's DNA — they define everything from how it greets callers to how it handles complex requests. Every effective prompt structures three layers:

  1. Identity Layer: Gives your agent a name, role ("virtual receptionist for Dr. Smith's Dental"), and business context
  2. Behavior Layer: Sets tone (professional vs. casual), topics to cover, and conversational boundaries
  3. Decision Layer: Rules for handling bookings, transfers, payments, and special requests

The Carousel Content Bottleneck

Creating engaging carousel posts is one of the most time-consuming tasks in social media marketing. Between researching topics, writing captions, designing visuals, and maintaining brand consistency - what should be a 15-minute task often stretches into hours. The result? Most businesses either post inconsistently or resort to generic templates that fail to stand out.

The breakthrough came when we realized AI could handle the heavy lifting while maintaining human oversight. By combining OpenClaw's content generation with Make.com's automation capabilities, we built a system that delivers ready-to-post carousels with 90% less manual effort.

Key insight: The average business spends 5-7 hours weekly creating carousel content. This automation reduces that to about 30 minutes of review time while increasing output by 3-5x.

OpenClaw + Discord: Your Content Command Center

Discord might seem like an unconventional choice for business content creation, but it's the perfect interface for AI-assisted workflows. The chat format allows natural conversation with OpenClaw while keeping different content streams organized in separate channels.

In our implementation, the "carousel-generation" channel becomes your content dashboard. OpenClaw monitors this space for:

  • Automated news story suggestions (twice daily)
  • Your manual post requests ("Create a carousel about X")
  • Draft approvals/revisions
  • Completed carousel deliveries

The magic happens through Discord's webhook integration with Make.com. When you approve a carousel concept, OpenClaw triggers a series of automated steps that handle everything from image generation to final composition.

Automated News Monitoring & Post Suggestions

At 9 AM and 4 PM daily, OpenClaw scans your pre-configured news sources (RSS feeds, Google Alerts, or niche publications) for stories relevant to your audience. It evaluates each piece for:

  • Relevance to your brand messaging
  • Educational value for your followers
  • Visual storytelling potential

The AI then presents 3-5 story options in Discord with suggested angles for carousel treatment. You simply reply with which one(s) to develop - or request alternate approaches. This curation step ensures your content stays on-brand while leveraging the AI's ability to process dozens of sources instantly.

Pro Tip: Train OpenClaw by reviewing its initial suggestions and providing feedback. Over 2-3 weeks, its recommendations become remarkably aligned with your content strategy.

Carousel Generation Workflow Step-by-Step

Step 1: Content Drafting

After you select a news story, OpenClaw generates:

  • A compelling headline (adjustable via chat)
  • 3-5 key points for carousel slides
  • Image prompts for each visual element

Step 2: Visual Concepting

The AI creates detailed DALL-E prompts for:

  • Header image (with brand style parameters)
  • Supporting visuals for each content point
  • Any decorative elements (icons, borders, etc.)

Step 3: Template Application

Using Placid's API, Make.com applies your pre-designed templates to:

  • Size and position all visual elements
  • Add your brand colors and fonts
  • Ensure consistent layout across slides

In summary: OpenClaw handles content strategy and creative direction, while Make.com executes the technical assembly. You provide final approval before posting.

Creating Consistent Image Templates

Brand consistency comes from predefined templates in Placid (a design automation tool). We create two core templates:

Header Template:

  • Fixed position for headline text
  • Spot for a circular featured image
  • Background image area with safe zones
  • Your logo placement

Body Template:

  • Consistent typography hierarchy
  • Image display area with caption space
  • Branded color blocks
  • Slide counter (e.g., "2 of 5")

These templates ensure visual coherence while allowing content flexibility. At $20/month, Placid provides enough credits for 100+ carousels monthly.

The Make.com Automation Backend

Two Make.com scenarios power this workflow:

1. Header Carousel Generation:

  • Receives webhook from OpenClaw with content specs
  • Generates header image via DALL-E API
  • Creates circular featured image
  • Composites elements using Placid template
  • Sends preview to Discord

2. Body Slide Generation:

  • Processes each content point sequentially
  • Generates supporting visuals
  • Applies body template formatting
  • Compiles complete carousel slides
  • Delivers final images to Google Drive/Discord

The entire process takes 2-3 minutes per carousel after approval, running unattended.

Flexible Approval & Editing Process

Before finalizing any carousel, you can:

  • Request alternate images (OpenClaw provides new prompts)
  • Edit slide text directly in Discord
  • Adjust the content flow (add/remove slides)
  • Modify the tone/formality

The system remembers your preferences, improving its initial drafts over time. At 2:15 in the tutorial video, you'll see how easy it is to request five alternate header image concepts with a single Discord command.

Why manual posting? We recommend downloading approved carousels and posting manually (rather than full automation) to maintain authentic engagement and allow last-minute tweaks.

How This Make.com Blueprint Solves It

The automation works like an intelligent assistant that never sleeps. When someone submits your DIVI contact form, Make.com instantly:

  1. Checks for spam using AI (saving 3-5 minutes per submission)
  2. Extracts all form fields into structured JSON data
  3. Logs the clean lead to Google Sheets with timestamp
  4. Sends personalized acknowledgment emails in seconds
  5. Optionally alerts your team via Slack or SMS

Unlike basic autoresponders, this system maintains full context. The AI understands if "Need pricing ASAP" requires different handling than "General question." At 2:00 AM when you're asleep, your business still provides professional, instant engagement.

AI-Powered Spam Filtering Workflow

The first line of defense is a GPT-4 spam classifier that analyzes each submission's content, metadata, and patterns. Unlike basic keyword filters that block legitimate messages containing words like "free" or "quote," this AI understands context.

In testing, the system correctly identified 98.7% of spam while having a false positive rate under 1%. This means almost no real leads get accidentally filtered out. The module scores each submission from 0-100% spam likelihood, and you can adjust the threshold based on your volume.

Smart JSON Data Extraction

Traditional form handlers struggle with varying field names and formats. This blueprint uses another GPT-4 module trained as a "JSON master" that reliably extracts:

  • Standard fields (name, email, phone)
  • Custom DIVI form fields (dropdowns, checkboxes)
  • Unstructured message content
  • Hidden fields like page URL and submission time

The result is perfectly structured data ready for your CRM, even when clients leave fields blank or enter information in unexpected formats (like "Bob Smith" in a phone field).

Automatic Google Sheets Integration

Every filtered lead automatically populates a Google Sheet with:

  • Timestamp of submission
  • All extracted form fields
  • Spam score (for review)
  • Response status (sent/failed)

This creates a searchable lead database that's always current. Sales teams can sort by urgency, contact method, or custom tags added by the AI. The sheet also serves as an audit trail showing exactly when each lead was processed.

Custom Response Templates That Save Time

The system sends two types of automated emails:

  1. Customer acknowledgment: Immediate "we got your message" with estimated response time
  2. Internal alert: Summary of the inquiry to your team with all key details

Using simple HTML templates, you can maintain brand consistency while personalizing with the lead's name and specific inquiry details. The system even adds proper line breaks and formatting for professional presentation.

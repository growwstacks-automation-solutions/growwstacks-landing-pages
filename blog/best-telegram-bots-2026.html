Manyhat: Best for Beginners

If you've never built a Telegram bot before, Manyhat is your safest starting point. The platform removes all technical complexity with its intuitive flow builder that lets you create functional automations in minutes - no coding required.

What makes Manyhat stand out is its multi-channel support. While we're focusing on Telegram here, the same bot can deploy across WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, SMS, and email. This makes it ideal for businesses managing customer conversations across multiple platforms.

Key feature: Manyhat's live chat handoff automatically routes conversations to human agents when the bot can't answer, creating seamless transitions between automation and personal support.

Pros:

  • Easiest learning curve of all platforms tested
  • Free plan supports up to 1,000 contacts
  • Direct integrations with ChatGPT, HubSpot, and major email platforms
  • Zapier/Make connections for unlimited tool combinations

Cons:

  • Cannot use bots in Telegram groups/channels
  • AI add-on has limited capabilities compared to specialized platforms
  • Need to create separate messages per channel

Pricing starts at $15/month and scales with your contact count. The AI add-on requires an additional subscription.

Bulress: Best AI Features

For businesses needing advanced AI capabilities in their Telegram bots, Bulress outperforms all competitors. Its document training feature lets you upload PDFs, websites, or knowledge bases to create an AI that answers with your specific information.

The platform shines in multilingual environments, automatically translating conversations while maintaining brand voice consistency. Image understanding capabilities allow your bot to interpret and respond to photos - a rare feature among Telegram bot platforms.

Standout feature: Bulress can automatically summarize long conversations into key points, saving support teams hours of review time each week.

Pros:

  • Most advanced AI training capabilities
  • Generous free plan (2,000 messages/month)
  • Automatic conversation summarization
  • Image recognition and response

Cons:

  • Steeper learning curve than Manyhat
  • No Telegram group/channel support
  • Live chat only on $89+/month plans

Starting at just $5/month, Bulress offers exceptional AI value but requires more technical comfort than Manyhat.

UAT: Multi-Channel Publishing

UAT solves a critical pain point for businesses managing multiple communication channels: creating one bot that publishes everywhere. Unlike Manyhat where you build per channel, UAT lets you design once and deploy across Telegram, WhatsApp, Facebook, and more.

The platform balances power with usability, offering robust marketing automation features like broadcast sequences, A/B testing, and CRM integrations while maintaining a relatively intuitive interface. Over 50 direct integrations eliminate much of the need for Zapier/Make connections.

Time saver: UAT's centralized bot management cuts setup time by 60-70% compared to building separate bots for each channel.

Pros:

  • Single bot publishes across all channels
  • Works in Telegram groups (with limitations)
  • 50+ direct integrations including major CRMs
  • Strong marketing automation features

Cons:

  • Feature overload can overwhelm beginners
  • Limited Telegram group-specific actions
  • AI features not as robust as Bulress

Pricing mirrors Manyhat at $15/month starting point, scaling with contact volume.

Samples: Telegram Groups

For communities and businesses relying on Telegram groups, Samples offers unique functionality the others can't match. It provides specialized triggers like automatic welcome messages for new members, join request approval automation, and member management tools.

The platform excels at content distribution, automatically posting website updates or announcements to your Telegram channels. Unlike the other bots reviewed here, Samples treats groups and channels as first-class citizens rather than afterthoughts.

Community builder: Samples can automatically add new blog posts or product updates to your Telegram channels, keeping your community engaged with fresh content.

Pros:

  • Best Telegram group/channel support
  • Automatic content distribution
  • Member management tools
  • Join request automation

Cons:

  • Need separate bots per channel
  • Interface less polished than Manyhat/UAT
  • Limited multi-channel support

Pricing varies based on features needed, with no standardized monthly plans.

Choosing the Right Bot

Selecting the ideal Telegram bot platform depends on three key factors: your primary use case, technical comfort level, and budget. Here's our quick decision framework:

Simple rule: Choose Manyhat for ease, Bulress for AI, UAT for multi-channel, or Samples for Telegram groups. All offer free plans to test before committing.

By Use Case:

  • Customer Support: Manyhat (easiest) or UAT (more channels)
  • AI Conversations: Bulress (most advanced)
  • Community Building: Samples (best group features)
  • Marketing Automation: UAT (best sequences) or Manyhat (simpler)

By Skill Level:

  • Beginners: Manyhat
  • Intermediate: UAT or Samples
  • Advanced: Bulress

Remember all platforms integrate with Make.com and Zapier, allowing you to connect your Telegram bot to thousands of other tools regardless of which one you choose.

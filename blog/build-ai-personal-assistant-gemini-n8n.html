n8n Configuration: Free License Activation

Once your VPS is running, you'll land in the n8n interface. Here's how to activate all premium features for free:

1. Owner Account Setup

Enter your email when prompted to create the admin account. This controls access to your automation server.

2. License Activation

Click "Send me a free license key" and check your email. Paste this key into n8n to unlock all features without paying $20+/month.

3. Interface Overview

The left sidebar shows your workflows, credentials (app connections), and execution history. The templates section contains pre-built automations you can import.

No coding needed: n8n uses a visual workflow builder with drag-and-drop nodes. Each automation is like a flowchart showing triggers → actions → results. Most users learn the basics in one afternoon.

Daily Brief Workflow: Emails + Calendar in One Report

The first template automates what wastes most mornings - processing emails and calendar events. Here's how it works at 7 AM daily:

1. Data Collection

Connects to your Gmail and Google Calendar to scan new messages and upcoming events. You'll need to authenticate these accounts once.

2. AI Processing

Gemini 3 analyzes the raw data, identifying important emails, summarizing threads, and highlighting critical calendar items.

3. Delivery

The formatted report arrives via Telegram (or email) with: Key emails needing response, Today's schedule at a glance, and Action items prioritized by importance.

Time saved: 2 hours daily (10+ hours weekly) by eliminating morning inbox/calendar review. The AI identifies what actually matters so you start your day focused.

Automated Financial Reporting (Monthly P&L Analysis)

Business owners waste days each month compiling financials. This workflow automates the entire process:

1. Monthly Reminder

On the 1st of each month, Telegram prompts you to upload your P&L statement and transaction spreadsheet.

2. AI Analysis

Gemini 3 reviews the documents, identifying: Revenue trends vs previous periods, Unexpected expense spikes, Cash flow projections, and Tax optimization opportunities.

3. Executive Summary

You receive a plain-English report highlighting key insights and recommended actions - no accounting degree required to understand your numbers.

Example finding: "Office supply costs increased 37% month-over-month. Three vendors were used where one could suffice, potentially saving $420/month."

3. Content Brief

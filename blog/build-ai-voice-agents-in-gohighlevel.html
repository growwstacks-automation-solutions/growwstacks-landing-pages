The Knowledge Base Setup Hack

Most users make the mistake of starting with voice agent configuration. The secret? Begin with the knowledge base - this cuts setup time in half and dramatically improves agent performance.

The knowledge base is where your AI pulls information to answer questions. Without proper training, your agent will struggle with basic inquiries. Here's the two-step training process:

Step 1: Import Website Content

Go to AI Agents → Knowledge Base and create a new knowledge base for your sub-account. The system will crawl your website to extract:

  • Service pages with pricing information
  • FAQ sections
  • About/company information
  • Blog posts with industry knowledge

Step 2: Add Niche-Specific Knowledge

For optimal performance, supplement your website content with niche-specific information. Use this prompt in ChatGPT to generate comprehensive industry knowledge:

"Act as a [your industry] expert. Provide complete information about common services, pricing ranges, frequently asked customer questions, industry terminology, and typical customer concerns in this field."

Paste the output into a Google Sheet and add the link to your knowledge base. This creates an AI trained on both your specific business and general industry knowledge.

Agent Configuration: Voice & Personality

With your knowledge base ready, navigate to AI Agents → Voice Agents to create your virtual receptionist. The key configuration options:

Voice Selection

GoHighLevel offers multiple voice options across different languages. Test each one thoroughly - some sound more natural than others. Consider:

  • Industry fit: A cheerful voice for hospitality vs. professional tone for legal
  • Regional preferences: Local accents may build more trust
  • Call duration: Some voices sound better in longer conversations

Personality Settings

Under Advanced Settings, customize:

  • Response speed: Slight delays (300-500ms) make conversations feel more natural
  • Call limits: Set maximum duration to control costs (10-15 minutes recommended)
  • Idle reminders: Prompt after 4-5 seconds of silence

Pro Tip: Set your initial greeting to match your brand voice. "Thanks for calling [Business], this is [Agent Name], how can I help?" sounds more professional than generic openings.

Advanced Training With Custom Prompts

The default agent settings produce mediocre results. For truly human-like interactions, switch to Advanced Mode and use this custom prompt structure:

Prompt Framework:
"You are [Agent Name], the AI voice assistant for [Business Name]. Your personality is [cheerful/professional/etc]. When answering questions:
1. Always verify understanding before responding
2. Speak conversationally using contractions
3. For pricing questions, explain variables before offering ranges
4. Never make promises you can't keep"

This prompt does three critical things:

  1. Establishes the agent's identity and role
  2. Sets conversation guidelines for natural flow
  3. Provides guardrails for sensitive topics like pricing

Test different prompt variations and refine based on actual call performance. The best prompts produce agents that customers can't distinguish from humans.

Setting Up Call Actions & Transfers

The real power comes from integrating your voice agent with other GoHighLevel features. Configure these essential actions:

1. Call Transfers

Set triggers to transfer calls to your team when:

  • Customer says "representative" or "human"
  • Specific services are requested
  • Call exceeds your set duration limit

2. Workflow Triggers

Automatically start nurture sequences when:

  • Call ends without booking
  • Customer requests information
  • Specific keywords are mentioned

3. SMS Follow-Ups

Send post-call texts with:

  • Appointment confirmations
  • Requested information
  • Customer satisfaction surveys

Implementation Tip: Create a "Voice Agent" tag in your CRM that automatically triggers specific workflows. This lets you customize follow-ups based on call content.

24/7 Appointment Booking Setup

The most valuable feature? Automated appointment booking. Configure your calendar integration with these settings:

Calendar Selection

Connect the specific calendar you want appointments booked into. Consider creating a dedicated calendar for AI-booked appointments if you want to separate them.

Availability Rules

Set:

  • Days/hours you accept appointments
  • Buffer time between appointments
  • Maximum appointments per time slot

Booking Flow

The AI will:

  1. Offer available time slots
  2. Confirm appointment details
  3. Send confirmation via SMS/email
  4. Add to your calendar with all contact info

Conversion Tip: Add a pre-appointment SMS reminder workflow that sends service preparation instructions 1 hour before appointments. This reduces no-shows by up to 30%.

Phone Number Solutions & Call Forwarding

You're not limited to GoHighLevel numbers. Implement call forwarding to use your existing business number:

Option 1: Forward Existing Number

Most carriers allow call forwarding setup through:

  • Carrier website/portal
  • Mobile app settings
  • Dial codes (*72 + number)

Option 2: GoHighLevel Number as Primary

Purchase a local number in GoHighLevel ($1/month) and:

  • Update all marketing materials
  • Set as primary on Google Business Profile
  • Configure website call tracking

Hybrid Solution

Forward your main number to GoHighLevel while running parallel numbers for tracking. This lets you:

  • Keep your established number
  • Measure source-specific performance
  • Test different greeting scripts

Implementation Note: Call forwarding typically takes 2-3 business days to fully propagate across carriers. Test thoroughly before going live.

Testing & Optimization Strategies

Never deploy your AI agent without thorough testing. Use this checklist:

1. Basic Functionality Tests

  • Call from different phone numbers
  • Test during/after business hours
  • Verify appointment booking flow

2. Edge Case Scenarios

  • Mumbled/partial responses
  • Interruptions during conversation
  • Requests outside your service area

3. Performance Tracking

Monitor:

  • Average call duration
  • Transfer rate to humans
  • Appointment show rate

Optimization Tip: Review call logs weekly for phrases your agent struggled with. Add these to your knowledge base or create specific responses in Advanced Settings.

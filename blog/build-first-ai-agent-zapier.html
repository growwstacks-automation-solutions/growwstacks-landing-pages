Building the Gmail to Calendar Agent

Let's walk through the actual setup process using the template:

Step 1: Connect Your Apps

After selecting the template, you'll connect your Gmail and Google Calendar accounts. Zapier uses OAuth for secure access without storing your login credentials.

Step 2: Review the Default Settings

The template comes pre-configured to:

  • Check emails from the last 24 hours
  • Look for event-like language
  • Propose calendar entries for your approval

Step 3: Activate Your Agent

Once connected, click "Create" to launch your live AI assistant. The agent will immediately scan your inbox for potential events.

Approval workflow: Unlike fully automated solutions, this agent shows you each proposed calendar entry before creating it — giving you control while saving the manual work of setting up events.

Testing and Refining Your Agent

After activation, your AI agent will begin scanning emails. In the demo at 4:15, you'll see how it:

  1. Identifies five potential meeting emails from the past day
  2. Correctly flags a school sports festival notification
  3. Proposes appropriate calendar details including time and location

The agent explains its reasoning for each suggestion, allowing you to approve or reject the action. This feedback helps the AI learn your preferences over time.

To improve accuracy:

  • Adjust the agent's instructions if it misses certain event types
  • Flag false positives to teach the AI what to ignore
  • Extend the email search window beyond 24 hours if needed

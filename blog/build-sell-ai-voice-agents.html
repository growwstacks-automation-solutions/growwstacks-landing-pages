The 3 Components of High-Ticket Voice AI

Every effective voice agent requires three technical components working in harmony:

1. The Ear (Speech-to-Text)

Services like Deepgram convert spoken words into text with 98%+ accuracy, even with background noise. This feeds the conversation context to the brain.

2. The Brain (LLM)

GPT-4 or Claude 3.5 analyze the conversation, reference your knowledge base, and determine the optimal response. The system prompt defines the agent's persona ("You are Sarah, a professional sales closer").

3. The Voice (Text-to-Speech)

ElevenLabs provides expressive voices that convey warmth and professionalism. Their $5/month "Amy" voice has closed over $2M in appointments for our clients.

Pro Tip: Add an automation layer with Make.com to update CRMs and send confirmation emails during the call. This 24/7 follow-up system is what justifies $10k pricing.

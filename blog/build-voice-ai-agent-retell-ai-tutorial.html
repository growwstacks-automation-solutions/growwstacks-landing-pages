Creating Your First AI Agent

Click "Create Agent" and choose between four agent types. For beginners, the Single Prompt Agent works best - it handles simple notification calls where the conversation follows a predictable path.

Retell provides ready-made templates like "Parking Notification" (shown at 6:20 in the video) that you can customize. These templates include optimized prompts that handle common objections and questions naturally.

Step 1: Choose Your AI Model

Select GPT-5 Nano for most use cases - it's the fastest and most cost-effective option. The system shows real-time cost estimates as you build your prompt.

Step 2: Configure the Voice

Retell integrates with ElevenLabs' ultra-realistic voices. Choose from 20+ options across genders and accents. The "Simo" voice works well for professional notifications.

Pro tip: Enable background noise (coffee shop or call center ambiance) to make calls feel more authentic. This setting is under Speech Settings.

Voice Configuration Options

Fine-tune how your agent interacts using these key settings (shown at 11:45 in the video):

  • Responsiveness (1-5): How quickly the AI responds after the user stops speaking. 1 is fastest but can feel robotic.
  • Interruption Sensitivity (0-1): Lower values (0.8) prevent background noise from cutting off the AI mid-sentence.
  • Backchanneling: Enables verbal nods ("uh-huh", "I see") during user speech for natural flow.
  • Speech Normalization: Converts numbers/dates to spoken form ("twenty-four" instead of "24").

The system shows real-time latency estimates (aim for under 1000ms) and cost per minute as you adjust these settings.

Twilio Phone Number Setup

To make/receive calls, you need a phone number. Retell offers numbers for $2/month, but Twilio provides cheaper options ($1.15/month) with more flexibility.

Step-by-Step Twilio Configuration:

  1. Sign up at twilio.com (get $15 free credit)
  2. Buy a US phone number ($1.15/month)
  3. Create a Studio Flow to forward calls to your international number
  4. Verify your identity (required for outbound calls)
  5. Connect the number to your Retell agent

At 18:30 in the video, you'll see the exact Twilio settings needed. The process takes about 10 minutes including identity verification.

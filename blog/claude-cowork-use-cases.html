1. File Management Like Magic

How many hours have you wasted searching through cluttered folders or manually organizing files? Claude Cowork solves this by acting as your digital organizer that understands natural language commands.

Simply grant access to a folder (like your Desktop or Downloads) and Claude can:

  • Organize files by type into subfolders (images, documents, etc.)
  • Find lost files by searching content and metadata
  • Summarize document contents without opening them
  • Rename batches of files following your naming conventions

Real-world example: One user saved 3 hours weekly by having Claude automatically organize client project folders - sorting contracts, invoices, and assets into the correct subfolders based on file content.

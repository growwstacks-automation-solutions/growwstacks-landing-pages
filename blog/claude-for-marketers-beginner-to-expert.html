Projects vs. Artifacts: Organizing Your Work

Claude offers two powerful ways to organize marketing work: projects and artifacts. Projects (shown at 7:20) are ideal for content creation and campaign management, where you need Claude to maintain persistent context about your brand and objectives.

Artifacts (demonstrated at 8:45) are better for complex, iterative work like design projects. The video shows how artifacts allow real-time modification of designs, with the ability to view changes in both 2D and 3D. This is particularly valuable for marketers working on:

  • Landing page redesigns
  • Email template creation
  • Social media graphics
  • Presentation decks

The key is matching the right organizational tool to your marketing task. Projects maintain narrative continuity for content, while artifacts enable visual iteration for design work.

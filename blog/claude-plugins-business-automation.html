What Are Claude Plugins? (And Why They're Different)

Most business owners struggle with hiring and training specialized talent - whether for customer support, sales outreach or legal compliance. Claude plugins solve this by packaging complete AI specialists that can be installed with one click. These aren't just chatbots - they're configured virtual employees that complete entire projects.

Each plugin bundles together skills, commands, tool connections and workflow configurations for a specific business function. Unlike individual skills that handle single tasks, plugins manage complete roles with contextual understanding of your business.

The key difference: Skills train Claude on one workflow. Plugins train Claude for an entire job function with multiple interconnected skills, commands and the ability to connect to your business tools.

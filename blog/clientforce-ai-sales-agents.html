The Sales Automation Problem

Most sales teams operate in constant frustration - juggling manual prospecting, scattered follow-ups across email/LinkedIn/WhatsApp, delayed responses to inbound leads, and proposals sent too late to capitalize on interest. By the time you follow up, the momentum is gone and the deal goes cold.

Traditional CRMs just track these failures. Outreach tools automate blasting generic messages. What's missing is an intelligent system that connects all channels, responds in real-time, and actually moves deals forward autonomously.

The cost of manual sales: For every 100 leads, the average rep only contacts 19% more than once. Clientforce increases this to 92% through persistent, multi-touch automation that adapts based on engagement.

How Clientforce AI Agents Work

Clientforce deploys autonomous AI sales agents that handle your entire sales process from first touch to closed deal. You start by training your agent with your sales materials - pricing pages, case studies, product documentation - and defining your ideal outcomes: book qualified demos, close lost deals, or revive cold leads.

The system then works 24/7 through three core functions:

  1. Finder v2 continuously sources new prospects matching your criteria while verifying and deduplicating any uploaded lists
  2. Website visitors are instantly routed into appropriate campaigns based on their behavior
  3. One-click additions let you instantly enroll prospects you find while browsing LinkedIn or other platforms

Multi-Channel Orchestration

Clientforce doesn't just blast messages - it runs intelligent, coordinated flows across email, WhatsApp, LinkedIn, and AI voice. The system monitors engagement in real-time and adapts its approach:

  • If they open but don't reply → pivot to WhatsApp
  • If they view LinkedIn profile but don't connect → send tailored InMail
  • If email goes cold → initiate AI voice call

Every interaction is logged in a unified thread with context carried across channels. You see the complete history with next best action prompts guiding each follow-up.

AI Voice That Actually Closes

When email and messaging stall, Clientforce's AI voice agents make the call. These aren't robotic IVRs - they conduct natural conversations that:

  • Run discovery to understand needs
  • Handle objections with tailored rebuttals
  • Suggest meeting times that work
  • Book directly into your calendar

62% conversion rate: AI-booked meetings show up at nearly twice the rate of email-scheduled meetings because the conversation confirms real interest and eliminates calendar tennis.

Automated Proposal Generation

After a successful meeting, Clientforce generates personalized proposals built from your materials and tailored to the prospect's specific pains. The system tracks engagement in real-time:

  • See exactly how long they spend on pricing
  • Which sections they revisit
  • When they forward to other stakeholders

This insight lets your human team focus follow-up on the hottest opportunities at exactly the right moment.

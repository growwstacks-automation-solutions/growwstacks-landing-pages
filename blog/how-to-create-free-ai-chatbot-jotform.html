Getting Started: Account Setup

Creating your JotForm account takes less than a minute with social login options (Google, Microsoft, Facebook). The platform doesn't require credit card details for the free plan, making it risk-free to test chatbot capabilities.

Creating Your AI Agent

The magic begins when you click "Create AI Agent." JotForm asks you to describe your chatbot's purpose in natural language - for example, "customer support for my eCommerce store" or "educational assistant for students." The AI analyzes this description to pre-configure appropriate settings.

At the 4:30 mark in the tutorial, you'll see how adding your website URL allows the chatbot to match your brand's style automatically. This small detail creates a seamless experience for customers who transition from your site to the chat interface.

Pro tip: Name your agent something memorable but descriptive. "Muffy" might work for internal use, but "Acme Support Bot" helps customers understand its purpose immediately.

Customizing Your Chatbot

JotForm offers deep customization without complexity. The "Build" tab lets you set welcome messages, add quick-reply buttons, and choose between minimalist or detailed conversation styles. At 6:45 in the video, notice how changing the agent's voice and tone affects sample responses.

The platform supports 20+ languages and various personality settings (professional, friendly, casual). These adjustments make your chatbot sound authentically like your brand rather than a generic assistant. Color schemes and layout options ensure visual consistency with your website.

Training the Knowledge Base

Your chatbot's intelligence comes from the Knowledge Base section where you upload company documents, FAQs, product manuals, or website links. JotForm's AI processes this information to answer questions accurately, as demonstrated at 9:20 in the tutorial.

For best results, include diverse materials: PDF brochures, help center articles, and even YouTube videos explaining your services. The system continuously improves as it handles real conversations, learning which responses satisfy users most effectively.

Training hack: Add a "Company Overview" document with your unique value proposition, common customer objections, and service guarantees. This helps your chatbot communicate your differentiators clearly.

Publishing Your Chatbot

When ready, publishing your chatbot takes one click. JotForm generates embed codes for website placement (floating button, inline, or popup) and provides direct links for standalone access.

Why Automate HubSpot with n8n

Manual data entry between HubSpot and other business tools creates bottlenecks and errors. Sales teams waste hours copying information between systems, while marketing struggles to keep customer data synchronized across platforms.

n8n solves this by creating automated workflows that connect HubSpot to thousands of other apps. Unlike Zapier or Make, n8n offers enterprise-grade automation with visual workflow building and advanced error handling - all without requiring developers.

Businesses automating HubSpot with n8n typically save 8-15 hours per week by eliminating manual data transfers between CRM, email, accounting, and project management systems.

Step 1: Access HubSpot Marketplace

The installation process begins in your HubSpot account. Make sure you're signed in with admin privileges before proceeding.

From your HubSpot dashboard:

  1. Locate the marketplace icon in the top navigation bar (it looks like a shopping bag)
  2. Click the icon and select "HubSpot Marketplace" from the dropdown
  3. You'll be redirected to HubSpot's app marketplace

Pro Tip: If you don't see the marketplace option, check your user permissions. You'll need at least Marketing or Sales Admin rights to install apps.

Step 2: Find n8n Integration

With the marketplace open, use the search functionality to locate the n8n integration.

  1. Click in the search bar in the upper right corner
  2. Type "n8n" and press Enter
  3. Look for "n8n Cloud" in the results (this is the hosted version)

At the time of this guide, n8n Cloud is the primary integration available through the HubSpot marketplace. Self-hosted n8n instances require additional configuration not covered in this installation walkthrough.

Step 3: Review Integration Details

Before installing, take a moment to understand what you're adding to your HubSpot account.

On the n8n listing page:

  1. Check the preview images to see the interface
  2. Review the key details sidebar for version info
  3. Scroll down to read the full description

This quick review helps confirm n8n meets your needs before proceeding with installation. Pay special attention to any listed limitations or requirements.

Step 4: Install and Authenticate

Once you've confirmed n8n meets your needs, click the install button to begin the process.

  1. Click the "Install" button on the n8n listing page
  2. A new tab will open for authentication
  3. Select "Sign In" or "Login" in the upper right
  4. Follow the on-screen prompts to authenticate

The exact screens may vary slightly, but the flow is straightforward. You'll be guided through connecting your HubSpot account to n8n Cloud.

Important: Have your HubSpot login credentials ready. You may need to re-authenticate during this process.

Permissions to Expect

During installation, you'll encounter a permissions screen listing what data n8n can access in your HubSpot account.

Typical permissions requested include:

  • Read/write access to contacts and companies
  • Deal pipeline visibility
  • Workflow automation capabilities
  • CRM object access

Always review these permissions carefully before approving. The exact permissions may vary based on your HubSpot subscription level and the specific n8n workflows you plan to implement.

Next Steps After Installation

With n8n successfully installed in HubSpot, you're ready to start building workflows.

Recommended first steps:

  1. Explore the n8n interface to familiarize yourself
  2. Review pre-built HubSpot workflow templates
  3. Start with simple automations like contact syncing
  4. Graduate to more complex workflows as you gain confidence

Most users create their first working HubSpot automation within 30 minutes of completing installation by starting with template workflows.

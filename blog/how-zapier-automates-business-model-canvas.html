Building Multi-Step Zaps for Complex Workflows

While single-step Zaps (like Gmail → Slack notifications) provide quick wins, the real power comes from multi-step workflows that mirror your operational processes:

Step 1: Trigger

Select an initiating event (new form submission, calendar event, file upload).

Step 2: Action

Define what happens next (create CRM record, send email, generate document).

Step 3: Filter

Add conditions (only if amount > $500, unless status = canceled).

Step 4: Secondary Action

Chain additional steps (notify manager, update spreadsheet, post to channel).

Example: A recruiting firm automated their entire candidate pipeline - from application to offer letter - with a 12-step Zap connecting LinkedIn, Greenhouse, Gmail, DocuSign, and their ATS.

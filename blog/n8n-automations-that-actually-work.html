1. Invoice Processing System (95% Time Savings)

Accounting teams waste hours each week manually entering invoice data - a process prone to errors and delays. The n8n invoice automation changes everything by extracting key fields automatically and updating your systems in seconds.

When a new PDF invoice lands in your Google Drive folder, the workflow:

  1. Extracts text from the PDF using OCR
  2. Parses key fields (invoice number, client details, amounts, dates)
  3. Adds structured data to your accounting database
  4. Sends email confirmation to your billing team

Real-world impact: Businesses processing 100+ invoices monthly save 15+ hours weekly. One accounting firm reduced data entry errors by 82% while cutting invoice processing time from 3 days to 15 minutes.

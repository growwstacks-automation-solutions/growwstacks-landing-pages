How Talk2Site Works

At its core, Talk2Site is an AI layer that understands your business by crawling your website and processing any additional documents you provide. Unlike basic chatbots, it doesn't rely on pre-written responses - it comprehends your actual services, pricing, and policies to give accurate answers.

The system uses OpenAI's GPT models combined with proprietary technology to create a vectorized knowledge base specific to your business. When a visitor asks a question, the AI searches this knowledge base rather than generating generic responses. This dramatically reduces incorrect information while maintaining natural conversation flow.

Facebook lead ads generate tire-kickers. Traditional follow-up burns staff time. Our secret sauce? Voice AI outbound calls triggered by form submissions (available in beta at recording time).

Not all businesses have outbound access yet. The workaround? Make calling part of your conversion path.

After form submission, direct leads to "Call Chloe at [number] to schedule." Your Voice AI then:

4. Luxury Appointment Reminders

Text reminders get ignored. Email reminders go to spam. Voice AI calls feel personal while being 100% automated.

Configure your AI to call 24-48 hours before appointments to:

  • Confirm attendance ("We're all set for tomorrow at 2 PM - still good for you?")

  • Reschedule if needed

Beauty industry insight: Salons using Voice AI reminders see 52% fewer cancellations while clients report feeling "pampered" by the extra touchpoint - all without staff lifting a finger.

  • Gather testimonials
  • Upsell related services

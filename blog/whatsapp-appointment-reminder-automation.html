How the Automation Works

The workflow triggers when a client books through your Calendly scheduling page. Within seconds, they receive a WhatsApp confirmation with their appointment details. Then exactly 24 hours before their scheduled time, they get a second reminder message.

Here's the technical flow:

  1. Client books appointment in Calendly
  2. PAB Connect detects new booking instantly
  3. First WhatsApp message sent immediately (confirmation)
  4. Workflow pauses until 24 hours before appointment
  5. Second WhatsApp message sent (reminder)

The entire process requires zero manual intervention after setup. At 4:32 in the video tutorial, you'll see how the delay function precisely calculates the 24-hour reminder timing.

Connecting Calendly to WhatsApp

The magic happens through PAB Connect's no-code integration platform. You'll connect:

  • Your Calendly account (trigger)
  • WhatsApp via PAB Chat Flow (actions)
  • Optional: Date formatter for timezone adjustments

Setting up the connection takes under 5 minutes:

  1. Log in to PAB Connect
  2. Create new workflow
  3. Select Calendly as trigger ("Invitee Created" event)
  4. Connect your Calendly account
  5. Add PAB Chat Flow as action ("Send Text Message")
  6. Authorize WhatsApp connection

Customizing Your Reminder Messages

Your WhatsApp messages should reflect your brand voice while providing clear value. We recommend this structure:

  • Confirm date/time in their timezone
  • Include meeting link if virtual
  • Add cancellation instructions

24-hour reminder message:

  • Friendly reminder of upcoming appointment
  • Reconfirm time (with timezone)
  • Add value tip related to the session

Setting the 24-Hour Reminder

The workflow's smart delay feature is what makes this automation truly valuable. Here's how it works:

  1. Captures exact appointment time from Calendly
  2. Subtracts exactly 24 hours
  3. Adjusts for timezone differences automatically
  4. Pauses workflow until calculated reminder time
  5. Triggers WhatsApp message at the perfect moment

This means your reminders always go out at the right time, whether your client is in New York or New Delhi. At 12:30 in the video, the tutorial shows the precise time calculation in action.

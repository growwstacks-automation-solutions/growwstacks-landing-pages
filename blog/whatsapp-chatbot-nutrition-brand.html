Why Every Nutrition Brand Needs a WhatsApp Chatbot

Nutrition businesses face unique customer service challenges - clients need immediate answers about meal contents, dietary restrictions, and personalized advice at all hours. Human staff can't be available 24/7, leading to frustrated customers and lost sales.

An AI chatbot solves this by providing instant, accurate responses to common queries while collecting necessary details for human follow-up when needed. The demo shows three critical advantages:

  • 24/7 availability: Answers questions anytime without staff overhead
  • Consistent information: Provides standardized responses about plans and policies
  • Lead qualification: Gathers key details (age, weight, goals) before human interaction

For nutrition brands scaling quickly, this automation means being able to handle 5x more customer inquiries without proportionally increasing support staff.

Configuring Your AI Nutrition Agent

Creating your first AI assistant (6:30 in the video) involves several key configuration steps that determine how your chatbot will interact with customers:

1. AI Instructions

Select "AI Agent" as the instruction type. The system pre-populates basic instructions that you'll enhance with your nutrition brand specifics. This determines the bot's conversational style and scope.

2. AI Configuration

The temperature setting (0-1) controls response creativity. For nutrition advice, 0.7 provides a balance between accuracy and natural conversation flow. You'll select OpenAI as the AI provider and choose a model (GPT-5 mini in the demo).

3. API Key Connection

You'll need an OpenAI API key from a paid account. The video shows generating this in the OpenAI dashboard - create a new secret key, copy it, and paste into Pably Chatflow to establish the connection.

Pro tip: The platform allows setting stop keywords (like "stop" or "human") that customers can use to exit automated conversations and request human assistance when needed.

Building Your Nutrition Knowledge Base

The most critical component (9:15 in the demo) is uploading your detailed nutrition knowledge base. This PDF or DOCX file should include:

  • Company overview and mission
  • Meal plan categories and descriptions
  • Nutrition logic and sample daily menus
  • Pricing structures and delivery information
  • Subscription and renewal policies
  • FAQ section with common questions
  • WhatsApp conversation logic examples

The AI will reference this document to answer questions, so completeness and clarity are essential. The demo shows a well-structured Google Doc containing all these elements being downloaded as a PDF and uploaded to the system.

Note: PDFs with images are limited to 10 pages - focus on concise, well-organized text information for best results.

Customizing the Chat Interface

At 10:40, the demo walks through personalizing how your chatbot appears to customers:

1. Assistant Interface

Change the heading name and subheading to match your brand. You can add initial greeting messages that set the tone for conversations.

2. Styling Options

Choose between light/dark mode, assistant shape (circle/square), and customize colors for background, text, and chat bubbles to match your brand identity.

3. Profile Pictures

Upload header images, assistant profile pictures, and background images to create a cohesive visual experience.

Once configured, toggle the assistant to "on" and save. Your chatbot is now ready to be assigned to conversations.

Assigning the AI Assistant to Chats

The final step (12:50 in the video) is determining which conversations your nutrition chatbot will handle. You have three assignment options:

1. Individual Chats

Manually assign the AI to specific conversations by toggling it on and selecting your assistant. This works for testing but isn't scalable.

2. Contact Lists

Create lists (like "New Leads" or "Active Subscribers") and assign different AI assistants to each list for targeted responses.

3. All Contacts

Enable auto-replies for all incoming messages, with rules to escalate complex queries to human staff.

The inbox settings panel lets you mix these approaches - perhaps using one AI for sales inquiries and another for customer support, all running automatically.

3 Ways to Use Make and HubSpot to Connect Business Processes and Create Complex Workflows

Nearly every business operation can benefit from integration and automation. The key lies in identifying the most efficient methods and partnering with technology you trust. Make and HubSpot offer a robust combination for achieving this.

By leveraging Make's powerful visual builder and HubSpot's comprehensive CRM capabilities, businesses can create seamless connections that transform their operational landscape. This section will explore three key strategies for maximizing the synergy between these platforms.

1. Create Complex Integrations and Workflows to Solve Business Challenges

Make scenarios are designed to execute multiple actions in a sequential chain across various platforms, enabling the automation of intricate business processes. This capability is particularly valuable for solving complex challenges that involve data transfer and actions across different software tools.

For instance, imagine a workflow triggered when a contact in your HubSpot CRM meets specific criteria, such as indicating readiness to sign a contract by scheduling a sales meeting. A Make scenario can then automatically create and edit a Google Doc template for the contract, populate it with relevant contact information from HubSpot, and send it as a PDF attachment via email.

This entire transaction is then automatically logged and captured within the HubSpot CRM, providing a complete, automated, end-to-end process without any manual intervention or coding. This ensures accuracy, saves time, and keeps all relevant information centralized.

You can further extend this workflow by adding an automated trigger to send an alert or a Slack notification to the sales team once the contract is signed. This ensures real-time updates and keeps all stakeholders informed, enhancing overall efficiency and responsiveness.

Pro tip: When designing complex workflows, always map out the entire process visually first. This helps identify all necessary steps, data points, and potential integration points, making the automation build much smoother and more effective.

2. Consolidate Workflows and Reduce Manual Tasks

If your team frequently switches between different applications to complete a single process, it's time to consolidate. Make allows you to combine disparate workflows into a single, streamlined automation that manages all incoming data efficiently. This approach centralizes information and reduces the likelihood of errors that often occur with manual data entry.

Consider a scenario where a new lead comes in through a Facebook Lead Ad. A Make automation can automatically add this lead as a contact in both MailerLite (for email marketing) and HubSpot CRM, ensuring that all relevant properties are consistently mapped across both tools. This eliminates the need for manual data entry into multiple systems, saving significant time and ensuring data accuracy from the outset.

By automating this lead capture and distribution process, businesses can ensure that new prospects are immediately entered into their marketing and sales funnels, allowing for quicker engagement and follow-up. This not only boosts efficiency but also improves the overall lead management experience.

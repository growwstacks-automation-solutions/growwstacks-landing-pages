Automate Your Intercom Tasks with Make

Integrating Intercom with Make.com is a straightforward process that can significantly reduce manual effort and boost efficiency. The initial setup involves a few simple steps to establish a secure connection between your Intercom account and the Make platform, paving the way for powerful automations.

To begin, you'll need to create your first scenario in Make that involves Intercom. When you select an Intercom module within your scenario, the module settings panel will prompt you to "Add a connection." This is where you'll initiate the link between the two platforms.

Upon clicking "Add," a pop-up window will appear, asking you to name your connection. While "My Intercom Connection" is a common choice, you can use any name that helps you identify it easily. After naming it, click "Continue." Make will then securely redirect you to the Intercom website. On the Intercom site, you'll be asked to grant Make access to your account. Simply click the "Connect" button to authorize this access. Once authorized, you will be automatically redirected back to your Make scenario, and your Intercom connection will be successfully established. From this point, you can proceed to build and refine your automation scenario, leveraging the newly created connection.

Pro tip: Always use a descriptive name for your connections in Make. This becomes especially helpful as you build more complex workflows with multiple connections, making it easier to manage and troubleshoot your automations.

1. Create Intercom Users from New Google Forms Entries

Many businesses rely on Google Forms for various purposes, including new user sign-ups, lead generation, or gathering customer feedback. Manually transferring this information into Intercom can be a time-consuming and error-prone task. Make offers a seamless solution to automate this process, ensuring that every new entry in your Google Form instantly translates into a new user in Intercom.

With this automation, every time a new response is submitted through your designated Google Form, Make will automatically trigger the creation of a new user profile within your Intercom account. This eliminates the need for manual data entry, reduces delays in engaging with new contacts, and ensures that your Intercom database is always up-to-date with the latest information from your forms. It's an efficient way to streamline your onboarding and initial outreach efforts.

Automate the creation of Intercom users directly from new Google Forms submissions.

2. Add New Copper Leads to Intercom as Users

For sales teams utilizing Copper CRM, efficiently moving leads into your customer communication platform is crucial for timely engagement. Manually transferring new leads from Copper to Intercom can introduce delays and potential data inconsistencies, hindering your sales pipeline's momentum. Make provides an automation to bridge this gap, ensuring your leads are always where they need to be.

By setting up a Make scenario, every time a new lead is created in Copper, a corresponding new user will be automatically generated in Intercom. This ensures that your sales and marketing teams have immediate access to the latest lead information in both systems, facilitating prompt follow-ups and personalized communication. This integration not only saves time but also helps maintain a consistent and accurate view of your leads across your CRM and messaging platforms.

Automatically sync new leads from Copper CRM to Intercom for streamlined engagement.

3. Add New Intercom Users to HubSpot as Users

Maintaining a unified customer database across all your business tools is essential for effective customer relationship management. If you're using Intercom for customer messaging and HubSpot CRM for managing your contacts, ensuring that new Intercom users are reflected in HubSpot can be a manual burden. Make offers a solution to automate this synchronization, keeping your CRM up-to-date effortlessly.

This Make scenario works in reverse, allowing you to automatically transfer new users created in Intercom directly into your HubSpot CRM as new contacts. This ensures that your CRM always has the most current list of your Intercom users, enabling consistent communication, targeted marketing campaigns, and accurate reporting across both platforms. This automation is vital for businesses looking to maintain a single source of truth for their customer data and avoid discrepancies between systems.

Seamlessly transfer new Intercom users to HubSpot CRM as contacts.

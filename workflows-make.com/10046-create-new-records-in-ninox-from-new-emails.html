What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of tracking and organizing email communications by automatically creating structured records in your Ninox database. Instead of manually copying information or losing important messages in crowded inboxes, every relevant email becomes a searchable, categorized database entry.

The workflow monitors your email account for new messages, extracts key information (sender, subject, body, attachments), and creates corresponding records in Ninox with all relevant metadata. This creates a centralized knowledge base for customer communications, support tickets, or internal correspondence that's easily searchable and reportable.

How It Works

1. Email Trigger

The workflow starts when a new email arrives in your connected email account. You can configure filters to only process emails matching specific criteria (sender, subject keywords, labels).

2. Data Extraction

Key information is extracted from each email including sender details, subject line, body content, timestamps, and any attachments. The system can be configured to parse specific data patterns if needed.

3. Ninox Record Creation

The extracted email data is mapped to corresponding fields in your Ninox database. The workflow creates a new record with all relevant information properly structured and categorized.

Pro tip: Add custom fields in Ninox to categorize emails by department, priority, or status for even better organization and reporting.

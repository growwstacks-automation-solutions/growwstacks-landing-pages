What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point in Sellsy CRM management - keeping opportunity statuses synchronized with their associated quotes. When sales teams mark a quote as "won" or "accepted" in Sellsy, the corresponding opportunity often remains in an earlier pipeline stage, creating inaccurate sales reports and forecasting.

The workflow automatically detects when a quote status changes to "won" or "accepted" in Sellsy, then updates the linked opportunity's status to "won" within seconds. This ensures your sales pipeline always reflects the true state of deals without requiring manual CRM updates from your team.

How It Works

1. Quote Status Monitoring

The workflow continuously monitors your Sellsy account for changes to quote statuses. It specifically watches for quotes transitioning to "won" or "accepted" status.

2. Opportunity Lookup

When a qualifying status change is detected, the system identifies the opportunity linked to that quote in Sellsy using the native relationship between these objects.

3. Status Update Execution

The workflow then makes an API call to Sellsy to update the opportunity's status field to "won", maintaining perfect synchronization between your quotes and opportunities.

4. Error Handling & Notifications

If any step fails (like if an opportunity can't be found), the system logs the error and can notify administrators via email for manual resolution.

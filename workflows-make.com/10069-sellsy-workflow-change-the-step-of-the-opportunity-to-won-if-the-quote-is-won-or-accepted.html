What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common challenge for sales teams using Sellsy CRM - keeping opportunity statuses synchronized with quote statuses. When a quote moves to "won" or "accepted" status, the corresponding opportunity should reflect this success by updating to "won" status, but manual updates often get overlooked in busy sales environments.

The workflow automatically detects when a quote status changes to won/accepted in Sellsy, then updates the linked opportunity to won status. This ensures your sales pipeline always accurately reflects closed deals, improving forecasting accuracy and eliminating manual data entry for your sales team.

How It Works

1. Quote Status Monitoring

The workflow continuously monitors your Sellsy account for changes to quote statuses. It specifically watches for when quotes transition to "won" or "accepted" status.

2. Opportunity Lookup

When a qualifying status change is detected, the system identifies the opportunity linked to that quote in Sellsy's database.

3. Status Update

The workflow then updates the identified opportunity's status to "won" in Sellsy, completing the sales cycle tracking automatically.

4. Error Handling

Built-in checks ensure the workflow only proceeds when appropriate, preventing incorrect updates if quotes or opportunities are modified separately.

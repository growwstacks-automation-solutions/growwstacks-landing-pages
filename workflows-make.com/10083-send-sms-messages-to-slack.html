Forward Telnyx SMS to Slack

Automatically route incoming SMS messages to Slack channels for better team visibility and response coordination.

Get This Workflow → Make.com · SMS · Free Template

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of SMS messages being trapped in individual employee phones or separate communication systems. When customer inquiries, appointment reminders, or order notifications come via SMS, they often get lost in personal inboxes where teams can't collaborate on responses.

The workflow automatically forwards every SMS message received through your Telnyx number to a designated Slack channel. This creates complete visibility across your team while maintaining a centralized communication record. Customer service teams can respond faster, sales teams stay informed about client interactions, and operations teams get real-time order updates - all within the Slack environment they already use daily.

How It Works

1. SMS Received at Telnyx

The workflow triggers whenever your Telnyx phone number receives an SMS message. Telnyx's robust API captures the message content along with sender details and timestamps.

2. Message Processing in Make

Make.com receives the SMS data and can optionally transform it - adding labels, filtering spam messages, or extracting key information like order numbers from the text.

3. Delivery to Slack

The processed message gets posted to your specified Slack channel. The notification includes the sender's phone number, message content, and timestamp for complete context.

Pro tip: Configure different Slack channels for various message types. Route customer support SMS to #help-desk while order confirmations go to #fulfillment.

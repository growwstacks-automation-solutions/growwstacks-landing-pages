What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of losing track of important SMS communications. Many businesses receive critical customer messages via text but struggle to maintain proper records or share these communications with team members who may not have access to the SMS platform.

The workflow automatically detects new SMS messages received through Telnyx and converts them into formatted email notifications. This creates a searchable archive of customer communications in your email system, enables easy forwarding to relevant team members, and ensures no important messages slip through the cracks.

How It Works

1. Telnyx triggers on new SMS

The workflow starts when Telnyx detects an incoming SMS message to your registered number. Make.com monitors this trigger continuously.

2. Message content processing

The system extracts key details from the SMS including sender number, timestamp, and message body. You can configure additional parsing rules if needed.

3. Email composition

Make.com formats the SMS content into a professional email template. You can customize the subject line, sender information, and message formatting.

4. Email delivery

The composed email is sent through Make's email module to your specified recipients. You can send to multiple addresses, CC relevant teams, or even route to different recipients based on message content.

Pro tip: Add a delay of 1-2 minutes before sending the email to allow for quick SMS replies that should be bundled into a single notification.

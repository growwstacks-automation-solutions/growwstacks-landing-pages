What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates manual work in hotel billing by automatically generating apaleo payment links and sending them to guests via Microsoft 365 email. Designed specifically for hospitality businesses using the apaleo property management system, it ensures guests receive payment requests promptly while reducing front desk workload.

The workflow connects apaleo's payment API with Microsoft 365's email capabilities to create a seamless billing experience. When a guest's folio reaches payment status, the system generates a secure payment link, composes a professional email with booking details, and delivers it through your official hotel email channel - all without staff intervention.

How It Works

1. Trigger on payment-ready folios

The workflow monitors your apaleo account for folios that require payment. This can be configured to trigger at checkout time, after a deposit deadline, or based on custom business rules in your PMS.

2. Generate secure payment links

For each qualifying folio, the system requests a unique payment link from apaleo's API. These tokens include encryption and can be set with specific payment methods, amounts, and expiration dates according to your property's policies.

3. Compose professional emails

Using Microsoft 365's email service, the automation drafts a complete message with the guest's booking details, payment amount, and the secure payment link. Templates can be customized with your hotel's branding and standard messaging.

4. Send and track delivery

The completed email is sent through your official Microsoft 365 account, maintaining brand consistency. Delivery status is logged for reporting, and failed sends automatically trigger retry protocols.

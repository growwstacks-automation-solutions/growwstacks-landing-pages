What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical pain point for hotels using Apaleo's property management system. When guests check out, some reservations may have open folios (outstanding charges) without corresponding invoices. Manually tracking these cases is time-consuming and prone to errors, leading to lost revenue and accounting discrepancies.

The workflow automatically identifies all checked-out reservations with open folios that lack invoices, then generates the necessary invoices with complete charge details. It runs on a schedule you define (typically daily), ensuring no revenue slips through the cracks while freeing your staff from repetitive manual billing tasks.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow activates automatically at your specified time (e.g., daily after night audit). It connects to your Apaleo account via API to fetch reservation data.

2. Reservation Filtering

The system scans all reservations, identifying those that meet three criteria: checked-out status, open folio balance, and no existing invoice. You can customize filters based on property needs.

3. Folio Analysis

For each qualifying reservation, the workflow retrieves the detailed folio containing all guest charges (room rates, amenities, taxes, etc.) to prepare for invoice generation.

4. Invoice Creation

The system automatically generates professional invoices in Apaleo with all folio items properly categorized. Invoice numbers follow your property's sequencing convention.

5. Notification & Logging

Optionally, the workflow can email summaries to accounting staff and log all actions for auditing purposes. Failed cases trigger alerts for manual review.

Pro tip: Run this automation multiple times daily during peak seasons to maintain real-time financial accuracy and prevent billing backlogs.

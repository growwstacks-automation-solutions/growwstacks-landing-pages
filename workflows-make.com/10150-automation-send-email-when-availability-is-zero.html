What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of real-time inventory monitoring in Apaleo, your property management system. When room availability for specific dates drops to zero, the workflow instantly sends email alerts to your designated team members. This eliminates manual checking while ensuring you never miss important inventory changes that could impact revenue.

For hoteliers and property managers, this automation provides peace of mind by creating an automatic safety net. Whether you're dealing with last-minute cancellations, overbookings, or unexpected sell-outs, the system keeps your team informed the moment availability changes occur in Apaleo.

How It Works

1. Apaleo Availability Monitoring

The workflow continuously checks your Apaleo account for availability changes using scheduled API calls. It specifically monitors for dates when inventory reaches zero across your property types.

2. Zero Availability Detection

When the system identifies a date with no available inventory, it triggers the alert sequence. The workflow captures all relevant details including property name, date, and current booking statistics.

3. Email Alert Generation

The template composes a professional email with all critical information formatted for quick action. You can customize the email template to include your branding, specific instructions, or additional data points.

4. Smart Notification Delivery

Emails are sent to your pre-configured distribution list through your connected email service. The system includes error handling to ensure delivery even during connectivity issues.

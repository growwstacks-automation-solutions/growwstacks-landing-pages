What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for hotels using Apaleo's property management system - accurately categorizing reservations by market segment without manual intervention. Market segmentation is essential for understanding guest demographics, optimizing pricing strategies, and personalizing marketing efforts, but manually assigning segments to each new reservation is time-consuming and prone to errors.

The workflow automatically analyzes new reservations as they're created in Apaleo and assigns the appropriate market segment based on predefined rules. This ensures consistent categorization across all bookings while eliminating the need for front desk staff or revenue managers to manually tag each reservation.

How It Works

1. New reservation trigger

The workflow monitors Apaleo for newly created reservations in real-time. When a booking comes in, the automation immediately captures all relevant details including booking source, rate plan, guest information, and special requests.

2. Segment determination

Based on your configured business rules, the system evaluates the reservation details to determine the appropriate market segment. Common segmentation factors include booking channel (OTA, direct, corporate), rate type (BAR, promotional), length of stay, and guest history.

3. Automatic assignment

The workflow updates the reservation in Apaleo with the correct market segment tag. This happens instantly, ensuring your reporting and analytics always reflect accurate segmentation data.

4. Optional notifications

You can configure the system to send alerts when specific segment types are booked (like high-value corporate accounts) or when exceptions occur that require manual review.

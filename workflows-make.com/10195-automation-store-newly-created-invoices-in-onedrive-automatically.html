What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point for businesses using Apaleo for invoicing - the manual process of downloading and storing invoice PDFs. Every time Apaleo generates a new invoice, the workflow automatically retrieves the document and saves it to your designated OneDrive folder with proper naming conventions.

Financial teams waste hours each month on repetitive file management tasks. This workflow eliminates that manual work while ensuring consistent document organization. Your invoices remain securely backed up in Microsoft's cloud storage, accessible from anywhere while maintaining a clear audit trail.

How It Works

1. New Invoice Detection

The workflow monitors your Apaleo account through its API, instantly detecting when a new invoice is generated. This trigger happens in real-time, not through periodic checks.

2. PDF Retrieval

Make.com securely accesses the invoice PDF through Apaleo's API, maintaining all document integrity and security protocols during the transfer process.

3. OneDrive Storage

The system then uploads the invoice to your pre-configured OneDrive folder. You can specify exact folder paths and naming conventions to match your existing document management system.

4. Confirmation & Error Handling

The workflow confirms successful transfers and includes built-in error recovery. If any issues occur during the process, you'll receive notifications to address them promptly.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Clone the template in your Make.com account
  2. Connect your Apaleo account via OAuth
  3. Authorize access to your OneDrive
  4. Specify your target folder path in OneDrive
  5. Test with a sample invoice to verify the flow
  6. Activate the scenario for automatic processing

Pro tip: Create separate OneDrive folders by year/month to maintain organized archives automatically. The workflow can append dates to filenames or create subfolders based on your preferences.

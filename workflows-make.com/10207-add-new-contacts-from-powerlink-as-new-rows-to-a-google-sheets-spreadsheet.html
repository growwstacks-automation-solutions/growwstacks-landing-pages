What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point for sales and marketing teams who use Fireberry for contact management but need to maintain a separate spreadsheet for reporting, analysis, or sharing with stakeholders. Manually copying contact data between systems is time-consuming and prone to errors.

The workflow automatically identifies new contacts added to Fireberry each day and adds them as new rows in your specified Google Sheets spreadsheet. It runs on a daily schedule (configurable to your needs) and only transfers contacts created since the last sync, ensuring your spreadsheet stays current without duplicate entries.

How It Works

1. Daily Trigger

The scenario runs automatically each day at your specified time, checking Fireberry for new contacts added in the past 24 hours.

2. Contact Retrieval

Using Fireberry's API, the workflow fetches all contact records created since the last successful run, including standard fields like name, email, phone, and any custom fields you specify.

3. Data Formatting

Contact information is formatted to match your Google Sheets column structure, with options to transform data (like combining first/last names) or filter out unwanted contacts.

4. Spreadsheet Update

Each new contact is added as a row at the bottom of your designated Google Sheet, with fields mapped to the appropriate columns.

Pro tip: Add a timestamp column to track exactly when each contact was added to your spreadsheet for better reporting.

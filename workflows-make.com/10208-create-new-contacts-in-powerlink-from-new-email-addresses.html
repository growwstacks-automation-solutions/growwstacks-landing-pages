What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of potential leads getting lost in email inboxes without being properly tracked in your CRM. Sales teams often miss opportunities because they forget to manually create contact records for every new person who emails them.

The workflow automatically monitors your email inbox for messages from new senders, extracts their contact information, and creates corresponding records in Fireberry. It runs continuously in the background, ensuring your CRM stays updated with all your email correspondents without any manual data entry.

How It Works

1. Email monitoring

The workflow connects to your email account (Gmail, Outlook, or any IMAP-compatible service) and checks for new messages at regular intervals you specify.

2. Sender identification

For each new email, the system identifies whether the sender's address exists in your Fireberry contacts. It focuses only on new email addresses not already in your CRM.

3. Data extraction

The workflow parses the email headers to extract the sender's name (when available), email address, and optionally other details like company name from signature blocks.

4. Fireberry contact creation

Extracted contact information is formatted according to your Fireberry field requirements and added as a new contact record, complete with a reference to the original email.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to add a tag like "From Email" to automatically created contacts, making it easy to filter and report on these records later.

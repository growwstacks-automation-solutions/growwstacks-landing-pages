What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious and error-prone process of manually transferring contact information from spreadsheets to your CRM. Every time a new row is added to your Google Sheets (like from web forms, imports, or manual entries), the workflow automatically creates a corresponding contact record in Fireberry with all the relevant details.

Businesses using this automation typically save 5-10 hours per week on data entry while eliminating the duplicate contacts and missing information that often occur with manual processes. The workflow runs on a schedule you set, ensuring your Fireberry contacts are always up-to-date without any manual intervention.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Google Sheets Check

The workflow checks your specified Google Sheet at regular intervals (every 15 minutes, hourly, or daily - your choice) to identify newly added rows.

2. Data Extraction

For each new row found, the workflow extracts the contact information from your configured columns (name, email, phone, etc.) while maintaining the spreadsheet's row structure.

3. Fireberry Contact Creation

The extracted data is formatted according to Fireberry's API requirements and used to create new contact records automatically.

4. Error Handling & Logging

If any contact creation fails (due to missing data or API issues), the workflow logs the error and can notify your team for follow-up.

Pro tip: Add a timestamp column to your Google Sheet that gets updated when contacts are created. This helps track sync status and prevents duplicate processing.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of leads getting stuck in Facebook Lead Ads without making it to your CRM. Every time someone submits your Facebook lead form, this workflow instantly creates a complete contact record in Fireberry with all their submission data.

Manual lead transfer is time-consuming and error-prone. Sales teams often waste hours copying data between systems, and some leads inevitably get missed. This workflow eliminates those problems by automatically syncing leads in real-time, ensuring your sales team always has fresh, complete contact information to work with.

How It Works

1. New lead detection

The workflow constantly monitors your Facebook Lead Ads account for new form submissions using Make.com's Facebook Lead Ads trigger.

2. Data extraction

All form field responses (name, email, phone, custom answers) are captured and prepared for transfer to Fireberry.

3. Contact creation

The workflow maps the lead data to corresponding Fireberry contact fields and creates a new record via Fireberry's API.

4. Error handling

If any issues occur during transfer, the workflow logs the error and can be configured to retry or notify your team.

Pro tip: Add a delay module to send leads to your sales team during business hours only, preventing after-hours notifications while still capturing all submissions.

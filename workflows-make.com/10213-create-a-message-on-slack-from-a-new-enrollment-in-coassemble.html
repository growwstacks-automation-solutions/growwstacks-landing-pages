What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually tracking new enrollments in your Coassemble training programs. Without automation, your team might miss important enrollments or waste time checking the platform repeatedly. The workflow instantly notifies your team in Slack whenever someone signs up for a course, keeping everyone informed in real-time.

The solution connects Coassemble with Slack through Make.com's automation platform. Whenever a new enrollment record appears in Coassemble, the system automatically formats a detailed Slack message containing relevant enrollment information. This eliminates manual tracking while ensuring your team never misses an important sign-up.

How It Works

1. Detecting New Enrollments

The workflow continuously monitors your Coassemble account for new enrollment records using scheduled checks or webhook triggers.

2. Extracting Enrollment Details

When a new enrollment is detected, the system pulls relevant information like learner name, course title, enrollment date, and any custom fields you've configured.

3. Formatting the Slack Message

The automation structures this information into a clear, actionable Slack message format that includes all essential details your team needs.

4. Posting to Slack

The formatted message is automatically sent to your designated Slack channel, where your entire team can see it immediately.

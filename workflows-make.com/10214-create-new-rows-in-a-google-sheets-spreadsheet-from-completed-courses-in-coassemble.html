What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious and error-prone process of manually tracking employee training completions. Whenever a learner finishes a course in Coassemble (your LMS), their completion details are automatically logged as a new row in your designated Google Sheets spreadsheet.

The system captures essential training metrics like student name, email, course title, completion date, and scores. This creates a centralized, real-time record of all training activity that's instantly available for reporting, compliance audits, and performance reviews without any manual data entry.

How It Works

1. Trigger: Course Completion Event

The workflow activates whenever Coassemble sends a webhook notification that a course has been marked complete. This happens instantly upon completion without any manual intervention.

2. Data Extraction

Make.com parses the completion notification to extract key details about the student, course, and completion metrics. This includes fields you've configured to track in your training records.

3. Google Sheets Update

The system connects to your specified Google Sheet and adds a new row with all the completion data. Each field is mapped to its designated column for clean, organized record-keeping.

Pro tip: Add timestamp columns to track when records were added. This helps verify data freshness during audits.

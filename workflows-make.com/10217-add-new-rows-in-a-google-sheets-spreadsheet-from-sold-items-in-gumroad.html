What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual work of tracking digital product sales. Whenever a customer purchases from your Gumroad store, the workflow instantly records all transaction details in your Google Sheets spreadsheet. No more copying data, no missed sales, and no spreadsheet maintenance headaches.

For digital creators selling ebooks, courses, software, or other products through Gumroad, this provides real-time visibility into your business. You'll see exactly what's selling, when, and to whom - with all data organized and ready for analysis.

How It Works

1. New Sale Trigger

The workflow monitors Gumroad's webhooks for new sale notifications. As soon as a purchase occurs, the automation activates.

2. Data Extraction

Key details like product name, price, customer email, and purchase timestamp are extracted from the Gumroad transaction.

3. Google Sheets Update

The system adds a new row to your designated Google Sheet with all relevant sales data formatted consistently.

Pro tip: Add conditional formatting in Google Sheets to highlight best-selling products or flag high-value transactions.

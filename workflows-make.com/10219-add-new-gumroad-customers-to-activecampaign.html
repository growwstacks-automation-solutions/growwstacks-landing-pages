What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the manual work of transferring customer data between Gumroad (your ecommerce platform) and ActiveCampaign (your email marketing system). Every time a customer makes a purchase on Gumroad, their details are automatically added to ActiveCampaign as a new contact.

The workflow ensures no customer falls through the cracks by eliminating the need for CSV exports, copy-pasting, or manual data entry. It creates a seamless bridge between your sales and marketing systems, allowing you to start email sequences immediately after purchase.

How It Works

1. New sale triggers the workflow

The automation monitors your Gumroad account for new sales. When a purchase occurs, it instantly captures the customer's information including name, email, and purchase details.

2. Data is formatted for ActiveCampaign

The workflow processes the Gumroad data to match ActiveCampaign's contact field structure. It can map product names to tags, purchase amounts to custom fields, and more.

3. Contact is added to ActiveCampaign

The system creates a new contact in ActiveCampaign with all relevant purchase information. You can configure it to add contacts to specific lists or apply tags based on what they bought.

Pro tip: Set up an ActiveCampaign automation to trigger immediately when new Gumroad customers are added. This lets you send welcome emails, product guides, or special offers automatically.

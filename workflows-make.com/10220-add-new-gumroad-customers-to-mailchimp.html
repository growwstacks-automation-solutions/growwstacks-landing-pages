Automatically sync Gumroad purchases with your Mailchimp email lists

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical problem for digital product sellers using Gumroad - the manual work required to add customers to email marketing lists. Every time you make a sale, the workflow automatically adds the customer's information to your Mailchimp audience, ensuring they receive your follow-up sequences and marketing emails.

The integration eliminates copy-paste errors and saves hours of administrative work, especially for businesses with frequent sales. It also ensures timely communication since customers are added to your lists immediately after purchase, not whenever you get around to manual updates.

How It Works

1. New Gumroad purchase trigger

The workflow activates whenever a new sale is recorded in your Gumroad account. Make.com monitors your Gumroad webhook for this event.

2. Customer data extraction

The system pulls all available customer information from the Gumroad transaction, including name, email, purchase details, and any custom fields you've configured.

3. Mailchimp audience update

The workflow checks if the customer exists in Mailchimp, then either creates a new contact or updates existing information based on your configuration.

4. Optional segmentation

You can configure the template to add tags or place customers in specific segments based on their purchased product, price point, or other Gumroad data.

What You'll Need

  1. A Gumroad account with products set up
  2. A Mailchimp account with an audience/list created
  3. A Make.com account (free tier available)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Copy this template to your Make.com account
  2. Connect your Gumroad account via the API
  3. Connect your Mailchimp account and select the target audience
  4. Map the Gumroad fields to corresponding Mailchimp fields
  5. Test with a new Gumroad purchase to verify the sync
  6. Turn on the workflow to run automatically

Pro tip: Set up a welcome email series in Mailchimp that triggers when new contacts are added. This creates an immediate touchpoint with customers after purchase.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common challenge for training providers and course creators who use Coassemble for their learning management system (LMS) and Mailchimp for email marketing. Without integration, administrators must manually export enrollment lists from Coassemble and import them to Mailchimp - a time-consuming process that often delays important learner communications.

The workflow automatically detects new enrollments in Coassemble and instantly creates corresponding subscriber records in Mailchimp. It transfers key learner information including name, email address, course details, and enrollment date. This ensures your email campaigns reach participants immediately after they sign up for your courses.

How It Works

1. New enrollment trigger

The workflow monitors Coassemble for new course enrollments via webhook or API polling. When a learner registers for a course, the system captures their details including contact information and course metadata.

2. Data transformation

The enrollment data is formatted to match Mailchimp's subscriber requirements. You can customize which fields to include and how they're mapped between systems.

3. Mailchimp subscriber creation

The processed enrollment data creates a new subscriber in your specified Mailchimp audience. The workflow can add tags, assign to segments, or apply other Mailchimp settings based on the course enrolled.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to add course-specific tags in Mailchimp. This lets you send targeted follow-up emails based on which courses learners take.

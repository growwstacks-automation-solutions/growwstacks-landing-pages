What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your learning management system (Coassemble) and marketing CRM (ActiveCampaign), ensuring every new course enrollment automatically creates or updates contact records. Without this integration, marketing teams often struggle with outdated learner data, manual imports, and missed opportunities to engage learners based on their course activity.

The workflow solves three key problems: eliminating duplicate manual data entry between systems, maintaining accurate learner profiles in your CRM, and enabling timely, personalized follow-up based on actual course participation. When a learner enrolls in Coassemble, their information flows instantly into ActiveCampaign where you can trigger targeted email sequences, update contact tags, or add them to relevant segments.

How It Works

1. New enrollment triggers the workflow

The system monitors Coassemble for new course enrollments. Each time someone signs up for a course, the workflow captures their details including name, email, course name, and enrollment date.

2. Contact lookup in ActiveCampaign

The automation checks ActiveCampaign to see if this learner already exists in your CRM by matching their email address. This prevents duplicate contacts while ensuring existing records stay current.

3. Create or update contact record

For new learners, the system creates a complete contact profile in ActiveCampaign with all enrollment details. For existing contacts, it updates their record with the new course information while preserving other CRM data.

4. Optional tagging and segmentation

You can configure the workflow to add specific tags based on the course enrolled or trigger automations like welcome sequences for new learners.

Pro tip: Map Coassemble custom fields to ActiveCampaign properties to track specialized learning data like certification status or assessment scores for more targeted marketing.

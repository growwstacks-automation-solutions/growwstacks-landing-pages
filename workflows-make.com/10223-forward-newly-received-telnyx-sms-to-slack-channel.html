What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of SMS messages getting lost or delayed when customer communications are spread across multiple platforms. When businesses receive important SMS messages through Telnyx (like customer inquiries, appointment confirmations, or delivery notifications), they often need to be shared with team members immediately.

The workflow automatically detects new incoming SMS messages in your Telnyx account and forwards them to a designated Slack channel. This creates a centralized communication hub where your entire team can view, discuss, and respond to SMS messages without needing direct access to the Telnyx dashboard or personal phone numbers.

How It Works

1. Telnyx receives an SMS message

The workflow triggers whenever a new message arrives in your connected Telnyx phone number. Telnyx sends the message data (including content, sender number, and timestamp) to Make.com.

2. Make.com processes the message

The automation platform formats the SMS data into a clean, readable structure. You can customize which details to include (like showing the sender's location based on area code) and add any relevant business context.

3. Message is posted to Slack

The formatted message is automatically sent to your specified Slack channel. The notification appears just like any other Slack message, making it immediately visible to your team members who can then discuss or take action.

Pro tip: Add a Slack emoji reaction system where team members can "claim" messages they're handling to avoid duplicate responses.

Send Telnyx SMS on New Pipedrive Deals

Automatically send personalized SMS messages when new deals enter your sales pipeline

Get This Workflow → Make.com · CRM · Free Template

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap in sales team responsiveness by instantly notifying leads via SMS when they enter your Pipedrive CRM pipeline. Traditional CRM notifications often rely on email, which averages just 20% open rates, while SMS boasts 98% open rates within minutes.

The workflow triggers whenever a new deal is created in Pipedrive, automatically sending a personalized SMS message through Telnyx's reliable communication platform. You can customize messages to include deal specifics like product interest, sales rep contact info, or appointment scheduling links.

How It Works

1. New Deal Detection

The Make.com scenario monitors your Pipedrive account for newly created deals using webhooks or API polling. It captures all relevant deal information including contact details, deal value, and custom fields.

2. Contact Validation

The system verifies the lead's mobile number exists in Pipedrive and meets Telnyx's formatting requirements. Invalid numbers trigger an alternate notification path (like email to the sales rep).

3. Message Personalization

Using template variables, the workflow constructs an SMS message incorporating deal specifics. Example: "Hi [First Name], thanks for your interest in [Product]! Your rep [Salesperson] will call you at [Time]."

4. SMS Delivery via Telnyx

The formatted message sends through Telnyx's API with delivery confirmation. Failed attempts automatically retry or escalate based on your rules.

5. Activity Logging

Each SMS sends a confirmation note back to Pipedrive as a deal activity, creating a complete communication audit trail for your sales team.

Pro tip: Add a second SMS 24 hours later with a calendar booking link to dramatically increase meeting conversion rates.

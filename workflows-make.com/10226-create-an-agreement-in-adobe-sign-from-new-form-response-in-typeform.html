What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the manual process of creating and sending documents for signature after receiving form submissions. When someone completes your Typeform, the workflow automatically generates a corresponding agreement in Adobe Sign and sends it to the specified recipients.

Businesses using this solution typically see an 80% reduction in document processing time and eliminate common errors like sending wrong document versions or forgetting to follow up with signers. The system works particularly well for service contracts, liability waivers, employment agreements, and client onboarding documents.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

The workflow monitors your Typeform for new responses. When someone submits the form, the automation immediately captures all response data including email addresses and any custom fields you've configured.

2. Document Preparation

Using your pre-configured Adobe Sign template, the system populates the agreement with data from the form submission. This can include names, dates, service details, and any other relevant information collected in the form.

3. Signature Request

The completed document is automatically sent to all required signers via Adobe Sign's secure platform. The workflow specifies signing order if multiple signatures are required and applies your branding to the signing experience.

4. Status Tracking

The system monitors the signing progress and can trigger follow-up reminders if documents aren't completed within your specified timeframe. Completed documents are automatically stored in your preferred cloud storage.

Who This Is For

This automation delivers the most value for businesses that regularly process signed documents from customer or employee forms. Ideal use cases include:

  • Service businesses sending contracts to new clients
  • HR departments onboarding employees with signed agreements
  • Educational institutions processing student waivers and consent forms
  • Healthcare providers collecting patient acknowledgments
  • Real estate agencies managing lease agreements

Pro tip: Configure your Typeform to collect not just email addresses but also phone numbers. This allows the workflow to send SMS reminders for critical documents that need urgent signing.

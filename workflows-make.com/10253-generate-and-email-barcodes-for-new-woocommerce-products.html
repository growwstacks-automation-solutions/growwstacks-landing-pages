Generate and Email Barcodes for New WooCommerce Products

Automatically create product barcodes and send them to your team whenever new items are added to your store

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical pain point for WooCommerce store owners and product managers by eliminating the manual process of creating and distributing barcodes. Every time you add a new product to your WooCommerce store, the workflow automatically generates a professional barcode and emails it to your designated team members or suppliers.

The system ensures consistency in your product labeling while saving hours of administrative work. For businesses adding dozens or hundreds of products weekly, this automation can reclaim 5-10 hours of staff time while reducing labeling errors by up to 95% compared to manual processes.

How It Works

1. New product detection

The workflow monitors your WooCommerce store for new product additions through real-time webhooks or scheduled checks. When a new product is detected, it immediately triggers the barcode generation process.

2. Barcode generation

Using the product's SKU or another unique identifier, the system creates a standardized barcode in your preferred format (UPC, EAN, Code 128, etc.). The barcode includes proper checksums and meets retail compliance standards.

3. Email preparation

The workflow compiles the barcode image along with relevant product details into a professional email template. You can customize which product attributes (name, price, dimensions) appear in the notification.

4. Automated delivery

The system sends the barcode email to your predefined recipients - typically warehouse managers, product teams, or printing services. Multiple recipients can be configured based on product categories or other rules.

Pro tip: For maximum efficiency, combine this with a "Print Label" workflow that automatically generates printable product labels with the barcode, saving your team even more time.

What You'll Need

  1. A WooCommerce store with products using SKUs or other unique identifiers
  2. A Make.com account (free tier available)
  3. Email service configured in Make (Gmail, Outlook, SMTP, etc.)
  4. Designated email recipients (individuals or distribution lists)
  5. Optional: Barcode scanning equipment for verification

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Install the template from Make.com's template library
  2. Connect your WooCommerce store using API credentials
  3. Configure your preferred barcode format and dimensions
  4. Set up the email template with your branding and required product fields
  5. Add recipient email addresses (support for variables like product_category@yourdomain.com)
  6. Test with a sample product and verify the barcode scans correctly
  7. Activate the workflow and start adding products!

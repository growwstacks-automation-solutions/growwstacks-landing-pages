What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point for sales teams and business owners who use HubSpot CRM but need their contacts available in Google Contacts for easy access across Gmail, Google Meet, and other Google Workspace apps. Every time a new contact is added to your HubSpot CRM - whether through a form submission, manual entry, or API integration - this workflow automatically creates a corresponding contact in Google Contacts.

The solution eliminates the need for manual data transfer between systems, reducing administrative work by 80-90% for most businesses. It ensures your sales team has immediate access to new leads in their preferred contact management system, improving response times and reducing missed opportunities from contacts being stuck in separate silos.

How It Works

1. New Contact Detection

The workflow continuously monitors your HubSpot CRM for new contact additions through Make.com's real-time trigger system. When a new contact appears, the workflow immediately captures all available details.

2. Data Mapping

The system maps HubSpot contact fields to their corresponding Google Contacts fields, ensuring information like name, email, phone number, company, and job title are transferred accurately.

3. Contact Creation

Using your authorized Google account, the workflow creates a new contact in Google Contacts with all the mapped information from HubSpot. You can customize which fields get transferred based on your needs.

4. Error Handling

The workflow includes built-in error handling to manage cases like duplicate contacts or missing required fields, ensuring smooth operation without manual intervention.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for eCommerce businesses using WooCommerce and ActiveCampaign - keeping your email marketing lists perfectly synchronized with your customer database. Without automation, you risk missing opportunities to engage new customers or wasting time manually exporting and importing customer data.

The workflow automatically detects when a new customer is added in WooCommerce, checks if they already exist in ActiveCampaign, and creates a new contact record if needed. This ensures your email campaigns, automation sequences, and segmentation always have access to your complete customer base.

How It Works

1. New customer detection

The workflow monitors your WooCommerce store for new customer registrations or first-time orders from new email addresses.

2. ActiveCampaign contact check

Before creating a duplicate, the system queries your ActiveCampaign account to verify if the contact already exists using their email address as the unique identifier.

3. Contact creation or update

If the contact doesn't exist, the workflow creates a new ActiveCampaign record with all available customer data from WooCommerce. If the contact exists, you can configure whether to update their information or leave it unchanged.

Pro tip: Add custom fields in ActiveCampaign to capture WooCommerce-specific data like customer lifetime value, order count, or preferred product categories for more targeted email marketing.

  1. Copy this template to your Make.com account
  2. Connect your WooCommerce store using the API credentials
  3. Connect your ActiveCampaign account with your API URL and key

  5. Test with a new customer registration to verify the sync works
  6. Activate the scenario to run automatically

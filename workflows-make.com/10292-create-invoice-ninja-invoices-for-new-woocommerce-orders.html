What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the tedious manual process of creating invoices for WooCommerce orders by instantly generating professional invoices in Invoice Ninja. Every time a customer places an order on your WooCommerce store, Make.com automatically captures the order details and creates a corresponding invoice with all relevant information - customer details, products purchased, quantities, prices, taxes, and totals.

The workflow ensures your accounting records stay perfectly synchronized with your sales without any manual data entry. It handles the entire process from order confirmation to invoice generation in seconds, reducing administrative overhead and minimizing errors that commonly occur with manual invoice creation.

How It Works

1. New WooCommerce order trigger

The workflow starts when Make.com detects a new order in your WooCommerce store. It immediately retrieves all order details including customer information, products, pricing, and payment status.

2. Data formatting and preparation

The system organizes the raw order data into the structure Invoice Ninja requires, mapping WooCommerce fields to corresponding invoice fields. This includes calculating totals, applying tax rules, and formatting customer addresses.

3. Invoice creation in Invoice Ninja

Make.com connects to your Invoice Ninja account via API and creates a new invoice with all the formatted data. The invoice includes your branding, payment terms, and any custom fields you've configured.

4. Success confirmation

The system verifies the invoice was successfully created and can optionally notify your team or send the invoice directly to the customer based on your preferences.

Pro tip: Add a step to automatically email invoices to customers by enabling Invoice Ninja's notification settings. This completes the order-to-invoice-to-customer communication cycle without any manual intervention.

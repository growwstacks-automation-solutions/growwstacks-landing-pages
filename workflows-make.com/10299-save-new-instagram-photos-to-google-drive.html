What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common problem for Instagram users: the risk of losing precious photos if something happens to your account. Whether you're a casual user or managing a business profile, this workflow automatically saves every new Instagram post to your Google Drive, creating a secure backup.

The system works in the background, monitoring your Instagram account for new uploads. When it detects a new photo, it downloads the high-quality version and saves it to your specified Google Drive folder. This ensures you always have access to your original images, even if you delete them from Instagram or encounter account issues.

How It Works

1. Instagram Connection

The workflow connects to your Instagram account through Make.com's secure integration. It periodically checks your profile for new posts using Instagram's API.

2. New Post Detection

When you upload a new photo to Instagram, the system detects the change and triggers the workflow. It captures the image URL, caption, and any other available metadata.

3. Image Processing

The workflow downloads the highest quality version of your Instagram photo available through the API, ensuring you get the best possible backup copy.

4. Google Drive Upload

The downloaded image is automatically uploaded to your specified Google Drive folder. You can organize photos by date, hashtag, or other criteria.

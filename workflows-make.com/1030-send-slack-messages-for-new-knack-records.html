What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of delayed team awareness when new records enter your Knack database. Whether you're tracking customer inquiries, support tickets, inventory changes, or form submissions, manually checking Knack for updates creates workflow bottlenecks and response delays.

The Make.com template creates a real-time bridge between Knack and Slack. Whenever a new record appears in your specified Knack object, the workflow automatically formats a notification with relevant details and posts it to your chosen Slack channel. This ensures your team can act immediately on new information without constantly refreshing Knack views.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New Knack Record Detection

The workflow continuously monitors your Knack database for new entries using scheduled API checks. When Make.com detects a new record in your specified Knack object, it immediately captures all available field data.

2. Data Processing & Filtering

Optional filters let you specify which records should trigger notifications based on field values. You can exclude certain record types, require specific field completion, or only alert for high-priority entries.

3. Slack Message Composition

The workflow constructs a detailed Slack message using a template you customize. You can include any Knack field values, format text with markdown, and add emoji reactions for visual scanning.

4. Channel Delivery

The completed message posts to your designated Slack channel, optionally tagging specific team members or user groups. The workflow can route different record types to separate channels based on your rules.

Pro tip: Add a direct link back to the Knack record in your Slack messages so team members can click through to view complete details without searching.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of maintaining parallel records between your Knack database and Google Sheets spreadsheets. Many businesses need their Knack data available in spreadsheet format for analysis, reporting, or sharing with team members who don't have Knack access.

The workflow automatically detects when new records are created in your Knack application and immediately adds them to your designated Google Sheet. This eliminates hours of manual data entry while ensuring your spreadsheet always reflects the current state of your Knack database.

How It Works

1. New record trigger

The workflow constantly monitors your Knack application for new record creations. When a record is added to your specified Knack table, the automation is triggered.

2. Data extraction

Make.com retrieves all field values from the new Knack record, including any related data from connected tables according to your configuration.

3. Spreadsheet update

The system adds a new row to your Google Sheet with all the extracted data fields properly mapped to your spreadsheet columns.

4. Error handling

The workflow includes basic error handling to notify you if the sync fails, ensuring data integrity between systems.

  1. Copy the template to your Make.com account

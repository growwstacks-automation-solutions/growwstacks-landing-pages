Automatically track Salla orders as conversions in Google Ads to measure campaign ROI

What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your Salla ecommerce store and Google Ads, ensuring every order is accurately tracked as a conversion. When customers complete purchases in Salla, the workflow automatically sends the conversion data to Google Ads, giving you clear visibility into which ads are driving actual sales.

Without this connection, you're essentially flying blind with your ad campaigns. You might know how many clicks you're getting, but not which ones turn into paying customers. This workflow solves that problem by creating a direct pipeline between your store's order data and your advertising platform.

How It Works

1. New Order Trigger

The workflow monitors your Salla store for new completed orders. It captures all relevant order details including transaction value, products purchased, and customer information.

2. Data Formatting

The raw order data is transformed into the specific format Google Ads requires for conversion tracking. This includes currency conversion if needed, proper value assignment, and conversion category classification.

3. Google Ads API Connection

Using Make.com's Google Ads connector, the workflow securely transmits the conversion data to your Google Ads account. The connection uses OAuth for security and can handle your account's specific configuration.

4. Conversion Verification

The workflow includes error handling to ensure conversions are properly recorded. If any issues occur during transmission, the system can retry or notify you about the problem.

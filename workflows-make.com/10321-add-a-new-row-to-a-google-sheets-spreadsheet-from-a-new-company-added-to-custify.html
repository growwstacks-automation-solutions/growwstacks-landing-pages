What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of CRM data being siloed from other business systems. When sales teams add new companies to Custify, that valuable information often needs to be manually copied to spreadsheets for reporting, analysis, or sharing with other departments.

The workflow eliminates this manual work by automatically creating a new row in Google Sheets whenever a company is added to Custify. All relevant company details - name, industry, contact information, custom fields - flow directly into your spreadsheet without any human intervention.

How It Works

1. Trigger on new Custify company

The workflow starts when Make.com detects a new company record in your Custify account through their API.

2. Extract company data

All available fields from the Custify company record are captured, including standard fields and any custom properties you've defined.

3. Format for Google Sheets

The data is transformed to match your spreadsheet's column structure, with options to rename fields or combine values.

4. Add row to spreadsheet

Make.com connects to your specified Google Sheet via API and appends the formatted company data as a new row.

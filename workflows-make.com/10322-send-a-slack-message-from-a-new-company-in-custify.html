Automate Custify-to-Slack Company Notifications

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of sales and customer success teams not being immediately notified when new companies are added to your Custify CRM. Without automation, team members must manually check the CRM or rely on email notifications that often get buried in inboxes.

The workflow automatically detects new company creations in Custify and sends a formatted Slack message to your designated channel. This ensures your entire team sees new business opportunities in real-time, without requiring everyone to have CRM access or remember to check it regularly.

How It Works

1. Custify Triggers the Workflow

The workflow starts whenever a new company record is created in your Custify account. Make.com monitors the Custify API for these events in real-time.

2. Company Data is Processed

The workflow extracts relevant company information like name, industry, contact details, and any custom fields you want to include in notifications.

3. Slack Message is Formatted

The system formats a clean, readable Slack message with all the key details your team needs to act on the new company.

4. Notification is Sent to Slack

The formatted message is delivered to your pre-configured Slack channel, complete with @mentions for relevant team members if desired.

Pro tip: Add custom fields like "Lead Source" or "Estimated Deal Size" to your Slack notifications to help prioritize follow-ups.

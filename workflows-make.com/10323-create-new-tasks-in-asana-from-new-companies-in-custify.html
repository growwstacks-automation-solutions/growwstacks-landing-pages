What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your CRM (Custify) and project management system (Asana), ensuring that every new company added to your customer database automatically generates relevant tasks for your team. It eliminates manual data entry and reduces the risk of new accounts slipping through the cracks in your workflow.

The workflow triggers whenever a new company record is created in Custify, capturing all relevant company information and using it to create a properly formatted task in Asana. This maintains data consistency between systems while saving your team valuable time that would otherwise be spent manually creating and assigning tasks.

How It Works

1. New company detection

The workflow monitors your Custify account for new company creations using Make.com's Custify trigger. When a new company is added, the workflow immediately captures all available company data.

2. Data transformation

The raw company data from Custify is processed to extract only the relevant fields needed for task creation in Asana. This step ensures clean, properly formatted data flows into your project management system.

3. Task creation in Asana

Using Make.com's Asana module, the workflow creates a new task in your specified Asana project. The task includes relevant company details from Custify and can be assigned to specific team members based on your business rules.

Pro tip: Configure task templates in Asana first, then map Custify fields to pre-defined custom fields in Asana for maximum consistency across your automated tasks.

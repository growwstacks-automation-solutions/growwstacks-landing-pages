What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of compiling invoice reports from accounting systems. Finance teams typically spend hours each month exporting, formatting, and consolidating invoice data for reporting, audits, and analysis.

The workflow automatically retrieves all invoices (including line-item details) for a specified period and compiles them into a standardized Excel spreadsheet. By default, it runs monthly but can be scheduled for any reporting frequency needed. This eliminates manual data entry errors while saving 5-10 hours of accounting staff time per month.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow activates automatically at your chosen interval (default: first day of each month). No manual initiation required.

2. Invoice Retrieval

Connects to your accounting system API to fetch all invoices within the date range, including detailed line items and customer information.

3. Data Transformation

Restructures the raw invoice data into a clean, analysis-ready format with consistent columns and calculated fields.

4. Excel Generation

Creates a properly formatted Excel workbook with separate sheets for summary data and detailed line items.

5. Delivery

Saves the completed report to your designated cloud storage or emails it to specified recipients.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solution solves the challenge of manually tracking payment statuses across multiple transactions. For ecommerce businesses, SaaS platforms, and subscription services, keeping payment data updated in real-time is critical for accurate financial reporting and customer communication.

The workflow automatically stores new payment records in Make.com's Data Store and refreshes the information every 20 minutes. It captures essential payment details including transaction tokens, payment status, and customer email addresses - creating a centralized, up-to-date payment database that integrates with your other business systems.

How It Works

1. Payment Data Collection

The workflow connects to your payment processor (Stripe, PayPal, etc.) to retrieve new transactions. Each payment is assigned a unique token and status code that gets stored in Make's Data Store.

2. Scheduled Data Refresh

Every 20 minutes, the system automatically checks for payment status updates. This ensures your records reflect the most current information without manual intervention.

3. Notification Triggers

Based on payment status changes, the workflow can trigger email notifications to customers or internal teams. Common triggers include successful payment confirmations and failed payment alerts.

Pro tip: Extend this workflow by adding integration with your CRM or accounting software to automatically update customer records with payment information.

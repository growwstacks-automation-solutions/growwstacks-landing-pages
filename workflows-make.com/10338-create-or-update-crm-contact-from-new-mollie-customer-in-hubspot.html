What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of payment system data being siloed from your CRM. When customers sign up or make payments through Mollie, their information often needs to be manually added to HubSpot, creating delays and potential errors.

The workflow automatically creates new HubSpot contacts whenever a customer is added in Mollie, or updates existing contacts if they're already in your CRM. This ensures your sales and marketing teams always have access to complete, up-to-date customer information without manual data entry.

How It Works

1. New customer trigger in Mollie

The workflow monitors your Mollie account for new customer creations through webhooks or API polling.

2. Data extraction and formatting

Key customer information (name, email, billing details) is extracted from Mollie and formatted for HubSpot's API requirements.

3. HubSpot contact lookup

The system checks HubSpot for an existing contact matching the customer's email address to prevent duplicates.

4. Create or update contact

Depending on the lookup result, the workflow either creates a new HubSpot contact or updates the existing record with the latest Mollie data.

Pro tip: Add a custom property in HubSpot to track which contacts came from Mollie for better segmentation in your marketing campaigns.

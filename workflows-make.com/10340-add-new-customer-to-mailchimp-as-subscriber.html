What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of manually transferring customer data between your payment processor and email marketing platform. Every time a new customer completes a transaction through Mollie, their information automatically gets added to your Mailchimp subscriber list.

The workflow eliminates hours of tedious data entry while ensuring you never miss an opportunity to nurture customer relationships. By capturing customers immediately after their first purchase, you can trigger targeted welcome sequences, product education emails, and loyalty programs from day one.

How It Works

1. Mollie detects new customer

The workflow monitors your Mollie account for new customer records. This triggers whenever someone makes their first payment through your Mollie integration.

2. Customer data is processed

Make.com extracts the relevant customer information including name, email address, and any other configured fields from Mollie's API response.

3. Subscriber added to Mailchimp

The system maps the Mollie customer data to Mailchimp's subscriber fields and adds them to your specified audience list. You can configure custom field mappings and tags during setup.

Pro tip: Add a 1-hour delay before the Mailchimp addition to allow for any immediate post-purchase communications from Mollie.

What This Workflow Does

Failed payments create administrative headaches and cash flow problems for any business. This automated workflow solves this by instantly detecting failed Mollie payments and proactively reaching out to customers with a convenient payment link.

The system periodically checks your Datastore for failed payments, creates personalized payment links for each case, sends them via email to customers, and updates your records to prevent duplicate follow-ups. This eliminates manual tracking while improving recovery rates.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Payment Status Check

The workflow runs at your configured interval (hourly, daily, etc.) to scan your payment Datastore for any transactions marked as failed in Mollie.

2. Payment Link Generation

For each failed payment found, the system automatically generates a new payment link through Mollie's API with the original payment amount and customer details.

3. Personalized Email Dispatch

The workflow composes and sends a professional email to the customer containing their payment link, using your customized email template with branding and messaging.

4. Status Update

Finally, the Datastore record is updated to indicate the payment link has been sent, preventing duplicate follow-ups while maintaining a complete audit trail.

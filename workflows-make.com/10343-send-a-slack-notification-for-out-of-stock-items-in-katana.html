What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical problem for manufacturing teams using Katana inventory management software - the risk of production delays when materials run out unexpectedly. The workflow continuously monitors your Katana inventory levels and instantly notifies your team in Slack when any item reaches its out-of-stock threshold.

Unlike manual inventory checks that can miss critical shortages, this system provides real-time visibility into stock levels. It eliminates the need for constant monitoring while ensuring your team is immediately aware of potential supply chain issues before they impact production schedules.

How It Works

1. Katana inventory monitoring

The workflow regularly checks your Katana inventory data through API connections, tracking stock levels for all configured items.

2. Threshold detection

When an item's available quantity drops below your predefined minimum level, the system triggers an alert.

3. Slack notification generation

The workflow formats a detailed Slack message containing the item name, current stock level, and any other relevant details you configure.

4. Alert delivery

The notification is sent to your specified Slack channel or direct messages, ensuring immediate visibility for your purchasing and production teams.

Pro tip: Configure different threshold levels for critical vs. non-critical items to prioritize alerts based on impact to production.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical problem of inventory stockouts by instantly notifying your team when products run low in Katana. Instead of manually checking inventory levels or discovering shortages when customers complain, you'll receive proactive email alerts the moment stock dips below your predefined thresholds.

The system monitors your Katana inventory in real-time, triggering customized email notifications to the appropriate team members. This prevents the revenue loss and customer dissatisfaction that occurs when popular items become unavailable. For manufacturers and distributors, maintaining optimal inventory levels directly impacts your ability to fulfill orders and meet service level agreements.

How It Works

1. Katana Inventory Monitoring

The workflow continuously checks your Katana inventory levels at regular intervals you configure. It compares current stock against your minimum quantity thresholds for each product.

2. Threshold Detection

When any product's available quantity falls below your specified reorder point, the system immediately identifies it as needing attention. This detection happens in real-time as inventory data updates in Katana.

3. Alert Generation

The automation compiles all relevant product details - including current stock level, supplier information, and purchase history - into a structured alert ready for email delivery.

4. Email Notification

Customized emails are sent to your designated recipients containing actionable information about the low-stock items. The emails include direct links to Katana for quick reordering.

  1. Install the template from Make.com's template library
  2. Connect your Katana account in the Make scenario
  3. Configure your email service provider settings

  6. Test with a product you manually set below threshold

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of tracking valuable discussions from Facebook Groups in your CRM or database. Community managers often waste hours manually copying posts between platforms, risking data errors and missing important interactions.

The workflow automatically detects new posts in your Facebook Group and creates corresponding records in Knack with all relevant details - post content, author information, timestamps, and engagement metrics. This creates a searchable archive of community discussions directly in your business database.

How It Works

1. Monitoring Facebook Group Activity

Make.com connects to the Facebook Graph API to monitor your specified Group for new posts. The trigger activates whenever members create new discussions.

2. Extracting Post Details

The workflow captures the post text, author name, creation time, post URL, and any attached media links. This data gets structured for clean transfer to Knack.

3. Creating Knack Records

Make.com authenticates with your Knack application and creates new records in your designated table, mapping Facebook post data to the appropriate Knack fields.

Pro tip: Add a filter step to only sync posts containing specific keywords or from certain members. This prevents irrelevant content from cluttering your Knack database.

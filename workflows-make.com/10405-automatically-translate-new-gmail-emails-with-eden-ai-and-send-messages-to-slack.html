What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for global businesses: overcoming language barriers in email communications. Every time you receive an email in a foreign language, the workflow automatically translates the content using Eden AI's advanced translation technology and forwards the translated version to your designated Slack channel.

The system eliminates manual translation work that typically creates bottlenecks in international communications. Whether you're dealing with customer inquiries, partner messages, or internal team emails across different regions, this solution ensures everyone receives information in their preferred language within seconds.

How It Works

1. New Email Trigger

The workflow monitors your Gmail inbox for new messages. When an email arrives, it immediately captures the sender information, subject line, and body content.

2. Language Detection

Eden AI analyzes the email content to determine its source language. This happens automatically without any configuration, supporting over 100 languages.

3. AI-Powered Translation

The system translates the email content into your specified target language(s) while preserving formatting and key information. Eden AI's context-aware translation handles business terminology better than basic tools.

4. Slack Notification

The translated email gets formatted into a clean Slack message with the original subject line, sender details, and translation. You can route different languages to separate channels if needed.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to add translation confidence scores in Slack, helping your team identify messages that might need human review.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solution addresses the critical business need for proactive content moderation in Google Drive. Every time a new image file is uploaded to your monitored folder, the workflow automatically submits it to Eden AI's content moderation system for analysis. The AI evaluates the image against multiple explicit content categories, then flags or quarantines problematic files based on your configured thresholds.

For organizations handling user-generated content, shared drives, or compliance-sensitive materials, this workflow provides an essential safeguard against accidental or intentional uploads of inappropriate imagery. It eliminates the need for manual review of every image while ensuring your digital assets maintain professional and legal standards.

How It Works

1. New File Detection

The workflow monitors your specified Google Drive folder for any new image uploads, triggering immediately when files arrive.

2. AI Content Analysis

Each image is automatically sent to Eden AI's moderation API, which evaluates it across multiple explicit content categories with confidence scoring.

3. Decision Routing

Based on preconfigured thresholds, the workflow determines whether the image requires moderation action (quarantine, alert) or can be cleared.

4. Notification & Logging

Administrators receive alerts about flagged content through their preferred channels (email, Slack, etc.), with all actions logged for compliance.

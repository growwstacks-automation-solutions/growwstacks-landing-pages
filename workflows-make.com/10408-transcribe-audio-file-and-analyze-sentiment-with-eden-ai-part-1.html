What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manually reviewing audio recordings for customer insights. It automatically processes audio files stored in Google Drive through Eden AI's speech-to-text technology, then analyzes the transcribed text for emotional sentiment.

The workflow is particularly valuable for businesses receiving customer feedback through call recordings, voice messages, or meeting minutes. By converting speech to text and measuring sentiment automatically, teams can quickly identify dissatisfied customers, track emotional trends over time, and prioritize follow-up actions.

How It Works

Step 1: Audio File Detection

The workflow monitors a designated Google Drive folder for new audio file uploads. When detected, it immediately begins processing while maintaining the original file security.

Step 2: Speech-to-Text Conversion

Eden AI's advanced transcription engine converts the audio to text with industry-leading accuracy. The system handles various accents, background noise, and technical vocabulary.

Step 3: Sentiment Analysis

The transcribed text is analyzed for emotional tone using natural language processing. Results categorize content as positive, negative or neutral with confidence scoring.

Pro tip: For best results, name your audio files consistently (e.g. "CustomerID_Date.mp3") to enable easy cross-referencing with your CRM data later.

What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your recruitment process by automatically extracting structured data from resumes received via Gmail. When a new resume arrives in your inbox, the workflow uses Eden AI's powerful parsing technology to identify and extract key candidate information like name, contact details, work history, education, and skills.

The extracted data is then formatted and sent as a notification to your Slack channel, giving your team immediate visibility of new candidates without manual processing. This eliminates hours of tedious data entry while ensuring no promising candidate slips through the cracks due to delayed processing.

How It Works

1. New Resume Detection

The workflow monitors your Gmail inbox for new messages with resume attachments. It identifies PDF, DOCX, and other common resume file formats.

2. AI-Powered Data Extraction

Each resume is processed by Eden AI, which uses machine learning to parse the document and extract structured candidate data regardless of the original format or layout.

3. Data Standardization

The extracted information is formatted into a consistent structure, making it easy to review and compare candidates.

4. Slack Notification

A detailed message containing the candidate's key information is automatically posted to your designated Slack channel, complete with the original resume attachment for reference.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to only process emails from specific senders or with certain subject lines to avoid parsing irrelevant attachments.

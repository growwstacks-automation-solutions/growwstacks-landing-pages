What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious process of manually removing backgrounds from photos stored in Google Photos. It automatically detects new images in your specified albums, processes them through an AI background removal service, and saves the cleaned versions back to Google Photos while preserving your originals.

Ideal for ecommerce businesses, photographers, and content creators, this workflow can process hundreds of product images or portraits in the time it would take to manually edit just a few. The automated system ensures consistent quality while freeing up creative time for more valuable work.

How It Works

1. New Photo Detection

The workflow monitors your designated Google Photos folders for newly added images. You can set filters to only process specific file types or albums.

2. Background Removal Processing

Each image gets sent to a professional background removal API that uses AI to cleanly separate subjects from backgrounds with pixel-level precision.

3. Quality Control

Optional modules can check processed images for quality, flagging any that might need manual review based on complexity thresholds you define.

4. Organized Saving

Finished transparent PNGs get saved to a "Processed" folder in Google Photos with the same metadata and organization as the originals.

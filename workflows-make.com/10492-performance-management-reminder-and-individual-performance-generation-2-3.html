Performance Management Reminder System

Automate employee review reminders and generate visual performance reports for managers

What This Workflow Does

This automation solution tackles the common challenge of incomplete employee performance reviews by automatically tracking submission status and sending targeted reminders. For completed reviews, it generates comprehensive visual reports that help managers quickly assess team performance without manual data analysis.

The system integrates with your existing HR tools to create a seamless performance management process. It eliminates the need for manual follow-ups while providing actionable insights through automatically generated charts and reports delivered directly to managers via Slack.

How It Works

1. Review Status Monitoring

The workflow continuously monitors which employees have completed their performance reviews by checking your HR system or Google Sheets data source.

2. Automated Reminders

For incomplete reviews, personalized reminders are sent at scheduled intervals through email or Slack, reducing the administrative burden on HR staff.

3. Report Generation

Completed reviews trigger the creation of visual performance reports using QuickChart, transforming raw data into easy-to-understand charts and graphs.

4. Manager Notifications

Final reports are automatically delivered to managers via Slack, complete with performance trends and comparison metrics for their team.

Who This Is For

This solution is ideal for HR teams and people managers in organizations with 10+ employees. It's particularly valuable for:

  • HR departments overwhelmed by manual review tracking
  • Managers who need better performance visibility
  • Companies transitioning to more frequent review cycles
  • Remote teams requiring automated coordination

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common HR challenge of ensuring managers complete employee performance reviews on time. Every quarter, the workflow automatically sends Google Forms used for performance evaluations to team leaders and managers, eliminating manual follow-ups and creating accountability.

The system is designed for organizations using Google Forms as their performance review tool. It handles the entire distribution process - from scheduling the quarterly reminders to tracking which managers have completed their evaluations. This first template in the series focuses on sending forms for up to 3 team members, with additional scenarios available to expand the system.

How It Works

1. Quarterly Trigger Activation

The workflow activates automatically at the start of each quarter, or can be manually triggered when needed. This ensures reviews happen consistently without HR having to remember scheduling.

2. Manager Identification

The system identifies which managers need to complete evaluations based on your organizational structure. It can pull this data from your HR system or use a simple spreadsheet.

3. Form Distribution

Personalized emails are sent to each manager containing links to the appropriate Google Forms for their team members. The email includes completion deadlines and instructions.

4. Completion Tracking

As managers submit their evaluations, the system tracks completion status. Optional follow-up reminders can be sent to managers who haven't completed their forms by specified checkpoints.

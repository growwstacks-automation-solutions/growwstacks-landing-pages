What This Workflow Does

This performance management workflow automates the final stage of team performance reporting - compiling individual metrics into comprehensive team reports. It solves the time-consuming manual process of gathering data from multiple sources, calculating averages and trends, and formatting professional reports.

By automating this third phase of performance management, HR teams and managers save 5-10 hours per reporting cycle while ensuring consistency in calculations and presentation. The workflow aggregates data from previous performance evaluations (individual assessments and peer feedback) to create department or company-wide insights.

How It Works

1. Data Collection

The workflow pulls completed individual performance assessments from your HR system or database, including scores, comments, and goal completion metrics.

2. Metric Calculation

Key performance indicators are automatically calculated across the team - averages, distributions, trends compared to previous periods, and benchmark comparisons.

3. Report Generation

The system compiles the analyzed data into formatted reports (PDF, PowerPoint, or web dashboards) with visual charts, executive summaries, and department breakdowns.

4. Distribution

Reports are automatically shared with stakeholders via email, Slack, or uploaded to your company intranet with appropriate access controls.

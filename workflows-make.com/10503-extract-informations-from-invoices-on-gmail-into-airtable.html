What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious problem of manually extracting data from invoices received via email. Every time you receive an invoice in Gmail, the workflow automatically processes the attachment, uses AI to extract key details like vendor information, invoice numbers, dates, line items, and totals, then organizes everything neatly in Airtable.

Traditional invoice processing requires employees to open each PDF or image file, manually type data into spreadsheets or accounting systems, and file documents appropriately. This workflow eliminates those repetitive tasks while reducing human errors that often occur during manual data entry. The AI-powered extraction achieves over 95% accuracy on standard invoice formats.

How It Works

1. New Invoice Detection

The workflow monitors your Gmail inbox for incoming messages with invoice attachments. You can configure it to watch specific labels or filter by sender domains to only process invoices from your vendors.

2. AI-Powered Data Extraction

When a new invoice arrives, the system uses Eden AI's document processing capabilities to analyze the attachment. The AI identifies and extracts structured data including vendor details, invoice numbers, dates, line items with quantities and prices, taxes, and totals.

3. Data Validation & Formatting

Extracted data goes through validation checks to ensure consistency. The system formats dates, normalizes currency values, and can apply business rules like rounding amounts or calculating additional fields.

4. Airtable Record Creation

The processed invoice data creates a new record in your Airtable base. You can map fields to match your existing accounting structure, with options to add custom fields for internal tracking or approval workflows.

Pro tip: Set up a separate Airtable view for "Needs Review" invoices where extraction confidence was below your threshold. This creates a quality control checkpoint without losing automation benefits.

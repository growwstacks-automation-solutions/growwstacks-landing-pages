What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of converting AI-generated text into engaging audio content and delivering it efficiently through messaging platforms. Many businesses and creators want to leverage AI text generation but need more personal, accessible formats for their audience.

The workflow automatically transforms ChatGPT responses into high-quality audio files using Eden AI's text-to-speech capabilities, then delivers them through Telegram. This eliminates manual conversion steps while maintaining the personal touch of voice communication.

How It Works

Step 1: Text Generation

The workflow starts by generating text content using ChatGPT's API. You can configure prompts tailored to your specific use case - whether it's customer responses, educational content, or marketing messages.

Step 2: Audio Conversion

Eden AI's text-to-speech engine converts the generated text into natural-sounding audio. The workflow handles formatting cleanup and optimal segmentation for the best audio quality.

Step 3: Telegram Delivery

The converted audio file is automatically sent to specified Telegram channels or contacts. The workflow includes error handling to ensure successful delivery even with larger files.

Pro tip: Configure different voice profiles for different message types to create a more branded audio experience.

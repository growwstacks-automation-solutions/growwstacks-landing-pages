What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms unstructured text responses from Google Forms into organized, actionable data in Airtable. When respondents submit open-ended answers, Eden AI's natural language processing automatically identifies and extracts the most relevant keywords, then stores them alongside the original response in your Airtable base.

Manual analysis of form responses is time-consuming and inconsistent. This workflow ensures every response gets analyzed with the same criteria, uncovering patterns and trends that might be missed by human reviewers. The AI considers context to distinguish meaningful terms from filler words, delivering business-ready insights without manual data processing.

How It Works

1. New form submission triggers the workflow

Whenever someone submits your Google Form, Make.com detects the new response and initiates the automation process. The system captures all text responses from the form submission.

2. Text responses are sent to Eden AI

The workflow sends each open-ended response to Eden AI's keyword extraction API. Eden AI analyzes the text using advanced natural language processing to identify nouns, verbs, and phrases that represent the core topics mentioned.

3. Keywords are processed and filtered

The AI evaluates each potential keyword for relevance, removing common words and prioritizing terms specific to your industry or survey context. You can customize the filtering parameters to focus on particular types of keywords.

4. Data is structured in Airtable

The original response and extracted keywords are saved to your Airtable base in a structured format. Each keyword becomes a separate record linked to the original response, enabling powerful filtering and analysis capabilities.

Pro tip: Create an Airtable view that counts keyword occurrences to quickly identify the most frequently mentioned topics across all responses.

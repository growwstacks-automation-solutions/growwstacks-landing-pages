What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of tracking daily Stripe balances across multiple accounts. Instead of requiring accounting staff to log in daily and record balances, the workflow automatically:

  • Pulls complete balance data from Stripe at a scheduled time each day
  • Stores the information in your chosen database (Google Sheets by default)
  • Sends formatted Slack notifications to relevant team members
  • Maintains a complete historical record for reporting and reconciliation

Pro tip: Combine this with monthly accounting workflows to automatically reconcile Stripe data with your general ledger, saving 10+ hours per month in manual reconciliation work.

How It Works

1. Daily Balance Trigger

The workflow runs automatically each morning, pulling complete balance information including available funds, pending amounts, and currency breakdowns from your Stripe account.

2. Data Transformation

Raw Stripe API responses are reformatted into clean, standardized records with proper date stamps and calculated fields like day-over-day changes.

3. Database Storage

Processed data gets appended to your database (Google Sheets or other supported systems), creating an auditable historical record with one row per day.

4. Team Notification

Key stakeholders receive Slack messages highlighting important metrics like total available balance, significant changes, or threshold alerts you configure.

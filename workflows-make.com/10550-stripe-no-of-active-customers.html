What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of tracking your Stripe customer growth by automatically recording your active customer count in Google Sheets and notifying your team via Slack. Designed to run on the first of each month, it provides consistent, accurate customer metrics without any manual work.

Businesses using Stripe often need to track customer growth for investor reporting, team performance metrics, or churn analysis. Without automation, this requires logging into Stripe, running reports, and manually updating spreadsheets - a process prone to human error and often neglected when teams are busy.

How It Works

1. Monthly Trigger

The workflow automatically activates on the first day of each month using Make.com's scheduler.

2. Fetch Stripe Data

It connects to your Stripe account via API to retrieve the current count of active customers.

3. Update Google Sheets

The customer count is appended to your specified Google Sheet with a timestamp, creating a historical record.

4. Slack Notification

Your team receives an instant Slack message with the updated customer count and any custom commentary you've configured.

Pro tip: Add conditional formatting in Google Sheets to highlight growth trends and set up charts for visual reporting.

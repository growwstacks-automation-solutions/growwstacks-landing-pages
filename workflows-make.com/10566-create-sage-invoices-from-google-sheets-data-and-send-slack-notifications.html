What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the tedious manual process of transferring billing data from Google Sheets to Sage accounting software. It automatically creates professional invoices in Sage based on your spreadsheet data, then notifies relevant team members via Slack when the invoices are ready.

Businesses using this solution typically save 5-10 hours per week on billing tasks while reducing data entry errors by 95%. The workflow handles all the repetitive work - pulling customer details, product information, quantities, and pricing from your Google Sheet, then generating perfectly formatted Sage invoices with the correct tax calculations and payment terms.

How It Works

1. Data Extraction

The workflow monitors your designated Google Sheet for new or updated invoice records. It identifies completed rows ready for processing based on your specified triggers (like a "Ready for Invoice" status column).

2. Sage Invoice Creation

For each valid record, the system creates a corresponding invoice in Sage with all line items, customer details, and pricing exactly as specified in your spreadsheet. It handles tax calculations, discounts, and any custom fields your business requires.

3. Slack Notifications

Once invoices are successfully created, the workflow sends customizable Slack messages to your accounting channel. These notifications can include invoice numbers, amounts, customer names, and links to view the documents in Sage.

4. Error Handling

If any issues occur (like missing customer data or invalid product codes), the system sends immediate alerts to your specified error channel with details about what needs manual correction.

Save New Sage Customers to HubSpot CRM Without Duplicates

Automatically sync your Sage Business Cloud Accounting customers with HubSpot CRM while preventing duplicate entries and maintaining data accuracy.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common challenge for businesses using Sage Business Cloud Accounting alongside HubSpot CRM - keeping customer data synchronized between both systems without creating duplicate records. Manual data entry between accounting and CRM systems is time-consuming and prone to errors, often leading to inconsistent customer information across departments.

The workflow automatically detects new customers added in Sage Accounting, checks HubSpot CRM for existing matches, and either creates a new contact or updates an existing one. This ensures your sales team always has access to accurate customer information while eliminating hours of manual data transfer each week.

How It Works

1. New customer detection in Sage

The workflow monitors Sage Business Cloud Accounting for newly added customers through regular API checks or real-time webhook triggers if available.

2. Data validation and preparation

Customer information is extracted and formatted to match HubSpot's expected data structure, with essential fields like company name, contact details, and billing information standardized.

3. Duplicate check in HubSpot

The system searches HubSpot CRM using unique identifiers (email, company name, or custom ID) to determine if the customer already exists in your database.

4. Conditional record handling

If no match is found, a new HubSpot contact is created. If a match exists, the existing record is updated with any new information from Sage, preventing duplicates while keeping data current.

Pro tip: Configure custom field mappings to ensure critical accounting data like payment terms or credit limits flow to relevant HubSpot contact properties for your sales team.

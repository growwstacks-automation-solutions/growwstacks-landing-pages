What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your spreadsheet data and AI-powered content creation. Whenever new rows are added to your Google Sheet, the system automatically sends the data to ChatGPT and returns generated completions based on your specified prompts.

The workflow solves the challenge of manually creating repetitive content by leveraging AI to generate high-quality outputs at scale. Whether you need product descriptions, SEO meta content, email responses, or creative copy, this integration transforms raw data into polished content automatically.

How It Works

1. New row detection

The workflow monitors your Google Sheet for new rows or changes to existing data. You can specify which columns trigger the automation and which contain the data to process.

2. Data preparation

The system extracts relevant information from your spreadsheet and formats it according to your predefined ChatGPT prompt templates. This ensures the AI receives properly structured input.

3. AI content generation

Each data set is sent to ChatGPT with your custom instructions. The AI analyzes the input and generates completions that match your requirements, whether that's creative writing, technical descriptions, or structured responses.

4. Results processing

The generated content is returned to your spreadsheet automatically, either in the same row or a designated output column. You can configure the workflow to append results or overwrite existing content.

Pro tip: Create a column in your sheet for prompt variations. This lets you generate different content types from the same data by applying different AI instructions.

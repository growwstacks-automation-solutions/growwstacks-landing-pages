What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common accounting headache of manually transferring invoice data between systems. Whenever a new invoice is created in Sage Business Cloud, the workflow automatically captures all relevant details and adds them as a new row in your designated Google Sheet.

By eliminating manual data entry, you reduce errors while giving your team instant access to invoice records without needing Sage login credentials. The synchronized data becomes searchable, sortable, and reportable in Sheets - perfect for creating custom financial dashboards or sharing specific records with team members.

How It Works

1. New Invoice Trigger

The workflow monitors your Sage account for newly created invoices, activating whenever accounting staff finalize a bill.

2. Data Extraction

Key fields like invoice number, date, client details, line items, taxes, and totals are extracted with proper formatting.

3. Google Sheets Update

The system adds a new row to your specified spreadsheet, maintaining consistent column structure across all entries.

4. Optional Notifications

Configure email/Slack alerts when high-value invoices sync or if errors occur during transfer.

Pro tip: Create a separate "Raw Data" tab in your Sheet for the automated entries, then build reporting tabs that reference this data. This preserves the automated structure while allowing custom views.

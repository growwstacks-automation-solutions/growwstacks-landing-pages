What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of outdated or generic Slack statuses that don't accurately reflect your current work. By connecting OpenAI with Slack, it intelligently generates status messages based on your actual work topics, keeping your team informed without manual updates.

The system analyzes your input topics (like "client meeting" or "project deadline") and uses OpenAI's natural language processing to craft a human-like status message. This eliminates the need to manually type status updates while providing more accurate availability information to colleagues.

How It Works

1. Topic Input

You provide keywords or topics describing your current work focus. This can come from calendar events, task managers, or manual input.

2. AI Processing

OpenAI analyzes your topics and generates a natural-sounding status message that communicates your availability and focus areas.

3. Status Update

The generated message automatically updates your Slack status, visible to your entire team in real-time.

Pro tip: Combine this with calendar integration to automatically update your status based on scheduled meetings and events.

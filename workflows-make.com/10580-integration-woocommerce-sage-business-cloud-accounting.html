Sync WooCommerce Products to Sage Accounting

Automatically transfer new products from your online store to accounting software

What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the tedious manual process of recreating WooCommerce products in Sage Business Cloud Accounting. Whenever you add a new product to your online store, the workflow automatically creates a corresponding product record in Sage with all the necessary financial details.

For ecommerce businesses using both platforms, this integration solves several critical problems: mismatched inventory counts between systems, delayed financial reporting due to manual data entry, and human errors in transferring product specifications. The automation ensures your accounting system always reflects your current product catalog.

How It Works

1. New Product Detection

The workflow monitors your WooCommerce store for newly created products. It triggers instantly when a product is added, before it goes live in your store.

2. Data Transformation

Product information is formatted to match Sage's requirements. This includes converting WooCommerce categories to Sage's accounting categories and ensuring proper tax mappings.

3. Sage Product Creation

The system creates a new product in Sage with all transferred details. Critical financial fields like cost price, sales price, and inventory tracking are populated accurately.

4. Verification & Error Handling

The workflow confirms successful creation in Sage and logs any errors for review. Failed attempts automatically retry with adjusted parameters when possible.

Who This Is For

This automation benefits ecommerce businesses that:

  • Manage more than 50 products across both systems
  • Frequently add new products or update existing ones
  • Need real-time accuracy between store inventory and accounting records
  • Have experienced errors from manual data entry between platforms

What You'll Need

  1. Active WooCommerce store with administrator access
  2. Sage Business Cloud Accounting subscription
  3. Make.com account (free tier available)
  4. Product taxonomy documentation for both systems

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Install the template in your Make.com account
  2. Connect your WooCommerce and Sage accounts
  3. Map product fields between the two systems
  4. Test with sample products to verify mappings
  5. Activate the workflow for live product additions

Pro tip: Create a dedicated "Sync Test" product in WooCommerce that you can modify to verify all field mappings work correctly before going live with real products.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solution eliminates the need for manual weather checks by automatically delivering tomorrow's forecast to your Slack channel. It connects OpenWeatherMap's reliable forecasting data with your team's communication platform, ensuring everyone stays informed about upcoming weather conditions.

For businesses with outdoor operations, distributed teams, or weather-sensitive scheduling needs, this workflow provides timely insights that help with planning and risk mitigation. The system runs automatically each day, saving your team valuable time while improving preparedness.

How It Works

1. Weather Data Collection

The workflow retrieves forecast data from OpenWeatherMap at a scheduled time each day, pulling temperature, precipitation probability, wind speed, and other relevant metrics for your specified location.

2. Data Processing

Make.com formats the raw weather data into a human-readable message, applying your preferred units (Celsius/Fahrenheit) and highlighting any significant weather events that might impact operations.

3. Slack Delivery

The formatted forecast is automatically posted to your designated Slack channel, complete with weather icons and conditional formatting (like warning symbols for severe weather).

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to run early in the morning so your team sees the forecast when they start their day.

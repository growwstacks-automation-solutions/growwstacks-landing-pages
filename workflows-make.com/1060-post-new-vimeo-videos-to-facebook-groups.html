What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually sharing videos across platforms. Content creators and marketers often upload videos to Vimeo for its superior playback quality and professional features, but need to share that content in Facebook Groups where their target audiences actively engage.

The workflow automatically detects new videos added to your specified Vimeo album or channel, then posts them to designated Facebook Groups with all relevant metadata. This ensures your community sees your latest content immediately, without you having to remember to share each upload manually.

How It Works

1. Vimeo Trigger

The workflow monitors your Vimeo account for new video uploads. You can configure it to watch specific albums, channels, or your entire portfolio.

2. Video Processing

When a new video is detected, the system extracts key details like title, description, thumbnail URL, and direct video link. Optional filters can be added to only share certain types of content.

3. Facebook Group Posting

The automation creates a new post in your specified Facebook Group(s) containing the video link and metadata. The post appears as a rich media preview with thumbnail.

4. Optional Enhancements

The template can be extended to add custom captions, hashtags, or schedule posts for optimal engagement times. You can also configure notifications when sharing completes.

Pro tip: Use Vimeo's privacy settings to control who can view your videos, while still allowing Facebook Group members to access them through the shared links.

What You'll Need

  1. A Vimeo Pro, Business, or Premium account (free accounts have API limitations)
  2. Admin or moderator access to at least one Facebook Group
  3. A Make.com account (free tier works for basic usage)
  4. Your videos uploaded to a specific Vimeo album or channel (recommended)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Clone the template in your Make.com account
  2. Connect your Vimeo account via OAuth
  3. Connect your Facebook account with Groups permission
  4. Specify which Vimeo album/channel to monitor
  5. Select the Facebook Group(s) to post to
  6. Optionally customize post formatting
  7. Turn on the scenario and test with a new video

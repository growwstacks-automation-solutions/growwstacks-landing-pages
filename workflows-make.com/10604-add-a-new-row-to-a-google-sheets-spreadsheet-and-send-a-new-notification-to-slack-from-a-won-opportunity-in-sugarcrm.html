What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of sales teams manually logging won opportunities across multiple systems. When a deal moves to "Closed-Won" in SugarCRM, the workflow automatically creates a detailed record in your Google Sheets revenue tracker and sends an instant notification to your Slack sales channel.

By eliminating manual data entry, you ensure accurate, real-time visibility of won deals across your organization. Finance gets clean data for forecasting, marketing can analyze win patterns, and leadership sees up-to-date KPIs—all without additional work from your sales team.

How It Works

1. SugarCRM Triggers the Workflow

The automation monitors your SugarCRM instance for any opportunity that changes to the "Closed-Won" stage. It captures all relevant deal details including amount, close date, account information, and custom fields.

2. Data Formats for Destination Systems

The workflow transforms SugarCRM data into the exact structure needed for your Google Sheets template and Slack notification format. This includes currency formatting, date standardization, and merging fields for readability.

3. Simultaneous Updates to Sheets & Slack

Within seconds, the system adds a new row to your designated Google Sheets spreadsheet with all deal details while simultaneously posting a formatted notification to your specified Slack channel with key win highlights.

Pro tip: Add conditional formatting in Google Sheets to automatically highlight your biggest wins and color-code deals by product line or territory for instant visual analysis.

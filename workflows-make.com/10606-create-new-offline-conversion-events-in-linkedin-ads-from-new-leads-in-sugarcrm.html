What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of measuring LinkedIn Ads performance accurately. Most businesses struggle to connect ad spend to actual lead generation because conversion tracking stops at website visits. When leads enter your SugarCRM but aren't reported back to LinkedIn, you lose visibility into which campaigns drive real business value.

The workflow automatically creates offline conversion events in LinkedIn Ads whenever new leads appear in SugarCRM. This closed-loop reporting gives you accurate ROI data to optimize campaigns toward leads that actually convert in your CRM rather than just clicks or form submissions.

How It Works

1. New lead detection

The workflow monitors your SugarCRM for newly created lead records using Make.com's SugarCRM module. It can watch specific lead statuses or all new entries.

2. Data preparation

Lead details are formatted according to LinkedIn's offline conversion API requirements. The system hashes email addresses for privacy while maintaining matchability.

3. Conversion event creation

Make.com sends the processed lead data to LinkedIn Ads as an offline conversion event, associating it with the original ad campaign that drove the lead.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to track different conversion types (lead created, qualified, opportunity created) to see which campaigns drive sales-ready leads versus just inquiries.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of keeping your audience engaged with new video content. Instead of manually notifying subscribers or hoping they'll check your Vimeo channel, the workflow automatically sends Chrome browser push notifications the moment new videos are added to specified albums.

Content creators, educators, and marketers lose potential views when audiences don't know about new uploads. This system ensures your videos get immediate attention with direct browser alerts that have significantly higher open rates than email notifications.

How It Works

1. Vimeo Album Monitoring

The workflow continuously watches your specified Vimeo albums for new video additions using Make.com's Vimeo integration.

2. New Video Detection

When a new video is detected, the system extracts key details like title, description, and thumbnail URL to include in the notification.

3. Chrome Push Notification

Make.com sends the formatted alert through Chrome's push notification system, appearing directly in subscribers' browsers even when minimized.

4. Click-to-View Action

Recipients can click the notification to immediately open and watch the new video on Vimeo, driving instant engagement.

Pro tip: Combine this with your email newsletter system to reach audiences through multiple channels simultaneously.

What You'll Need

  1. A Vimeo Pro, Business, or Premium account with API access
  2. A Make.com account (free plan works)
  3. Google Chrome browser installed on recipient devices
  4. Notification permissions enabled in Chrome
  5. The album ID(s) you want to monitor

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Clone the template in your Make.com account
  2. Connect your Vimeo account in the "Watch Videos" module
  3. Specify which albums to monitor for new content
  4. Customize the notification message format if desired
  5. Test with a new video upload to verify delivery
  6. Activate the scenario to begin automatic monitoring

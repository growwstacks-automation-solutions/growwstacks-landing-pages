What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your sales engagement platform (Salesloft) and marketing automation system (Marketo). When sales reps add new prospects in Salesloft, they're instantly created as contacts in Marketo - or existing records are updated with fresh information.

The workflow solves the common problem of sales-marketing misalignment where valuable prospect data gets stuck in silos. Marketing teams often don't know about new sales contacts until days later, missing crucial engagement windows. This sync ensures every Salesloft addition becomes immediately available for targeted marketing campaigns.

How It Works

1. New Contact Trigger

The workflow monitors Salesloft for new contact additions or updates. This can be configured to watch specific lists, cadences, or all contacts depending on your sales process.

2. Marketo Record Check

Before creating a new record, the system checks Marketo for existing contacts matching the email address or other unique identifiers to prevent duplicates.

3. Smart Record Handling

If the contact exists, Marketo updates the record with new Salesloft data. If not, it creates a complete new contact with all relevant fields mapped from Salesloft.

4. Program Enrollment

Optionally, new contacts can be automatically added to specific Marketo programs or campaigns based on criteria like sales stage, territory, or lead source.

Pro tip: Configure field mappings carefully to ensure Salesloft data populates the correct Marketo fields, especially custom fields that might differ between systems.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical disconnect between marketing-qualified leads in HubSpot and sales-ready prospects in Salesforce/Salesloft. When marketing captures a new lead in HubSpot CRM, the workflow automatically:

Creates a corresponding contact record in Salesforce, adds the prospect to Salesloft for outreach sequencing, and notifies the assigned sales rep via Slack - all within seconds. This eliminates manual data transfer between systems and ensures sales teams engage leads while their interest is highest.

How It Works

1. New HubSpot Contact Trigger

The workflow monitors your HubSpot CRM for new contact creations or lead status changes that indicate sales readiness (like "Marketing Qualified Lead").

2. Salesforce Contact Creation

The system maps relevant HubSpot fields to Salesforce contact/lead fields, creating a standardized record with all essential marketing attribution data preserved.

3. Salesloft Prospect Addition

The new contact is added to Salesloft with appropriate cadence assignments, ensuring they enter the sales engagement workflow immediately.

4. Slack Notification

Assigned sales reps receive a detailed Slack message with prospect details and direct links to all relevant records across systems.

Pro tip: Configure lead scoring thresholds in HubSpot to only sync high-quality leads that meet your sales-ready criteria.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical bottleneck in sales operations - the delay between lead generation in Salesloft and follow-up by sales reps. When new leads enter your Salesloft cadences, they often need to be manually transferred to Salesforce before your team can act on them, creating frustrating delays.

The workflow automatically creates Salesforce lead records (or other object types) whenever new people are added to Salesloft, then instantly notifies your sales team via Slack. This eliminates manual data entry, reduces lead response time from hours to seconds, and ensures no prospect falls through the cracks during handoffs between marketing and sales.

How It Works

1. New lead detection in Salesloft

The workflow continuously monitors your Salesloft account for new people added to cadences or lists. When a new prospect appears, it immediately triggers the automation process.

2. Data mapping and transformation

The system extracts all relevant lead information from Salesloft and maps it to corresponding Salesforce fields. You can customize which data points get transferred and apply any necessary formatting transformations.

3. Salesforce record creation

The workflow creates a new lead (or contact/opportunity) in Salesforce with all the mapped data. It can be configured to handle duplicates by either updating existing records or skipping them based on your preferences.

4. Slack notification delivery

Immediately after the Salesforce record is created, the system sends a customizable Slack message to your designated channel. The notification can include key lead details, priority indicators, and even @mention specific team members based on assignment rules.

Pro tip: Configure your Slack notifications to include a direct link to the new Salesforce record. This lets reps jump straight into action with one click.

What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms raw leads into sales-ready prospects by automatically enriching contact data and distributing it across your sales tech stack. When a new contact enters HubSpot, the workflow:

  1. Enhances the profile with Clearbit intelligence (company details, technographics, social data)
  2. Creates complete prospect records in Salesforce and Salesloft
  3. Sends real-time Slack alerts to sales reps with key enrichment insights

Eliminating manual data entry and research, this pipeline ensures sales teams always work with complete, up-to-date prospect information while saving 2-3 hours per rep daily.

How It Works

1. New Contact Trigger

The workflow activates when a new contact is created in HubSpot, capturing all submitted information from forms, imports, or integrations.

2. Clearbit Enrichment

The contact record is automatically sent to Clearbit which appends 50+ data points including company size, technologies used, funding status, and social profiles.

3. CRM Synchronization

The enriched data is mapped to corresponding fields in Salesforce (as a Lead/Contact) and Salesloft (as a Prospect), creating complete records in both systems.

4. Sales Team Notification

A formatted Slack message is sent to designated channels with the prospect's key details, enrichment highlights, and direct links to CRM records for immediate follow-up.

Pro tip: Configure your Slack notification to include the most valuable enrichment fields for your sales process - like technologies used or recent funding events that indicate buying capacity.

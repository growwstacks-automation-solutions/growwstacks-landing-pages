What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of form submissions going unnoticed for hours or days. When customers, leads, or event attendees submit your Paperform, your team often doesn't know until someone manually checks the submissions dashboard. This delay can mean missed business opportunities and frustrated users.

The workflow automatically detects new Paperform submissions and sends customized notifications to your Slack channel in real-time. You can configure exactly what information gets shared - whether simple alerts or detailed summaries with key submission data. This ensures your team can respond immediately while the interaction is still fresh.

How It Works

1. Paperform Trigger

The workflow starts whenever a new submission is received in your Paperform account. Make.com detects this event instantly through Paperform's API.

2. Data Processing

The workflow extracts specific fields from the submission based on your configuration. You can choose which questions/answers to include in the Slack notification.

3. Slack Notification

A formatted message gets sent to your designated Slack channel. You can customize the message template to highlight the most important information for your team.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to route different form types to separate Slack channels. For example, send job applications to #hr and product feedback to #product-team.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of lost leads and delayed follow-ups when form submissions sit in spreadsheets or email inboxes. Every time someone completes your Paperform, their information instantly becomes a fully-tracked contact in ActiveCampaign - ready for immediate nurturing through your email sequences.

The workflow eliminates manual data transfers between systems, reducing errors while ensuring timely engagement. Marketing teams using this integration typically see 20-40% higher response rates to initial follow-ups compared to manual processes.

How It Works

1. Paperform submission trigger

The workflow activates whenever someone submits your Paperform. Make.com instantly receives all form field data including custom fields you've configured.

2. Data formatting and validation

The automation checks for required fields, formats phone numbers or addresses consistently, and can apply conditional logic based on responses before sending to ActiveCampaign.

3. ActiveCampaign contact creation

The system creates a new contact record with all submitted information, applying appropriate tags based on the form type (e.g. "Webinar Signup" or "Product Inquiry").

4. Automation triggering

Optionally, the workflow can add the contact to specific automation sequences based on their form responses, ensuring immediate, relevant follow-up.

Pro tip: Map custom fields in both systems to capture detailed information like lead source, interest level, or product preferences for more targeted nurturing.

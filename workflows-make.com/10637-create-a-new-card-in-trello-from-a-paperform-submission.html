What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of form submissions getting lost in email inboxes or requiring manual transfer to your task management system. When someone submits a Paperform, the workflow automatically creates a corresponding Trello card with all the relevant submission details.

By connecting these two platforms, you ensure every form response becomes an actionable item in your workflow. The template maps form fields to Trello card properties, allowing you to customize which information appears where. This eliminates duplicate data entry and reduces the risk of missed submissions.

How It Works

1. New Paperform Submission Trigger

The workflow starts when a new form is submitted in Paperform. Make.com detects this event and retrieves all the submission data.

2. Data Processing

The workflow processes the form responses, extracting key information like the submitter's name, email, and any custom fields you've set up in your form.

3. Trello Card Creation

Using the Trello API, the automation creates a new card in your specified board and list. You can configure which form fields map to the card title, description, labels, and custom fields.

4. Optional Notifications

The template includes optional steps to send Slack or email notifications when new cards are created, keeping your team informed in real-time.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of transferring information from Paperform submissions into Notion. Every time someone completes your Paperform, this workflow automatically creates a new Notion page with all their responses neatly organized.

Businesses using Paperform for client intake, employee onboarding, or event registrations often waste hours copying form data into Notion. This automation eliminates that repetitive work while ensuring no submissions get missed or misplaced. The Notion pages are created instantly with consistent formatting according to your preferred structure.

How It Works

1. Paperform submission trigger

The workflow starts when someone submits your Paperform. The automation immediately captures all their responses including text answers, file uploads, and selection choices.

2. Data processing

The form responses are processed to match your Notion page structure. You can map specific Paperform fields to designated Notion properties, format text, and organize content sections.

3. Notion page creation

The automation creates a new page in your specified Notion database with all the form data properly organized. It can add tags, assign relationships to other pages, and include formatted content blocks.

Pro tip: Use Notion's template button feature to pre-design your page layouts. The automation can then apply these templates to ensure consistent formatting for every new form submission.

What You'll Need

  1. A Paperform account with at least one active form
  2. A Notion account with edit permissions to the workspace
  3. A Make.com account (free tier works for basic automation)
  4. A Notion database ready to receive the new pages
  5. API connections between Make.com and both Paperform/Notion

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Install the template from Make.com

  5. Map form fields to Notion properties in the workflow editor
  6. Test with a sample form submission
  7. Activate the automation

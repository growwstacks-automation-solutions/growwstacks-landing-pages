What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manually transferring form submissions to your email marketing platform. Every time someone completes your Paperform, their information automatically syncs to your specified Mailchimp list as either a new subscriber or updated contact information.

The workflow intelligently checks whether each submitter already exists in your Mailchimp audience before taking action. This prevents duplicate entries while ensuring existing contacts have their information updated with the latest details from your form. It handles all the field mapping between Paperform responses and Mailchimp contact fields automatically.

How It Works

1. Paperform submission triggers the workflow

When someone submits your Paperform, the workflow immediately captures all their responses including email address, name, and any custom fields you've set up.

2. Mailchimp list verification

The system checks your specified Mailchimp list to determine if the submitter's email already exists in your audience.

3. Add or update subscriber

If the email is new, it creates a Mailchimp subscriber with all mapped fields. If the email exists, it updates that contact's information with the new form data.

4. Optional tagging and segmentation

You can configure the workflow to add tags or update segments based on specific form responses, enabling smarter email targeting.

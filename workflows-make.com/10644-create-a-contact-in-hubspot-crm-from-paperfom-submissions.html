What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical problem for businesses using Paperform for lead generation and HubSpot CRM for customer relationship management. Every time a visitor submits your Paperform, the workflow instantly creates a corresponding contact record in HubSpot with all their submission data.

Manual data entry between systems is not only time-consuming but prone to errors. This workflow eliminates those pain points by automatically mapping form fields to CRM properties, ensuring accurate data transfer while freeing up your team to focus on sales conversations rather than administrative tasks.

How It Works

1. New form submission trigger

The workflow monitors your Paperform account for new submissions. As soon as someone completes your form, the automation is triggered.

2. Data extraction and formatting

Make.com extracts all submitted form data, including custom fields, and prepares it for HubSpot's API requirements.

3. HubSpot contact creation

The system creates a new contact in HubSpot CRM, mapping each form field to the corresponding contact property in HubSpot.

4. Error handling and logging

The workflow includes built-in error handling to retry failed operations and logs all activity for troubleshooting.

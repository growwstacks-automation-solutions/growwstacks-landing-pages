What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common productivity bottleneck - the manual transfer of information from online forms to task management systems. Every time someone submits a Paperform, the workflow automatically creates a corresponding task in ClickUp with all the relevant details.

The template eliminates hours of repetitive data entry while ensuring no form submission falls through the cracks. It's particularly valuable for businesses that receive client requests, service inquiries, or internal submissions through forms and need to track them as actionable items in ClickUp.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

The workflow starts when someone submits your Paperform. Make.com instantly detects the new submission and retrieves all the response data.

2. Data Processing

The automation extracts key information from the form fields you specify, such as the requester's name, submission date, and any custom fields relevant to your workflow.

3. Task Creation in ClickUp

Using ClickUp's API, the workflow creates a new task in your specified list or space. You can configure which form fields map to which task properties, including custom fields.

4. Optional Notifications

The template can be extended to send Slack messages, emails, or other notifications when important submissions are converted to tasks.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of repurposing long-form articles into social media content. Marketing teams often struggle to extract the most engaging elements from their blog posts and research articles to share on social platforms. This workflow automatically analyzes published content, identifies key points, and generates platform-optimized social posts ready for scheduling.

By connecting ChatGPT with your content sources, the system creates multiple post variations including attention-grabbing hooks, key takeaways, and discussion questions. It maintains brand voice consistency while adapting tone for different platforms - professional for LinkedIn, conversational for Twitter, and engaging for Instagram.

How It Works

Step 1: Article Ingestion

The workflow monitors your content sources (blogs, news sites, or internal knowledge bases) for new articles. When detected, it extracts the full text while preserving formatting and structure.

Step 2: AI Analysis

ChatGPT processes the article to identify: core themes, statistics, quotable insights, and actionable advice. It scores content elements by engagement potential based on your historical social performance data.

Step 3: Post Generation

The AI creates 3-5 post variations per article, each with different angles: statistic highlights, how-to tips, discussion questions, or quote graphics. All versions include relevant hashtags and emojis based on your brand guidelines.

Step 4: Platform Formatting

Posts are automatically formatted for each social network - character limits for Twitter, paragraph breaks for LinkedIn, and image caption styles for Instagram. The workflow can even create basic visual assets using DALL-E if configured.

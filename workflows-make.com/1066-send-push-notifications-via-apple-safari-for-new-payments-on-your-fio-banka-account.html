What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of manually checking your Fio banka account for incoming payments. Instead of logging in repeatedly throughout the day, you'll receive instant Safari push notifications the moment funds arrive. The workflow connects your Fio banka account to Make.com, which monitors for new transactions and triggers browser-based alerts.

For freelancers and small businesses, this means faster payment confirmation without constant account monitoring. You'll know immediately when client payments clear, enabling quicker invoicing follow-ups and better cash flow management. The system works 24/7, ensuring you never miss a payment notification during off-hours.

How It Works

1. Payment Detection

Make.com polls your Fio banka account at regular intervals (typically every 5-15 minutes) through the bank's API. When a new credit transaction appears, the system identifies it as an incoming payment.

2. Notification Processing

The workflow extracts key details like payment amount, sender information, and transaction reference. It formats this data into a clean notification message according to your preferences.

3. Safari Push Delivery

Make.com sends the formatted alert to Apple's push notification service, which delivers it to all your Safari browsers signed in with the same Apple ID. Notifications appear even when Safari isn't actively open.

Pro tip: Combine this with accounting software integrations to automatically record payments and match them with invoices.

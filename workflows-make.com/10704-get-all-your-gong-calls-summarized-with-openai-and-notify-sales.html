What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of sales teams spending excessive time reviewing call recordings while potentially missing critical insights. Every morning at 8:00 AM, it automatically processes all Gong calls from the previous 24 hours through OpenAI to generate concise, actionable summaries.

The AI identifies key discussion points including customer pain points, buying signals, competitor mentions, and common objections. These insights are then delivered to your sales team via Slack, ensuring everyone starts their day with fresh intelligence about customer conversations.

How It Works

1. Call Retrieval

The workflow connects to your Gong account and retrieves all call recordings from the past 24 hours, filtering for completed sales conversations.

2. AI Processing

Each call transcript is analyzed by OpenAI's language model, which extracts the most relevant discussion points and patterns across multiple calls.

3. Insight Generation

The system creates structured summaries highlighting key metrics, customer quotes, and emerging trends from the call data.

4. Team Notification

Summaries are formatted and delivered to designated Slack channels, organized by sales team or territory for easy reference.

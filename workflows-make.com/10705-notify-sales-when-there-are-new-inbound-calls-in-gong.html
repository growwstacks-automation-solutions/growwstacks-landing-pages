What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for sales teams using Gong - the delay between receiving an inbound call and taking action. Without automation, valuable leads can slip through the cracks when reps don't notice new calls promptly.

The workflow monitors Gong for new inbound calls and immediately posts detailed notifications to your team's Slack channel. Each alert includes the caller's information, call duration, and direct links to review the recording - enabling faster, more informed follow-ups that can increase conversion rates by 20-30%.

How It Works

1. Gong detects a new inbound call

The workflow continuously monitors your Gong account for new call activity through Make.com's Gong integration. When a new call is logged, it triggers the automation sequence.

2. Call details are extracted and formatted

The system pulls key information from Gong including caller name/company, call duration, participants, and recording link. This data is structured into a clear, actionable message format.

3. Notification is sent to Slack

A formatted message containing all essential call details is posted to your designated Slack channel. The notification includes quick action buttons allowing reps to immediately access the recording or add follow-up tasks.

Pro tip: Configure your Slack notifications to @mention specific team members based on call characteristics like territory or product interest for even faster response times.

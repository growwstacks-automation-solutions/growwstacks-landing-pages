What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually downloading and formatting LinkedIn Ads performance data for daily reporting. Marketing teams often spend hours each week collecting metrics across campaigns, copying them into spreadsheets, and preparing them for analysis.

The workflow automatically retrieves yesterday's performance data from your LinkedIn Ads account and transfers it to a Microsoft Excel 365 workbook with proper formatting. It captures all key metrics including impressions, clicks, conversions, spend, and engagement rates - ready for immediate analysis when your team starts their day.

How It Works

1. Daily Trigger

The workflow activates each morning to collect the previous day's performance data from LinkedIn Ads.

2. Data Retrieval

It pulls comprehensive campaign metrics including CTR, CPC, conversions, and spend across all your active campaigns.

3. Data Transformation

The raw data is formatted for Excel with proper column headers, data types, and consistent structure.

4. Excel Integration

Formatted data is automatically added to your specified Excel 365 workbook, either appending to existing data or creating new sheets as needed.

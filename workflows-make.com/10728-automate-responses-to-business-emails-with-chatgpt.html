What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how you handle incoming business emails by combining AI-powered drafting with human oversight. When a new email arrives in your Gmail inbox, the system analyzes its content, references your Airtable knowledge base, and uses ChatGPT to generate a professional draft response.

The key advantage is that all responses are saved as drafts rather than sent automatically. This gives you or your team the opportunity to review, edit if needed, and approve each response before it goes out to customers. It's perfect for businesses that want to maintain personalization and quality control while automating the time-consuming first draft process.

How It Works

1. Email Trigger

The workflow activates whenever a new email arrives in your designated Gmail inbox. It filters out newsletters and internal emails to focus only on customer inquiries.

2. Content Analysis

The system extracts key information from the email including the sender's question, any mentioned products/services, and the general inquiry type (pricing, support, scheduling etc.).

3. Knowledge Base Lookup

Your Airtable base serves as the single source of truth, providing accurate product details, pricing, FAQs, and approved response templates that ChatGPT references.

4. AI Response Generation

ChatGPT crafts a personalized response using your brand voice guidelines from Airtable. The AI considers previous correspondence with the sender for continuity.

5. Draft Creation

The generated response is saved as a draft in Gmail with clear labeling indicating it's an AI-assisted draft awaiting review.

Pro tip: Maintain a spreadsheet of common customer questions and your ideal responses in Airtable. This trains ChatGPT to produce drafts that already match your preferred style.

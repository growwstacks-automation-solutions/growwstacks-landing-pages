What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your sales pipeline and accounting system by automatically creating Xero contacts whenever new organizations are added to Pipedrive CRM. It solves the common problem of sales teams adding new clients to the CRM while accounting teams manually recreate the same contacts in Xero - a process prone to errors and time delays.

The workflow runs on a schedule (default every 15 minutes) to check for new Pipedrive organizations, then maps relevant fields like company name, address, phone numbers, and email addresses to create corresponding contact records in Xero. This ensures your accounting team always has up-to-date client information without manual intervention.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow activates automatically at your chosen interval (default 15 minutes) to check Pipedrive for new organizations added since the last run.

2. Retrieve New Organizations

Make.com queries Pipedrive's API to fetch details of any organizations created within the monitoring period, including all available contact information and custom fields.

3. Data Transformation

The workflow maps Pipedrive organization fields to Xero's contact schema, ensuring proper formatting of addresses, phone numbers, and other details that may use different structures in each system.

4. Contact Creation in Xero

Using Xero's API, the workflow creates new contact records with all mapped information. You can configure whether to skip or update existing contacts to prevent duplicates.

Pro tip: Add a custom "Accounting ID" field in Pipedrive that gets copied to Xero's contact reference field. This creates a permanent link between systems for future updates.

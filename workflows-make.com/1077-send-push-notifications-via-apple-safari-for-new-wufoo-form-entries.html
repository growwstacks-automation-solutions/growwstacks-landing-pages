What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of delayed response to important form submissions. Traditional email notifications often get buried in inboxes, causing businesses to miss time-sensitive inquiries or registrations. The workflow instantly pushes Wufoo form entries to Apple devices via Safari notifications, ensuring your team never overlooks a submission.

When configured, the system monitors your selected Wufoo form for new entries. Each submission triggers a customizable push notification that appears on Macs, iPhones, and iPads - even when Safari isn't actively open. This creates a foolproof alert system for customer requests, lead forms, event sign-ups, or any other critical form submissions.

How It Works

1. Wufoo Form Trigger

The workflow starts when someone submits your Wufoo form. Make.com detects the new entry through Wufoo's API in real-time, typically within seconds of submission.

2. Data Processing

The system extracts key information from the form response, such as the submitter's name, email, or specific answers you want to highlight in the notification.

3. Notification Generation

Make.com formats a concise push notification using your preferred template, including the most relevant form data for quick context.

4. Safari Push Delivery

The notification gets pushed to all subscribed Apple devices via Safari's push notification service, appearing on screens immediately with a distinctive alert sound.

Pro tip: Include a unique identifier (like last name or reference number) in notifications to help staff quickly locate the full submission in your Wufoo dashboard or connected CRM.

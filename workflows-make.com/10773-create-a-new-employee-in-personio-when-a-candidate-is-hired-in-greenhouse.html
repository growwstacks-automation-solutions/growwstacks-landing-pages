What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your recruitment and HR systems by automatically creating a new employee record in Personio the moment a candidate's hiring is confirmed in Greenhouse. It solves the common problem of HR teams manually transferring candidate data between systems - a process prone to errors and delays that can impact the new hire experience.

When implemented, this workflow ensures your HRIS (Personio) always stays perfectly synchronized with your ATS (Greenhouse). New employees appear in Personio with all their relevant information - from personal details to job specifications - without any human intervention. This creates a seamless transition from candidate to employee status across your HR tech stack.

How It Works

1. Greenhouse triggers the workflow

The automation monitors your Greenhouse account for new "Hired" status changes. When a candidate's status updates to hired, the workflow immediately captures all their application and offer details.

2. Data transformation and validation

The system maps Greenhouse candidate fields to corresponding Personio employee fields, formatting data correctly for each system's requirements. It validates phone numbers, email formats, and required fields before proceeding.

3. Personio employee creation

Using Personio's API, the workflow creates a new employee record with all transferred information. It handles department mappings, manager assignments, and custom field population according to your HR configuration.

4. Confirmation and error handling

The system verifies successful record creation and can trigger notifications to HR or the new hire. If any errors occur, it retries the creation process and alerts administrators.

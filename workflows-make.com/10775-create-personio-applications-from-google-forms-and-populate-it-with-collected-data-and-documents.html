Automate HR Onboarding: Google Forms to Personio

Streamline your hiring process by automatically creating Personio applications from Google Forms submissions with collected data and documents.

Get This Workflow → Make.com · HR Automation · Free Template

What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your HR onboarding process by seamlessly connecting Google Forms with Personio. When candidates submit their applications through your Google Form, the workflow automatically creates a corresponding application in Personio, complete with all submitted data and attached documents.

The system eliminates manual data entry, reduces errors, and ensures no application gets lost in the shuffle. HR teams benefit from having all candidate information neatly organized in Personio from the moment applications are submitted, allowing for faster processing and better candidate experiences.

How It Works

1. Candidate Submits Google Form

Applicants complete your customized job application form, providing their information and uploading any required documents like resumes or cover letters.

2. Automation Triggers on Submission

The workflow detects new form submissions and immediately begins processing the data, extracting all relevant information from the form fields.

3. Data Validation and Processing

The system checks for complete information, formats data appropriately, and prepares it for transfer to Personio according to your specified mappings.

4. Personio Application Creation

The workflow creates a new candidate application in Personio, populating all relevant fields with the data from the Google Form submission.

5. Document Attachment

Any files uploaded through the form are automatically attached to the Personio application, ensuring all candidate materials are properly stored.

6. Confirmation and Notifications

The system can send confirmation emails to applicants and notifications to your HR team about new applications received.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of staying engaged with active Facebook Group discussions without constantly checking the platform manually. Community managers, moderators, and group administrators often miss time-sensitive comments because Facebook's native notifications can be delayed or buried in other alerts.

The workflow monitors your specified Facebook Group for new comments on your posts and instantly delivers them as Safari push notifications to your Apple devices. Unlike email notifications that may go unread, these native alerts appear immediately on your Mac, iPhone, or iPad with customizable message content.

How It Works

1. Facebook Group Monitoring

Make.com continuously checks your specified Facebook Group for new activity using Facebook's API. The integration has permission to read posts and comments without requiring you to manually approve each check.

2. Comment Detection

When a member adds a new comment to one of your posts, the workflow identifies the comment content, author, and post context. Advanced filters can be added to only notify about specific types of comments if desired.

3. Notification Formatting

The system formats a concise notification message including the commenter's name, first few words of their comment, and a direct link to the discussion. You can customize the message template to include any relevant details.

4. Safari Push Delivery

Using Apple's push notification service, the alert is delivered to all your signed-in Apple devices within seconds. Notifications remain visible until dismissed and sync across your Apple ecosystem.

Pro tip: Add a filter to only notify about comments containing questions or @mentions to reduce notification overload in highly active groups.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of delayed engagement with your Disqus comment community. Traditional email notifications often get buried in inboxes, causing you to miss time-sensitive discussions or fail to respond promptly to important questions.

The workflow monitors your Disqus forums through Make.com's integration, then instantly delivers push notifications through Google Chrome whenever new comments are posted. This creates a real-time connection between your audience's activity and your ability to participate, without requiring constant manual checking of your website.

How It Works

1. Disqus Comment Monitoring

The workflow begins by regularly checking your specified Disqus forums through their API. You can configure which forums or threads to monitor based on your website's structure.

2. New Comment Detection

Make.com compares each scan with previous results, identifying only genuinely new comments to avoid duplicate notifications. The system can be adjusted to check as frequently as every 5 minutes.

3. Notification Processing

When new comments are found, the workflow extracts key details like the commenter's name, comment excerpt, and direct link to the discussion. This data is formatted for optimal notification display.

4. Chrome Push Delivery

Using Chrome's notification API, the system delivers a desktop alert containing the comment details. Clicking the notification takes you directly to the conversation on your site.

Pro tip: Configure your Make.com scenario to only notify about comments containing specific keywords or from certain users to reduce notification fatigue.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common HR challenge of maintaining accurate, up-to-date employee contact information across your organization. When employee details change in Personio (your HR system), those changes automatically propagate to Google Contacts where your team accesses them daily.

Manual updating of contact details is error-prone and time-consuming. This workflow eliminates the need for HR to notify everyone when phone numbers change, departments shift, or new employees join. The sync ensures everyone in your organization has immediate access to current contact details through the Google ecosystem they already use.

How It Works

1. Personio Employee Data Retrieval

The workflow starts by fetching employee records from Personio's API at scheduled intervals. It captures essential contact fields like name, email, phone number, department, and job title.

2. Data Transformation

Retrieved data is formatted to match Google Contacts' expected structure. Special handling ensures proper name formatting, phone number standardization, and department categorization.

3. Google Contacts Update

The system checks if each employee already exists in Google Contacts. New employees are added as contacts, while existing ones have their details updated. Contacts can be organized into groups like "Current Employees" for easy access.

Pro tip: Configure the sync to run daily during off-hours to minimize impact on HR systems while ensuring contact details stay current.

The frequency depends on your employee change rate. A 500-person company with 5% monthly turnover might sync daily, while a stable 50-person team could sync weekly. Monitor your Personio change logs to determine the optimal schedule.

Essential fields include name, work email, phone number, job title, department, and manager. Optional useful fields are office location, work anniversary date, and profile photo. Avoid syncing sensitive personal data like home addresses or national IDs.

Consider creating different contact groups in Google for different data needs. "Basic Contacts" might show minimal info for all employees, while "Full Team" could include additional fields for department heads and executives who need more context.

This template syncs one-way from Personio to Google Contacts. Two-way syncing requires additional validation to prevent accidental overwrites of manually updated contacts. Most businesses prefer the control of one-way HR system to contacts syncing.

If you need two-way syncing, we recommend implementing approval workflows where Google Contact changes are flagged for HR review before updating Personio. This maintains data integrity while allowing for emergency contact updates when needed.

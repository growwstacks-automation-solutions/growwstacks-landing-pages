What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of staying current with forum discussions by delivering real-time Safari notifications whenever new Disqus comments appear. Community managers often waste hours manually refreshing forums to monitor activity - this workflow eliminates that inefficiency.

The system connects directly to your Disqus moderation dashboard, detecting new comments within seconds of posting. It then pushes native notifications to all your Apple devices through Safari's notification API, ensuring you can respond promptly to important discussions.

How It Works

1. Disqus Comment Detection

The workflow continuously monitors specified Disqus forums through API checks every 5 minutes (configurable interval). When new comments meet your criteria, they're immediately flagged for processing.

2. Notification Processing

Each qualifying comment triggers the creation of a rich notification containing the author's name, comment excerpt, and direct link to the discussion thread.

3. Safari Delivery

Notifications are delivered through Apple's Safari Push Notification service, appearing on all signed-in devices including Macs, iPhones and iPads with your permission.

Pro tip: Combine this with Make.com's filtering options to only receive notifications for comments containing specific keywords or from high-priority users.

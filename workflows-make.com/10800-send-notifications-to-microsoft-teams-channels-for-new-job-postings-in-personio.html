What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your Personio HR system and Microsoft Teams collaboration platform. Whenever a new job posting is created in Personio, it automatically sends a notification to designated Teams channels with all relevant details.

Recruitment teams often waste valuable time manually announcing new openings across departments. This workflow eliminates that friction by instantly notifying all relevant stakeholders the moment a position goes live. Hiring managers get immediate visibility, recruiters can start sourcing faster, and department heads stay informed without extra effort.

How It Works

1. New job posting detected in Personio

The workflow continuously monitors your Personio account for newly published job positions. As soon as one appears, it captures all relevant details including job title, department, location, and description.

2. Data formatted for Teams notification

The job details are structured into a clean, readable format optimized for Microsoft Teams. You can customize which fields appear and how they're presented to match your organization's preferences.

3. Notification sent to designated Teams channels

The formatted message is automatically posted to your specified Microsoft Teams channels. You can route different job types to different channels (e.g., technical roles to engineering-team, marketing roles to growth-team).

Pro tip: Add quick action buttons to your Teams notifications that link directly to the Personio posting or your referral form to encourage immediate engagement.

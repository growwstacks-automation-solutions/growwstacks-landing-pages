What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of missing important customer interactions on Facebook Page posts. Many businesses struggle to monitor comments in real-time, especially when managing multiple social channels or during busy periods. The workflow bridges Facebook and Pushbullet to deliver instant, cross-platform notifications whenever someone comments on your business posts.

Unlike Facebook's native notifications that can get buried in the app, Pushbullet delivers prominent alerts across all your devices simultaneously. This ensures you never miss time-sensitive customer inquiries, feedback, or discussions about your brand. The automation runs continuously in the background, requiring no manual checking of your Facebook Page.

How It Works

1. Monitoring Facebook Page Posts

The workflow constantly checks your specified Facebook Page for new comments on selected posts. You can configure it to monitor all posts or specific ones relevant to campaigns or promotions.

2. Detecting New Comments

When a user posts a new comment, Make.com captures the comment details including the text, author, timestamp, and post context. Advanced filters can be applied to only trigger notifications for specific types of comments.

3. Sending Pushbullet Notifications

The system formats the comment information into a concise notification and pushes it through Pushbullet to all your connected devices. Each notification includes actionable details to help you respond appropriately.

Pro tip: Combine this with a CRM integration to automatically log Facebook interactions alongside customer records for complete context.

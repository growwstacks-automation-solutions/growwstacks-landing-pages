What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your sales pipeline and accounting system by automatically generating Xero invoices when deals reach specific stages in Pipedrive. It eliminates manual data entry, reduces errors, and ensures invoices go out immediately when deals are ready to bill.

The workflow checks for new qualifying deals every 15 minutes (configurable), extracts all relevant product and pricing information, and creates corresponding invoices in Xero with proper line items, taxes, and customer details. This maintains perfect synchronization between your CRM and accounting records.

How It Works

1. Pipedrive Deal Monitoring

The workflow watches for deals entering your specified "Ready to Invoice" stage in Pipedrive. You configure which stage triggers the automation based on your sales process.

2. Product Data Extraction

For each qualifying deal, the system retrieves all associated products with their quantities, prices, and any custom fields you need on invoices.

3. Customer Information Mapping

Contact details from Pipedrive map to the corresponding Xero customer record, creating new contacts in Xero if necessary while preventing duplicates.

4. Invoice Generation

The system creates a complete Xero invoice with all line items, applying your configured tax rates and accounting codes automatically.

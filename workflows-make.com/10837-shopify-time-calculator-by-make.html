What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how Shopify businesses approach meeting culture by quantifying the true cost of every call and sync. It automatically calculates the financial impact of meetings based on participant salaries, opportunity costs, and duration, creating tangible accountability for time management.

By making meeting costs visible, teams naturally become more selective about scheduling calls. One merchant reduced unnecessary meetings by 37% in the first month, reclaiming over $12,000 in productive time. The workflow integrates with your existing calendar and payroll systems to provide real-time cost analysis without manual calculations.

How It Works

1. Calendar Integration

The system connects to your Google Calendar or Outlook to pull all scheduled meetings with their participants and durations.

2. Payroll Data Sync

It securely accesses your payroll system or manually entered salary data to determine each participant's hourly rate.

3. Cost Calculation

For each meeting, the system calculates: (Number of Participants × Hourly Rate × Duration) + Opportunity Cost based on your Shopify store's average revenue per hour.

4. Reporting

Automated reports show meeting costs by team, individual, and type - helping identify the most expensive (and often least productive) recurring meetings.

Pro tip: Set up automated alerts when any meeting's calculated cost exceeds your predefined threshold to trigger mandatory agenda reviews.

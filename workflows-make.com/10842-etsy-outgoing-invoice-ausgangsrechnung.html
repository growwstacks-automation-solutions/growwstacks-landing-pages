What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of transferring Etsy order data into your FreeFinance accounting system. Etsy sellers typically spend 5-15 minutes per order creating invoices—time that adds up quickly when processing dozens of orders daily.

The workflow checks your Etsy shop hourly for new orders, extracts all relevant customer and product information, then generates corresponding invoices in FreeFinance with perfect accuracy. It handles:

  • Automatic order data collection from Etsy
  • Mapping product details to invoice line items
  • Applying correct tax rates based on buyer location
  • Including shipping costs and discounts
  • Setting proper invoice dates and payment terms

How It Works

1. Scheduled Etsy Order Check

The workflow runs automatically every 60 minutes (configurable) to check your Etsy store for new orders through Etsy's API. It only processes orders that haven't been invoiced yet.

2. Data Extraction and Validation

For each new order, the system extracts buyer information, item details, quantities, prices, shipping costs, and order notes. It validates all financial data matches Etsy's records before proceeding.

3. FreeFinance Invoice Creation

Using your pre-configured FreeFinance invoice template, the workflow creates a complete outgoing invoice with all extracted Etsy order data. It maintains your branding and standard payment terms.

4. Confirmation and Logging

Each successful invoice creation is logged with timestamps. Optionally, you can configure email confirmations sent to your accounting team.

Pro tip: Add a second workflow that checks Etsy for cancellations and automatically creates corresponding credit memos in FreeFinance.

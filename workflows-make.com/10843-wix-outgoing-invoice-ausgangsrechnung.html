What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges your Wix ecommerce store with FreeFinance accounting software, eliminating manual invoice creation. Every time a customer completes an order in your Wix store, the workflow instantly generates a corresponding outgoing invoice in FreeFinance with all relevant details.

Manual invoice creation is time-consuming and prone to errors - especially during busy periods when orders flood in. This solution ensures your billing stays synchronized with sales in real-time, improving cash flow visibility and reducing administrative overhead.

How It Works

1. New order detection

The workflow monitors your Wix store for new orders through Wix's API. It triggers immediately when an order is marked as paid.

2. Data extraction

Key order details are extracted including customer information, purchased items, quantities, prices, and payment method.

3. Invoice creation

The system formats the data according to FreeFinance's requirements and creates a draft invoice with all line items.

4. Validation & submission

Before finalizing, the workflow verifies all required fields are populated correctly then submits the invoice to FreeFinance.

Pro tip: For businesses with complex tax requirements, extend this workflow to automatically apply the correct VAT rates based on customer location.

  1. A Wix store with API access (Business plan or higher)

  3. Make.com account (free tier works for basic usage)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Install the template in your Make.com account
  2. Connect your Wix store by authenticating via OAuth
  3. Enter your FreeFinance API credentials
  4. Map Wix order fields to FreeFinance invoice fields
  5. Test with a sample order and verify invoice creation
  6. Activate the workflow for live processing

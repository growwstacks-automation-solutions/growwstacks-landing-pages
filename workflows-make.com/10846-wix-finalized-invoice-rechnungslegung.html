What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point for e-commerce businesses using Wix - the manual transfer of order data into accounting systems. Every time a customer places an order on your Wix store, this workflow automatically creates a corresponding finalized invoice in FreeFinance, Austria's popular accounting software.

The solution eliminates hours of manual data entry while reducing errors that often occur when transferring information between systems. By automating this financial process, you ensure accurate bookkeeping from the moment an order is placed, giving you real-time visibility into your revenue without additional administrative work.

How It Works

1. New order detection

The workflow constantly monitors your Wix store for new orders. As soon as a customer completes a purchase, the system detects the new order event.

2. Data extraction

Key order details like customer information, products purchased, quantities, prices, and totals are extracted from the Wix order.

3. Invoice creation

The system formats the extracted data according to FreeFinance's requirements and creates a new finalized invoice in your accounting system.

4. Error handling

If any issues occur during the process, the workflow can notify administrators or retry the operation according to predefined rules.

Pro tip: For high-volume stores, consider adding a delay step to batch process invoices at set intervals rather than creating them individually.

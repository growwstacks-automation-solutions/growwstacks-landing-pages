What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges your Shopify store with FreeFinance accounting software, automatically generating finalized invoices for every new order. It eliminates the tedious manual process of recreating order details in your accounting system, reducing errors and saving valuable time.

The workflow checks for new Shopify orders at regular intervals (default every 60 minutes), extracts all relevant order information including customer details, products, quantities, prices, and taxes, then creates corresponding invoices in FreeFinance with matching line items and totals. This ensures your financial records stay perfectly synchronized with your ecommerce sales.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Order Checks

The workflow automatically polls your Shopify store for new orders at your specified interval. You can adjust the frequency based on your order volume and accounting needs.

2. Order Data Extraction

For each new order, the system retrieves complete details including customer information, shipping address, product SKUs, quantities, unit prices, discounts, taxes, and payment method.

3. Invoice Creation in FreeFinance

The workflow maps all order details to corresponding fields in FreeFinance, creating a finalized invoice with proper line items, totals, and customer references. The invoice is marked as finalized immediately upon creation.

4. Error Handling

If any issues occur during processing (like missing customer data), the workflow can be configured to notify your team or retry the operation after a delay.

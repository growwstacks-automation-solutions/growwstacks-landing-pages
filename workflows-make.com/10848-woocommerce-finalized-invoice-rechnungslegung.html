What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical pain point for WooCommerce merchants using FreeFinance for accounting. Manually creating invoices for every order is time-consuming and prone to errors, especially during peak sales periods.

The workflow automatically detects new WooCommerce orders, extracts all relevant customer and product information, and creates perfectly formatted finalized invoices in FreeFinance. It runs every 60 minutes by default (configurable) to ensure no orders slip through the cracks.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Order Check

The automation checks your WooCommerce store for new orders at regular intervals. You can adjust the frequency from minutes to days based on your sales volume.

2. Order Data Extraction

For each new order, the workflow retrieves customer details, purchased items, quantities, prices, taxes, shipping costs, and payment method.

3. Invoice Creation

The system formats this data into FreeFinance's required structure and creates a finalized invoice with all line items properly categorized.

4. Status Verification

After invoice creation, the workflow can optionally mark WooCommerce orders as processed or trigger notifications to your accounting team.

Pro tip: For high-volume stores, consider running this automation hourly during business hours and less frequently overnight to balance responsiveness with resource usage.

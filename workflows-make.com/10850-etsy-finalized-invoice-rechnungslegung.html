What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical pain point for Etsy sellers using FreeFinance for accounting. Every time you make a sale on Etsy, the workflow automatically creates a corresponding finalized invoice in FreeFinance with all order details accurately transferred.

Manual invoice creation from Etsy orders typically takes 5-7 minutes per sale. For shops with dozens of daily orders, this adds up to hours of repetitive data entry each week. The automation eliminates this tedious work while ensuring 100% accuracy in your financial records.

How It Works

1. Monitoring New Etsy Orders

The workflow checks your Etsy store at regular intervals (default: hourly) for new orders. When detected, it retrieves complete order details including buyer information, items purchased, quantities, prices, taxes, and shipping costs.

2. Data Transformation

Order data is reformatted to match FreeFinance's invoice requirements. This includes currency conversion (if needed), tax calculations, and proper field mapping between the two systems.

3. Invoice Creation in FreeFinance

The processed data automatically generates a finalized invoice in FreeFinance with all line items, totals, and customer details. The invoice is marked as complete and ready for your accounting processes.

Pro tip: Set the automation to run every 15-30 minutes during peak sales periods to keep your financial records completely up-to-date.

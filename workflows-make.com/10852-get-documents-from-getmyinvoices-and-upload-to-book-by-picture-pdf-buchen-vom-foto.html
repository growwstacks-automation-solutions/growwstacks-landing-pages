Automated PDF Upload from GetMyInvoices to FreeFinance

Eliminate manual document transfers with this workflow that automatically uploads PDFs to FreeFinance's Book by Picture section

Get This Workflow → Make.com · Accounting · Free Template

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of downloading PDF documents from GetMyInvoices and uploading them to FreeFinance's Book by Picture section. Accounting teams typically spend 5-10 hours per month on this repetitive task, which is prone to human errors like misfiled documents or missed uploads.

The workflow automatically checks GetMyInvoices for new documents every hour, extracts the relevant PDF files along with their metadata, and uploads them to the correct category in FreeFinance. This ensures all financial documents are properly archived without manual intervention while maintaining a complete audit trail.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Document Check

The workflow runs on a schedule (default hourly) to check your GetMyInvoices account for newly added documents. The schedule can be adjusted based on your document volume.

2. Document Retrieval

For each new document found, the system retrieves both the PDF file and associated metadata including invoice number, date, amount, and vendor information.

3. PDF Processing

The workflow prepares the PDF for upload by verifying its integrity and extracting any required information needed for proper categorization in FreeFinance.

4. FreeFinance Upload

The processed PDF is uploaded to FreeFinance's Book by Picture section with all relevant metadata preserved. The system confirms successful upload before moving to the next document.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Install the template in your Make.com account
  2. Connect your GetMyInvoices account via OAuth
  3. Authenticate with your FreeFinance credentials
  4. Configure document filters if needed (optional)
  5. Set your preferred schedule frequency
  6. Test with sample documents before going live

Pro tip: Start with hourly runs and adjust frequency based on your document volume. Businesses with high transaction volumes may benefit from running every 15-30 minutes.

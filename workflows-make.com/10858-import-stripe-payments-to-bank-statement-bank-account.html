What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of reconciling Stripe payments with bank statements in FreeFinance. Every time a customer makes a payment through Stripe, the workflow instantly creates a corresponding bank statement line in FreeFinance with all relevant transaction details.

The integration eliminates accounting errors that occur during manual data entry and ensures your financial records are always up-to-date. Financial teams save hours each week previously spent cross-referencing payments and updating accounting systems.

How It Works

1. Payment Trigger

The workflow monitors your Stripe account for new successful payments. Each time a customer completes a payment, Stripe sends real-time data about the transaction.

2. Data Transformation

The payment details are formatted to match FreeFinance's bank statement requirements. This includes converting timestamps, mapping payment methods to account codes, and calculating net amounts after fees.

3. FreeFinance Update

The formatted transaction data is pushed to FreeFinance as a new bank statement line. The entry includes all necessary references for easy reconciliation and audit purposes.

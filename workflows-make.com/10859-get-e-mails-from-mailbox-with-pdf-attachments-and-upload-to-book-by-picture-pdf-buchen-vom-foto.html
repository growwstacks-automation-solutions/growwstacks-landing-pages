What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of downloading PDF attachments from emails and uploading them to your FreeFinance accounting software. Many businesses receive invoices, receipts, and financial documents via email that need to be properly filed in their accounting system. The manual process is time-consuming and prone to human error.

The workflow automatically checks your email inbox at regular intervals (default every 60 minutes), identifies new messages with PDF attachments, extracts these documents, and uploads them directly to the Book by Picture section in FreeFinance. This eliminates the need for employees to manually download and upload documents, saving significant time while ensuring no important financial documents are missed.

How It Works

1. Email Monitoring

The workflow connects to your email account (IMAP or Office 365) and scans for new messages containing PDF attachments. You can specify which folders to monitor and set filters based on sender, subject, or other criteria.

2. PDF Extraction

When a qualifying email is found, the system extracts all PDF attachments while preserving the original file names and metadata. The workflow can process multiple attachments per email.

3. FreeFinance Upload

Each extracted PDF is automatically uploaded to the designated Book by Picture section in your FreeFinance account. The system maintains a log of processed documents for auditing purposes.

4. Scheduled Execution

The workflow runs on a schedule you control (default hourly). You can adjust the frequency based on your document volume and processing needs.

What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your Pipedrive CRM and Mems documentation system, automatically creating detailed Mem documents whenever a new deal is added to Pipedrive. It eliminates manual data entry, ensures consistent documentation, and helps your sales team stay organized.

When a sales rep creates a new opportunity in Pipedrive, the workflow instantly generates a corresponding Mem containing all relevant deal information. This includes contact details, deal value, expected close date, custom fields, and any notes from the CRM. The automation maintains data accuracy while saving your team hours of repetitive documentation work.

How It Works

1. New deal detection

The workflow monitors your Pipedrive account for newly created deals. You can configure it to watch specific pipelines or deal stages to match your sales process.

2. Data extraction

When a new deal is detected, the automation pulls all relevant information from Pipedrive including deal name, value, contacts, company information, custom fields, and notes.

3. Mem creation

The system formats the extracted data into a clean Mem document with structured sections for easy reading and reference. You can customize the template to match your company's documentation standards.

4. Team notification

Optionally, the workflow can notify relevant team members via email or Slack when a new Mem is created, ensuring everyone stays informed about new opportunities.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to only create Mems for deals above a certain value or in specific pipelines to avoid documentation overload for early-stage opportunities.

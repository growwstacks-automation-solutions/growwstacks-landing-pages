What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of creating audio versions of your WordPress content without manual effort. Many content creators want to offer voice overs for accessibility and engagement but lack the time or resources to produce them consistently.

The workflow automatically detects new WordPress articles, converts them to natural-sounding speech using ElevenLabs' advanced AI voices, then organizes the resulting audio files in Airtable with proper metadata. This creates a searchable audio library that can be shared with your audience or team members.

How It Works

1. WordPress article detection

The system monitors your WordPress site for new posts either through RSS feed or direct API connection. When a new article is published, it triggers the workflow.

2. Content preparation

The workflow extracts the article text, cleans it for optimal voice over quality (removing code blocks, excessive links, etc.), and formats it for ElevenLabs processing.

3. Voice over generation

ElevenLabs converts the prepared text to speech using your selected voice profile. You can configure parameters like speaking rate, tone, and emotional emphasis.

4. Airtable storage

The generated MP3 file is uploaded to Airtable along with metadata including the original article title, publication date, word count, and any custom tags you specify.

Pro tip: Create different Airtable views filtered by content category to quickly find relevant voice overs for repurposing in podcasts or social media clips.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solution transforms written WordPress content into professional-quality voice overs using ElevenLabs' advanced text-to-speech technology. It eliminates the need for manual recording sessions while maintaining high audio quality. The generated voice files are automatically organized in Google Sheets for easy access and management.

The workflow solves several pain points for content creators: it reduces production time from hours to minutes, ensures consistent voice quality across all content, and provides centralized tracking of all audio assets. Businesses can repurpose written content into podcasts, audio articles, or multilingual versions without additional production costs.

How It Works

1. WordPress Article Detection

The workflow monitors your WordPress site for new or updated articles. It can be configured to process specific categories or tags, giving you control over which content gets converted to audio.

2. Content Preparation

The system extracts and cleans the article text, removing any HTML tags or elements that shouldn't be read aloud. It can handle complex formatting while preserving the article's structure and meaning.

3. ElevenLabs Voice Generation

The prepared text is sent to ElevenLabs' API, which converts it to natural-sounding speech. You can specify voice characteristics like tone, speed, and style to match your brand identity.

4. Google Sheets Integration

Each generated voice over is uploaded to your preferred storage solution, with all details automatically recorded in Google Sheets. The sheet includes article titles, voice parameters, audio lengths, and direct access links.

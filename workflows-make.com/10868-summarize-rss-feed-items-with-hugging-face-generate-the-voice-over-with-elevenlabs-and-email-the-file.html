What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of information overload by transforming RSS feeds into convenient audio summaries. Instead of manually reading through dozens of articles, the workflow uses Hugging Face's AI to extract key points, converts them to natural-sounding voiceovers with ElevenLabs, and emails the audio files directly to you or your team.

The system handles the entire process automatically: fetching new feed items, analyzing content, generating concise summaries, creating high-quality voice recordings, and distributing the finished audio files. It's particularly valuable for professionals who need to stay informed but have limited time for reading, or for teams that benefit from audio-based knowledge sharing.

How It Works

1. RSS Feed Monitoring

The workflow continuously monitors your specified RSS feeds for new content. You can configure multiple feeds from different sources, with options to filter for specific keywords or content types.

2. AI-Powered Summarization

Each new feed item gets processed by Hugging Face's natural language processing models. The AI identifies and extracts the most important information, creating summaries that preserve key facts while reducing content length by 60-80%.

3. Voice Generation

The summarized text passes to ElevenLabs' voice synthesis platform, which converts it to natural-sounding speech. You can customize voice characteristics like tone, speed, and accent to match your preferences.

4. Email Delivery

The final audio files attach to automated emails sent to your specified recipients. Each email includes contextual information about the source content and summary highlights.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to run at specific times (like early morning) to receive your audio news digest at the most convenient time for your routine.

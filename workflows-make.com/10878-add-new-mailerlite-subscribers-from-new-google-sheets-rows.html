What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the tedious manual process of transferring new email subscribers from spreadsheets to your MailerLite account. It continuously monitors your Google Sheet for new rows containing subscriber information, then automatically creates corresponding contacts in MailerLite with all their details.

The workflow runs every 15 minutes by default (configurable to any interval), ensuring your email list stays current without any manual exports or imports. It handles the entire process from data validation to field mapping, while maintaining proper subscriber attribution and timestamps.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Google Sheets check

The scenario starts by checking your specified Google Sheet at regular intervals. It looks for newly added rows since the last run, using timestamp tracking to ensure no duplicates or missed entries.

2. Data validation and formatting

Each new row is validated to ensure required fields (especially email addresses) are present and properly formatted. Invalid entries are flagged for review while valid ones proceed to the next step.

3. MailerLite subscriber creation

Validated contact information is mapped to MailerLite's subscriber fields. The template handles standard fields (name, company) and can be extended for custom fields. Each new subscriber is added with proper attribution as coming from your Google Sheet source.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the template to your Make.com account

  4. Map your Google Sheet columns to MailerLite fields if different from default
  5. Test with a few sample rows to verify data flows correctly
  6. Adjust the sync frequency if needed (default is every 15 minutes)

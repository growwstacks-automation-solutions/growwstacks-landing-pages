What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common problem for businesses using both MailerLite for email marketing and Stripe for payments. When you gain new subscribers through forms, lead magnets, or other MailerLite integrations, they aren't automatically added to your Stripe customer database. This creates disjointed customer records and requires manual copying of contact information between systems.

The workflow automatically creates a corresponding customer record in Stripe whenever someone subscribes through MailerLite. It transfers key information like email addresses, names, and any custom fields you configure. This ensures your payment system stays synchronized with your marketing contacts without any manual work.

How It Works

1. New subscriber detection

The workflow continuously monitors your MailerLite account for new subscribers through Make.com's polling mechanism. When a new signup occurs, the automation is triggered.

2. Data preparation

The subscriber's information is extracted from MailerLite and formatted to match Stripe's customer record structure. You can customize which fields get transferred during setup.

3. Stripe customer creation

The workflow uses Stripe's API to create a new customer record with the subscriber's information. The system checks for existing customers with the same email to prevent duplicates.

4. Success confirmation

After successful creation, the workflow can optionally log the transaction or notify your team through email/Slack. Failed attempts trigger automatic retries with error handling.

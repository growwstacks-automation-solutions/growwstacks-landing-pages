What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually creating Pinterest pins for multiple products or content pieces. By connecting Airtable (your data source) with Bannerbear (image generation) and Pinterest (distribution), it eliminates repetitive design work while ensuring brand consistency across all your pins.

The workflow automatically generates visually appealing pins based on templates you design in Bannerbear, populated with real-time data from your Airtable base. This is particularly valuable for e-commerce businesses, content creators, and marketers who need to maintain an active Pinterest presence with fresh, on-brand content.

How It Works

1. New record triggers the workflow

When a new item is added to your designated Airtable table (or when existing records meet your criteria), the workflow automatically begins processing.

2. Data is prepared for image generation

The relevant fields from your Airtable record (product name, description, price, etc.) are extracted and formatted for Bannerbear's template variables.

3. Bannerbear generates the pin image

Using your pre-designed Bannerbear template, the system creates a customized pin image incorporating all the dynamic data from Airtable.

4. Pin is published to Pinterest

The completed image, along with metadata from Airtable, is automatically posted to your Pinterest account as a new pin.

Pro tip: Create multiple Bannerbear templates with different layouts to automatically generate pin variations for A/B testing.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually sharing Pinterest content across multiple platforms. It automatically detects new pins from your Pinterest account and instantly shares them to designated Discord and Telegram channels.

For marketers and community managers, this means your audience gets fresh visual content without you having to constantly monitor Pinterest and manually cross-post. The workflow maintains engagement across platforms while saving you hours each week.

How It Works

1. Monitor Pinterest for new pins

The workflow continuously checks your Pinterest account or specific boards for newly created pins. You can configure it to check as frequently as every 15 minutes.

2. Extract pin details

When new pins are detected, the automation captures all relevant information including the image, description, link, and creator details.

3. Format for messaging platforms

The pin content is automatically formatted appropriately for Discord and Telegram, ensuring optimal display with proper image previews and text formatting.

4. Post to Discord channel

The formatted pin is shared to your specified Discord channel, complete with rich embed formatting that shows the image preview and description.

5. Post to Telegram channel

Simultaneously, the pin is shared to your Telegram channel with proper media attachment and caption formatting.

Pro tip: Set up separate Discord/Telegram channels specifically for Pinterest content to keep your main channels uncluttered while giving fans a dedicated space for visual inspiration.

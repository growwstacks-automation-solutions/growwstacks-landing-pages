What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manually sharing content across multiple social media platforms. It automatically retrieves your newest Pinterest pins and posts them to both Instagram and Facebook with appropriate formatting for each platform.

Social media managers and content creators often struggle to maintain consistent posting schedules across multiple platforms. This workflow ensures your Instagram and Facebook feeds stay active with fresh content, while driving traffic back to your Pinterest profile. It's particularly valuable for ecommerce brands, bloggers, and digital marketers who use Pinterest as a content hub.

How It Works

1. Monitor Pinterest for New Pins

The workflow regularly checks your specified Pinterest boards or profile for newly added pins. You can configure it to look at specific boards or your entire profile.

2. Retrieve Pin Details

For each new pin found, the system extracts the image, description, link, and other metadata. This information is then prepared for cross-posting.

3. Format for Instagram

The pin image is automatically resized and optimized for Instagram's specifications. The description is converted into an engaging caption with relevant hashtags.

4. Post to Instagram

The formatted content is published to your Instagram account either immediately or according to your preferred posting schedule.

5. Format for Facebook

Simultaneously, the pin is prepared for Facebook with appropriate image dimensions and a slightly modified caption style that performs better on that platform.

6. Post to Facebook

The Facebook-optimized version is shared to your page or profile, completing the cross-posting cycle.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to add platform-specific calls-to-action. For Instagram, emphasize "Tap the link in bio" while on Facebook, use "Click the link below" for better conversion rates.

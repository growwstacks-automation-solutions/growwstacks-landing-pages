What This Workflow Does

This automation protects your Shopify store from fraudulent orders by automatically screening new customers through SEON's fraud detection system. When a customer places an order, the workflow instantly checks their email address and phone number against SEON's global fraud databases and behavioral analysis tools.

The system flags high-risk orders before fulfillment, allowing you to manually review suspicious purchases or automatically cancel clearly fraudulent attempts. This prevents chargebacks, protects your inventory, and maintains your store's reputation with payment processors.

How It Works

1. New order trigger

The workflow activates whenever a new customer completes checkout in your Shopify store. It captures their contact information and order details.

2. SEON fraud analysis

The customer's email and phone number are sent to SEON's API, which performs real-time analysis including email validity checks, phone carrier verification, and reputation scoring.

3. Risk assessment

SEON returns a comprehensive fraud score based on hundreds of data points. The workflow evaluates this against your predefined risk thresholds.

4. Action routing

Low-risk orders proceed normally. Medium-risk orders trigger a manual review notification. High-risk orders can be automatically canceled with a fraud alert added to the Shopify order notes.

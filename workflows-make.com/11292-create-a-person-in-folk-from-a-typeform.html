What This Workflow Does

This automation instantly transforms Typeform submissions into organized Folk contacts, eliminating manual data entry. When a prospect completes your Typeform—whether it's a job application, event registration, or product inquiry—their information automatically populates as a fully-formed contact in your Folk CRM.

The workflow ensures no lead falls through the cracks by processing submissions in real-time. It handles all the tedious work of transferring names, emails, phone numbers, and custom form fields while maintaining data consistency across both platforms.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New Typeform Response

The workflow activates immediately when someone submits your Typeform. Make.com monitors your form 24/7 and captures every response as it arrives.

2. Data Parsing

The system extracts all submitted information, including answers to custom questions. It standardizes formats (like phone numbers) and can apply basic validation rules.

3. Contact Creation in Folk

Using Folk's API, the workflow creates a new contact with all relevant details. You can configure exactly which Typeform fields map to which Folk properties, including custom fields.

4. Optional Duplicate Checking

The template can be enhanced to check for existing contacts before creating new ones, preventing duplicates in your CRM.

What This Workflow Does

This automation connects your Folk CRM with Gmail to automatically send emails whenever a contact's status changes. Instead of manually tracking status updates and composing follow-up emails, this workflow handles it instantly, ensuring timely communication that matches each contact's current position in your sales pipeline.

For example, when a lead moves from "New" to "Qualified" status, you can automatically send them a personalized email with relevant case studies. Or when a client's status changes to "Renewal Due," they receive a tailored message about contract options. This eliminates manual follow-up tasks while maintaining consistent, personalized communication.

How It Works

1. Folk CRM Status Change Detection

The workflow constantly monitors your Folk CRM for any changes to contact statuses. It can track specific status transitions you define as triggers for email sending.

2. Email Template Selection

Based on the type of status change detected, the system selects the appropriate pre-written email template from your Gmail drafts or a connected template system.

3. Personalization & Sending

The workflow merges contact details from Folk CRM into the email template, personalizing fields like name, company, and specific status-related content before sending through your Gmail account.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for:

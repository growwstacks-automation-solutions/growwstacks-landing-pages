What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of stale contact information in sales and client management. When team members work from outdated CRM records, they miss opportunities and damage relationships with incorrect details. The workflow instantly bridges Folk CRM and Slack, creating a real-time notification system for any contact updates.

Whenever a contact's information changes in Folk—whether it's a new job title, updated phone number, company change, or status update in your pipeline—the workflow automatically sends a formatted message to your designated Slack channel. This eliminates manual checking while ensuring your team always has the freshest information for outreach and relationship management.

How It Works

1. Folk CRM detects contact changes

The workflow monitors your Folk account for any modifications to contact records, using Make.com's instant trigger capability. This could include new contacts added, existing contact updates, or status changes in your sales pipeline.

2. Data is processed and formatted

Make.com extracts the relevant changed fields and prepares them for Slack, highlighting what changed (old vs new values) and including any important context about the contact's status or relationship with your company.

3. Notification sent to Slack

The formatted message is delivered to your specified Slack channel, complete with @mentions for relevant team members and a direct link back to the Folk contact record for quick access. The message structure is customizable to show only the most important information for your team.

Pro tip: Configure your workflow to only notify on high-value changes (like job title or company updates) to avoid alert fatigue from minor field changes.

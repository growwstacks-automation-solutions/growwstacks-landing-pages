Automate Company Status Updates in Folk CRM

Sync contact status changes to company records automatically - no manual data entry required

Get This Workflow → Make.com · Free Template

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common CRM data consistency challenge. When sales teams update a contact's status in Folk CRM, the associated company record doesn't automatically reflect this change. This workflow bridges that gap by instantly propagating status updates from contacts to their related company profiles.

Manual status updates are time-consuming and error-prone, especially when dealing with hundreds of contacts. This template eliminates duplicate data entry while ensuring your sales pipeline reporting always reflects current reality. Company-level dashboards will now accurately show the status of all associated contacts.

How It Works

1. Trigger: Contact Status Change

The workflow monitors Folk CRM for any changes to contact status fields. When a salesperson updates a lead stage, opportunity status, or customer lifecycle phase, the automation immediately captures this event.

2. Lookup: Find Related Company

The system identifies all company records linked to the updated contact using Folk's built-in relationship mapping. This handles one-to-many relationships where multiple contacts may belong to the same company.

3. Update: Sync Status to Company

The contact's new status value is propagated to all related company records. You can configure whether to overwrite existing company statuses or only update blank fields.

4. Logging: Audit Trail

Every automated update is logged with timestamp, user who triggered the change, and before/after status values for compliance and troubleshooting.

Pro tip: Combine this with lead scoring automation to trigger status changes based on engagement thresholds rather than manual updates.

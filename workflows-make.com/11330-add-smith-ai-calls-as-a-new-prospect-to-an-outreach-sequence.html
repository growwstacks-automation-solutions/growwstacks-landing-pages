What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap in sales processes where phone leads often get lost between initial contact and follow-up. When Smith.ai's virtual receptionists answer calls from potential customers, their information typically sits isolated from your email outreach system. This workflow bridges that gap by instantly creating Outreach prospects and enrolling them in sequences.

The solution eliminates manual data transfer between systems, ensuring every phone lead receives timely, consistent follow-up. Sales teams using this automation typically see 30-50% more conversions from phone inquiries because prospects receive immediate email touchpoints while the initial call is still fresh in their minds.

How It Works

1. New call trigger

The workflow activates whenever Smith.ai logs a new incoming call from a potential customer. The virtual receptionist's notes and caller details become available to the automation.

2. Prospect creation

Make.com formats the caller information into Outreach's required fields, creating a new prospect record with all relevant call details attached.

3. Sequence enrollment

The new prospect automatically joins your predefined Outreach email sequence, triggering the first follow-up message according to your sequence timing rules.

Pro tip: Add a 15-30 minute delay before the first sequence email to allow sales reps time for immediate callbacks when appropriate.

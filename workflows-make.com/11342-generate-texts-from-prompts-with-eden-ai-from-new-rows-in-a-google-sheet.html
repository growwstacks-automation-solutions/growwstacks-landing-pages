What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually generating AI content from spreadsheet data. Whenever new rows are added to your Google Sheet containing text prompts, the workflow automatically sends them to Eden AI's powerful text generation models and returns polished content ready for use.

Businesses use this to create product descriptions at scale, generate marketing copy variations, produce first drafts of blog content, and automate repetitive writing tasks. The integration handles all the API calls, error checking, and formatting automatically, saving hours of manual work each week.

How It Works

1. New row detection

The workflow monitors your designated Google Sheet for new rows containing text prompts in a specified column. It triggers whenever content is added or modified.

2. Prompt processing

Each new prompt is extracted from the spreadsheet and formatted with any additional context or instructions you've configured in the workflow settings.

3. AI text generation

The processed prompt is sent to Eden AI's API, which routes it to the most appropriate text generation model based on your configuration and content requirements.

4. Result validation

The generated text is checked for quality, length, and formatting before being returned to your workflow.

5. Output delivery

The final AI-generated content is written back to your Google Sheet in a designated output column or sent to other connected apps like your CMS, email platform, or document storage.

Pro tip: Structure your prompts with clear instructions, examples, and tone guidelines in separate spreadsheet columns to get more consistent results from the AI models.

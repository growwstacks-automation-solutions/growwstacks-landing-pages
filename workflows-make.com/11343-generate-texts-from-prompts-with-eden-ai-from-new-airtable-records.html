What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of creating consistent, high-quality content at scale by connecting Airtable with Eden AI's powerful text generation capabilities. When new records are added to your Airtable base, the workflow automatically generates multiple text variations based on your predefined prompts.

Businesses use this to streamline content creation for marketing campaigns, product descriptions, social media posts, and more. Instead of manually writing each piece of content, you can generate dozens of variations in seconds, then select the best options for your needs.

How It Works

1. New Record Trigger

The workflow monitors your Airtable base for new records containing content prompts or product information that needs text generation.

2. AI Processing

Each record's data is sent to Eden AI, which analyzes the prompt and generates multiple text variations using advanced natural language processing.

3. Quality Filtering

The system can be configured to automatically filter out low-quality outputs or flag content that needs human review based on your criteria.

4. Results Storage

Generated texts are saved back to Airtable or your preferred destination, organized by source prompt and generation parameters for easy reference.

Pro tip: Create a separate Airtable view for your best-performing prompts so you can easily reuse them across campaigns.

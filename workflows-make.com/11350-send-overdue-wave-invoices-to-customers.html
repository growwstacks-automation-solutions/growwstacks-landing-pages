What This Workflow Does

Late payments create cash flow challenges for businesses of all sizes. This automation solves the problem by automatically identifying and following up on overdue Wave invoices, ensuring you get paid faster without manual effort.

The workflow checks your Wave account daily for unpaid invoices past their due date, then sends professional, personalized reminders to clients via email. It maintains all the human touchpoints of manual follow-ups while eliminating the time-consuming tracking and sending process.

How It Works

1. Daily Invoice Status Check

The workflow connects to your Wave account each morning to scan for invoices that became overdue overnight or remain unpaid from previous days.

2. Smart Filtering

It intelligently filters invoices based on your configured grace period (e.g., only invoices 7+ days overdue) to avoid premature reminders.

3. Personalized Reminder Generation

For each overdue invoice, the system generates a customized email with the client's name, invoice details, and payment instructions pulled directly from Wave.

4. Automated Delivery

Reminders are sent through your connected email service with professional templates that maintain your brand voice while gently prompting payment.

5. Activity Logging

Each sent reminder is recorded with timestamps, allowing you to track follow-up history and identify chronic late payers.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of extracting information from resumes received via email. When candidates send their resumes to your Gmail, the workflow automatically processes the attachments using Eden AI's powerful parsing technology, then organizes the extracted data into a structured Google Doc.

Recruiters and hiring managers waste countless hours manually reviewing resumes and copying information into tracking systems. This workflow eliminates that repetitive work by instantly converting unstructured resume data into organized candidate profiles with consistent formatting. The AI understands different resume layouts and extracts key details like contact information, work history, education, and skills.

How It Works

1. New Resume Detection

The workflow monitors your Gmail account for new messages with resume attachments. It can be configured to watch specific labels or folders, or scan all incoming mail for PDF or DOCX attachments that likely contain resume content.

2. AI-Powered Data Extraction

When a new resume is detected, the file is sent to Eden AI's parsing engine. The AI analyzes the document structure, identifies sections, and extracts relevant candidate information with impressive accuracy, regardless of the resume's original format.

3. Structured Data Organization

The extracted information is then formatted into a clean, standardized Google Doc. The workflow creates a new document for each candidate, with consistent section headings and logical organization of the parsed data.

4. Automatic Storage & Notification

Completed candidate profiles are saved to a designated Google Drive folder, and the workflow can optionally send notifications to your team via email or Slack when new profiles are added.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to add candidate ratings based on keyword matches for skills or qualifications you're seeking. This creates an instant scoring system to prioritize top applicants.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the growing problem of email overload by using artificial intelligence to identify and extract the most important information from your Gmail messages. Instead of your team wasting time reading lengthy email threads, the system automatically generates concise summaries highlighting key decisions, action items, and updates.

The processed summaries are then posted to designated Slack channels, creating a searchable knowledge base that keeps everyone informed without inbox clutter. This is particularly valuable for customer support teams, project managers, and executives who receive high volumes of critical communications but need to stay focused on strategic work.

How It Works

1. Email Trigger

The workflow monitors your Gmail account for new messages matching your criteria (specific senders, labels, or keywords). You can configure it to process all incoming mail or only tagged messages.

2. AI Processing

Each selected email is sent to Eden AI's natural language processing engine, which analyzes the content to identify the core message, extract names/dates/actions, and generate a summary at your preferred length (bullet points or short paragraphs).

3. Slack Delivery

The system formats the summary with the original sender, subject line, and urgency indicators, then posts it to your chosen Slack channel. You can set different routing rules for various email types (e.g., customer inquiries vs. internal updates).

Pro tip: Combine this with Slack reminders to create follow-up tasks directly from summarized emails, closing the loop on action items.

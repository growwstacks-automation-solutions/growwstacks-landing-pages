What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the growing problem of email overload by using Eden AI's natural language processing to extract key information from incoming messages. Instead of your team wasting time reading lengthy threads, they receive concise, actionable summaries directly in Slack where collaboration happens.

The system maintains context while removing redundant information, attachments, and signatures. It's particularly valuable for customer support teams, project managers, and executives who receive high volumes of information-dense emails but need to focus on decision-making rather than processing raw data.

How It Works

1. Email Trigger

The workflow monitors your specified email inbox(es) for new messages. You can filter by sender, subject keywords, or labels to focus on priority communications.

2. AI Processing

Eden AI analyzes the email content, identifying main topics, action items, and key details. The algorithm preserves critical context while typically reducing content length by 70-80%.

3. Slack Formatting

The summary gets formatted with clear section headers, bullet points, and optional emoji indicators. You can customize the template to highlight deadlines, requests, or sentiment.

4. Channel Routing

Based on rules you set, summaries get posted to specific Slack channels. For example, customer emails might go to #support while project updates route to relevant team channels.

Pro tip: Add a "summary quality" reaction button in Slack. Team members can quickly flag when summaries miss key points, helping train the AI over time.

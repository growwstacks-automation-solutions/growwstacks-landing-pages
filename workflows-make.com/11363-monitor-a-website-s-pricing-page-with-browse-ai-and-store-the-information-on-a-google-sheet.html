What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming task of manually checking competitor websites for pricing changes. It combines Browse AI's web scraping capabilities with Make.com's automation power to create a reliable pricing monitoring system.

The workflow periodically checks specified pricing pages, extracts the current pricing information, and logs it to a Google Sheet with timestamps. This creates a historical record you can use to identify trends, spot promotions, and make data-driven pricing decisions.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Monitoring Trigger

The workflow starts on a schedule you define (daily, weekly, etc.) to check your competitor's pricing page.

2. Browse AI Extracts Pricing Data

Browse AI visits the target website and extracts the current pricing information using its trained AI models.

3. Data Processing

Make.com processes the scraped data to format it consistently and prepare it for storage.

4. Google Sheets Logging

The processed pricing data gets appended to your designated Google Sheet with a timestamp for tracking changes over time.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solution streamlines the process of welcoming new Discord guild members by automatically delivering customized onboarding materials and community guidelines. It eliminates the need for manual welcome messages while ensuring every new member receives consistent, professional introductions to your community.

The workflow triggers when a new member joins your Discord server, then executes a series of personalized welcome actions. This typically includes sending direct messages with community rules, highlighting important channels, and providing role-specific resources - all while freeing up your moderators to focus on higher-value community interactions.

How It Works

1. New Member Detection

The workflow monitors your Discord server for new member join events in real-time through Make.com's Discord integration.

2. Initial Welcome Message

Immediately sends a personalized greeting with basic community introduction and next steps.

3. Resource Delivery

Distributes onboarding materials like rulebooks, channel guides, and role explanations through direct messages or designated channels.

4. Follow-up Sequence

Triggers additional messages over subsequent days to gradually introduce community features without overwhelming new members.

5. Moderation Alert

Optionally notifies your moderation team about the new member for manual follow-up if needed.

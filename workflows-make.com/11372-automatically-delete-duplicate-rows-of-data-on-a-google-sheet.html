What This Workflow Does

Duplicate data plagues spreadsheets, causing reporting errors, wasted storage, and operational inefficiencies. This automation identifies and removes redundant rows from your Google Sheets based on your custom criteria, ensuring clean datasets for accurate analysis and decision-making.

The solution goes beyond simple duplicate detection - it can be configured to handle partial matches, fuzzy logic comparisons, and complex multi-column validation rules. Unlike manual cleanup that consumes hours each month, this workflow runs automatically on your schedule without human intervention.

How It Works

1. Data Scanning

The workflow connects to your specified Google Sheet and scans all rows against your defined duplicate criteria (specific columns or combinations).

2. Duplicate Identification

Using your configured matching rules (exact, partial, or fuzzy), the system flags duplicate entries while preserving the original record.

3. Verification & Action

Depending on your settings, duplicates can be: deleted permanently, moved to an archive sheet, or flagged for manual review in a separate tab.

4. Logging & Notification

The system maintains an audit log of all actions and can notify your team via email or Slack when duplicates are processed.

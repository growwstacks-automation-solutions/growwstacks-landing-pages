Automatically Add Folk CRM Leads to Lemlist Campaigns

Instantly transfer qualified contacts from Folk to your Lemlist outreach sequences when their status changes

What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your CRM (Folk) and outreach platform (Lemlist), ensuring sales-ready leads immediately enter your email sequences. When a contact's status changes in Folk - like moving from "New" to "Qualified" - the workflow automatically adds them to your specified Lemlist campaign.

Manual lead transfers between systems create bottlenecks in sales pipelines. This solution eliminates that friction point, reducing the time between lead qualification and first outreach from hours to seconds. The template handles the entire transfer process including contact details, custom fields, and any relevant notes from Folk.

How It Works

1. Monitor Folk CRM for status changes

The workflow continuously watches your Folk account for any contact status updates. You can configure which specific status transitions should trigger the automation (e.g., only when leads become "Sales Ready").

2. Retrieve complete contact details

When a qualifying status change occurs, the workflow fetches the contact's full profile from Folk including name, email, company, custom fields, and any notes that might inform your outreach strategy.

3. Add to Lemlist campaign

The contact is automatically added to your predefined Lemlist sequence. The template maps all relevant fields between systems, maintaining data consistency for personalized outreach.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to only trigger for high-intent status changes rather than every update. This prevents overloading your Lemlist campaigns with unqualified leads.

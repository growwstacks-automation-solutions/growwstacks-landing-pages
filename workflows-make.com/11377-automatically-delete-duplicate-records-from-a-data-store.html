What This Workflow Does

Duplicate records plague databases of all sizes, creating inconsistencies that affect reporting accuracy and operational efficiency. This Make.com workflow automatically identifies and removes duplicate entries from your Data Store, ensuring clean, reliable data.

The system scans your records based on configurable matching criteria (like email addresses or customer IDs), flags potential duplicates for review, and automatically removes confirmed duplicates according to your business rules. This eliminates manual cleanup efforts while maintaining data integrity.

How It Works

1. Record Scanning

The workflow periodically scans your Data Store, comparing records against your specified duplicate detection fields. You can configure the scan frequency based on your data volume needs.

2. Duplicate Identification

Using fuzzy matching logic, the system identifies potential duplicates even with minor variations (like "gmail.com" vs "gmail.co"). You can adjust matching sensitivity to balance thoroughness with false positives.

3. Verification & Action

Depending on your configuration, the workflow can either automatically remove confirmed duplicates or flag them for manual review. Optional email notifications keep you informed of actions taken.

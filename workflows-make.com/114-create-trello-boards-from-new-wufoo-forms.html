What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of form submissions getting lost in email inboxes or requiring manual data entry into project management tools. When clients submit Wufoo forms, this workflow instantly creates corresponding Trello boards with all submission details pre-populated.

The solution eliminates repetitive work for teams that use forms to initiate projects or service requests. Marketing agencies can automatically create client project boards, HR teams can process applications faster, and service businesses can ensure no customer inquiry falls through cracks.

How It Works

1. New form triggers the workflow

The automation monitors your Wufoo account for new form submissions. Each time form is completed, it instantly initiates the workflow without any manual intervention.

2 Data extracted from Wufoo

The workflow captures all submitted form fields - names, email addresses, project details, or whatever information your form collects. This data becomes available for mapping to Trello.

3 New Trello board created

Using the form data, workflow generates a new Trello board with: Custom board name based on form fields, Cards for each team member or task, Pre-filled checklists from form responses, and Labels for easy categorization.

4 Notification sent to team

Optionally, the workflow can notify your team via Slack, email, or other channels that a new project board has created with link for immediate access.

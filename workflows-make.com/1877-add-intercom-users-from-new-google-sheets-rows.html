What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the tedious manual process of transferring customer data from spreadsheets to your Intercom CRM. Whenever your sales or support teams add new customer information to a designated Google Sheet, the workflow instantly creates corresponding user profiles in Intercom with all relevant details.

The template handles field mapping automatically, ensuring spreadsheet columns are correctly matched to Intercom user attributes. It's particularly valuable for businesses onboarding new customers, managing lead lists, or coordinating between teams that use different tools.

How It Works

1. New row detection

The workflow monitors your Google Sheet for new rows added to a specified worksheet. You can configure it to watch specific columns or the entire sheet.

2. Data validation

Each new row is checked for required fields (like email) and formatted correctly before being sent to Intercom. Invalid entries can be flagged for review.

3. Intercom user creation

Validated data is used to create new user profiles in Intercom, with customizable field mapping to ensure data lands in the right places.

Pro tip: Add a "Status" column to your sheet to track which rows have been processed. The workflow can update this automatically upon successful Intercom creation.

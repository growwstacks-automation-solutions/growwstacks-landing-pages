Automatically archive valuable discussions and resources from your Facebook Groups into organized Evernote notebooks

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of losing valuable discussions and resources shared in Facebook Groups. Many community managers, educators, and professionals struggle to keep track of important posts amidst the constant flow of group activity. The workflow automatically captures each new Facebook Group post and creates a corresponding note in Evernote with the full content, author information, and timestamps.

By connecting these platforms, you create a permanent, searchable archive outside of Facebook's ecosystem. This is particularly valuable for reference materials, instructional content, and community knowledge that you want to preserve long-term. The automation runs continuously in the background, ensuring no important discussions slip through the cracks.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New Facebook Group Post

The workflow monitors your specified Facebook Group for new posts. You can choose to watch an entire group or filter for specific types of content.

2. Data Extraction

Make.com captures the post content, author details, timestamps, and any media attachments. This information is structured for clean transfer to Evernote.

3. Note Creation

The system creates a new Evernote note with the Facebook post content formatted for optimal readability. You can customize the note template to include specific metadata fields.

4. Organization

Notes are automatically filed in your designated Evernote notebook and can be tagged based on content type or keywords for easy retrieval.

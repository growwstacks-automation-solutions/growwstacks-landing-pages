What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between idea capture in Evernote and task execution in ClickUp. When you or your team adds notes to specified Evernote notebooks, the system automatically generates corresponding tasks in your ClickUp workspace with all relevant content preserved.

The workflow solves the common productivity bottleneck where valuable ideas get stuck in notes without becoming actionable items. Consultants, creative teams, and project managers can capture thoughts naturally in Evernote while ensuring nothing falls through the cracks in execution.

How It Works

1. Evernote Trigger

The workflow monitors your specified Evernote notebooks for new notes. You can choose specific notebooks for work-related content while excluding personal ones.

2. Content Processing

Make.com extracts the note title as the task name and includes the full note content in the description. Important metadata like creation date and tags are preserved.

3. ClickUp Task Creation

The system creates a new task in your designated ClickUp list, with options to set default assignees, due dates, and priorities based on your workflow rules.

4. Link Preservation

Each ClickUp task includes a direct link back to the original Evernote note, maintaining context for team members who need to reference source materials.

Pro tip: Create an "Ideas" notebook in Evernote specifically for task-worthy content, and pair it with a "Backlog" list in ClickUp for maximum workflow efficiency.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the manual process of transferring information from Typeform submissions to ClickUp tasks. Every time someone completes your Typeform survey or application, the system automatically creates a corresponding task in your specified ClickUp list with all relevant form data attached.

The workflow eliminates copy-paste work, reduces human error in task creation, and ensures no form submission gets overlooked. It's ideal for teams that use Typeform for customer onboarding, job applications, service requests, or any process that requires follow-up actions.

How It Works

1. Trigger on new Typeform entry

The workflow activates whenever a new submission is received in your connected Typeform. Make.com instantly detects the new entry and prepares the data for transfer.

2. Map form fields to ClickUp task

The system extracts all form responses and maps them to corresponding ClickUp task fields. You can customize which Typeform questions become task names, descriptions, custom fields, or attachments.

3. Create task in designated ClickUp list

The workflow creates a new task in your specified ClickUp space, folder, and list. You can set default assignees, priorities, due dates, or tags based on form responses.

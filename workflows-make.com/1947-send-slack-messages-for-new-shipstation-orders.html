What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical communication gap for ecommerce businesses by instantly alerting your team whenever new orders arrive in ShipStation. Instead of requiring staff to constantly check ShipStation or wait for email notifications, this workflow pushes real-time updates directly to your Slack channels.

The integration ensures your fulfillment team, customer service reps, and management are immediately aware of new orders without manual monitoring. You can customize the Slack messages to include all relevant order details like customer information, items purchased, shipping method, and any special instructions.

How It Works

1. ShipStation detects new order

The workflow continuously monitors your ShipStation account for new orders through Make.com's polling mechanism. Each new order triggers the automation sequence.

2. Order data is processed

Make.com extracts all relevant order details from ShipStation including customer information, shipping address, line items, and order value. This data is formatted for Slack notification.

3. Custom Slack message is sent

The workflow posts a formatted message to your specified Slack channel with all key order details. You can customize the message template to include exactly what your team needs to see.

Pro tip: Add emoji reactions or @mentions in your Slack notifications to draw attention to high-priority orders or specific team members who need to take action.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of order visibility across teams. When new orders come into ShipStation, your fulfillment team often doesn't know about them until someone manually checks the dashboard - creating delays in processing and shipping.

The workflow automatically detects every new order in ShipStation and posts a detailed notification to your designated Mattermost channel. This gives your entire team real-time visibility into incoming orders without requiring constant dashboard monitoring. You'll see customer details, order contents, shipping information, and other key data immediately when orders are placed.

How It Works

1. ShipStation triggers on new orders

The workflow monitors your ShipStation account for new orders using Make.com's instant webhook trigger. As soon as an order is created, the automation begins processing it.

2. Order data is formatted for Mattermost

Make.com extracts all relevant order details from ShipStation and structures them into a clean, readable format. You can customize which fields to include like customer name, order value, shipping method, and item details.

3. Notification posts to Mattermost

The formatted order information is sent to your specified Mattermost channel via webhook. The message can include @mentions for specific team members or include priority flags for urgent orders.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to highlight VIP customers or high-value orders with special formatting in Mattermost for faster team recognition.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming manual process of sending order confirmation emails for every new ShipStation order. Ecommerce businesses often waste hours each week copying order details into email templates, increasing the risk of errors and delaying customer communications.

The workflow automatically detects new orders in ShipStation and sends customized Gmail notifications containing all relevant order details. This ensures customers receive immediate confirmation while freeing your team to focus on fulfillment rather than administrative tasks.

How It Works

1. New order detection

The workflow continuously monitors your ShipStation account for new orders through Make.com's instant polling system. When an order appears, it triggers the automation immediately.

2. Data extraction

All relevant order information is pulled from ShipStation including customer details, items purchased, shipping method, and any custom fields. This data is structured for use in your email template.

3. Email composition

The workflow populates your pre-designed Gmail template with the extracted order data. You can customize the template with your branding and include any order details that are important for your customers.

4. Automated sending

Gmail sends the composed message directly from your business email account, appearing identical to manually sent confirmations but with perfect accuracy every time.

Pro tip: Add tracking information to your template once it becomes available in ShipStation by setting up a second automation that runs when orders ship.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of transferring ecommerce order data from ShipStation to Xero for invoicing. Every time a new order is created in ShipStation, the workflow automatically generates a corresponding invoice in Xero with all relevant details including customer information, products, quantities, prices, taxes, and shipping costs.

For ecommerce businesses processing dozens or hundreds of orders daily, this eliminates hours of repetitive data entry while ensuring 100% accuracy in financial records. The real-time sync means invoices are created immediately when orders are received, improving cash flow by accelerating billing cycles.

How It Works

1. New Order Trigger

The workflow monitors ShipStation for new orders, either through webhooks or periodic polling depending on your configuration.

2. Data Extraction

Key order details are extracted including customer details, line items, pricing, taxes, shipping information, and any custom fields.

3. Xero Invoice Creation

The workflow maps ShipStation data to Xero's invoice structure, creating a draft or approved invoice based on your settings.

4. Error Handling

Built-in error checking ensures data quality, with options to retry failed operations or notify administrators of issues.

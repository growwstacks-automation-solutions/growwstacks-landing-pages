What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious process of manually transferring new leads from Google Sheets to ActiveTrail. Every time your team adds a new row to your designated spreadsheet (like from form submissions or CRM exports), the workflow instantly creates a corresponding contact in ActiveTrail with all relevant details.

The template handles field mapping between your spreadsheet columns and ActiveTrail contact properties, ensuring data arrives in the correct format. It can process multiple contacts simultaneously and includes error handling to alert you if any records fail to sync properly.

How It Works

1. New Row Detection

The workflow monitors your specified Google Sheet for new rows added, either through real-time triggers or scheduled checks at your preferred interval.

2. Data Validation

Each new row is checked for required fields (like email address) and formatted correctly for ActiveTrail's API requirements.

3. ActiveTrail Contact Creation

Validated data is sent to ActiveTrail's API to create a new contact record, including any custom fields you've mapped from your spreadsheet.

4. Success/Failure Handling

The workflow logs each attempt and can notify your team via email or Slack if any records fail to process, with details about the error.

Pro tip: Add a "Tags" column in your Google Sheet to automatically categorize contacts in ActiveTrail based on lead source, product interest, or campaign name.

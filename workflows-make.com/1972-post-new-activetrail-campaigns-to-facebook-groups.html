What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges your email marketing and social media efforts by automatically posting new ActiveTrail campaigns to selected Facebook Groups. Whenever you launch an email campaign, the workflow instantly shares it with your target communities - eliminating manual copy-pasting and ensuring consistent cross-channel promotion.

The solution solves the common challenge of repurposing email content across social platforms. Marketing teams often create valuable email campaigns that would benefit Facebook Group members, but lack time to manually reformat and share them. This workflow handles the entire process automatically while maintaining proper formatting for each platform.

How It Works

1. New campaign detection

The workflow monitors your ActiveTrail account for newly created campaigns using webhooks or scheduled checks. It captures all relevant campaign details including subject lines, content previews, and links.

2. Content formatting

The system automatically reformats email content for optimal Facebook Group presentation. It extracts key elements and structures them into an engaging social post while preserving all important links and calls-to-action.

3. Targeted posting

Using your Facebook Group credentials, the workflow posts the formatted content to specified groups. You can configure different posting rules per group, such as adding group-specific introductions or hashtags.

  • Coaches and educators who distribute content via email but want to reach students in Facebook Groups

What You'll Need

  1. An ActiveTrail account with admin access to set up webhooks
  2. Facebook Group admin privileges or moderator access in target groups
  3. A Make.com account to host and run the automation

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Connect your ActiveTrail account in Make.com using API credentials

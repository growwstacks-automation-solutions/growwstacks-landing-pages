What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manually transferring new contacts between systems. When businesses collect leads through various channels into Google Contacts, those contacts often need to be added to email marketing platforms like ActiveTrail for campaigns and follow-ups.

The workflow automatically detects new contacts added to Google Contacts and pushes them to your specified ActiveTrail group. This eliminates:

  • Manual copy-pasting between systems
  • Delays in adding contacts to campaigns
  • Human errors in data transfer

How It Works

1. Trigger: New Google Contact

The workflow monitors your Google Contacts for new additions. Each time a contact is created, it triggers the automation.

2. Data Mapping

The system extracts relevant contact details (name, email, phone, etc.) and formats them according to ActiveTrail's requirements.

3. ActiveTrail Integration

The contact data is sent to your ActiveTrail account and added to the specified mailing list or group.

4. Error Handling

The workflow includes validation to handle cases where contacts might already exist or data formats don't match.

This automation benefits:

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Install the template from Make.com
  2. Connect your Google Contacts account
  3. Connect your ActiveTrail account
  4. Specify which ActiveTrail group to add new contacts to
  5. Test with a sample contact to verify the flow
  6. Activate the scenario

Pro tip: Create a dedicated Google Contacts group for contacts that should sync to ActiveTrail, then modify the trigger to only watch that specific group.

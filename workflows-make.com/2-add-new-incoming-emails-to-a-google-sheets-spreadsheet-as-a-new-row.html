What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common business challenge of manually tracking important emails and their contents. Every time you receive a new email, the workflow automatically extracts key information and adds it as a new row in your Google Sheets spreadsheet.

The template eliminates hours of tedious copy-pasting while creating a searchable database of your communications. You can capture sender details, subject lines, message content, timestamps, and any other relevant metadata - all organized in structured columns for easy analysis and reporting.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New Email Arrives

The workflow activates whenever a new email hits your inbox. You can configure it to watch specific folders or apply filters to only process relevant messages.

2. Data Extraction

Make.com parses the email to extract the fields you want to log - from basic headers to full message content and attachment details.

3. Google Sheets Update

The selected data gets formatted and appended as a new row in your designated spreadsheet, with each piece of information in its proper column.

Pro tip: Add conditional formatting in Google Sheets to highlight important messages or flag emails from key contacts automatically.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of missed appointments by sending timely iPhone push notifications whenever a new SuperSaaS booking is made. Traditional email reminders often get buried in inboxes, but push notifications appear instantly on the lock screen with a 90%+ open rate.

The workflow automatically detects new SuperSaaS appointments and sends customized push notifications containing the appointment details. This eliminates manual reminder processes while ensuring clients never forget their bookings. Businesses using this system typically see 30-40% reductions in no-show rates.

How It Works

1. SuperSaaS Appointment Trigger

The workflow monitors your SuperSaaS account for new appointments. Whenever a booking is created or updated, it instantly captures all relevant details including time, service, and client information.

2. Notification Content Assembly

The system formats the appointment details into a clear, actionable push notification message. You can customize the template to include your branding, specific service details, and any preparation instructions.

3. iPhone Push Delivery

Using Apple's push notification service, the reminder is delivered instantly to your iPhone (or multiple team members' devices). The notification appears on the lock screen and in Notification Center until dismissed.

Pro tip: Set up multiple reminder notifications - one when the appointment is booked, another 24 hours before, and a final alert 1 hour prior. This multi-touch approach maximizes attendance rates.

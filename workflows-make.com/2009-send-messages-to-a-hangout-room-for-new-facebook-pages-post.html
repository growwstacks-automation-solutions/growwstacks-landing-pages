Facebook to Hangout Notifications

Get instant alerts in Hangouts whenever new posts are published on your Facebook pages

Get This Workflow → Make.com · Free Template

What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges your Facebook pages and Google Hangouts, creating real-time notifications for your team whenever new content gets published. Instead of manually checking pages or setting up unreliable browser alerts, this workflow automatically detects new posts and delivers formatted notifications directly to your designated Hangout room.

For community managers and marketing teams, this means instant awareness of published content without the constant page refreshing. The template includes basic post information but is designed for easy customization to include additional details like engagement metrics or direct links to boost interaction.

How It Works

1. Facebook Page Monitoring

The workflow continuously checks your connected Facebook pages for new posts using Make.com's reliable polling system.

2. New Post Detection

When Make detects a new post, it captures all available data including content, author, timestamp, and any media attachments.

3. Message Formatting

The system formats this information into a clean Hangouts message, highlighting key details your team needs to see immediately.

4. Hangout Delivery

Finally, the workflow sends the formatted message to your specified Hangout room, ensuring your entire team gets synchronized notifications.

Pro tip: Add filters to only notify about posts containing specific keywords or published during business hours to reduce notification fatigue.

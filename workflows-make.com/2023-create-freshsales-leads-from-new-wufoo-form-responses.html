What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manual lead entry from web forms into CRM systems. Every time a potential customer fills out your Wufoo form, this workflow instantly creates a complete lead record in Freshsales with all submitted information.

The solution eliminates time-consuming copy-paste work while ensuring no leads fall through the cracks. Sales teams get immediate notifications about new prospects with complete context from their form submission, allowing faster and more personalized follow-up.

How It Works

1. New Wufoo form submission trigger

The workflow monitors your Wufoo form for new responses. Each submission instantly triggers the automation process.

2. Data extraction and formatting

The system extracts all form fields and formats them according to Freshsales' lead structure. This includes mapping custom fields and cleaning data as needed.

3. Freshsales lead creation

The workflow creates a new lead in Freshsales with all relevant information from the form submission. You can configure which fields map to which CRM properties.

4. Duplicate checking

Optional duplicate prevention checks ensure the same person isn't entered multiple times if they submit the form again.

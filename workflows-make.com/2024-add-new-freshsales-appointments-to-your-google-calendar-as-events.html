What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of sales teams manually transferring appointment details from their CRM to their calendar. Every time a new appointment is created in Freshsales, the workflow automatically creates a corresponding event in Google Calendar with all relevant meeting details.

The integration eliminates double data entry, reduces scheduling errors, and ensures your calendar always reflects your current sales pipeline. Sales reps can focus on preparing for meetings rather than administrative tasks, while managers gain better visibility into team schedules.

How It Works

1. Freshsales triggers the workflow

The automation monitors your Freshsales account for new appointments. When a sales rep schedules a meeting with a prospect or client, the workflow immediately detects this new entry.

2. Data is extracted and formatted

The system pulls all relevant appointment details including meeting title, date/time, duration, description, and attendee information. It formats this data to match Google Calendar's event structure.

3. Event is created in Google Calendar

Using your connected Google account, the workflow creates a new calendar event with all the Freshsales appointment details. The event appears in your calendar with proper timing, reminders, and metadata.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to add a "Freshsales" label to all synced events so you can easily identify CRM-originated meetings in your calendar.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of CRM contacts being siloed in your sales platform while your team needs access to them across all communication channels. Every time your sales team adds a new contact in Freshsales, this workflow instantly adds that contact to Google Contacts.

The integration eliminates hours of manual data entry while reducing errors from copy-paste transfers. Your marketing, support, and executive teams will always have access to updated contact information without needing direct CRM access.

How It Works

1. Freshsales triggers the workflow

The automation monitors your Freshsales account for new contact creation events. As soon as a sales rep adds a lead or customer, the workflow activates.

2. Contact data is formatted for Google

The workflow extracts all relevant contact information including name, email, phone numbers, company details, and custom fields. It reformats this data to match Google Contacts' structure.

3. Contact is added to Google Contacts

Using your Google account credentials, the workflow creates a new contact entry with all the mapped fields. You can configure which contact groups or labels to apply automatically.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to only sync contacts that meet specific criteria (like lead status or deal size) to prevent cluttering your Google Contacts with irrelevant entries.

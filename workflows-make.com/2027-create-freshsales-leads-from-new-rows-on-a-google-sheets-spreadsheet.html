What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point for sales teams: manually transferring lead information from spreadsheets into your CRM. Every time you or your team adds a new row to your designated Google Sheet, this Make.com workflow instantly creates a corresponding lead in Freshsales with all the contact details properly mapped.

The template handles all the field mapping between your spreadsheet columns and Freshsales lead fields. You can customize which columns correspond to which CRM fields during setup. This eliminates repetitive data entry while ensuring your sales team always has the most current lead information in your central CRM system.

How It Works

1. New row detection

The workflow continuously monitors your Google Sheet for new rows being added. You can configure how frequently it checks for updates (typically every 5-15 minutes).

2. Data extraction

When a new row appears, Make.com extracts all the cell values and prepares them for transfer to Freshsales. The template includes default field mappings for common lead attributes.

3. Lead creation

The system creates a new lead in Freshsales using the extracted data. All contact details, company information, and custom fields are properly mapped to their corresponding CRM fields.

4. Error handling

If any required fields are missing or data formatting issues occur, the workflow can notify your team via email while skipping that particular record to prevent CRM pollution.

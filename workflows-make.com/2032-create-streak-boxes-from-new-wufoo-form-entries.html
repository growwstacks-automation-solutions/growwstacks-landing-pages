What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your Wufoo forms and Streak CRM, eliminating manual data entry and ensuring no lead falls through the cracks. Every time someone submits your Wufoo form, the workflow instantly creates a corresponding box in your Streak pipeline with all the submitted information.

Sales teams waste countless hours copying form data into their CRM. This workflow solves that problem by automatically transferring lead information the moment it's submitted, allowing your team to focus on selling rather than data entry. The integration maintains data accuracy while reducing response times to new inquiries.

How It Works

1. Form submission triggers the workflow

When a visitor completes your Wufoo form, the submission activates the Make.com scenario. The workflow immediately retrieves all form field data including contact information, submission details, and any custom fields you've added.

2. Data mapping and transformation

The workflow maps each Wufoo form field to corresponding Streak box properties. You can customize this mapping to ensure all relevant information transfers correctly. The system can also reformat data (like splitting names or standardizing phone numbers) before sending to Streak.

3. Box creation in Streak

Using the Streak API, the workflow creates a new box in your specified pipeline. All mapped fields populate automatically, and you can configure additional properties like stage, priority, or tags based on form responses.

4. Error handling and logging

The workflow includes built-in error detection that retries failed operations and logs any issues. You can configure notifications for critical failures while maintaining a complete audit trail of all processed submissions.

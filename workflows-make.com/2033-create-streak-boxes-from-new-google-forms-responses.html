What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of leads falling through the cracks between form submission and CRM entry. When potential customers complete your Google Form, their information often sits idle until someone manually transfers it to your CRM. This delay can mean missed opportunities and slower response times.

The workflow automatically creates a new Streak box for every Google Forms response, instantly converting submissions into trackable sales opportunities. It eliminates manual data entry while ensuring complete, accurate records in your CRM system. Fields from your form map directly to corresponding properties in Streak, maintaining data consistency across platforms.

How It Works

1. New form response trigger

The workflow monitors your Google Form for new submissions. Each time someone completes the form, Make.com detects the response immediately.

2. Data parsing and preparation

The system extracts all response data and formats it for Streak CRM. You can configure which form fields map to which Streak properties, including custom fields.

3. Streak box creation

Make.com creates a new box in your specified Streak pipeline, populating all mapped fields with the form response data. The box appears in your CRM within seconds of form submission.

Pro tip: Add a hidden field in your Google Form to track the original submission URL. Map this to a custom Streak field for quick reference during follow-ups.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap in lead management by instantly creating Streak boxes (deal records) for every new Jotform submission. Manual data entry between forms and your CRM creates delays and errors - this workflow eliminates that friction point entirely.

When potential customers complete your Jotform (whether it's a contact request, application, or quote form), Make.com detects the new entry and automatically creates a corresponding box in your Streak pipeline with all the submitted information. This gives your sales team immediate visibility into new opportunities with complete context.

How It Works

1. New form submission trigger

The workflow monitors your specified Jotform for new entries using Make.com's instant webhook trigger. No polling delays - submissions are processed in real-time.

2. Data parsing and validation

Make.com extracts all form field values and performs basic validation to ensure required fields are present before proceeding to Streak.

3. Streak box creation

The system creates a new box in your designated Streak pipeline, mapping form fields to appropriate box properties. You can customize which fields transfer and how they're labeled in Streak.

4. Error handling and logging

If any step fails, the workflow automatically retries or notifies your team while maintaining a complete audit log of all processing attempts.

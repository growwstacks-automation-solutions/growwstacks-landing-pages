What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your lead capture forms and sales pipeline management. Every time someone submits your Cognito Forms form, Make instantly creates a corresponding box in Streak CRM with all the submitted information. This eliminates manual data entry while ensuring no lead falls through the cracks.

The workflow transforms form submissions into actionable sales opportunities. Your team gets complete lead details automatically organized in Streak's pipeline view, with timestamps showing exactly when each lead came in. This creates a seamless transition from marketing to sales without spreadsheet exports or copy-paste work.

How It Works

1. New form submission triggers the workflow

Make monitors your Cognito Forms account for new entries. The moment someone submits your form, the automation begins processing the data.

2. Data is formatted for Streak

The workflow maps form fields to Streak box properties. Contact details, lead source information, and any custom form responses are structured to fit your Streak pipeline requirements.

3. New box created in Streak

Make authenticates with your Streak account and creates a new box in your specified pipeline. All mapped data populates the box fields automatically.

4. Optional notifications sent

You can configure the workflow to notify your sales team via email or Slack when high-priority leads come in, or trigger follow-up sequences.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to route different form types to separate Streak pipelines. For example, demo requests vs. support inquiries.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of disconnected form submissions and email marketing systems. When businesses collect leads through Cognito Forms but use SendGrid for email campaigns, manually transferring contact information becomes time-consuming and prone to errors.

The workflow automatically creates or updates SendGrid recipients whenever someone submits your Cognito Form. It intelligently checks whether the contact already exists in SendGrid, then either adds them as a new recipient or updates their existing record with the latest information from the form.

How It Works

1. New form submission triggers the workflow

Whenever someone completes your Cognito Form, the workflow immediately detects the new entry through Make.com's real-time monitoring.

2. Data is extracted and formatted

The system pulls all relevant contact information from the form submission, including email address, name, and any custom fields you've configured to sync.

3. SendGrid recipient list is checked

The workflow searches your SendGrid contact list to determine if this email address already exists as a recipient.

4. Contact is created or updated

If the contact doesn't exist, they're added as a new recipient. If they do exist, their record is updated with the latest information from the form submission.

5. Success confirmation

The system logs each successful synchronization and can optionally notify your team through email or Slack.

Pro tip: Add custom fields from your form to SendGrid to enable advanced segmentation in your email campaigns based on form responses.

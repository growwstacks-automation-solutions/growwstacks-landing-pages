Automatically send professional email responses when users submit your Cognito Forms

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of manually responding to form submissions. When a user completes your Cognito Form, the workflow automatically triggers a SendGrid email notification. This ensures immediate acknowledgment while eliminating the need for constant monitoring of your form inbox.

The solution is particularly valuable for businesses receiving high volumes of form submissions or those needing timely responses. It maintains professional communication standards while freeing up staff time previously spent on repetitive notification tasks.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

The workflow starts when a new entry is detected in your Cognito Form. Make.com monitors your form submissions in real-time.

2. Data Extraction

The system extracts all relevant form fields including the respondent's email address, name, and any custom form data you need to reference.

3. Email Composition

Using SendGrid's powerful email templating, the workflow constructs a professional response email. You can include dynamic content from the form submission.

4. Delivery & Tracking

SendGrid delivers the email with enterprise-grade reliability. The system logs the delivery status for future reference and analytics.

Who This Is For

This automation benefits any organization using Cognito Forms that needs to:

  • Send immediate confirmation emails for registrations or applications
  • Notify internal teams about new leads or inquiries
  • Provide automated customer support responses
  • Distribute digital assets after form completion

Pro tip: Use conditional logic in Make.com to send different email templates based on specific form responses, creating a more personalized experience.

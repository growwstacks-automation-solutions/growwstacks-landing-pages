What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of transferring contact information from Google Forms submissions to your SendGrid email marketing platform. Every time someone fills out your form - whether it's for event registration, lead capture, or customer feedback - their information is instantly added to your SendGrid recipient list or updated if they already exist.

The workflow eliminates spreadsheet exports, CSV imports, and the inevitable human errors that come with manual data entry. It ensures your email campaigns reach everyone who has expressed interest through your forms, with their most current contact information.

How It Works

1. Trigger on new form submissions

The workflow activates whenever a new response is submitted to your connected Google Form. Make.com monitors the form continuously for new entries.

2. Extract contact details

The system pulls all relevant information from the form response, including email address, name, and any other fields you've configured to capture.

3. Check for existing contacts

Before adding a new recipient, the workflow queries your SendGrid account to see if this email already exists in your contact database.

4. Add or update records

If the contact is new, they're added to your SendGrid list with all collected information. If they exist already, their record is updated with any new data from the form.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of manually following up with everyone who submits a Google Form. Instead of copying email addresses and crafting individual responses, the workflow automatically sends professional, branded emails through SendGrid whenever a new form submission arrives.

The system captures all form responses, including custom fields, and can personalize each email with the respondent's specific answers. This ensures timely communication while maintaining consistency in your messaging and reducing administrative overhead.

How It Works

1. New Form Submission Trigger

The workflow starts when someone submits your Google Form. Make.com monitors the form continuously for new entries.

2. Data Extraction

The system extracts all relevant form fields including the respondent's email address, name, and any custom questions you've included in your form.

3. SendGrid Email Assembly

Using a pre-designed SendGrid template, the workflow dynamically inserts the form responses into appropriate email fields, creating a personalized message for each recipient.

4. Email Delivery

SendGrid delivers the completed email with your branding, tracking when it's opened and whether any links are clicked.

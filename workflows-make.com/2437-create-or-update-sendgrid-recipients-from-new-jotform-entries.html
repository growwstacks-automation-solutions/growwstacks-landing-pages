What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of transferring contact information from JotForm submissions to your SendGrid email marketing platform. Every time someone fills out your form, their information is automatically added or updated in your SendGrid recipient lists.

The workflow eliminates spreadsheet exports, CSV imports, and the risk of human error in your email marketing data management. It ensures your campaigns reach the right people with their most current information, improving deliverability and engagement rates.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New JotForm Submission

The workflow starts whenever someone submits your JotForm. Make.com instantly receives the form data including all fields you've configured.

2. Data Processing

The system maps form fields to corresponding SendGrid recipient fields. You can customize which information transfers and how it's structured.

3. Recipient Management

Make.com checks if the email already exists in your SendGrid lists. Based on your configuration, it either updates the existing record or creates a new recipient.

4. List Assignment

The contact is automatically added to your specified SendGrid lists or segments, ready for your next email campaign.

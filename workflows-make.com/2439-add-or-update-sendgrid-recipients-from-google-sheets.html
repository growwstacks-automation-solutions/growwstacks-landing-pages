What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your customer data in Google Sheets and your email marketing in SendGrid. Every time you add a new contact to your spreadsheet or update existing information, the workflow instantly syncs those changes to your SendGrid recipient lists.

Marketing teams waste countless hours manually exporting CSV files and uploading them to email platforms. This template eliminates that friction while ensuring your campaigns always target the most current contact information. Whether you're managing newsletter signups, customer segments, or event attendees, the automation keeps your lists perfectly synchronized.

How It Works

1. Google Sheets Trigger

The workflow monitors your specified Google Sheet for new or modified rows. You can configure it to watch specific columns or the entire sheet.

2. Data Processing

Make.com extracts the relevant contact information from the spreadsheet row, including email, name, and any custom fields you've defined.

3. SendGrid Recipient Management

The system checks if the email already exists in your SendGrid contacts. If found, it updates the record; if new, it creates a recipient with all provided details.

4. List Assignment

Contacts are automatically added to your designated SendGrid mailing lists based on your configuration, with support for multiple list assignments.

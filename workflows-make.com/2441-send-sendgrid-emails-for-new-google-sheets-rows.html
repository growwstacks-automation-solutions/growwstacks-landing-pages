What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious process of manually sending emails based on spreadsheet data. Whenever your team adds new customer information, order details, or event registrations to Google Sheets, the workflow automatically sends customized emails through SendGrid.

Businesses use this to automate customer communications like welcome emails, order confirmations, appointment reminders, and marketing campaigns. The system eliminates human error from manual copying/pasting while ensuring timely, consistent messaging.

How It Works

1. New row detection

Make.com continuously monitors your Google Sheet for new rows added to a specified worksheet. The workflow triggers whenever it detects fresh data.

2. Data extraction

The system pulls all relevant information from the new row, including email addresses, names, and any other personalization fields you need for your messages.

3. Email composition

Using SendGrid's template system, the workflow dynamically inserts spreadsheet data into pre-designed email layouts. You can customize subject lines, body content, and attachments.

4. Recipient management

The automation handles recipient lists intelligently, sending to individual addresses or groups based on your spreadsheet configuration and SendGrid contact lists.

What You'll Need

  1. A Google Sheets spreadsheet with your data
  2. A SendGrid account with API access

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Connect your Google Sheets account in Make.com
  2. Authorize access to your SendGrid account
  3. Select your spreadsheet and worksheet
  4. Map spreadsheet columns to email fields
  5. Choose your SendGrid template
  6. Test with sample data
  7. Activate the automation

Pro tip: Create a "status" column in your spreadsheet to track which rows have been processed. This prevents duplicate emails if the workflow runs multiple times.

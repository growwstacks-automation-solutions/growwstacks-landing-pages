What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your Salesforce CRM and SendGrid email marketing platform. Whenever a new contact is created in Salesforce, the workflow automatically adds them to your designated SendGrid contact list with all relevant fields mapped.

Marketing teams waste countless hours manually exporting and importing contacts between systems. This template eliminates that friction, ensuring your email campaigns always target your complete, up-to-date audience while maintaining data accuracy and compliance.

How It Works

1. New Contact Trigger

The workflow monitors your Salesforce instance for new contact creations using Make.com's real-time trigger.

2. Data Validation

Each contact is checked for required fields (especially email) and marketing consent flags to ensure compliance.

3. Field Mapping

Salesforce contact properties are mapped to corresponding SendGrid contact fields according to your configuration.

4. List Assignment

The contact is added to your specified SendGrid list, with options for conditional routing based on Salesforce data.

5. Duplicate Prevention

The system checks for existing contacts in SendGrid before adding, preventing duplicate entries.

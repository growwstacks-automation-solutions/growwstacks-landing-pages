What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap between sales and marketing teams by instantly syncing new Pipedrive contacts to your SendGrid email lists. Every time your sales team adds a new lead in Pipedrive, the workflow automatically adds them to the appropriate SendGrid list with all relevant contact information.

Manual data transfer between CRMs and email platforms is time-consuming and prone to errors. This workflow eliminates those problems by creating a seamless, real-time connection between your sales pipeline and email marketing system. Marketing teams gain immediate access to new leads for nurturing campaigns, while sales teams can focus on selling rather than data entry.

How It Works

1. New Contact Detection

The workflow monitors your Pipedrive account for newly added people. It triggers instantly when any new contact is created in your specified Pipedrive pipeline.

2. Data Extraction

All relevant contact information is pulled from Pipedrive, including name, email, company, phone number, and any custom fields you've configured.

3. SendGrid Integration

The workflow connects to your SendGrid account and prepares the contact data for import according to your specified field mappings.

4. List Assignment

Based on your configuration, new contacts are added to specific SendGrid lists or segments. You can set rules based on Pipedrive deal stages, tags, or custom fields.

Pro tip: Use Pipedrive custom fields to categorize leads by interest or industry, then map these to SendGrid segments for targeted email campaigns.

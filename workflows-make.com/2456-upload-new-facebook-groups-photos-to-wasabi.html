What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of losing valuable photos shared in Facebook Groups. Many community managers, educators, and event organizers rely on Facebook Groups as a photo-sharing hub, but Facebook doesn't provide reliable long-term storage or easy backup options.

The workflow automatically detects new photos added to your Facebook Group albums and uploads them directly to your Wasabi cloud storage. This creates a secure secondary copy of all your group's visual content while maintaining the original folder structure.

How It Works

1. Facebook Group Photo Monitoring

The workflow continuously checks your specified Facebook Group for new photo uploads using Facebook's API. It can monitor specific albums or the entire group's photo feed.

2. Photo Metadata Extraction

For each new photo detected, the system captures important metadata including upload date, uploader information, and any associated captions or comments.

3. Wasabi Cloud Upload

The original photo file is downloaded from Facebook and uploaded to your designated Wasabi bucket. The workflow maintains folder organization matching your Facebook Group structure.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of preserving valuable video content from Facebook Groups. Many community managers and moderators struggle with content disappearing when posts are removed or accounts face restrictions. The workflow automatically detects new videos posted in your Facebook Groups and securely transfers them to your Wasabi cloud storage.

Instead of manually downloading and re-uploading videos—a time-consuming process that's easy to forget—this system creates a reliable backup of all your group's video content. It maintains an organized archive that remains accessible regardless of what happens to the original Facebook posts.

How It Works

1. Monitoring Facebook Groups

The workflow continuously checks your specified Facebook Groups for new video posts using Make.com's Facebook connector. It can monitor multiple groups simultaneously.

2. Detecting New Videos

When a member uploads a video, the system immediately identifies the new content and extracts the video file along with relevant metadata like post date and author information.

3. Transferring to Wasabi

The video file is securely transmitted to your designated Wasabi bucket. You can configure specific folder structures (e.g., by group name or date) for better organization.

4. Confirmation & Logging

The system records each successful transfer and can optionally notify you via email or Slack. Failed transfers trigger automatic retries with error reporting.

What You'll Need

  1. A Facebook account with admin/mod rights to the target groups
  2. A Wasabi cloud storage account (free trial available)

  4. The Facebook Group IDs you want to monitor

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Clone the template from Make.com to your account
  2. Connect your Facebook account in the Facebook module
  3. Enter the Group IDs you want to monitor
  4. Configure your Wasabi connection with bucket details
  5. Set your preferred folder structure naming convention
  6. Test with a recent video post to verify the transfer
  7. Activate the scenario to begin automatic monitoring

Pro tip: Create separate buckets or folders for different groups to maintain organization. Consider adding date-based subfolders (YYYY/MM) for easier historical searching.

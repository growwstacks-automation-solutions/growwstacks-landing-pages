What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical need for redundant backup of precious memories and important visual assets stored in Google Photos. Many users don't realize that Google Photos doesn't provide true backup protection - your photos could be lost due to account issues or accidental deletion.

The workflow automatically detects when new photos or videos are added to your specified Google Photos album and immediately copies them to your Wasabi cloud storage account. This creates a secure secondary copy with enterprise-grade durability at a fraction of the cost of other cloud providers.

How It Works

1. New photo detection

The workflow continuously monitors your Google Photos account for new uploads using Make.com's polling system. When new media is detected, it triggers the backup process.

2. File retrieval

Make.com retrieves the full-resolution version of each new photo or video from Google Photos, ensuring you're backing up the original quality files.

3. Secure transfer

The files are securely transferred to your Wasabi storage bucket using encrypted connections. You can configure the destination path structure to match your organizational preferences.

4. Verification

The workflow confirms successful transfer and can optionally notify you via email or other channels if any issues occur during the backup process.

Pro tip: Set up a dedicated Wasabi bucket just for photo backups to simplify cost tracking and access management.

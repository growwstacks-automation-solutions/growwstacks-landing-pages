What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point for businesses transitioning between email marketing platforms or maintaining subscriber lists across multiple services. Instead of manually exporting CSV files from MailerLite and importing them into Campaign Monitor - a process that's time-consuming and prone to errors - this workflow handles the entire transfer automatically.

The template connects to both platforms' APIs to securely pull subscriber data from your specified MailerLite group and push it to your chosen Campaign Monitor list. It preserves essential subscriber information including email addresses, names, and custom fields while handling the technical complexities of API integration behind the scenes.

How It Works

1. Retrieving MailerLite subscribers

The workflow starts by connecting to your MailerLite account via API and fetching all subscribers from your specified group. It handles pagination automatically to ensure all subscribers are retrieved, even from large lists.

2. Processing subscriber data

Each subscriber record is processed to extract relevant information and format it according to Campaign Monitor's API requirements. The system can optionally filter subscribers based on custom criteria you define.

3. Adding to Campaign Monitor

The processed subscriber data is then sent to Campaign Monitor's API, where it's added to your designated list. The workflow includes error handling to manage cases where subscribers already exist in the destination list.

Pro tip: Schedule this workflow to run automatically after your regular MailerLite email broadcasts to keep your Campaign Monitor list continuously updated with new subscribers.

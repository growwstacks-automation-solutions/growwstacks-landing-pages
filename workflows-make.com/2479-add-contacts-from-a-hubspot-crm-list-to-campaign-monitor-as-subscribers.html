What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your HubSpot CRM and Campaign Monitor email marketing platform. Whenever a new contact is added to a specified list in HubSpot, the workflow automatically adds them as a subscriber in Campaign Monitor with all relevant contact information.

Marketing teams waste countless hours manually exporting contacts from CRMs and importing them into email platforms. This solution eliminates that friction point, ensuring your email campaigns always have access to your most current CRM data while maintaining proper list segmentation.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New HubSpot Contact

The workflow monitors your specified HubSpot contact list for new additions. You can choose to watch all contacts or filter by specific properties.

2. Data Mapping

Contact fields from HubSpot (name, email, company, custom properties) are mapped to corresponding subscriber fields in Campaign Monitor.

3. List Assignment

The contact is added to your designated Campaign Monitor subscriber list, with options to apply tags or custom fields during the transfer.

4. Error Handling

The workflow includes built-in checks to handle duplicates, invalid emails, and API rate limits, with notifications for any failed transfers.

Pro tip: Create a dedicated "Email Subscribers" list in HubSpot to maintain clear segmentation between contacts who should and shouldn't receive marketing emails.

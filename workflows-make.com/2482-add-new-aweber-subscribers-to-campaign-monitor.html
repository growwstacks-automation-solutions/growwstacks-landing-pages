What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point for marketers using both AWeber and Campaign Monitor. When you collect new email subscribers through AWeber forms, landing pages, or other sources, they're automatically added to your specified Campaign Monitor lists without any manual work.

The workflow eliminates the need to export/import CSV files or maintain duplicate data entry processes. It ensures your Campaign Monitor lists always reflect your latest AWeber subscribers, helping you maintain list hygiene and campaign effectiveness across both platforms.

How It Works

1. New subscriber trigger

The workflow monitors your AWeber account for new subscribers being added to your lists.

2. Data mapping

Subscriber details (email, name, custom fields) are extracted and formatted for Campaign Monitor's API.

3. List assignment

The subscriber is added to your specified Campaign Monitor list with proper field mapping.

4. Error handling

The system automatically retries failed attempts and notifies you of any persistent issues.

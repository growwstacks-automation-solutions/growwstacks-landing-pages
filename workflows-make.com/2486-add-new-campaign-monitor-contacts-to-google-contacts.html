What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of disconnected marketing and CRM systems. When new subscribers join your Campaign Monitor email list, their contact information often needs to be manually added to Google Contacts for sales teams or other business purposes. This workflow eliminates that manual process by automatically creating Google Contacts entries whenever someone subscribes.

The integration maintains data consistency across platforms while saving significant administrative time. Marketing teams can focus on campaigns rather than data entry, while sales teams immediately gain access to new leads in their familiar Google Contacts interface.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New Campaign Monitor subscriber

The workflow activates whenever a new contact is added to your selected Campaign Monitor list. Make.com monitors your account in real-time for these events.

2. Data extraction

The system collects all available subscriber information including name, email address, custom fields, and subscription metadata. This data is formatted for Google Contacts compatibility.

3. Contact creation

Make.com authenticates with your Google account and creates a new contact entry with the subscriber's information. The template includes mapping logic to ensure proper field matching between platforms.

Pro tip: Configure custom field mappings to include valuable segmentation data from Campaign Monitor as notes or labels in Google Contacts.

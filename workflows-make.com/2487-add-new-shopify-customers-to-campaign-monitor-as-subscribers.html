What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manually transferring new customer data from Shopify to email marketing platforms like Campaign Monitor. Every time a customer creates an account or makes a purchase in your Shopify store, their information is automatically added to your Campaign Monitor subscriber list.

The workflow eliminates the need for CSV exports, spreadsheet manipulation, and manual imports - processes that often lead to data errors and missed opportunities. By ensuring every new customer immediately joins your email list, you can start building relationships through welcome sequences and targeted campaigns right away.

How It Works

1. Shopify triggers the workflow

The automation monitors your Shopify store for new customer creation events. Each time a customer registers or completes a purchase, Shopify sends their details to Make.com.

2. Data processing in Make

Make receives the customer data (name, email, purchase details) and formats it according to Campaign Monitor's API requirements. You can customize which Shopify fields map to which Campaign Monitor fields.

3. Subscriber addition to Campaign Monitor

The processed customer data is sent to your specified Campaign Monitor list. The workflow can handle duplicate prevention, custom field mapping, and segmentation based on your preferences.

Pro tip: Add a short delay (5-10 minutes) before adding subscribers to Campaign Monitor. This gives customers time to complete any post-purchase actions that might provide additional segmentation data.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for ecommerce businesses: capturing every new customer in your email marketing system without manual data entry. When customers register on your WooCommerce store, they're automatically added as subscribers to your designated Campaign Monitor list with all their contact information.

The workflow eliminates the risk of missing potential subscribers due to human error or delayed imports. It ensures your email list grows in real-time, enabling you to send timely welcome sequences, promotional offers, and lifecycle campaigns that convert browsers into repeat buyers.

How It Works

1. New customer trigger

Make.com monitors your WooCommerce store via API for new customer registrations. This includes both checkout creations and manual admin additions.

2. Data mapping

The workflow extracts relevant customer details (name, email, optional custom fields) and formats them for Campaign Monitor's subscriber requirements.

3. List addition

Make.com's Campaign Monitor module adds the customer to your specified list with proper field mapping, respecting your double opt-in settings if enabled.

4. Error handling

The workflow includes basic error handling to retry failed additions or notify you of issues like duplicate emails or invalid formats.

Pro tip: Enhance this workflow by adding conditional logic to segment subscribers based on their first purchase category or order value.

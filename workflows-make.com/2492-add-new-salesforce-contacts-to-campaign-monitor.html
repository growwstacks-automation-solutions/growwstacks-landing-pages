What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of manually transferring new Salesforce contacts to email marketing platforms like Campaign Monitor. Marketing teams often waste hours each week copying and pasting contact details between systems, risking data errors and delayed campaign delivery.

The workflow automatically detects new contact records in Salesforce and adds them as subscribers to your specified Campaign Monitor list. It maintains data integrity by mapping Salesforce fields to corresponding Campaign Monitor fields, ensuring all relevant contact information transfers accurately.

How It Works

1. New Contact Detection

The workflow monitors Salesforce for newly created contact records using real-time triggers or scheduled checks.

2. Data Validation

Each contact is validated to ensure required fields (like email address) are present and properly formatted before processing.

3. Field Mapping

Configured field mappings transfer Salesforce data to the correct Campaign Monitor subscriber fields, including custom fields.

4. List Subscription

The contact is added to your specified Campaign Monitor list with all mapped data, ready for email campaigns.

Pro tip: Add a custom field in Salesforce to track which contacts have been synced, creating an audit trail for troubleshooting.

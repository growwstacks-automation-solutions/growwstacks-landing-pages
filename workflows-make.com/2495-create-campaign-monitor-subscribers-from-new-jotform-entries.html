What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of lost leads and manual data entry between form submissions and email marketing systems. Every time someone fills out your Jotform, their information is automatically added as a subscriber to your Campaign Monitor email list without any manual intervention.

The workflow eliminates the risk of human error in transcribing form data and ensures immediate follow-up opportunities through your email marketing campaigns. It's particularly valuable for businesses running lead generation forms, event registrations, or customer feedback surveys who want to grow their email lists efficiently.

How It Works

1. New Jotform Submission Trigger

The workflow monitors your Jotform account for new submissions. Each time someone completes your form, it instantly triggers the automation process.

2. Data Extraction and Mapping

Make extracts the relevant form fields (name, email, custom fields) and maps them to corresponding Campaign Monitor subscriber fields according to your configuration.

3. Campaign Monitor Integration

The system connects to your Campaign Monitor account via API and creates a new subscriber with all the mapped information. You can specify which list they should be added to.

4. Optional Custom Fields

The workflow can also populate custom fields in Campaign Monitor with additional form data, enabling more sophisticated segmentation and personalization in your email campaigns.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Copy the template to your Make.com account
  2. Connect your Jotform account in the trigger module
  3. Connect your Campaign Monitor account in the action module
  4. Map your form fields to Campaign Monitor subscriber fields
  5. Specify which Campaign Monitor list to add subscribers to
  6. Test with a form submission and verify the subscriber appears
  7. Turn on the scenario to go live

Pro tip: Add a custom field in Jotform asking how they heard about you, then use this to tag subscribers in Campaign Monitor for better segmentation.

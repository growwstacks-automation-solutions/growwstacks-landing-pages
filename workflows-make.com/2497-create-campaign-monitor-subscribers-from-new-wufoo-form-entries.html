What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point for marketers and business owners who collect leads through Wufoo forms but manually transfer them to their Campaign Monitor email lists. The manual process is time-consuming, prone to errors, and delays subscriber onboarding.

The workflow automatically detects new Wufoo form submissions and instantly adds them as subscribers to your specified Campaign Monitor list. It preserves all collected form data while maintaining proper consent management for GDPR compliance. Businesses using this automation typically save 5-10 hours per month on manual data entry while seeing faster email list growth.

How It Works

1. Trigger on new Wufoo form entries

The workflow monitors your Wufoo form for new submissions, triggering immediately when someone completes the form.

2. Extract and format submission data

All form fields are extracted and mapped to corresponding Campaign Monitor fields - email address, name, custom fields, and subscription preferences.

3. Validate and filter submissions

Optional validation steps can check for required fields, email format validity, or specific opt-in consent before proceeding.

4. Add subscriber to Campaign Monitor

The contact is added to your specified Campaign Monitor list with all mapped data, ready for your email campaigns.

5. Handle duplicates and errors

The workflow can skip existing subscribers, update their records, or flag errors for review based on your preferences.

Pro tip: Add a conditional step to only include subscribers who explicitly opted in for marketing emails, ensuring GDPR compliance and higher engagement rates.

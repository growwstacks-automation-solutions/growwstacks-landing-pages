What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of transferring new leads from Google Forms submissions to your Campaign Monitor email list. Every time someone completes your form - whether it's an event registration, contact request, or newsletter signup - their information automatically becomes a subscriber in your chosen Campaign Monitor list.

The workflow eliminates spreadsheet exports, copy-pasting email addresses, and the inevitable human errors that come with manual data entry. It ensures your email marketing campaigns reach everyone who's expressed interest through your forms while maintaining accurate subscriber records.

How It Works

1. New form submission triggers the workflow

The automation monitors your specified Google Form for new responses. Each submission instantly initiates the workflow without any manual intervention.

2. Data extraction and formatting

The system extracts all relevant information from the form response, including email addresses, names, and any custom fields you've configured. It formats this data to match Campaign Monitor's subscriber requirements.

3. Subscriber creation in Campaign Monitor

The workflow connects to your Campaign Monitor account via API and creates a new subscriber with all the collected information. You can specify which list they should be added to and configure any tags or custom fields.

Pro tip: Use hidden fields in your Google Form to capture additional metadata like the submission timestamp or source URL, which can then be passed to Campaign Monitor as custom fields.

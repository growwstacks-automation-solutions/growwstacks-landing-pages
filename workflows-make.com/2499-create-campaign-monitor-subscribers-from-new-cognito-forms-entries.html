What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of leads getting stuck between your contact forms and email marketing system. When potential customers fill out your Cognito Form, their information often sits dormant until someone manually transfers it to Campaign Monitor. This delay creates missed opportunities and increases the risk of human error in data entry.

The workflow automatically captures every form submission and instantly adds the contact as a subscriber to your designated Campaign Monitor list. It handles field mapping between systems, ensuring names, email addresses, and custom form data flow accurately into your email marketing platform. This creates a seamless lead-to-nurture pipeline that operates 24/7 without manual intervention.

How It Works

1. New form submission trigger

The workflow monitors your Cognito Forms account for new entries. Each time someone completes your form, it instantly triggers the automation process.

2. Data validation and processing

The system checks for required fields (like email address), validates formatting, and applies any spam filters you've configured. This ensures only qualified leads proceed to the next step.

3. Campaign Monitor subscriber creation

Validated form data is mapped to Campaign Monitor's subscriber fields according to your configuration. The workflow then creates a new subscriber record in your specified list, including all mapped custom fields.

4. Success confirmation

The system verifies the subscriber was successfully added and can optionally notify your team or trigger follow-up actions in other connected apps.

What You'll Need

  1. Active Cognito Forms account with at least one published form
  2. Campaign Monitor account with API access enabled
  3. Make.com account (free plan sufficient for basic implementation)
  4. List of form fields you want to map to subscriber fields

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Clone the template from Make.com to your account
  2. Connect your Cognito Forms account in the first module
  3. Connect your Campaign Monitor account in the second module
  4. Map form fields to corresponding Campaign Monitor subscriber fields
  5. Specify which Campaign Monitor list should receive new subscribers
  6. Test with a form submission and verify the subscriber appears correctly
  7. Activate the workflow to begin automatic processing

Pro tip: Create a dedicated "Form Submissions" list in Campaign Monitor to keep these automated additions separate from other subscriber sources for better tracking.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solution delivers current weather conditions directly to your mobile devices, tablets, or browsers through Pushbullet notifications. Whether you're managing outdoor operations, planning events, or simply need weather awareness for personal activities, this workflow eliminates the need to manually check forecasts.

The system pulls real-time weather data from reliable sources and pushes concise, actionable alerts to all your connected devices. You'll receive temperature, precipitation chances, wind conditions, and severe weather warnings exactly when you need them - without opening a weather app or refreshing a webpage.

How It Works

1. Location Configuration

Set your desired location once during setup. The system will continuously monitor weather conditions for this specific area.

2. Data Retrieval

Make.com connects to weather APIs to fetch the latest forecast data at regular intervals you specify.

3. Notification Processing

The workflow formats the weather data into clear, concise notifications with only the most relevant details for quick reading.

4. Push Delivery

Pushbullet instantly delivers the weather alert to all your registered devices simultaneously, ensuring you never miss important updates.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of copying form submissions from Cognito Forms into your Coda documents. Every time someone completes your form, this workflow instantly captures their responses and adds them as a new row in your specified Coda table.

The solution eliminates spreadsheet exports, copy-paste errors, and delays in data availability. Your team gets immediate access to form responses in a structured database where you can filter, sort, and analyze the data without leaving Coda.

How It Works

1. New form submission trigger

The workflow monitors your Cognito Forms account for new submissions. When someone completes your form, Make.com detects the new entry instantly.

2. Data extraction and formatting

The workflow extracts all form field responses, including text inputs, dropdown selections, file uploads, and more. It prepares this data for insertion into your Coda doc.

3. Coda document update

Make.com connects to your specified Coda doc and adds a new row to your chosen table. Each form field maps to its corresponding column in Coda.

Pro tip: Use this workflow with Coda's views and filters to create dynamic dashboards that automatically update with each new form submission.

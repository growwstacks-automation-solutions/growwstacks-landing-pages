What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious problem of manually copying data from Jotform submissions into your Coda documents. Every time someone completes your form, the workflow instantly creates a new row in your specified Coda table with all the response data perfectly formatted.

Businesses waste countless hours transferring form responses between systems. This workflow eliminates that manual work while ensuring data accuracy. Whether you're collecting customer feedback, event registrations, or internal requests, your information automatically flows into Coda where you can analyze, sort, and collaborate on it.

How It Works

1. Form submission trigger

The workflow monitors your specified Jotform for new submissions. When someone completes the form, Make.com detects the new entry immediately.

2. Data extraction

The workflow pulls all response data including text answers, multiple choice selections, file upload links, and metadata like submission timestamps.

3. Coda document update

Make.com connects to your Coda account and adds a new row to your designated table. Each form field maps to the appropriate column in your Coda doc.

Pro tip: Add a "Status" column in Coda to track which submissions need follow-up. The workflow can automatically set this to "New" for each incoming entry.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Clone the template in your Make.com account
  2. Connect your Jotform account and select your form
  3. Connect your Coda account and select your target doc/table
  4. Map form fields to Coda columns in the workflow settings
  5. Test with a form submission and verify data appears in Coda
  6. Activate the workflow for automatic processing

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of copying product information from Shopify to Coda documents. Every time your team adds a new product in Shopify, this Make.com workflow instantly captures all the product details and creates a corresponding row in your specified Coda table.

The system handles all standard product fields including titles, descriptions, variants, pricing, images, and inventory levels. It transforms your Coda doc into a living product catalog that stays perfectly synchronized with your Shopify store, eliminating version control issues and ensuring all departments work from the same up-to-date information.

How It Works

1. Shopify Triggers the Workflow

Make.com monitors your Shopify store for new product creation events. The instant a product is added, the workflow activates with all the product data.

2. Data Transformation

The workflow maps Shopify's product data structure to your Coda table columns, handling complex fields like variants and images. You can customize which fields transfer and how they're formatted.

3. Coda Row Creation

Make.com authenticates with your Coda doc and adds a new row to your specified table with all the product information. The workflow can update existing rows if products change in Shopify.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to only sync products with specific tags or inventory status, keeping your Coda doc focused on relevant products.

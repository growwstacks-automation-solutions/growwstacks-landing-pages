What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of business SMS messages getting lost in personal phone logs or requiring constant phone checking. When customers text your RingCentral business number, the message instantly appears in your designated Slack channel where your entire team can see and respond.

The workflow eliminates manual forwarding of texts while ensuring no customer inquiry falls through the cracks. It's particularly valuable for sales teams, customer support departments, and any business that receives time-sensitive SMS communications from clients or partners.

How It Works

1. Trigger on New SMS

The workflow monitors your RingCentral account for incoming SMS messages through its API. Each new message triggers the automation instantly.

2. Format Message Content

The system extracts key details like sender number, message content, and timestamp, then formats them into a clean Slack-friendly message with appropriate metadata.

3. Post to Slack Channel

The formatted message gets posted to your specified Slack channel with clear labeling, making it immediately actionable for your team members.

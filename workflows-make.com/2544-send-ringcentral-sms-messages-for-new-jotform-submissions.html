What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the communication delay that occurs between form submissions and team response. When customers submit information through your Jotform (like service requests, appointments, or inquiries), there's often a lag before your staff sees and acts on it. Manual SMS notifications are time-consuming and prone to human error.

The workflow automatically triggers an SMS message through your RingCentral account the moment a form is submitted. This can be configured to notify internal teams for immediate follow-up, or send confirmation messages directly to customers - like appointment reminders, order confirmations, or status updates.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

The workflow monitors your specified Jotform for new submissions in real-time. Each new entry serves as the trigger to initiate the SMS sequence.

2. Data Processing

Make.com extracts relevant information from the form response, such as customer phone numbers, names, or specific request details that should be included in the SMS.

3. SMS Composition

The template merges form data with your predefined message template, creating personalized SMS content. You can include dynamic fields like appointment dates or service types.

4. RingCentral Delivery

The composed message is instantly sent through your RingCentral SMS gateway, appearing as coming from your business phone number with full delivery tracking.

Pro tip: Add a conditional filter to only send SMS for specific form responses. For example, only notify staff for "Urgent" service requests marked in the form.

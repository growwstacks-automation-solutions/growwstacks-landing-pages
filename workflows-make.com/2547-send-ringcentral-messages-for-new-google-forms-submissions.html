What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of delayed response to form submissions by instantly notifying your team via SMS whenever someone completes your Google Form. Instead of manually checking for new submissions or relying on email notifications that might get buried, your team receives immediate RingCentral text messages with key submission details.

The workflow is particularly valuable for time-sensitive scenarios like service requests, appointment bookings, or emergency contacts where rapid response improves customer satisfaction. Businesses using this automation typically see a 30-50% reduction in first response times compared to manual notification methods.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

The workflow starts when a new submission is detected in your Google Form. Make.com monitors your form continuously and captures all response data including timestamps and field values.

2. Data Processing

The system extracts relevant information from the form response based on your configuration. You can choose which fields to include in the SMS notification and format the message content.

3. SMS Delivery via RingCentral

Make.com connects to your RingCentral account and sends the composed SMS message to your specified phone numbers. The message can include dynamic data from the form submission like the respondent's name or selected options.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to only send SMS for specific form responses (like high-priority requests) or to different team members based on the submission content.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical problem of delayed response times to form submissions. When potential customers fill out your Wufoo contact form, service request, or lead gen form, your team receives an immediate SMS alert via RingCentral - before the submission even hits your inbox.

Unlike email notifications that get buried or delayed, SMS messages achieve 98% open rates within 3 minutes. The workflow captures all form data (name, phone, message) and formats it into a concise mobile-friendly alert, enabling your sales or support team to respond while the lead is still warm.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

Make.com monitors your Wufoo form for new entries, checking every few minutes (or instantly via webhook if you upgrade).

2. Data Parsing

The system extracts key fields like customer name, contact info, and submission details while filtering out spam.

3. SMS Composition

Critical information gets formatted into a 160-character SMS preview with a direct link to the full submission.

4. Team Notification

RingCentral delivers the SMS to designated team numbers or distribution lists based on form type or urgency.

Pro tip: Add conditional routing to send high-priority submissions (like "Emergency Service" checkbox) to managers while standard inquiries go to general staff.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of manually documenting your location history by automatically creating Evernote notes whenever you check in on Foursquare. Business travelers, field researchers, and location-based professionals often need to keep records of their movements, but manually creating notes for each location is time-consuming and easy to forget.

The workflow captures all relevant details from your Foursquare checkins - including location name, address, timestamp, and any photos or notes you added - and formats them into a structured Evernote entry. This creates a searchable digital logbook of your movements that integrates seamlessly with your existing note-taking system.

How It Works

1. Trigger on new Foursquare checkins

The workflow monitors your Foursquare account for new checkin activity. Each time you check in at a location, the automation is triggered to capture all available data about that visit.

2. Extract checkin details

The system pulls structured data from the checkin including venue name, address, coordinates, timestamp, and any additional notes or photos you included with your checkin.

3. Format for Evernote

The workflow organizes the checkin information into a clean, readable format optimized for Evernote. You can customize the template to include only the fields you need.

4. Create note in specified notebook

Finally, the automation creates a new note in your chosen Evernote notebook, tagged and organized according to your preferences. The note becomes instantly searchable within your Evernote account.

Pro tip: Use Evernote's powerful search to quickly find all checkins near a specific address or during a particular date range by combining location data with note creation dates.

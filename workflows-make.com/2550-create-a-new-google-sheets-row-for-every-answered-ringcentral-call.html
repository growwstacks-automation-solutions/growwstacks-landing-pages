What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious and error-prone process of manually logging business calls. Every time a call is answered in your RingCentral phone system, this Make.com workflow automatically creates a detailed row in your Google Sheets spreadsheet with all relevant call information.

The system captures essential call details like caller ID, call duration, timestamps, and direction (inbound/outbound). This creates a searchable database of customer interactions that can be used for analytics, reporting, or integration with other business systems.

How It Works

1. RingCentral triggers on answered calls

The workflow activates whenever RingCentral detects a new answered call in your account. Make.com receives the call details through RingCentral's API.

2. Data is formatted for Google Sheets

The workflow maps RingCentral's call data to your preferred Google Sheets column structure. You can customize which fields are included and how they're labeled.

3. New row created in Google Sheets

Make.com adds a new row to your specified Google Sheet with all the call details. The sheet becomes a comprehensive log of all business calls.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to only log calls meeting specific criteria (like minimum duration) to avoid cluttering your sheet with irrelevant entries.

What This Workflow Does

This automation instantly sends SMS messages through RingCentral whenever a new order is placed in your Shopify store. It eliminates manual order confirmation processes while providing customers with real-time updates about their purchases.

For ecommerce businesses, immediate order confirmations via SMS can significantly improve customer satisfaction. Customers appreciate the instant acknowledgment of their purchase, which reduces anxiety about whether the order was successfully placed. The workflow automatically pulls relevant order details from Shopify and formats them into a concise SMS message.

Pro tip: Combine this with abandoned cart SMS reminders to recover 10-15% of potentially lost sales.

How It Works

1. New Order Trigger

The workflow monitors your Shopify store for new orders through Make.com's instant webhook notifications.

2. Data Extraction

Key order details like customer phone number, order ID, items purchased, and total amount are extracted from the Shopify order object.

3. SMS Composition

The template formats this information into a customer-friendly SMS message with your brand voice and required order details.

4. RingCentral Delivery

Make.com authenticates with your RingCentral account and sends the composed SMS to the customer's phone number.

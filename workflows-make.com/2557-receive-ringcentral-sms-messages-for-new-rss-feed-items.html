What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of missing critical updates from important RSS feeds. Many professionals monitor industry news, competitor blogs, or regulatory feeds, but constantly checking feeds manually is inefficient. Missed updates can mean lost opportunities or being unprepared for market changes.

The workflow automatically detects new RSS feed items and delivers them directly to your mobile device via RingCentral SMS. Unlike email alerts that get buried in inboxes, SMS messages have a 98% open rate within 3 minutes, ensuring you're immediately aware of time-sensitive information.

How It Works

1. RSS Feed Monitoring

The workflow regularly checks your specified RSS feed URLs (typically every 15-60 minutes) for new content. Make.com's RSS module identifies new items since the last check.

2. Content Processing

Each new feed item is parsed to extract key information like title, publication date, author, and content summary. The system can filter items based on keywords or other criteria if needed.

3. SMS Message Composition

The workflow formats a concise SMS alert containing the most important details from the feed item. You can customize which fields are included and how they're presented.

4. RingCentral SMS Delivery

The composed message is sent via your RingCentral account to one or more mobile numbers. RingCentral's reliable SMS infrastructure ensures timely delivery worldwide.

Pro tip: Add a short prefix like "[ALERT]" to your SMS messages so they stand out in your text history and can be filtered/sorted.

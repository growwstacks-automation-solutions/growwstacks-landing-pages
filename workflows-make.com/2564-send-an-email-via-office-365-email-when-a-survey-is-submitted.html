What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually acknowledging every survey submission, which becomes impractical as response volumes grow. When customers, employees, or event attendees complete your Survey123 forms, this workflow instantly sends them a professional email via your Office 365 account.

The system goes beyond basic acknowledgments by allowing you to personalize emails with survey response data. Thank respondents by name, reference their specific answers, or route notifications to different teams based on survey content - all automatically.

How It Works

1. Survey Submission Trigger

The workflow activates whenever someone submits your Survey123 form. Make.com detects the new response instantly, capturing all survey data including email addresses and answers.

2. Data Processing

The system extracts relevant information from the survey response to personalize the email. You can map any survey field into your email template, creating dynamic content that references specific answers.

3. Email Composition

Using Office 365's email service, the workflow generates a professional message with your branding. The template supports HTML formatting, attachments, and conditional content based on survey responses.

4. Recipient Routing

Emails can be sent to the survey respondent, internal teams, or both. Advanced routing lets you notify different departments based on survey answers, creating an intelligent distribution system.

  1. Clone the template in your Make.com account

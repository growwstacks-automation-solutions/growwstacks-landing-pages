What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your scheduling system (Acuity Scheduling) and your project management platform (Podio). Every time a client books an appointment through Acuity, the workflow automatically creates a corresponding task in Podio with all the relevant details.

Manual data entry between scheduling and task management systems is time-consuming and prone to errors. This workflow eliminates those pain points by instantly converting appointments into properly formatted Podio tasks, complete with client information, appointment details, and any custom fields you specify.

How It Works

1. New appointment trigger

The workflow monitors your Acuity Scheduling account for new appointments. As soon as a client books a session, the automation is triggered.

2. Data extraction

Make.com retrieves all relevant appointment details including client name, contact information, appointment type, date/time, duration, and any custom form responses.

3. Task creation in Podio

The system then creates a new task in your specified Podio workspace, populating fields with the appointment information. You can customize which Podio fields get which Acuity data points.

4. Optional notifications

The workflow can be extended to notify team members via email, Slack, or other channels when a new appointment-task is created.

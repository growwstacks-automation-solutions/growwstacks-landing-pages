What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between Acuity Scheduling and Streak, eliminating the manual work of creating follow-up tasks when clients book appointments. Every new Acuity appointment automatically generates a corresponding task in your Streak CRM, complete with relevant client and appointment details.

Service businesses waste countless hours copying information between scheduling tools and their CRM. This workflow solves that by creating instant visibility of new appointments in your sales pipeline, ensuring no client falls through the cracks between scheduling and follow-up.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New Acuity Appointment

The workflow monitors your Acuity Scheduling calendar for new appointments. When a client books a session, consultation, or meeting, the automation is triggered.

2. Extract Appointment Details

Key information like client name, email, appointment type, scheduled time, and any custom intake form responses are captured from the Acuity booking.

3. Create Streak Task

The system then creates a new task in your specified Streak pipeline, populating it with the appointment details to create context for your follow-up actions.

Pro tip: Configure the task due date to be 1-2 days before the appointment for optimal follow-up timing.

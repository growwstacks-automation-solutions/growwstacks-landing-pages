What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical problem for service businesses using Acuity Scheduling - the manual transfer of client information from booking forms to your CRM. Every time a new appointment gets scheduled, valuable lead data sits isolated in Acuity until someone copies it over to HubSpot, creating delays in follow-up and potential data entry errors.

The workflow automatically creates a complete HubSpot contact record whenever someone books an appointment, including all their intake form responses. Sales teams get immediate visibility into new leads with full context of their booked service, availability, and any pre-appointment notes - enabling faster, more personalized outreach.

How It Works

1. Trigger on new Acuity appointments

The workflow monitors your Acuity Scheduling account for new appointments in real-time. Each booking triggers the automation, capturing all submitted client information.

2. Extract and format contact details

Key details like name, email, phone number, and custom form responses are extracted from the appointment data. The system can reformat information (like combining first/last names) to match your HubSpot field structure.

3. Create HubSpot contact record

The workflow creates a new contact in HubSpot with all collected information. You can map Acuity fields to specific HubSpot properties and even add internal tags or lead scores automatically.

4. Log appointment details

Beyond basic contact info, the system records the appointment type, scheduled time, and any notes in the HubSpot contact timeline. This gives your sales team full context for follow-up conversations.

Pro tip: Add a conditional step to only create HubSpot contacts for paid appointments or specific services, focusing your sales team on high-value leads.

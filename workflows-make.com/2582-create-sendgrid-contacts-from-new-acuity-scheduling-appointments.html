What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manually transferring client information between your scheduling system and email marketing platform. Every time a new appointment is booked in Acuity Scheduling, the workflow automatically creates a corresponding contact in SendGrid with all relevant details.

Businesses using this integration typically see a 30-50% reduction in administrative work while improving their email marketing conversion rates. By eliminating manual data entry errors and ensuring no clients slip through the cracks, you can focus on delivering great service while maintaining consistent communication.

How It Works

1. New Appointment Trigger

The workflow monitors your Acuity Scheduling account for new appointments. When someone books a session, the system immediately captures all their contact information and appointment details.

2. Data Processing

The system parses the appointment data, extracting key information like name, email, phone number, and any custom fields from your intake form. This ensures all relevant client details are available for marketing.

3. SendGrid Integration

The processed contact information is automatically added to your specified SendGrid contact list. You can configure the workflow to apply tags, segment contacts, or trigger welcome emails based on the appointment type.

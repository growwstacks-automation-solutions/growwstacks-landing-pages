What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap between scheduling tools and CRM systems. When clients book appointments through Acuity Scheduling, their information often gets stuck in the scheduling system instead of flowing into your sales pipeline. This workflow bridges that gap by automatically creating Kommo CRM contacts the moment an appointment is booked.

The solution eliminates manual data transfer between systems, reduces administrative overhead, and ensures your sales team has immediate access to new leads while appointment details are fresh. Businesses using this automation typically save 5-10 hours per week on manual contact creation while improving lead follow-up speed by 80%.

How It Works

1. Trigger on New Acuity Appointments

The workflow monitors Acuity Scheduling for new appointment bookings in real-time. Each time a client schedules an appointment, the system captures all their details including name, contact information, appointment type, and any custom intake form responses.

2. Format Contact Data for Kommo

Make.com processes the raw appointment data to match Kommo's contact field structure. This step ensures proper mapping of phone formats, name fields, and custom properties while filtering out any unnecessary scheduling-specific data.

3. Create Kommo Contact Record

The system creates a new contact in Kommo with all relevant appointment details. Advanced configurations can add tags based on service type, assign leads to specific team members, or trigger follow-up sequences based on the appointment context.

Pro tip: Extend this workflow to add appointment notes to the Kommo contact timeline, helping your sales team understand the client's needs before follow-up calls.

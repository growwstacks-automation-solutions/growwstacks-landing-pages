What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for service businesses using Acuity Scheduling for appointments and Infusionsoft (now Keap) for CRM and marketing. Without automation, staff must manually copy client information from booking confirmations into the CRM - a time-consuming process prone to errors and delays.

The workflow automatically creates a complete Infusionsoft contact record whenever someone books an appointment in Acuity Scheduling. It captures all relevant details including contact information, appointment type, date/time, and any custom intake form responses. This ensures your sales and marketing teams have immediate access to new leads without any manual data entry.

How It Works

1. Trigger on new Acuity appointments

The workflow monitors your Acuity Scheduling account for new appointments. Each time a client books a session, the automation immediately captures all their details.

2. Format data for Infusionsoft

The system processes the appointment data to match Infusionsoft's contact field requirements. It maps Acuity fields to the corresponding Infusionsoft fields, ensuring clean data import.

3. Create or update Infusionsoft contact

The workflow checks if the contact already exists in Infusionsoft. For new clients, it creates a complete contact record. For existing clients, it can update their profile with the new appointment details.

4. Add tags and trigger follow-up

Optionally, the automation can add relevant tags based on appointment type, assign follow-up tasks to team members, or trigger email sequences - all configured to your business processes.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to add specific tags in Infusionsoft based on the appointment type (e.g., "Consultation", "Return Client", "VIP Service"). This enables powerful segmentation for targeted marketing.

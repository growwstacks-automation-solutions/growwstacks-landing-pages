What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of losing potential email subscribers when clients book appointments through Acuity Scheduling. Without automation, you'd need to manually copy client information from Acuity to AWeber - a time-consuming process that's easy to forget.

The workflow automatically captures every new Acuity Scheduling appointment and adds the client as a subscriber to your AWeber email list. It ensures you can immediately begin nurturing these new contacts with automated email sequences, special offers, and educational content - all without any manual data entry.

How It Works

1. Trigger on new Acuity appointments

The workflow monitors your Acuity Scheduling account for new appointments. Each time a client books a session, the trigger activates.

2. Extract client information

The system pulls the client's name, email address, and any custom fields from the Acuity appointment. This data is formatted for AWeber's requirements.

3. Add to AWeber list

The workflow creates a new subscriber in your specified AWeber list using the collected information. You can configure custom fields and tags based on appointment details.

4. Handle duplicates

If the email already exists in AWeber, the workflow can update the existing record or skip the addition based on your preferences.

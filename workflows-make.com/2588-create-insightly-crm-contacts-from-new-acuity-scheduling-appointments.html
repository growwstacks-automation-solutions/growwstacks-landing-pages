What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point for service businesses using Acuity Scheduling for appointments while managing client relationships in Insightly CRM. Every time a new client books an appointment through Acuity, their information must be manually entered into Insightly - a tedious and error-prone process that wastes valuable time.

The workflow automatically creates a new Insightly CRM contact record whenever an appointment is scheduled in Acuity Scheduling. It transfers all relevant client information including name, contact details, appointment specifics, and any custom fields you've configured. This ensures your CRM stays perfectly synchronized with your scheduling system without any manual intervention.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New Acuity Scheduling Appointment

The workflow activates whenever a new appointment is booked in your Acuity Scheduling account. Make.com monitors your Acuity account in real-time for these events.

2. Extract Client Information

The system retrieves all available details about the new appointment, including client name, email, phone number, appointment type, scheduled time, and any custom intake form responses.

3. Format Data for Insightly

Client information is formatted to match Insightly CRM's expected data structure. You can customize how fields map between systems to ensure data consistency.

4. Create Insightly Contact

The workflow creates a new contact record in Insightly CRM containing all the appointment details. This happens within seconds of the booking being made.

5. Optional Follow-up Actions

You can extend the workflow to trigger additional actions like sending welcome emails, assigning tasks to team members, or logging the appointment as an opportunity.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Copy this template to your Make.com account
  2. Connect your Acuity Scheduling account in the Make.com interface
  3. Connect your Insightly CRM account
  4. Map Acuity fields to corresponding Insightly CRM fields
  5. Test with a new appointment to verify the contact creation
  6. Activate the workflow to run automatically

Pro tip: Create a custom field in Insightly for "Initial Appointment Date" to track when clients first booked with you. This helps with reporting and client lifecycle analysis.

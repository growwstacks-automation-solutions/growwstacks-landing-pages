What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of customer calls slipping through the cracks when support teams manually log interactions. Every time a call is completed or missed in Yodel.io (a popular VoIP service), this Make.com workflow instantly creates a corresponding ticket in Zendesk with all relevant call details.

The system ensures complete visibility into customer communications by automatically documenting every phone interaction in your help desk. This eliminates forgotten callbacks, provides agents with full context, and creates an audit trail for quality assurance purposes.

How It Works

1. Yodel.io Triggers the Workflow

The workflow activates whenever Yodel.io registers either a completed or missed call. Make.com receives the call data including timestamp, duration, caller information, and disposition (missed/completed).

2. Data Transformation

The raw call data gets formatted into Zendesk's required ticket structure. This includes setting appropriate priority levels based on call duration or multiple missed calls from the same number.

3. Ticket Creation in Zendesk

The workflow creates a new Zendesk ticket with all call details pre-populated. Subject lines automatically indicate whether the ticket originated from a missed or completed call for easy triaging.

Pro tip: Customize the ticket description template to include clickable call recording links (if recorded) for maximum agent efficiency.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap in customer support operations by connecting phone calls directly to your ticketing system. When high-value clients or specific phone numbers call your Yodel.io line, the system automatically generates a Freshdesk ticket with all relevant call details.

Manual ticket creation for phone support leads to missed issues, inconsistent records, and wasted agent time. This workflow ensures every call from your monitored numbers gets tracked with the same rigor as email or chat support, while saving your team 5-10 minutes per call on administrative work.

How It Works

1. Call detection from specified numbers

The workflow monitors your Yodel.io account for incoming calls from the phone numbers you specify. You can configure multiple numbers or patterns (like area codes) that should trigger ticket creation.

2. Call data collection

When a matching call comes in, the system captures all available details: caller ID, call duration, timestamp, recording link (if enabled), and any notes from Yodel.io.

3. Ticket creation in Freshdesk

Make.com formats the call data into a complete Freshdesk ticket with appropriate priority, category, and custom fields. The ticket includes all relevant context so agents have full visibility when responding.

Pro tip: Configure your workflow to add internal tags or priority flags when specific VIP numbers call, ensuring those tickets get immediate attention from your team.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of losing valuable call details by automatically creating Evernote entries for every completed or missed call in Yodel.io. Instead of manually taking notes during calls or trying to remember details later, the workflow captures all relevant information the moment a call ends.

The system creates structured notes in your designated Evernote notebook containing caller information, call duration, timestamps, and any notes added during the call. This creates a searchable archive of all communications that integrates seamlessly with your existing note-taking workflow.

How It Works

1. Yodel.io triggers on call completion

The workflow activates whenever a call is marked as completed or missed in Yodel.io. The trigger captures all available call data including participants, duration, and timestamps.

2. Data is formatted for Evernote

Make.com processes the call information into a structured format optimized for Evernote. You can customize which fields are included and how they're presented in the final note.

3. Note is created in designated notebook

The workflow creates a new note in your specified Evernote notebook with all call details. Notes are tagged with relevant metadata for easy searching and organization.

Pro tip: Add custom tags based on call type (e.g., "inbound", "sales", "support") to create powerful search filters in Evernote.

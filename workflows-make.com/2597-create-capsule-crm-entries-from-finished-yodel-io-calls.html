What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical pain point for sales teams and customer support agents who waste valuable time manually logging call details in their CRM. Every time a call completes in Yodel.io (whether inbound or outbound), this Make.com workflow automatically creates a corresponding entry in Capsule CRM with all relevant call details.

The system captures caller information, call duration, timestamps, notes, and any custom fields you configure - then transfers this data to the appropriate contact record in Capsule CRM. This ensures your team has complete visibility into all customer interactions without the administrative overhead.

How It Works

1. Trigger: Call completion in Yodel.io

The workflow activates whenever a call status changes to "completed" in your Yodel.io account. Make.com instantly receives the call data through Yodel.io's API.

2. Data processing and filtering

The system can filter calls based on your criteria (duration, caller type, tags etc.) to only log relevant interactions. You can also transform data formats or enrich records before CRM creation.

3. CRM entry creation

Make.com creates a new entry in Capsule CRM, either as a new contact or adding to an existing record. The workflow maps all selected Yodel.io fields to corresponding Capsule CRM fields automatically.

4. Optional follow-up actions

You can configure additional steps like assigning tasks to team members, triggering email follow-ups, or updating deal stages based on call outcomes.

Pro tip: Add a 2-minute delay before CRM creation to allow agents time to add post-call notes in Yodel.io that will then be included in the CRM entry.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Copy the template from Make.com to your account
  2. Connect your Yodel.io account in the "Watch Calls" module
  3. Connect your Capsule CRM account in the "Create Entry" module
  4. Map Yodel.io call fields to corresponding Capsule CRM fields
  5. Set any filters for calls that shouldn't create CRM entries
  6. Test with sample calls and verify CRM entries appear correctly
  7. Activate the scenario and monitor for first few days

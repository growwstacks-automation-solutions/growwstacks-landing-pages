What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap in sales team productivity - manually logging every customer call in your CRM. When sales reps make or receive calls through Yodel.io, this workflow automatically creates corresponding activity records in Copper CRM with all relevant call details.

The system captures caller information, call duration, timestamps, and direction (inbound/outbound), then logs it as a completed activity against the correct contact in Copper. This eliminates hours of manual data entry while ensuring your CRM always reflects the most accurate customer interaction history.

How It Works

1. Call completion triggers the workflow

Whenever a call finishes in your Yodel.io account, the system sends a webhook notification to Make.com with all call details.

2. Data is processed and formatted

The workflow extracts key information like caller ID, duration, and timestamps, then structures it according to Copper's activity API requirements.

3. Activity is created in Copper

Make.com connects to your Copper account via API and creates a new activity record linked to the appropriate contact, with all call details included.

Pro tip: Configure custom activity types in Copper to distinguish between sales calls, support calls, and other conversation types for better reporting.

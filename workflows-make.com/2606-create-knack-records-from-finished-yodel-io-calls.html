What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of logging call data from Yodel.io into your Knack CRM. Every time a call is completed or missed in Yodel.io, the workflow automatically creates a corresponding record in Knack with all relevant call details.

By eliminating manual data entry, you ensure no calls slip through the cracks while saving your team hours of repetitive work each week. The integration maintains data consistency between your call tracking system and CRM, giving your sales and support teams complete visibility into customer interactions.

How It Works

1. Yodel.io triggers the workflow

The workflow starts whenever a call is marked as finished or missed in Yodel.io. It captures all available call data including timestamps, duration, caller information, and any notes.

2. Data is processed and formatted

Make.com processes the raw Yodel.io data, transforming it into the correct format for Knack. This step can include filtering calls, calculating metrics, or enriching data with additional information.

3. Record is created in Knack

The workflow creates a new record in your specified Knack application, mapping all relevant call fields to your custom Knack object structure. Failed attempts automatically retry with error handling.

Pro tip: Add a validation step to exclude very short calls (under 30 seconds) which are often wrong numbers or accidental dials.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manually transferring call data between communication platforms and tracking systems. Every time a call is completed or missed in Yodel.io, the workflow automatically creates a corresponding row in your Smartsheet with all relevant call details.

Sales and support teams waste valuable time copying call logs between systems. This workflow eliminates that manual work while ensuring your Smartsheet always contains the most current call data for reporting and analysis. The integration captures essential details like call duration, timestamps, caller information, and call outcome.

How It Works

1. Yodel.io triggers on call completion

The workflow starts whenever a call ends in your Yodel.io account, whether completed or missed. Make.com receives the call data through Yodel.io's webhook notifications.

2. Data is formatted for Smartsheet

The workflow maps Yodel.io's call data fields to your Smartsheet column structure. You can customize which call details transfer and how they're formatted.

3. New row created in Smartsheet

Make.com adds the call as a new row in your specified Smartsheet, populating all configured columns with the call details. The process happens in seconds without manual intervention.

Pro tip: Add a "Call Type" column in Smartsheet to categorize calls (sales, support, etc.) based on the phone number or caller information.

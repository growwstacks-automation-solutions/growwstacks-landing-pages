What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of manually tracking customer support follow-ups by automatically converting tagged Yodel.io calls into organized Trello cards. Every time your team tags a call (like "Follow-up" or "Technical Issue"), the workflow instantly creates a corresponding card in your designated Trello board with all relevant call details.

Support teams often waste valuable time transcribing call notes into task management systems. This workflow eliminates that manual work while ensuring no important customer requests slip through the cracks. The integration maintains context by including call recordings, duration, and tags directly in the Trello card description.

How It Works

1. Trigger when a call is tagged

The workflow monitors your Yodel.io account for new tags added to calls. You can configure it to watch for specific tags or process all tagged calls.

2. Extract call details

Make retrieves key information from the call including customer name, phone number, call duration, recording URL, and the specific tag applied.

3. Create Trello card

The system automatically generates a Trello card in your specified board and list, populating the title with the call tag and description with all relevant details.

Pro tip: Configure your Trello board with lists that match your common call tags (like "Follow-ups", "Technical Issues") for perfect organization.

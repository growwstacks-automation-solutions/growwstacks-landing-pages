What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of manually sharing important call recordings with team members. When customer support agents, sales reps, or managers tag calls in Yodel.io (such as marking an escalation or training example), the system automatically posts the full recording to your designated Telegram channel.

The workflow eliminates time-consuming manual uploads while ensuring critical conversations are immediately available to relevant team members. This is particularly valuable for quality assurance, dispute resolution, training purposes, and maintaining organizational transparency around customer interactions.

How It Works

1. Tag detection in Yodel.io

The workflow monitors your Yodel.io account for new tags applied to call recordings. You can configure which specific tags trigger the automation (like "Escalation" or "Training Example").

2. Recording retrieval

When a matching tag is detected, the system fetches the complete call recording from Yodel.io along with relevant metadata like call duration, participants, and timestamp.

3. Telegram delivery

The recording is then securely transmitted to your specified Telegram channel with contextual information. Team members receive instant notifications with direct access to the audio file.

Pro tip: Create a dedicated Telegram channel for call recordings with clear naming conventions (e.g., "Support Escalations - Q3 2023") to maintain organization as your library grows.

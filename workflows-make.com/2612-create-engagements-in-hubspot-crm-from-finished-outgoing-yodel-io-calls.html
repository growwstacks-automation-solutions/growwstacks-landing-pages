What This Workflow Does

Sales teams waste countless hours manually logging call activities in their CRM. This automation eliminates that friction by automatically creating HubSpot engagements whenever your team completes outgoing calls through Yodel.io.

The workflow captures essential call details like duration, timestamps, and participants, then creates a complete engagement record in HubSpot. This ensures your CRM always reflects the latest customer interactions without requiring reps to switch between systems or remember to log calls after busy days.

How It Works

1. Yodel.io triggers on call completion

The workflow activates whenever an outgoing call finishes in your Yodel.io account. Make.com receives the call data including duration, participants, and timestamps.

2. Data formatting and enrichment

The raw call data gets transformed into HubSpot's required format. You can customize this step to add call notes, score the interaction, or flag important calls for follow-up.

3. HubSpot engagement creation

Make.com creates a new engagement in HubSpot associated with the correct contact record. The engagement includes all call details and can be configured to trigger workflows in HubSpot like task creation or deal stage changes.

Pro tip: Add a second module to update custom contact properties in HubSpot with last call date or total call time for better reporting.

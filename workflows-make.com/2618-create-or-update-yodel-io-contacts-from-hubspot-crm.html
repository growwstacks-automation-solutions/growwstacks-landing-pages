What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for sales teams using both HubSpot CRM and Yodel.io - maintaining consistent, up-to-date contact information across both platforms. Without automation, sales reps waste valuable time manually copying contact details between systems, leading to errors and outdated information.

The workflow automatically creates new contacts in Yodel.io whenever they're added to HubSpot CRM, and updates existing Yodel.io contacts when their HubSpot records change. This ensures your sales team always has access to the most current contact information whether they're working in HubSpot or communicating through Yodel.io.

How It Works

1. Trigger on new or updated HubSpot contacts

The workflow starts whenever a contact is created or modified in your HubSpot CRM account. Make.com monitors HubSpot's API for these changes in real-time.

2. Retrieve complete contact details

The workflow fetches all available contact information from HubSpot including standard fields like name, email, and phone number, plus any custom properties you've defined.

3. Check for existing Yodel.io contact

Before creating a new record, the automation searches Yodel.io to see if the contact already exists, typically using email address as the unique identifier.

4. Create or update Yodel.io contact

Depending on whether the contact exists in Yodel.io, the workflow either creates a new record or updates the existing one with the latest information from HubSpot.

5. Handle errors and notifications

If any step fails, the workflow logs the error and can notify your team via email so you can manually resolve the issue.

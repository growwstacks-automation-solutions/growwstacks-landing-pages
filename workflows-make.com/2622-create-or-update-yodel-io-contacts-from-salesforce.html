What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for sales teams using both Salesforce and Yodel.io - maintaining synchronized customer data across both platforms. Manual contact entry leads to errors, duplicates, and outdated information that can damage customer relationships and sales effectiveness.

The workflow automatically creates new contacts in Yodel.io whenever they're added to Salesforce, and updates existing Yodel.io records when Salesforce contact details change. This ensures your sales team always works with current customer information, whether they're in Salesforce for CRM management or Yodel.io for communication.

How It Works

1. Salesforce Contact Trigger

The workflow monitors Salesforce for new or updated contact records. Using Make.com's real-time polling, it detects changes immediately without delay.

2. Contact Data Mapping

Essential contact fields (name, email, phone, company) are extracted from Salesforce and formatted for Yodel.io's API requirements. The template includes standard field mappings that can be customized.

3. Yodel.io Contact Lookup

Before creating a new contact, the system checks Yodel.io for existing records matching the Salesforce contact's email address to prevent duplicates.

4. Create or Update Operation

Based on the lookup results, the workflow either creates a new Yodel.io contact or updates the existing record with current Salesforce data.

Pro tip: Extend this workflow to sync custom Salesforce fields by adding additional mapping steps in Make.com's visual editor.

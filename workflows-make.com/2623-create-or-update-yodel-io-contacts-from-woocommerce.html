What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of customer data silos between your eCommerce store and CRM. Every time a new customer registers or places an order in WooCommerce, the workflow automatically creates or updates their contact record in Yodel.io.

Manual data entry between systems is time-consuming and prone to errors. This workflow eliminates those issues by maintaining a real-time sync between WooCommerce and Yodel.io. Your sales and support teams will always have access to up-to-date customer information without any manual work.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New WooCommerce customer or order

The workflow activates whenever a new customer registers or places an order in your WooCommerce store. Make.com monitors these events in real-time.

2. Data extraction

The system collects all relevant customer information including name, email, phone number, billing/shipping addresses, and order details from WooCommerce.

3. Yodel.io contact lookup

The workflow checks if the customer already exists in Yodel.io by searching for matching email addresses or other unique identifiers.

4. Create or update contact

Depending on the lookup result, the workflow either creates a new Yodel.io contact or updates the existing record with the latest information from WooCommerce.

5. Error handling

The system includes built-in error handling to notify you if any sync issues occur, ensuring data integrity between both platforms.

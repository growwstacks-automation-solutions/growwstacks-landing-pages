What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical problem for businesses using Copper CRM for sales and Yodel.io for customer communications. When contact information changes in one system, it often doesn't get updated in the other, leading to missed opportunities and customer frustration.

The workflow automatically creates or updates Yodel.io contacts whenever new people are added or modified in Copper. This ensures your sales and support teams always work with the same accurate customer data, eliminating manual data entry and reducing errors.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New or Updated Copper Contact

The workflow monitors your Copper account for new contacts or updates to existing ones. You can configure it to watch specific pipelines or apply to all contacts.

2. Check for Existing Yodel.io Contact

Before creating a new record, the automation checks if the contact already exists in Yodel.io using email matching or other unique identifiers you specify.

3. Create or Update Yodel.io Record

Depending on whether the contact exists, the workflow either creates a new Yodel.io contact or updates the existing one with the latest information from Copper.

4. Field Mapping & Data Transformation

The template includes preconfigured field mappings between Copper and Yodel.io, which you can customize to match your specific data structure and business needs.

5. Error Handling & Notifications

If any step fails, the workflow logs the error and can notify your team via email or Slack for manual intervention when needed.

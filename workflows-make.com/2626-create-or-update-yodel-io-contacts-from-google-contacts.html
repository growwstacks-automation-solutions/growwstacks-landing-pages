What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of maintaining duplicate contact lists across different business tools. When your sales team uses Yodel.io for outreach while your organization manages contacts in Google Contacts, manually transferring contact information becomes time-consuming and error-prone.

The workflow automatically creates or updates Yodel.io contacts whenever changes occur in Google Contacts. It checks for existing records to prevent duplicates and ensures both systems stay perfectly synchronized without manual intervention. This eliminates hours of administrative work while improving data accuracy across your sales and marketing tools.

How It Works

1. Trigger on new Google Contacts

The workflow activates whenever a new contact is added to your Google Contacts. Make.com monitors your Google account for these changes in real-time.

2. Retrieve contact details

The system pulls all available contact information including name, email, phone numbers, company details, and any custom fields you've configured.

3. Check for existing Yodel.io contact

Before creating a new record, the workflow searches Yodel.io to see if this contact already exists, typically matching by email address.

4. Create or update record

Depending on whether the contact exists in Yodel.io, the workflow either creates a new contact record or updates the existing one with the latest information from Google Contacts.

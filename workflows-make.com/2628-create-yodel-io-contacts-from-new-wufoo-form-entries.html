What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious and error-prone process of manually transferring contact information from Wufoo forms to your Yodel.io CRM. Every time someone submits your Wufoo form, the workflow instantly creates a corresponding contact record in Yodel.io with all the submitted information.

The solution eliminates the risk of missed submissions, duplicate data entry, and transcription errors that commonly occur when moving data between systems manually. It ensures your sales team has immediate access to fresh leads while maintaining complete data integrity between platforms.

How It Works

1. Trigger on new Wufoo form submission

The workflow activates whenever a new form is submitted to your Wufoo account. Make.com monitors your form in real-time and captures all submitted data fields.

2. Map form fields to Yodel.io contact properties

The workflow intelligently maps each Wufoo form field to the corresponding contact property in Yodel.io. This includes standard fields like name, email, and phone, plus any custom fields you've created.

3. Create or update Yodel.io contact

The system checks if the contact already exists in Yodel.io (typically by email matching) and either creates a new record or updates the existing one with the latest form submission data.

4. Handle errors and notifications

If any issues occur during processing, the workflow can notify your team via email or Slack while preserving the form data for manual review.

  1. Copy this template to your Make.com account
  2. Connect your Wufoo account in the first module
  3. Connect your Yodel.io account in the contact creation module

  5. Test with a form submission and verify the contact appears in Yodel.io

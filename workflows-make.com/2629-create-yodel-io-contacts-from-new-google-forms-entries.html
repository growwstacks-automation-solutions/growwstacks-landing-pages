What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manually transferring contact information from Google Forms submissions to your CRM. Every time someone fills out your form, their responses automatically create a new contact in Yodel.io with all relevant fields properly mapped.

The workflow eliminates hours of tedious data entry while ensuring your CRM stays perfectly synchronized with all new leads, event registrations, or customer feedback submissions. It handles field mapping, data formatting, and error checking automatically.

How It Works

1. Trigger on new form submission

The workflow activates whenever someone submits your Google Form. Make.com instantly receives the response data including all field values.

2. Process and format the data

The system parses the form responses, extracts key information like name, email, and phone number, and formats it according to Yodel.io's contact requirements.

3. Create Yodel.io contact

Make.com connects to your Yodel.io account via API and creates a new contact record with all the mapped form data. Custom fields and tags can be automatically applied based on form responses.

4. Error handling and logging

The workflow includes validation to ensure data quality and can notify your team if any submissions fail to process correctly.

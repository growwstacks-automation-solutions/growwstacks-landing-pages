What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical business problem: the time-consuming manual process of transferring contact information from web forms to your CRM. Every time a potential customer fills out your Cognito Forms form, this Make.com workflow instantly creates a corresponding contact record in Yodel.io.

The solution eliminates human error in data transfer and ensures no leads slip through the cracks. Sales teams gain immediate visibility into new prospects without waiting for manual data entry. Marketing teams get accurate contact data for nurturing campaigns from the moment of form submission.

How It Works

1. New form submission triggers the workflow

Make.com monitors your Cognito Forms account for new submissions. The moment someone completes your form, the workflow activates.

2. Data extraction and formatting

The system extracts all submitted information and formats it according to Yodel.io's contact field requirements. This includes standard fields like name, email, and phone number, plus any custom fields you've configured.

3. Contact creation in Yodel.io

Make.com sends the formatted contact data to your Yodel.io account via API, creating a new contact record with all the submitted information.

Pro tip: Add a conditional step to only create contacts for qualified leads based on specific form responses, saving your sales team from chasing unqualified prospects.

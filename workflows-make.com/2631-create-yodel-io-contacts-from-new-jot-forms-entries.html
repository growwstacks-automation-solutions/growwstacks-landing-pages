What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the tedious manual process of transferring contact information from Jotform submissions to Yodel.io. Every time someone completes your form, the workflow instantly creates a properly formatted contact record in your CRM with all submitted details.

Businesses using Jotform for lead generation often waste hours each week copying and pasting form responses into their CRM. This workflow solves that problem by automatically mapping form fields to the corresponding CRM fields, ensuring data consistency while saving valuable time.

How It Works

1. Form submission triggers the workflow

When a visitor submits your Jotform, the workflow immediately detects the new entry through Jotform's webhook or API connection.

2. Data extraction and formatting

Make.com parses the form response, extracting all submitted fields and preparing them for CRM import according to your field mapping configuration.

3. Contact creation in Yodel.io

The workflow connects to Yodel.io's API and creates a new contact record with all mapped information, including custom fields if configured.

4. Optional confirmation steps

You can add steps to send confirmation emails, notify your team, or trigger follow-up sequences—all automatically after the contact is created.

Pro tip: Add a delay before CRM creation to allow for form validation or spam filtering if needed.

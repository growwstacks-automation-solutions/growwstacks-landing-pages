What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between form submissions and task management by instantly converting Cognito Forms entries into Pipefy cards. Every time someone completes your form, whether it's a customer inquiry, job application, or service request, the system automatically creates a corresponding card in your Pipefy pipeline.

The workflow eliminates the tedious manual process of copying form data into Pipefy, reducing administrative overhead while ensuring no submissions fall through the cracks. It maintains all the form field data within the Pipefy card, giving your team immediate access to complete information for follow-up actions.

How It Works

1. New form submission detection

The workflow continuously monitors your Cognito Forms account for new submissions. When detected, it captures all the form response data including custom fields.

2. Data processing and mapping

The system processes the form data, allowing you to map specific form fields to corresponding Pipefy card attributes. You can customize which information goes where in your Pipefy card structure.

3. Card creation in Pipefy

Using Pipefy's API, the workflow creates a new card in your specified pipeline and phase. All mapped data populates the card fields automatically, ready for your team to take action.

Pro tip: Use Pipefy's email notifications feature to automatically alert team members when new cards are created from form submissions.

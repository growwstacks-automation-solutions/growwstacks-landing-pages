What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of important client requests or support issues getting lost in email inboxes. When team members label specific emails in Gmail, workflow automatically creates corresponding cards in your Pipefy boards, turning unstructured email communication into properly tracked work items.

The solution eliminates manual copying and pasting between systems, reducing human error and saving 10-15 minutes per task. It ensures critical email content becomes immediately visible to your entire team in Pipefy boards, with all relevant context preserved.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New labeled email detection

Make.com monitors your Gmail account for new emails tagged with your specified label. This could be "To Process" or "Client Request" depending on your workflow.

2. Email content extraction

The system extracts key email elements including the subject line, sender information, body content, and any attachments.

3. Pipefy card creation

The workflow creates a new card in your designated Pipefy board, populating fields with the extracted email data. You can be configured to map specific email elements to particular Pipefy card fields.

4. Optional notifications

The system can notify team members via Slack or email when new cards are created from important emails, ensuring prompt attention.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common challenge for professionals and businesses who publish content on WordPress but struggle to consistently share it on LinkedIn. Manual posting is time-consuming and easy to forget, causing missed opportunities to engage your professional network.

The workflow automatically detects new WordPress posts and publishes them to your LinkedIn profile or company page with your preferred formatting. It handles the entire process from content extraction to posting, ensuring your professional audience sees your latest insights without any manual intervention.

How It Works

1. WordPress publishes new content

The workflow monitors your WordPress site's RSS feed or uses webhooks to detect immediately when new posts go live.

2. Content is prepared for LinkedIn

The automation extracts key elements like title, excerpt, featured image, and author information, then formats them according to LinkedIn's best practices.

3. Post is published to LinkedIn

Using LinkedIn's API, the workflow creates a new post on your specified profile or company page, including all relevant content with proper formatting and optional hashtags.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to add 3-5 relevant industry hashtags to each post for 28% better discoverability on LinkedIn.

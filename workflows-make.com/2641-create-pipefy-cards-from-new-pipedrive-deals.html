What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your sales CRM (Pipedrive) and workflow management system (Pipefy). Whenever your sales team closes a new deal in Pipedrive, the system automatically creates a corresponding card in Pipefy with all relevant deal information.

The workflow eliminates manual data transfer between systems, ensuring operations teams receive complete, accurate information immediately when deals are won. This reduces handoff delays between sales and fulfillment teams while preventing errors from manual data entry.

How It Works

1. New deal detection

Make.com monitors your Pipedrive account for new deals created in specific pipelines or stages you configure.

2. Data extraction

The workflow retrieves all relevant deal information including customer details, deal value, custom fields, and attachments.

3. Pipefy card creation

The system creates a new card in your specified Pipefy board, mapping Pipedrive fields to corresponding Pipefy card fields.

4. Error handling

If card creation fails, the system retries and notifies administrators with details about the failed transaction.

Pro tip: Configure the automation to only trigger for deals in specific stages (like "Closed Won") to prevent premature workflow creation for early-stage deals.

This automation benefits any business using Pipedrive for sales pipeline management and Pipefy for operational workflows. It's particularly valuable for:

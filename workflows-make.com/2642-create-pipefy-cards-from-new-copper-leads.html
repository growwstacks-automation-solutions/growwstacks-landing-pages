Create Pipefy Cards from New Copper Leads

Automatically transfer new leads from Copper to Pipefy for streamlined sales pipeline management

Get This Workflow → Make.com · Free Template

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of leads getting stuck between your CRM and pipeline management system. When a sales team uses Copper for lead capture but Pipefy for pipeline management, valuable leads often require manual transfer—a time-consuming process prone to delays and errors.

The workflow automatically creates a new Pipefy card whenever a lead enters Copper, with all relevant contact details, notes, and custom fields preserved. This eliminates duplicate data entry while ensuring leads move instantly into your sales process, reducing response times and improving conversion rates.

Pro tip: Combine this with a follow-up email automation in Pipefy to engage leads within minutes of their submission.

How It Works

1. New Lead Detection

The workflow monitors your Copper account for new lead creations in real-time using Make.com's instant trigger.

2. Data Mapping

Essential lead information (name, email, company, phone, custom fields) is extracted and formatted for Pipefy's card structure.

3. Pipefy Card Creation

A new card is automatically created in your specified Pipefy board and pipeline, with the lead details pre-populated.

4. Status Synchronization

Optionally, you can configure the workflow to update Copper when the Pipefy card moves stages, keeping both systems in sync.

Create Pipefy Cards from New WooCommerce Orders

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the manual and error-prone process of transferring WooCommerce orders to your fulfillment system. Every time a customer places an order on your WooCommerce store, this workflow automatically creates a corresponding card in Pipefy with all relevant order details.

The integration eliminates duplicate data entry between systems, reduces fulfillment errors, and gives your team better visibility into order status through Pipefy's workflow visualization. Cards are created instantly with customer information, ordered items, shipping details, and order totals - ready for your fulfillment team to process.

How It Works

1. New order trigger

The workflow monitors your WooCommerce store for new orders through Make.com's WooCommerce integration. When an order is placed, the workflow captures all order details.

2. Data transformation

Order information is formatted to match Pipefy's card structure. You can customize which WooCommerce fields map to which Pipefy card fields based on your fulfillment needs.

3. Card creation

The workflow creates a new card in your specified Pipefy board and phase, populating all relevant fields with the WooCommerce order data. You can configure different boards/phases for different order types.

4. Confirmation

The workflow verifies successful card creation and can optionally send notifications to your team or update the WooCommerce order with a tracking reference.

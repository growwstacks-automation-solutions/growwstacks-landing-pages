Create Pipefy Cards from New Xero Purchase Orders

Automatically convert Xero purchase orders into Pipefy cards to streamline your procurement workflow

Get This Workflow → Make.com · Free Template

What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges your accounting and procurement systems by automatically creating Pipefy cards whenever new purchase orders appear in Xero. It eliminates manual data entry between systems, reducing errors and accelerating your procurement cycle.

When finance teams create purchase orders in Xero, the relevant details automatically flow into Pipefy as new cards in your procurement pipeline. This gives purchasing teams immediate visibility into new requests while maintaining a clean audit trail between the systems.

How It Works

1. New Purchase Order Detection

The workflow monitors your Xero account for newly created purchase orders. You can filter which POs trigger the automation based on amount, vendor, or other criteria.

2. Data Extraction

Key details like vendor information, line items, amounts, and due dates are extracted from the Xero purchase order.

3. Pipefy Card Creation

The workflow creates a new card in your designated Pipefy board, populating all relevant fields with the purchase order data.

4. Notification & Routing

Optional steps can notify approvers or route cards to specific pipeline phases based on purchase amount or department.

  • Process more than 20 purchase orders monthly

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of missed deadlines in team workflows by instantly notifying relevant team members when Pipefy cards become overdue. Instead of manually checking Pipefy boards or relying on email notifications that might get buried, team members receive prominent Chrome browser notifications the moment a task passes its due date.

The workflow monitors your Pipefy boards in real-time, identifies overdue cards based on their due date fields, and pushes customized notifications directly to team members' browsers. This creates immediate visibility into overdue work items without requiring constant manual checking of your Pipefy boards.

How It Works

1. Monitoring Pipefy for overdue cards

The workflow regularly checks your specified Pipefy boards for cards where the due date has passed but the card remains incomplete. You can configure how frequently checks occur based on your team's needs.

2. Filtering relevant overdue items

Only cards matching your criteria trigger notifications. You can filter by phase, assignee, priority level, or custom fields to ensure only the most important overdue items generate alerts.

3. Generating Chrome notifications

For each overdue card, the system creates a Chrome notification containing the card title, due date, assignee, and a direct link back to the Pipefy card. Notifications appear even when Chrome is minimized.

Pro tip: Add escalation rules to notify managers if high-priority tasks remain overdue for more than 24 hours.

  1. Clone the template in your Make.com account

  3. Configure which Pipefy phases and fields indicate overdue status

  5. Test with a sample overdue card to verify notifications appear
  6. Deploy the scenario to run automatically

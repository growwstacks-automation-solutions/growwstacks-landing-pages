What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap between project management and customer relationship management systems. When sales teams capture leads in Pipefy cards or support teams log customer issues, these valuable contacts often get stuck in silos rather than flowing into your marketing and sales CRM.

The workflow automatically creates ActiveCampaign contacts whenever new cards are added to specified Pipefy pipelines. It maps relevant card fields (like email, phone, company) to corresponding ActiveCampaign contact fields, ensuring your CRM stays updated without manual data entry. This eliminates duplicate work and ensures no lead falls through the cracks.

How It Works

1. Pipefy Triggers the Workflow

Whenever a new card is created in your designated Pipefy pipeline, Make.com detects the change instantly through Pipefy's API.

2. Data Extraction and Mapping

The workflow extracts all relevant card information including custom fields you've defined in Pipefy. You can configure exactly which fields should transfer to ActiveCampaign.

3. ActiveCampaign Contact Creation

Make.com formats the extracted data according to ActiveCampaign's API requirements and creates a new contact record. The workflow can also add tags, assign to lists, or trigger automations based on Pipefy card properties.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to add Pipefy card URLs to ActiveCampaign contact notes. This gives your sales team one-click access back to the original context in Pipefy.

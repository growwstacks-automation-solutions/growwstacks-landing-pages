What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap between lead management systems and email marketing platforms. Many businesses capture leads in Pipefy but then manually transfer them to AWeber for email campaigns - a time-consuming process prone to errors and delays.

The workflow automatically creates AWeber subscribers whenever new cards are added to specified Pipefy pipelines. It maps relevant contact information from Pipefy fields to corresponding AWeber subscriber fields, ensuring complete and accurate data transfer without human intervention.

How It Works

1. Pipefy triggers the workflow

The automation monitors your Pipefy board for new card creations. Each time a card is added to your selected pipeline, Make.com receives the card details including all field values.

2. Data is formatted for AWeber

The workflow extracts specific fields from the Pipefy card (like name, email, custom fields) and structures them according to AWeber's subscriber API requirements. You can customize which Pipefy fields map to which AWeber fields.

3. Subscriber is created in AWeber

Make.com sends the formatted data to AWeber's API, creating a new subscriber in your specified list. The workflow can optionally apply tags or add subscribers to multiple lists based on Pipefy card properties.

Pro tip: Add a delay module before the AWeber step to schedule subscriber additions for optimal email send times based on your audience's timezone.

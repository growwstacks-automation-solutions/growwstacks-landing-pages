Create GetResponse Contacts from New Pipefy Cards

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of manually transferring contact information between project management tools and email marketing platforms. Every time your team creates a new card in Pipefy - whether it's a lead form submission, customer inquiry, or event registration - the workflow instantly captures that contact data and adds it to your designated GetResponse list.

The integration eliminates repetitive data entry tasks while ensuring your marketing team never misses a new contact. By automating this process, you maintain data consistency between systems and can trigger immediate email follow-ups through GetResponse's automation features.

How It Works

1. Pipefy Card Creation Trigger

The workflow monitors your specified Pipefy board for new card creations. Whenever a card matching your criteria is added, the automation captures all relevant fields including contact information, custom fields, and any tags or labels.

2. Data Transformation

Make.com processes the Pipefy card data, mapping fields to match GetResponse's contact structure. You can configure custom field matching to ensure all relevant information transfers correctly.

3. GetResponse Contact Creation

The processed data creates a new contact in your specified GetResponse list. The workflow handles all API connections securely, maintaining data integrity between systems.

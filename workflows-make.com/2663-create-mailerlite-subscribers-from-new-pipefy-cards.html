What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of disconnected systems between lead capture (Pipefy) and email marketing (MailerLite). Marketing teams often waste hours manually transferring contact information between platforms, risking data entry errors and missed opportunities.

The workflow automatically creates MailerLite subscribers whenever new cards are added to your Pipefy board. It maps relevant contact fields between the systems, ensuring your email list stays synchronized with your latest leads without any manual intervention.

How It Works

1. Pipefy triggers the workflow

The automation monitors your specified Pipefy board for new card creation events. Each time a card is added, the workflow immediately captures all its field data.

2. Data transformation

The workflow maps Pipefy card fields to MailerLite subscriber fields. You can customize which information transfers, such as name, email, phone number, and custom fields like lead source or product interest.

3. Subscriber creation in MailerLite

Make.com adds the contact to your specified MailerLite group with all mapped fields. The workflow handles error cases like duplicate emails according to your configuration preferences.

Pro tip: Add a delay step before MailerLite creation to implement a double opt-in process that complies with GDPR and other privacy regulations.

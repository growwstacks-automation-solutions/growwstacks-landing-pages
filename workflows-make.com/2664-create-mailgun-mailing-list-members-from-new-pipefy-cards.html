What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your Pipefy workflow management and Mailgun email marketing. Whenever a new card is created in your Pipefy pipeline (representing a new contact, lead, or customer), the workflow automatically adds them as a subscriber to your specified Mailgun mailing list.

The solution eliminates manual data transfers between systems, ensuring your email campaigns always have access to fresh contacts from your sales, support, or marketing pipelines. It maintains data accuracy by directly mapping Pipefy card fields to Mailgun subscriber properties.

How It Works

1. Pipefy Triggers the Workflow

The automation starts when a new card is created in your specified Pipefy pipeline. Make.com monitors your Pipefy board for these events in real-time.

2. Card Data is Extracted

The workflow pulls all relevant information from the new Pipefy card, including email address, name, custom fields, and any tags that should inform email segmentation.

3. Data is Formatted for Mailgun

Make.com transforms the Pipefy data into Mailgun's required subscriber format, mapping fields appropriately and handling any necessary data cleansing.

4. Subscriber is Added to Mailgun

The processed contact information is sent to your specified Mailgun mailing list via API. The workflow can handle success responses or error conditions appropriately.

Pro tip: Use Pipefy card phases to trigger different Mailgun list additions. For example, move contacts from a "Welcome" to "Nurture" list when their card progresses to a specific pipeline stage.

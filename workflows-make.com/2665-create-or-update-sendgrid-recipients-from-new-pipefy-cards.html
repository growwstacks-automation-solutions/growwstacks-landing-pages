What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your project management in Pipefy and email marketing in SendGrid. Every time a new card is created in your Pipefy pipeline (like a new lead, customer, or event registrant), the workflow automatically adds that contact to your SendGrid recipient lists or updates their information if they already exist.

Manual data entry between systems creates errors, delays, and missed opportunities. This solution ensures your marketing communications always work with complete, up-to-date contact information while saving your team hours of repetitive copying and pasting between platforms.

How It Works

1. Pipefy triggers the workflow

The automation monitors your specified Pipefy board for new card creations. Each new card serves as a trigger containing contact information and other relevant data fields.

2. Data is extracted and formatted

The workflow parses the Pipefy card to extract email addresses, names, and any other configured fields. It structures this data to match SendGrid's contact format requirements.

3. SendGrid recipient check

Before creating a new contact, the system checks if the email already exists in your SendGrid account to prevent duplicates.

4. Create or update contact

Depending on the check result, the workflow either creates a new SendGrid recipient or updates the existing one with the latest information from Pipefy.

5. List management

Optionally, the contact can be added to specific SendGrid lists based on Pipefy card properties like pipeline stage, labels, or custom fields.

Pro tip: Use Pipefy card fields to add custom SendGrid contact properties like preferences, lead scores, or segmentation tags for more targeted email campaigns.

What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the tedious manual process of copying customer data from Pipefy cards to ActiveTrail contacts. Every time your sales team creates or updates a card in Pipefy, the workflow instantly syncs the relevant contact information to ActiveTrail - creating new contacts or updating existing ones automatically.

The solution solves three critical problems: 1) Marketing teams working with outdated contact lists, 2) Sales teams wasting time on data entry instead of selling, and 3) Inconsistent customer data between systems causing failed email deliveries or irrelevant messaging.

How It Works

1. Pipefy card trigger

The workflow monitors your specified Pipefy board for new or updated cards. When detected, it captures all relevant contact fields from the card including custom fields you've defined.

2. Data validation

The system checks for required fields (like email address) and formats data to match ActiveTrail's requirements. Invalid entries trigger error notifications while valid data proceeds.

3. ActiveTrail contact lookup

Before creating a new contact, the workflow searches ActiveTrail for existing records matching the email address. This prevents duplicate contacts while ensuring updates reach the right profile.

4. Contact creation/update

Depending on the lookup result, the workflow either creates a new ActiveTrail contact with all mapped fields or updates the existing contact with new information from Pipefy.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to only sync cards from specific pipeline phases, creating automatic segmentation in ActiveTrail based on sales funnel position.

What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your project management in Pipefy and your scheduling in Google Calendar. Every time a new card is created in your Pipefy board, the workflow automatically generates a corresponding event in your Google Calendar with all the relevant details.

For teams juggling multiple projects and deadlines, this eliminates the manual work of transferring due dates and task details between systems. The automation ensures your calendar always reflects your current project commitments, reducing scheduling conflicts and missed deadlines.

How It Works

1. Pipefy triggers the workflow

The workflow activates whenever a new card is created in your specified Pipefy board. Make.com detects the new card and collects all its details including title, description, due date, and custom fields.

2. Data is formatted for Google Calendar

The workflow maps Pipefy card fields to Google Calendar event properties. The card title becomes the event name, the due date becomes the event date/time, and additional details populate the event description.

3. Event is created in Google Calendar

Make.com connects to your Google Calendar account and creates a new event with all the mapped details. You can specify which calendar the events should be added to during setup.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to only create calendar events for cards with specific labels or in certain phases of your Pipefy pipeline.

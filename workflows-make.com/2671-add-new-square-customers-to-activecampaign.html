What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the manual process of transferring customer data between your Square point-of-sale system and ActiveCampaign marketing platform. Every time a new customer makes a purchase through Square, their contact information is automatically added to ActiveCampaign as a new contact.

The workflow ensures your marketing team has immediate access to new customer data, enabling timely follow-up emails, personalized offers, and segmented marketing campaigns. By removing the need for manual data entry, you reduce errors and save hours each week that can be better spent on customer engagement strategies.

How It Works

1. New customer trigger

The workflow monitors your Square account for new customer records. When a customer makes their first purchase, Square creates a customer profile with their contact details and purchase history.

2. Data extraction

Make.com retrieves the new customer's information including name, email address, phone number, and any custom fields you've configured in Square.

3. Contact creation

The workflow maps the Square customer data to ActiveCampaign's contact fields and creates a new contact record. You can configure which Square fields map to which ActiveCampaign properties.

4. Tagging and segmentation

Optionally, the workflow can apply specific tags or add the contact to segments based on their Square purchase data, enabling targeted marketing campaigns.

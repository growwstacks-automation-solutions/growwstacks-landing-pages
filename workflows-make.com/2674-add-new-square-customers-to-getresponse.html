What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common problem for businesses using Square for payments: manually transferring customer information to email marketing platforms. When customers make purchases through Square, their contact information sits isolated in your transaction records unless you manually export and import it to GetResponse.

The workflow automatically detects new customer creations in Square and instantly adds them as contacts in your GetResponse account. This ensures your email marketing list stays current with your actual customer base, enabling timely welcome emails, promotional campaigns, and customer follow-up sequences without any manual intervention.

How It Works

Step 1: Monitor Square for New Customers

The workflow continuously watches your Square account for new customer records. Using Make.com's Square module, it detects whenever a customer is created through your POS system, online store, or appointment bookings.

Step 2: Extract Customer Information

When a new customer is detected, the automation pulls relevant contact information including name, email address, phone number, and any custom fields you've configured in Square. This data is formatted for optimal use in email marketing campaigns.

Step 3: Create GetResponse Contact

The workflow then connects to your GetResponse account and creates a new contact using the customer data from Square. You can configure custom fields, tags, and list assignments based on your marketing segmentation strategy.

Step 4: Trigger Follow-up Actions

Optionally, the workflow can trigger additional actions like sending welcome emails, adding customers to specific automation sequences, or updating other systems with the new customer information.

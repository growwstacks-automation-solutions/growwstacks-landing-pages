What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually tracking new music releases from SoundCloud artists. For music professionals, journalists, or superfans who follow multiple artists, staying on top of every new upload can be time-consuming and disorganized.

The workflow automatically detects when artists you follow release new tracks on SoundCloud and creates detailed notes in your Evernote account. Each note contains the track details, links, and any additional information you want to capture - creating a searchable archive of music releases.

How It Works

1. Monitoring SoundCloud for new tracks

The workflow continuously checks your followed artists on SoundCloud for new uploads. You can configure how frequently it checks (from every 15 minutes to daily).

2. Extracting track information

When a new track is detected, the system pulls key details like title, artist, description, genre, release date, and the direct SoundCloud URL.

3. Creating Evernote notes

The workflow formats this information into a structured note in your specified Evernote notebook. You can customize the note template to include only the fields you need.

4. Adding optional annotations

The system can append your predefined tags, ratings, or comments to each note, making your music catalog more useful for future reference.

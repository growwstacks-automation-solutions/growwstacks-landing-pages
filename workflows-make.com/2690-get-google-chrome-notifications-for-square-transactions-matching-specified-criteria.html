What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of missing important Square transactions in your busy workflow. Instead of constantly checking your Square dashboard or relying on generic email notifications, this system delivers instant Chrome browser alerts only for transactions that meet your specific criteria.

Retail stores, service businesses, and ecommerce operations can configure custom filters to receive notifications for high-value purchases, specific products, VIP customers, or suspicious activity. The alerts appear directly in your browser, ensuring immediate visibility without email clutter.

How It Works

1. Monitor New Square Transactions

The workflow continuously checks your Square account for new transactions using Make.com's Square integration. It runs in the background without manual intervention.

2. Apply Your Custom Filters

Each new transaction is evaluated against your predefined rules. You can filter by amount, customer, product SKU, location, payment method, or any other Square transaction field.

3. Trigger Chrome Notifications

When a transaction matches your criteria, Make.com sends a customized notification through the Chrome browser notification API. The alert appears instantly with your specified details.

Pro tip: Combine this with other automations to automatically create follow-up tasks in your CRM or accounting system when important transactions occur.

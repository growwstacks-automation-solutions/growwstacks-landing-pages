What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of missing important emails buried in crowded inboxes. It monitors your Zoho Mail account for new messages in specified folders and instantly forwards them to Telegram as notifications.

Business professionals waste an average of 2.1 hours daily managing email overload. This workflow eliminates the need to constantly check your inbox while ensuring urgent messages get immediate attention through Telegram's push notifications.

How It Works

1. Zoho Mail Trigger

The workflow starts when a new email arrives in your selected Zoho Mail folder. Make.com detects this event instantly through Zoho's API.

2. Email Content Extraction

Key details like sender name, email subject, and a message snippet are extracted from the email. You can customize which information gets included.

3. Telegram Message Formatting

The email details are formatted into a clean Telegram message with proper spacing, emojis for visual scanning, and clickable links where applicable.

4. Instant Delivery

The formatted message is sent to your specified Telegram chat or channel, appearing as a notification on all your devices within seconds.

Pro tip: Create a dedicated Telegram channel for email alerts to keep them separate from personal chats and enable message history search.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of important emails getting buried in busy inboxes or requiring manual forwarding to team members. By connecting Zoho Mail to Slack, you ensure time-sensitive messages receive immediate attention right where your team collaborates.

The workflow monitors specified Zoho Mail folders and instantly delivers new email notifications to designated Slack channels. Each notification includes key details like sender, subject line, and priority indicators—allowing your team to triage and respond without switching between applications.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New email detection

The workflow continuously monitors your specified Zoho Mail folder using Make.com's polling mechanism. When a new email arrives that matches your criteria, it triggers the automation sequence.

2. Email content processing

Make.com extracts relevant email data including sender information, subject line, received timestamp, and optionally the first few lines of the message body or important headers.

3. Slack message formatting

The system formats a clean, actionable Slack message with appropriate emoji indicators for priority, clickable links back to the original email, and structured fields for easy scanning.

4. Channel delivery

The formatted notification gets posted to your pre-configured Slack channel, complete with @mentions for relevant team members when specific conditions are met (like high-priority flags).

Pro tip: Set up separate workflows for different email types—route customer support emails to your #support channel while forwarding sales inquiries to #leads.

What You'll Need

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Connect your Zoho Mail account in Make.com using OAuth
  2. Specify which folders to monitor and set up any filtering rules
  3. Connect your Slack workspace and select target channels
  4. Customize the message template with relevant email fields
  5. Test with sample emails and refine formatting as needed
  6. Activate the workflow and monitor initial notifications

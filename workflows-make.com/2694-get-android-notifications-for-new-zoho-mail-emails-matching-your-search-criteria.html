What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of missing important emails in your Zoho Mail inbox when you're away from your desk. Instead of constantly checking your email or receiving notifications for every message, this workflow only alerts you when emails matching your specific search criteria arrive.

The system monitors your Zoho Mail account in real-time, using the same powerful search operators available in Zoho's interface. When a matching email is detected, it instantly sends a customized push notification to your Android device through Make.com's notification system. This ensures you're immediately aware of critical communications without notification overload.

How It Works

1. Email Monitoring

The workflow continuously checks your Zoho Mail account for new messages using Make.com's Zoho Mail integration. It applies your predefined search filters to identify only the emails you care about.

2. Filter Processing

When a new email arrives, the system evaluates it against your search criteria (like specific senders, keywords, or labels). Only emails that match all conditions proceed to the next step.

3. Notification Trigger

For each matching email, the workflow extracts key details (sender, subject, priority flags) and formats them into a concise notification payload optimized for mobile viewing.

4. Android Delivery

Make.com sends the notification to your Android device through Firebase Cloud Messaging, appearing in your notification shade just like other app alerts. Tapping the notification can optionally open the email in your Zoho Mail app.

Pro tip: Combine this with Zoho Mail's filtering rules to pre-sort incoming emails before they reach this workflow, reducing processing time and improving accuracy.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of customer inquiries getting lost in email inboxes. When a new email arrives in your specified Zoho Mail folder, the workflow automatically creates a corresponding card in your Pipefy board with all relevant details.

For support teams, this means every customer email becomes a trackable ticket with full context. The automation captures sender information, email content, attachments, and timestamps - transforming unstructured emails into organized Pipefy cards that follow your established workflow.

How It Works

1. Email Monitoring

The workflow continuously monitors your specified Zoho Mail folder for new incoming messages.

2. Data Extraction

When a new email arrives, the system extracts key details including sender, subject, body content, and any attachments.

3. Card Creation

Using Pipefy's API, the workflow creates a new card in your designated board with all extracted email information mapped to the appropriate fields.

4. Attachment Handling

Any files attached to the email are automatically uploaded and attached to the newly created Pipefy card.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of important emails getting lost in crowded inboxes. When critical requests or action items arrive via email, they often require manual conversion into tasks or projects in your task management system.

The workflow automatically monitors specified Zoho Mail folders for new emails and creates corresponding Trello cards with all relevant details. This ensures no important request falls through the cracks while eliminating tedious manual data entry between systems.

How It Works

1. Email Monitoring

The workflow continuously checks your designated Zoho Mail folders for new incoming messages based on your specified criteria (specific labels, senders, or keywords).

2. Data Extraction

When a new email arrives, the system extracts key information including the subject line, sender details, email body content, and any attachments.

3. Card Creation

The extracted email data gets formatted into a new Trello card in your specified board and list. You can customize which email fields map to which Trello card fields.

Pro tip: Set up separate workflows for different email folders to automatically sort cards into different Trello lists based on email source.

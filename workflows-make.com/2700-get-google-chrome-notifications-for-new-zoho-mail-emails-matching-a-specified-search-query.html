What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of missing critical emails buried in overflowing inboxes. By connecting Zoho Mail with Chrome's notification system, it creates a real-time alert system for messages that truly matter to your business.

Every time a new email arrives in your Zoho Mail account matching your predefined search criteria (like specific senders, keywords, or labels), the system instantly pushes a notification to your Chrome browser. This eliminates constant inbox checking while ensuring you never miss high-priority communications.

How It Works

1. Zoho Mail Monitoring

The workflow continuously checks your Zoho Mail account for new messages using the Zoho Mail API. You define the search parameters that determine which emails should trigger notifications.

2. Filter Matching

When a new email arrives, the system compares it against your configured filters. These can include sender addresses, subject keywords, labels, importance flags, or attachment presence.

3. Notification Delivery

For matching emails, Make.com formats a Chrome notification containing key details like sender name, subject line, and a preview snippet. This appears as a native browser alert even when your email client is closed.

Pro tip: Combine this with other automations to create clickable notifications that open tickets in your helpdesk or log communications in your CRM.

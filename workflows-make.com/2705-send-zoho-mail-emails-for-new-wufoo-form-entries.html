What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of delayed follow-up after form submissions. When potential customers fill out your Wufoo form, they expect a prompt response. Manually checking for new submissions and sending emails wastes valuable time and risks missing opportunities.

The workflow automatically detects each new Wufoo form entry and sends a customized email through Zoho Mail. You can include form responses in the email, route notifications to different team members, or trigger multi-step follow-up sequences—all without lifting a finger.

How It Works

1. New form submission triggers the workflow

Make.com constantly monitors your Wufoo form for new entries. The moment someone submits the form, the workflow springs into action.

2. Form data is processed

The system extracts all submitted information—names, email addresses, responses to questions—and prepares it for use in your email template.

3. Custom email is generated

Using Zoho Mail's API, the workflow creates a personalized email that can include specific form responses, attachments, or conditional content based on submission details.

4. Notification is delivered

The completed email sends immediately to your designated recipients through Zoho Mail, ensuring your team knows about the submission within seconds.

Pro tip: Add a second email step to send an auto-response to the form submitter with confirmation details or next steps.

What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates manual email responses when customers submit forms on your website. Every time someone completes your Cognito Form, Make instantly triggers a personalized email through your Zoho Mail account.

The workflow captures all form submission data and uses it to create professional, customized email responses. This ensures timely communication with leads or customers while saving your team hours of repetitive work.

How It Works

Step 1: New form submission detection

Make continuously monitors your Cognito Forms account for new entries. The moment someone submits your form, the workflow activates.

Step 2: Data extraction and processing

The system pulls all submitted form fields - names, email addresses, responses, and any attachments. This data gets formatted for use in your email template.

Step 3: Email composition and sending

Using your pre-designed Zoho Mail template, the workflow inserts the form responses into appropriate fields and sends the email to your specified recipients.

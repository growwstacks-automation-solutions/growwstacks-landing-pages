What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common business challenge: manually sending email responses when form submissions come in. Every time someone fills out your Jotform, the workflow automatically sends a customized email through your Zoho Mail account.

The system eliminates repetitive administrative work while ensuring prompt, professional communication with your customers, leads, or event attendees. You can customize the email content to include specific form responses, attachments, or conditional messaging based on the submission data.

How It Works

1. New Jotform submission detected

The workflow constantly monitors your Jotform for new entries. When someone submits your form, Make.com instantly detects the new data.

2. Form data processed

The system extracts all relevant information from the form submission, including text responses, file uploads, and metadata like submission time.

3. Email composed in Zoho Mail

Using your predefined email template, the workflow dynamically inserts form responses into appropriate fields. You can personalize subject lines, body content, and attachments.

4. Automated email delivery

The completed email sends through your Zoho Mail account with full tracking and delivery confirmation. Failed attempts automatically retry according to your settings.

Pro tip: Use conditional logic to send different email templates based on specific form responses. For example, send a sales follow-up for product inquiries but a support response for technical questions.

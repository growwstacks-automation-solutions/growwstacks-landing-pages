What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the manual process of sending email acknowledgments for Google Forms submissions. Every time someone completes your form—whether it's a customer inquiry, event registration, or feedback survey—the system automatically sends a professional email through your Zoho Mail account.

The workflow ensures no submission goes unanswered while saving your team hours of repetitive work. You can customize email content to include specific form responses, attachments, or conditional messaging based on how users answer questions. This creates a personalized experience at scale while maintaining consistent communication standards.

How It Works

1. New form submission triggers the workflow

Make.com constantly monitors your Google Form for new responses. The moment someone submits the form, the automation begins processing their answers.

2. Data is prepared for email creation

The system extracts relevant information from the form response—such as the respondent's name, email address, and specific answers—to personalize the outgoing email.

3. Zoho Mail composes and sends the email

Using your Zoho Mail account, the automation generates a properly formatted email with your branding and sends it to the respondent. You can configure different email templates for different form types or responses.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to send different emails based on how users answer specific questions. For example, send a sales follow-up for product inquiries but a support response for technical questions.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manually transferring new email contacts from Zoho Mail to your AWeber email marketing platform. Every time a new email arrives in a specified Zoho Mail folder, the workflow automatically extracts the sender's contact information and adds them as a subscriber to your AWeber list.

For businesses receiving dozens or hundreds of inquiries daily, this eliminates hours of tedious data entry while ensuring no potential customer falls through the cracks. The integration maintains data accuracy by directly transferring contact details without manual transcription errors.

How It Works

1. New Email Detection

The workflow monitors your specified Zoho Mail folder for new incoming messages. You can choose to watch your inbox, a specific label, or a custom folder where you route certain types of emails.

2. Contact Extraction

When a new email arrives, the automation extracts the sender's name, email address, and any other relevant information from the email headers or body that you want to capture for your mailing list.

3. AWeber Subscriber Addition

The extracted contact information is then formatted according to AWeber's API requirements and added to your specified subscriber list. You can map Zoho Mail fields to corresponding AWeber fields for precise data transfer.

4. Optional Custom Fields

The workflow can be extended to include custom fields like tags, notes, or segmentation criteria based on the source email's content or metadata, helping you better organize your subscribers from the start.

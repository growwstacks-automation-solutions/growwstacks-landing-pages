Add GetResponse Contacts from New Zoho Mail Emails

Automatically grow your email list by adding contacts from incoming Zoho Mail messages to your GetResponse campaigns

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of missed lead capture opportunities when potential customers email your business. Without automation, sales and support teams must manually add each new contact to your email marketing system - a time-consuming process that often gets deprioritized.

The workflow monitors a designated folder in your Zoho Mail account (like "Sales Inquiries" or "Support Tickets"). Whenever a new email arrives, it automatically extracts the sender's contact information and adds them to your specified GetResponse list. This ensures every customer interaction contributes to your marketing funnel without requiring manual data entry.

How It Works

Step 1: Trigger on New Zoho Mail

The workflow activates whenever a new email arrives in your monitored Zoho Mail folder. It captures the sender's name, email address, and any other relevant information from the message headers.

Step 2: Validate Contact Information

Before adding to GetResponse, the system checks for valid email formats and filters out known domains (like noreply@ or mailer-daemon). This maintains list hygiene and prevents invalid entries.

Step 3: Add to GetResponse List

The verified contact information is then added to your designated GetResponse audience list. You can configure custom fields to capture additional data like the original email subject or received date.

Pro tip: Set up a separate GetResponse list for contacts from different Zoho folders (like "Sales" vs "Support") to enable targeted follow-up campaigns.

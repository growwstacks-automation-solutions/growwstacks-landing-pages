What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manually transferring contact information from email communications to your CRM system. Every time a new email arrives in your specified Zoho Mail folder, the workflow automatically extracts the sender's details and creates a corresponding contact record in ActiveCampaign.

Businesses using this automation eliminate hours of repetitive data entry while ensuring no potential leads fall through the cracks. The workflow maintains data consistency between your email communications and CRM system, providing a seamless experience for both your sales team and customers.

How It Works

1. Trigger on new Zoho Mail emails

The workflow monitors a specific folder in your Zoho Mail account for new incoming messages. You can configure it to watch your inbox, a dedicated "Contact Form" folder, or any other designated location.

2. Extract sender information

For each new email, the workflow parses the sender's name, email address, and other available metadata. It can also extract relevant information from the email body if needed.

3. Create ActiveCampaign contact

The extracted information is formatted according to ActiveCampaign's API requirements and added as a new contact. The workflow can be configured to add tags, assign to lists, or set custom field values.

4. Error handling and logging

The workflow includes mechanisms to handle API errors, duplicate contacts, and other potential issues. Failed operations can trigger notifications or be retried automatically.

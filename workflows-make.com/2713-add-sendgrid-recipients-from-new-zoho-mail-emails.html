What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manually transferring new email contacts from your Zoho Mail inbox to your SendGrid marketing lists. Every time a new email arrives in a specified Zoho Mail folder, the workflow automatically extracts the sender's contact information and adds them as a recipient in your SendGrid account.

The solution eliminates hours of repetitive data entry while ensuring your marketing lists stay current. It's particularly valuable for businesses that receive lead inquiries or customer communications via email but run campaigns through SendGrid's more advanced marketing tools.

How It Works

1. New Email Trigger

The workflow monitors a designated folder in your Zoho Mail account for new incoming messages. You can specify which folder to watch (e.g., "Leads" or "Inquiries").

2. Contact Extraction

Make.com parses each new email to extract the sender's name and email address, along with any other relevant contact information from the message headers or signature.

3. SendGrid Integration

The extracted contact details are then automatically added to your specified SendGrid contact list. The workflow can be configured to include custom fields or tags based on the email content or source.

What This Workflow Does

This automation monitors your Square transactions for refunds that exceed a specified dollar amount you define. When such a refund occurs, the system automatically sends a detailed alert email through Zoho Mail to your designated recipients. This helps businesses maintain financial oversight and quickly address potentially problematic transactions.

The workflow solves the challenge of manually monitoring refunds, which can be time-consuming and prone to oversight. By automating alerts for significant refund amounts, you can promptly investigate potential fraud cases, customer satisfaction issues, or accounting discrepancies without constantly checking your Square dashboard.

How It Works

1. Square Refund Monitoring

The workflow continuously checks your Square account for new refund transactions. It compares each refund amount against your predefined threshold to determine if it requires an alert.

2. Threshold Evaluation

When a refund is detected, the system evaluates whether the amount exceeds your configured limit. This threshold is completely customizable based on your business needs and risk tolerance.

3. Alert Generation

For qualifying refunds, the automation compiles relevant transaction details including amount, date, customer information, and any available refund reason into a structured email format.

4. Zoho Mail Delivery

The system then sends this alert through your Zoho Mail account to your specified recipients, ensuring timely notification of significant refund activity.

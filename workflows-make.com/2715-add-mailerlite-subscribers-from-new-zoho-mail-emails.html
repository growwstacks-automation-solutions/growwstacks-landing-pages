Automatically sync your Zoho Mail contacts with MailerLite subscriber lists in real-time

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manually transferring email contacts between your business communication platform (Zoho Mail) and your email marketing system (MailerLite). Every time a new email arrives in a specified Zoho Mail folder, the workflow automatically extracts the sender's contact information and adds them as a subscriber to your chosen MailerLite group.

The solution eliminates hours of tedious copy-paste work while ensuring no potential leads fall through the cracks. Marketing teams can focus on crafting campaigns rather than data entry, and sales teams can be confident their outreach contacts are automatically added to nurture sequences.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New Email in Zoho Mail

The workflow monitors a specific folder in your Zoho Mail account for new incoming messages. You can choose to watch your inbox, a "Leads" folder, or any other designated location.

2. Extract Contact Information

For each new email, the system extracts the sender's name and email address. Advanced configurations can also capture phone numbers, company names, or other details from the email signature or body.

3. Format for MailerLite

The contact data is formatted to match MailerLite's subscriber requirements, ensuring proper field mapping and data cleanliness before import.

4. Add to MailerLite Group

The processed contact is automatically added to your specified MailerLite subscriber group, ready to receive your email campaigns and marketing communications.

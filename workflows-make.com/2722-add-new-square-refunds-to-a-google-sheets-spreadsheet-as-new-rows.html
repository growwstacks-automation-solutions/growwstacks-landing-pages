What This Workflow Does

Manual tracking of refunds in Square is time-consuming and prone to errors. This automation solves that by instantly recording every Square refund directly to your designated Google Sheet, creating a complete audit trail for your financial records.

The workflow monitors your Square account for new refunds and automatically adds them as new rows in your spreadsheet with all relevant details - transaction ID, amount, date, reason, and customer information. This eliminates the need for manual data entry and ensures your financial records stay perfectly synchronized with actual refund activity.

How It Works

1. Square Refund Detection

The workflow continuously monitors your Square account through its API, instantly detecting whenever a new refund is processed.

2. Data Extraction

Key details about the refund are extracted, including the transaction amount, date, customer information, and the original payment reference.

3. Google Sheets Update

The workflow adds a new row to your specified Google Sheet with all the refund details in predefined columns, maintaining consistent formatting.

Pro tip: Add conditional formatting in Google Sheets to highlight high-value refunds or frequent refunders for quick visual analysis.

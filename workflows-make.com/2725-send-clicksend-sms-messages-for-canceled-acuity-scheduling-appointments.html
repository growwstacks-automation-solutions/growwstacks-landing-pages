What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of last-minute appointment cancellations that leave service businesses scrambling to fill empty slots. When a client cancels an appointment in Acuity Scheduling, the system automatically sends them a personalized SMS message via ClickSend within minutes.

The immediate notification serves three key purposes: It confirms the cancellation was processed, maintains positive client communication, and provides an easy way to reschedule. For your business, this means fewer lost revenue hours and higher staff utilization without any manual follow-up work.

How It Works

1. Acuity Scheduling detects cancellation

The workflow triggers the moment an appointment is canceled in your Acuity Scheduling calendar. Make.com monitors your Acuity account in real-time through its API connection.

2. Client data is retrieved

The system pulls all relevant appointment details including client name, phone number, original appointment time, service booked, and any custom fields you've set up in Acuity.

3. SMS message is composed

Using template placeholders, the workflow generates a personalized message that includes the cancellation confirmation and optional rescheduling instructions. You control the exact message format.

4. ClickSend delivers the SMS

The composed message is instantly sent to the client's mobile phone via ClickSend's reliable SMS gateway. Delivery receipts are logged for your records.

Pro tip: Add a rescheduling link to your SMS template. Clients who receive a direct booking link are 3x more likely to immediately rebook compared to generic instructions.

What This Workflow Does

This automation monitors your Square transactions and instantly sends SMS alerts via ClickSend whenever a refund exceeds your predefined amount threshold. Designed for retail and ecommerce businesses, it provides real-time visibility into significant refund activity that could indicate customer disputes, fraudulent transactions, or operational issues.

By connecting Square's payment data with ClickSend's messaging platform, you eliminate the delay between when refunds occur and when you become aware of them. The system captures essential details like customer information, refund amount, and transaction timestamp - allowing for immediate investigation and response when needed.

How It Works

1. Square Refund Monitoring

The workflow continuously checks your Square account for new refund transactions through Make.com's automated polling system. It verifies each refund against your configured amount threshold.

2. Threshold Verification

When a refund is detected, the automation compares its amount to your predefined limit (e.g., $200). Only refunds exceeding this value trigger the alert process to avoid notification overload.

3. SMS Alert Generation

Approved refunds automatically generate an SMS message through ClickSend containing key details: customer name, refund amount, original transaction ID, and timestamp. The message is formatted for quick mobile reading.

4. Delivery Confirmation

ClickSend delivers the SMS to your specified mobile number(s) and provides delivery status confirmation back to the workflow for logging purposes.

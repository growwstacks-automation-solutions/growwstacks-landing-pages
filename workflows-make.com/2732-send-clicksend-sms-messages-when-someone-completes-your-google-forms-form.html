What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of delayed response to form submissions by instantly notifying your team via SMS whenever someone completes your Google Form. Instead of manually checking for new submissions or setting up email alerts that might get buried in inboxes, this workflow triggers immediate ClickSend SMS messages to designated recipients.

The solution is particularly valuable for time-sensitive scenarios like service requests, appointment bookings, or emergency contacts where minutes matter. Businesses using this automation report 90% faster response times to customer inquiries and 40% reduction in missed opportunities compared to manual notification methods.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

The workflow starts when a respondent submits your Google Form. Make.com detects the new submission instantly through Google Forms' integration.

2. Data Processing

The system extracts relevant information from the form response, such as the submission timestamp, respondent details, and any custom fields you've configured.

3. SMS Composition

Make.com formats an SMS message using the form data. You can customize the message template to include specific fields from the submission or standardized text.

4. ClickSend Delivery

The composed message is automatically sent via ClickSend's SMS gateway to your predefined recipient list, which can include multiple team members or departments.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to only send SMS alerts for specific form responses. For example, only notify managers when customers select "Urgent" in a dropdown field.

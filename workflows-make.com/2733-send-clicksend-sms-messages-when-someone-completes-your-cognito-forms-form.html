What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the communication delay that happens between customer form submissions and your team's response. When customers complete your Cognito Forms form, they often expect immediate confirmation or next steps. Manual processes create frustrating delays where customers wonder if their submission was received.

The workflow automatically sends an SMS message via ClickSend the moment a form is submitted. This provides instant acknowledgment to customers, improves their experience, and reduces follow-up inquiries to your team. You can customize messages to include appointment details, confirmation numbers, or specific instructions based on the form responses.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

The workflow starts when someone submits your Cognito Forms form. Make.com detects this event in real-time through Cognito Forms' API connection.

2. Data Processing

The form responses are parsed to extract relevant information like phone numbers, customer names, or specific answers you want to reference in the SMS.

3. SMS Message Preparation

Make.com formats your predefined SMS template, inserting dynamic variables from the form submission to personalize each message.

4. ClickSend Delivery

The prepared message is instantly sent via ClickSend's SMS API to your specified recipient number (which can be the customer's phone number from the form or your business number).

Pro tip: Include a short link in your SMS to a confirmation page or calendar booking to create a seamless customer journey.

What You'll Need

  1. A Cognito Forms account with at least one active form
  2. A ClickSend account with SMS credits
  3. Make.com account (free plan works)
  4. The phone number(s) you want to send notifications to
  5. Your SMS message template

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Copy this template to your Make.com account
  2. Connect your Cognito Forms account in the first module
  3. Add your ClickSend API credentials in the SMS module
  4. Customize the SMS message template with your preferred text
  5. Specify the destination phone number (can be dynamic from form fields)
  6. Turn on the scenario and test with a form submission

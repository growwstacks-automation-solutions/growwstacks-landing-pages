What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of manually transferring new contacts from Google Contacts to ClickSend's SMS marketing platform. Businesses often waste hours copying contact details between systems, risking errors and delays in communication.

The workflow automatically detects when a new contact is added in Google Contacts and immediately adds it to your ClickSend account. This ensures your SMS marketing lists are always current without any manual intervention, improving campaign effectiveness and saving valuable time.

How It Works

1. New Contact Detection

Make.com monitors your Google Contacts for new additions through regular API checks. When a new contact appears, the workflow triggers automatically.

2. Contact Data Extraction

The workflow extracts relevant contact details including name, phone number, email address, and any custom fields you've configured to transfer.

3. ClickSend Integration

Extracted contact information is formatted according to ClickSend's API requirements and securely transmitted to your ClickSend account.

4. List Assignment

The new contact is added to your specified ClickSend contact list(s), ready for inclusion in your SMS marketing campaigns.

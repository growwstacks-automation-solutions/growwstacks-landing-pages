What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point for businesses using ClickSend for SMS marketing while managing contacts in Google. Manually exporting and importing contacts wastes valuable time and introduces errors. The workflow automatically:

  • Pulls contact data from your Google Contacts
  • Formats it for ClickSend's requirements
  • Creates or updates contacts in your ClickSend account
  • Optionally adds tags or groups based on Google labels

By automating this process, you ensure your SMS campaigns always target your most current contact list without manual intervention. Marketing teams report saving 2-3 hours per campaign setup while reducing bounced messages from outdated contact info.

How It Works

1. Trigger Setup

The workflow starts either on a schedule (daily/weekly) or when new contacts are added to specific Google groups. You configure the trigger based on your contact update frequency.

2. Contact Retrieval

Make.com authenticates with your Google account via OAuth and retrieves contacts matching your criteria. You can specify which contact groups or labels to include.

3. Data Transformation

The workflow maps Google contact fields to ClickSend's expected format, handling phone number standardization and field conversions automatically.

4. ClickSend Integration

Each processed contact gets created or updated in ClickSend via their API. The template includes duplicate checking to prevent creating multiple entries for the same contact.

Pro tip: Add a filter step to only sync contacts with mobile numbers, since ClickSend primarily uses these for SMS campaigns.

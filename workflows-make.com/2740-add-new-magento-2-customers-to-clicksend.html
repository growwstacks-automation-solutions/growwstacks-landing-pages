What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap in ecommerce marketing by instantly connecting your Magento 2 customer database with ClickSend's SMS marketing platform. Every time a new customer registers or makes a purchase in your Magento store, their contact information is automatically added to your ClickSend account.

Without this integration, store owners typically waste hours manually exporting customer lists from Magento and importing them into ClickSend - a process prone to errors and delays. This workflow ensures your SMS marketing campaigns always have the most current customer data without any manual intervention.

How It Works

1. New customer trigger

The workflow monitors your Magento 2 store for new customer registrations through webhooks or API polling. When a new customer is detected, their profile data is immediately captured.

2. Data formatting

Customer information (name, phone number, email, etc.) is formatted to match ClickSend's contact requirements. You can customize which fields to include based on your SMS marketing strategy.

3. ClickSend integration

The formatted customer data is securely transmitted to your ClickSend account via API, where it's added to your specified contact list or group.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to add tags in ClickSend based on customer attributes from Magento (like first purchase date or location) for more targeted SMS campaigns.

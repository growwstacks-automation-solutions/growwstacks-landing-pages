What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of manually transferring customer data between Shopify and ClickSend. When running SMS marketing campaigns, having up-to-date customer contact information is crucial, but maintaining synchronized lists across platforms often requires tedious spreadsheet exports and imports.

The workflow automatically creates new ClickSend contacts whenever a customer completes a purchase in Shopify. It captures essential details like phone numbers, email addresses, and order history, ensuring your SMS marketing campaigns can target the right customers with personalized messages based on their purchase behavior.

How It Works

1. New customer trigger

The workflow monitors Shopify for new customer creation events, which occur whenever someone completes their first purchase or creates an account.

2. Data extraction

Key customer information is pulled from Shopify including name, contact details, and order information. The system can be configured to include specific data points relevant to your marketing needs.

3. Contact creation

The extracted data is formatted according to ClickSend's API requirements and a new contact is created in your ClickSend account. The workflow handles all the technical integration details automatically.

Pro tip: Add custom fields in ClickSend to store Shopify-specific data like customer lifetime value or favorite product categories for more targeted messaging.

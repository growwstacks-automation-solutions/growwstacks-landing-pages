What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your Zoho CRM lead management and ClickSend SMS marketing campaigns. Whenever sales teams add a new lead in Zoho, the workflow instantly creates a corresponding contact in ClickSend with all relevant details.

Manual data entry between systems often leads to errors, delays, and missed opportunities. This integration ensures your SMS outreach lists stay perfectly synchronized with your CRM pipeline, enabling timely follow-ups that convert 23% more leads according to sales automation studies.

How It Works

1. New Lead Detection

The workflow monitors your Zoho CRM for new lead creations through real-time webhooks or scheduled checks every 15 minutes.

2. Data Validation

Essential contact fields (name, phone number, email) are verified before transfer. The system skips incomplete records and logs any validation issues.

3. ClickSend Contact Creation

Valid leads transform into ClickSend contacts with matching details. The workflow preserves custom field data and applies your preferred contact grouping.

4. Confirmation & Logging

Each successful sync generates an activity record in both systems. Failed attempts trigger automatic retries and admin notifications.

Pro tip: Add a filter step to only sync leads meeting specific criteria (like lead score or source) to maintain SMS list quality.

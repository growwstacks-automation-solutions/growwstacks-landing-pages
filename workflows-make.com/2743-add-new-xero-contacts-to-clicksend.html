What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the manual data transfer problem between Xero accounting software and ClickSend SMS marketing. Every time you create a new customer in Xero, their contact details automatically appear in your ClickSend account - ready for SMS campaigns, appointment reminders, or payment notifications.

Businesses waste hours each week copying phone numbers between systems. This workflow eliminates that repetitive task while reducing human error. The instant sync means new customers can receive timely SMS communications while their purchase is still fresh.

How It Works

1. New contact detection

Make.com monitors your Xero account for newly created contacts. It checks at regular intervals (typically every 5 minutes) or can use instant webhook triggers for premium accounts.

2. Data mapping

The workflow extracts key fields like name, phone number, and optional custom fields. You configure which Xero contact properties map to which ClickSend list columns.

3. Validation & filtering

Before transferring, the system verifies phone numbers are valid and checks against your exclusion rules (like "Do Not SMS" tags). Invalid or filtered contacts skip the sync process.

4. ClickSend integration

Validated contacts get added to your specified ClickSend contact list. The workflow handles API authentication and formats data to ClickSend's requirements.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point for sales and marketing teams using Pipedrive CRM alongside ClickSend for SMS campaigns. Every time your sales team adds a new contact in Pipedrive, this workflow automatically creates a corresponding contact in ClickSend - complete with name, phone number, and any custom fields you specify.

The integration eliminates the manual, error-prone process of copying contact details between systems. Sales teams can focus on building relationships while marketing gains immediate access to new leads for targeted SMS campaigns. The automation runs 24/7, ensuring no potential customer falls through the cracks due to delayed list updates.

How It Works

1. New Contact Trigger

The workflow monitors your Pipedrive account for new person records being created. It instantly detects additions through Pipedrive's webhook notifications.

2. Data Validation

Each new contact is checked for required fields like a valid mobile number. You can configure additional validation rules based on your business needs.

3. ClickSend Contact Creation

Valid contacts are automatically added to your specified ClickSend contact list with all mapped fields. The template includes field mapping for common attributes.

Pro tip: Add a custom field in ClickSend to track which Pipedrive pipeline the contact came from. This enables targeted SMS campaigns based on sales funnel position.

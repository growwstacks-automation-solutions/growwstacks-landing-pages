What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your ShipStation shipping operations and ClickSend SMS marketing platform. It automatically imports customer contact details from ShipStation orders into your ClickSend contact lists, eliminating the need for manual CSV exports and imports.

For ecommerce businesses using both platforms, this workflow solves the critical challenge of keeping customer communication channels synchronized. When new orders come into ShipStation, the workflow ensures those customers are immediately available in ClickSend for shipping notifications, post-purchase follow-ups, and promotional SMS campaigns.

How It Works

1. ShipStation Order Trigger

The workflow monitors your ShipStation account for new orders or order status changes, depending on your configuration.

2. Customer Data Extraction

Key customer information is extracted from the ShipStation order, including name, phone number, email, and relevant order details.

3. Data Formatting

The workflow formats the data to match ClickSend's contact import requirements, handling phone number normalization and field mapping.

4. ClickSend Contact Creation

The processed customer data is sent to ClickSend where new contacts are created or existing ones are updated in your specified contact list.

5. Error Handling

The workflow includes error checking to ensure data quality and provides notifications if any records fail to process correctly.

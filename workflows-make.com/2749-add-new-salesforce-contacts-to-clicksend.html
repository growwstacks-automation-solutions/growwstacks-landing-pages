What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap between CRM and marketing systems. When sales teams add new contacts in Salesforce, marketing teams often don't get immediate access to these leads for campaigns. Manual exports and imports waste time and create errors.

The workflow automatically detects new Salesforce contacts and syncs them to ClickSend with all relevant details (name, email, phone, company, etc.). This ensures your SMS and email marketing campaigns can engage new leads within minutes of capture, significantly improving conversion rates.

How It Works

1. New Contact Detection

The workflow monitors Salesforce for newly created contact records using Make.com's instant trigger. No polling delays - new contacts are processed as soon as they appear.

2. Data Mapping

Key contact fields are mapped from Salesforce format to ClickSend's expected structure. The template includes standard field mappings that you can customize for your specific Salesforce configuration.

3. ClickSend Integration

The workflow authenticates with your ClickSend account via API and creates a new contact record with all mapped data. You can configure whether to add contacts to specific lists or groups.

Pro tip: Add a custom field in Salesforce to track which contacts have been synced to ClickSend. This creates an audit trail and prevents duplicate sync attempts.

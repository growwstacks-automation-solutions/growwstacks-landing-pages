What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of manually transferring customer data between your eCommerce store and marketing platform. Every time a new customer makes a purchase in WooCommerce, their complete profile - including contact details, order history, and purchase behavior - is automatically added to Autopilot as a new contact or updated if they already exist.

The integration ensures your marketing team has immediate access to fresh customer data without CSV exports, manual imports, or data entry delays. This enables timely, personalized marketing campaigns from the moment a customer makes their first purchase.

How It Works

1. New customer trigger

The workflow monitors your WooCommerce store for new customer registrations or guest checkouts. Each new customer record triggers the automation.

2. Data extraction

Make.com retrieves all available customer data including name, email, phone, billing address, and initial order details from WooCommerce.

3. Contact verification

The system checks Autopilot to see if a contact with the same email already exists, preventing duplicate entries.

4. Contact creation/update

If new, a complete contact profile is created in Autopilot. If existing, their profile is updated with the latest purchase information.

5. List assignment

The contact is automatically added to designated Autopilot lists for segmentation and campaign targeting.

Pro tip: Add custom fields in Autopilot to capture specific WooCommerce data points like lifetime spend, product categories purchased, or subscription status for more advanced segmentation.

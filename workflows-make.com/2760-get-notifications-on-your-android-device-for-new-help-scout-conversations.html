What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for customer support teams using Help Scout - the risk of missing new customer conversations when away from your desk. Traditional email notifications can get buried in inboxes, while Help Scout's mobile app may not always provide reliable push notifications.

The workflow instantly detects when a new conversation is created in your Help Scout mailbox and sends a real-time push notification to your Android device. This ensures you're immediately aware of incoming customer messages, allowing for faster response times and better customer service.

How It Works

1. Help Scout Triggers the Workflow

Whenever a new conversation is created in your connected Help Scout mailbox, Make.com detects this event through Help Scout's API.

2. Notification Details Are Prepared

The workflow extracts key information from the conversation including customer name, subject line, and a message preview to include in your notification.

3. Push Notification is Sent

Make.com delivers the formatted notification directly to your Android device through a secure push notification service.

Pro tip: Configure different notification sounds for different mailboxes or priority customers to help triage incoming messages.

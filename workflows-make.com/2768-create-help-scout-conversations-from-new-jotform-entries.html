What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point for customer-facing teams - manually creating support tickets from form submissions. Every time a customer fills out your Jotform (whether it's a contact form, support request, or feedback survey), this workflow automatically creates a corresponding conversation in Help Scout with all the submitted information.

The system eliminates the risk of form submissions getting lost in email inboxes or requiring manual data entry. It ensures every customer inquiry is properly logged in your help desk system with complete context, enabling faster and more informed responses from your support team.

How It Works

1. Jotform submission trigger

The workflow starts when a new form submission is received in your Jotform account. Make.com detects this event instantly through Jotform's API.

2. Data extraction and formatting

The system extracts all form responses including customer contact information, submission details, and any custom fields you've added to your form.

3. Help Scout ticket creation

A new conversation is created in your Help Scout mailbox with the form responses neatly organized. The customer's email becomes the requester, form content becomes the ticket body, and you can configure custom tags based on form fields.

4. Optional notifications

The workflow can be extended to send confirmation emails to customers or internal alerts to specific team members based on form responses.

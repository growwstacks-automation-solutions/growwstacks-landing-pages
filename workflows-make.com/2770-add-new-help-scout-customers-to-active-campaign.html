What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your customer support and marketing teams by instantly transferring new Help Scout customer profiles to ActiveCampaign. Every time a customer is created in Help Scout (typically when they first contact your support team), the workflow automatically creates a corresponding contact record in ActiveCampaign with all available customer data.

The integration solves the common problem of marketing teams working with outdated or incomplete customer lists. Without automation, support agents must manually notify marketers about new contacts, leading to delays in follow-up sequences and missed engagement opportunities. This workflow ensures your email campaigns can begin nurturing customers immediately after their first support interaction.

How It Works

1. Trigger on new customer creation

The workflow monitors Help Scout's customer API for new profile creations. This typically happens when a customer submits their first support ticket or chat message.

2. Extract customer data

Make retrieves all available customer information from Help Scout, including name, email, company, and any custom fields you've configured in Help Scout's customer profiles.

3. Format for ActiveCampaign

The workflow maps Help Scout's data structure to ActiveCampaign's contact fields, ensuring proper field matching and data normalization between the systems.

4. Create/update ActiveCampaign contact

The processed data creates a new ActiveCampaign contact or updates an existing one if the email already exists in your marketing database.

5. Optional tagging and segmentation

You can configure the workflow to apply specific tags or add contacts to lists in ActiveCampaign based on Help Scout customer properties.

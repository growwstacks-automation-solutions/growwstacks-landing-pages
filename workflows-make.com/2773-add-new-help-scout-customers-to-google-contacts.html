What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common problem for customer-facing teams: manually transferring customer information from your help desk to your contact management system. Every time a new customer is created in Help Scout, this workflow automatically creates a corresponding contact in Google Contacts with all relevant details.

The integration ensures your sales, marketing, and support teams always have access to the same updated customer information. No more copying and pasting emails or missing important customer details because they weren't properly recorded in your CRM.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New customer in Help Scout

The workflow activates whenever a new customer profile is created in your Help Scout account. This could be from a new support ticket, chat conversation, or manual entry by your team.

2. Data extraction

Make retrieves all available customer information from Help Scout, including name, email address, phone number, and any custom fields you've configured in Help Scout.

3. Contact creation in Google

The system maps the Help Scout data to corresponding fields in Google Contacts and creates a new contact record. You can customize which fields transfer and how they're formatted.

4. Error handling

If a contact already exists with the same email address, the workflow can either skip creation or update the existing record based on your configuration.

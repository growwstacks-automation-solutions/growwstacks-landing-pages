What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between Help Scout (your customer support inbox) and Trello (your task management system). Whenever a conversation is assigned to a specific team member in Help Scout, the workflow automatically creates a corresponding card in your designated Trello board.

The solution eliminates manual data transfer between systems, reducing errors and saving valuable time for your support team. Each Trello card contains relevant conversation details, creating a visual workflow that helps teams prioritize and track support tickets more effectively.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New assignment in Help Scout

The workflow monitors Help Scout for conversations assigned to your specified team member. This could be your support lead, technical specialist, or any role that needs task-based follow-up.

2. Data extraction

Key details are pulled from the Help Scout conversation including customer name, subject line, tags, and any custom fields you've configured. The system can also capture conversation excerpts or important notes.

3. Card creation in Trello

The workflow creates a new card in your selected Trello board with all extracted information. You can customize which fields appear on the card and how they're formatted.

Pro tip: Add Trello labels based on Help Scout tags to automatically categorize cards by priority, ticket type, or department.

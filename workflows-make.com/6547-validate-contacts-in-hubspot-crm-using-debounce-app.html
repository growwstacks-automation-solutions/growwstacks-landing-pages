What This Workflow Does

A dirty CRM is a silent revenue killer. Invalid email addresses from web forms, list uploads, or sales entries pollute your database, leading to wasted marketing spend, damaged sender reputation, and sales teams chasing ghosts. This automation solves that problem at the source.

This Make.com workflow acts as a gatekeeper for your HubSpot CRM. Every time a new contact is created, it instantly routes the email address to DeBounce for validation. Based on the result—whether the email is valid, risky, or invalid—the workflow automatically updates the contact record in HubSpot. This ensures your marketing lists are clean, your sales outreach is targeted, and your overall data integrity is maintained without manual intervention.

How It Works

The automation operates on a simple but powerful trigger-action principle, creating a seamless bridge between your CRM and email verification service.

Step 1: New Contact Trigger

The workflow is activated the moment a new contact is added to a specified HubSpot list or created in the CRM. Make.com watches for this event in real-time, capturing the contact's details, primarily the email address.

Step 2: Email Validation with DeBounce

The captured email is sent to the DeBounce API. DeBounce performs a deep check, verifying syntax, domain existence, mailbox availability, and checking for disposable or role-based addresses. It returns a detailed status (e.g., "Valid", "Invalid", "Risky").

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to handle "Risky" emails (like catch-alls) by tagging them for manual review instead of outright deactivation. This can save potentially good leads.

Step 3: Smart CRM Update

Based on DeBounce's response, the workflow takes predefined action in HubSpot. For invalid emails, it can deactivate the contact, add a "Invalid Email" tag, or move it to a separate list. For valid emails, it can add a "Verified" tag, boosting its score for sales prioritization.

Step 4: Notification & Logging (Optional)

You can add modules to notify your team (via Slack or email) when a batch of invalid contacts is caught or to log all validations to a Google Sheet for audit and analysis of data quality trends.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 20 minutes.

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the template into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect HubSpot: In the first module, authorize Make.com to access your HubSpot account and select the trigger (e.g., "Contact Created").
  3. Connect DeBounce: In the validation module, add your DeBounce API key from your account settings.
  4. Configure Actions: Map the DeBounce response (like `result` = "Invalid") to the desired action in HubSpot (e.g., "Update Contact" to set a property like `hs_contact_is_deactivated` to true).
  5. Test & Activate: Run a test with a known invalid email (like `test@example.com`) to see the flow in action. Once confirmed, turn the scenario on.

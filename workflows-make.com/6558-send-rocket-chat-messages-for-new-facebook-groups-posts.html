What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your Facebook community and team collaboration by instantly delivering new Group posts directly to your Rocket.Chat channels. It solves the problem of fragmented communication where community managers must constantly switch between platforms to monitor discussions and respond to members.

By automating post notifications, your team can maintain real-time awareness of community activity without being logged into Facebook. The workflow captures all new posts including text, images, and links, then formats them into clean Rocket.Chat messages with direct links back to the original discussion.

How It Works

1. Facebook Group Monitoring

The workflow continuously checks your specified Facebook Group for new posts using Make.com's Facebook connector. It can monitor both public and private groups where you have admin or moderator access.

2. Content Processing

Each new post is analyzed to extract key elements like the author name, post text, media attachments, and engagement metrics. You can configure filters to only notify about posts containing specific keywords or meeting certain criteria.

3. Rocket.Chat Delivery

The processed post information is formatted into a structured Rocket.Chat message and delivered to your specified channel or direct messages. The notification includes actionable links that let your team jump straight into the Facebook conversation.

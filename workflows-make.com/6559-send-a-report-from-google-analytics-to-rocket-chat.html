What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming manual process of extracting Google Analytics data and sharing it with your team. Marketing teams often waste hours each week compiling reports when they could be analyzing insights and optimizing campaigns.

The workflow automatically pulls key metrics from Google Analytics for the previous day, formats them into an easy-to-read report, and posts them directly to your designated Rocket.Chat channel. This ensures your entire team wakes up to fresh performance data without any manual intervention.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow runs daily at your specified time (typically early morning) using Make.com's scheduler module.

2. Google Analytics Data Fetch

It connects to your Google Analytics account via API and retrieves pre-configured metrics like sessions, bounce rate, conversions, and traffic sources.

3. Report Formatting

The raw data gets transformed into a clean, branded report format with visual elements like progress bars or comparison metrics.

4. Rocket.Chat Delivery

The formatted report gets posted to your specified Rocket.Chat channel, complete with @mentions for key stakeholders.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to highlight significant metric changes (like conversion rate drops over 15%) with alert emojis to draw attention.

What This Workflow Does

For businesses that rely on SMS for marketing, notifications, or customer service, running out of credits is more than an inconvenience—it's a operational risk. Manually logging into your FireText dashboard to check your balance is a repetitive, easily forgotten task that can lead to costly interruptions.

This automation solves that problem completely. It acts as your personal SMS credit watchdog. On a schedule you set (daily by default), it securely connects to your FireText account, retrieves the exact number of remaining credits, and sends that information directly to your Gmail inbox. This turns a manual chore into a reliable, hands-off system that provides proactive visibility into a critical business resource.

Pro tip: Use this alert not just to monitor, but to forecast. If you notice your credits are depleting faster than usual, it's an early signal of increased campaign activity or potential issues worth investigating.

How It Works

The workflow is elegantly simple, built on three core actions in Make.com.

Step 1: Scheduled Trigger

The automation is initiated by a Schedule module. By default, it's set to run every day at 8:00 AM, but you can easily change this to hourly, weekly, or on any custom timetable that fits your SMS usage patterns.

Step 2: Retrieve Live Credit Balance

Immediately after triggering, the scenario uses the FireText module to make an authenticated API call to your account. It executes the "Get Credits" function, which fetches the real-time, accurate number of SMS credits available in your balance.

Step 3: Format & Send Email Alert

The retrieved credit count is then passed seamlessly to a Gmail module. The workflow composes a clear, concise email—you can customize the subject and body—and sends it directly to your specified email address. You'll have your update waiting in your inbox, no login required.

Who This Is For

This template is designed for any business or individual using FireText for SMS communications. It's particularly valuable for marketing teams running SMS campaigns, e-commerce stores sending order updates, service businesses with appointment reminders, or IT departments that send system alerts. Essentially, if SMS is a channel you depend on, and the cost of it failing is high, this automation is for you.

What You'll Need

To clone and run this template, you'll need just a few things set up:

  1. A Make.com account (free tier is sufficient to start).
  2. An active FireText account with SMS credits.
  3. Your FireText API key, which you can generate from your account dashboard.
  4. A Gmail account to receive the alerts (the sender will be your own connected Gmail in Make.com).

Quick Setup Guide

Getting your credit monitor live takes about five minutes.

  1. Clone the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button above. This will copy the scenario into your own Make.com account.
  2. Connect FireText: In the first FireText module, click to add a connection. Enter your FireText API key when prompted.
  3. Connect Gmail: In the Gmail module, authorize Make.com to send emails from your Gmail account (this uses secure OAuth).
  4. Customize the Email: Optionally, edit the email subject and body text in the Gmail module to include your company name or preferred wording.
  5. Set Your Schedule: Open the Schedule module and adjust the run time if 8:00 AM daily isn't ideal. You can set it for multiple times a day or specific days of the week.
  6. Turn It On: Flip the scenario switch to "ON". It will run at the next scheduled time and send your first credit alert.

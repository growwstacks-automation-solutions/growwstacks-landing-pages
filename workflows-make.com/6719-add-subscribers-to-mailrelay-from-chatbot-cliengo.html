What This Workflow Does

Website chatbots are powerful for capturing leads, but that data often gets stuck in a separate system. Manually copying emails from Cliengo into Mailrelay is tedious, error-prone, and leads to missed opportunities. This automation solves that by creating a seamless bridge between conversation and campaign.

Every time a chat session ends on your website via Cliengo ChatBot, this Make.com workflow triggers. It takes the visitor's contact information—typically their email and name—and automatically adds them as a new subscriber to a specified list in your Mailrelay account. This turns real-time engagement into a long-term marketing relationship without a single manual step.

The business value is clear: you grow your email list consistently from your most engaged visitors. These are warm leads who have already shown interest by initiating a chat. By adding them to your email sequences instantly, you increase conversion rates and save your team hours of administrative work each week.

How It Works

The workflow operates on a simple trigger-action principle, orchestrated by Make.com to ensure reliability and speed.

Step 1: Monitor Cliengo for Completed Conversations

Make.com watches your Cliengo account using a scheduled trigger or webhook. When a chat conversation concludes, it captures the event data. This includes the timestamp, the contact details provided by the visitor, and any custom fields you've set up in your chatbot flow.

Step 2: Parse and Format the Lead Data

The workflow's router and data transformation modules extract the essential information. It maps the Cliengo fields (like 'email' and 'firstName') to the corresponding subscriber fields required by Mailrelay's API. This step ensures clean, structured data is ready for transfer.

Step 3: Add Subscriber to Mailrelay

Make.com sends an API call to your Mailrelay account with the formatted lead data. The contact is added to your chosen subscriber list. You can configure options like double opt-in or tagging based on the chat topic, making the integration adaptable to your email marketing strategy.

Pro tip: Use this workflow to segment leads automatically. Route contacts from chats about "pricing" to a "Hot Leads" list in Mailrelay, and those asking for "brochures" to a "Nurture" list for different email sequences.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 15 minutes.

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to open the template in your Make.com account and create a copy.
  2. Connect Cliengo: In the first module, authorize Make.com to access your Cliengo account using your API credentials.
  3. Connect Mailrelay: In the "Add Subscriber" module, authorize the connection to your Mailrelay account.
  4. Configure Mapping: Ensure the data from Cliengo (e.g., 'email') is correctly mapped to the required fields in Mailrelay (e.g., 'email').
  5. Select Your List: Choose the specific Mailrelay list where new subscribers should be added.
  6. Test & Activate: Run a test by having a simulated chat end. Check that a test subscriber appears in your Mailrelay list, then turn the scenario on.

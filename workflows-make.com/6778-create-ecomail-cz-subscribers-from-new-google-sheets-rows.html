What This Workflow Does

Manually copying and pasting email addresses from a spreadsheet into your email marketing platform is a tedious, error-prone task that wastes valuable time. This automation solves that problem by creating a seamless bridge between Google Sheets, where leads are often collected, and Ecomail.cz, your powerful email marketing engine.

Every time a new row is added to your designated Google Sheet—whether from a website form, an event sign-up, or a sales report—this Make.com workflow instantly detects it. It then takes the subscriber's information (like email, name, and other details) and automatically creates a new contact in your specified Ecomail.cz mailing list. This means your marketing campaigns can reach new leads within minutes, not days, dramatically improving engagement and conversion opportunities.

How It Works

The workflow operates on a simple but powerful trigger-action principle, ensuring a hands-off process once set up.

Step 1: Monitor Your Google Sheet

The automation continuously watches a specific Google Sheets spreadsheet for any new rows. You define the sheet and worksheet. The moment a new entry appears, the workflow is triggered, capturing all the data in that row.

Step 2: Process the Subscriber Data

Make.com reads the data from the new row. You map each column (e.g., Column A for email, Column B for first name) to corresponding variables in the automation. This step ensures the right information is sent to the right place.

Pro tip: Structure your Google Sheet with clear column headers (Email, First Name, Last Name, etc.). This makes field mapping during setup intuitive and prevents errors.

Step 3: Create the Ecomail.cz Subscriber

The workflow then uses the Ecomail.cz module to create a new subscriber. It connects to your Ecomail.cz account, selects the target mailing list, and passes the mapped data (email, name, custom fields) to create a fully formed contact, ready for your next email campaign.

What You'll Need

To use this template, you only need a few things set up beforehand:

  2. An active Ecomail.cz account with at least one mailing list created to receive the new subscribers.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 10 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Get the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the template into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Your Apps: Authorize the Google Sheets and Ecomail.cz modules within Make.com by logging into your accounts.
  3. Configure the Trigger: Select your source Google Sheet and the specific worksheet that contains your subscriber list.
  4. Map the Data: In the Ecomail.cz "Create a Subscriber" action, select your target mailing list and map the spreadsheet columns (like email, first name) to the correct Ecomail.cz contact fields.
  5. Test & Activate: Add a test row to your Google Sheet to trigger the workflow once. Verify the subscriber appears correctly in your Ecomail.cz list, then turn the scenario on to run automatically.

What This Workflow Does

For businesses using HubSpot for sales and SALESmanago for marketing, a critical gap often exists: new customer data entered by the sales team doesn't automatically reach the marketing team. This manual disconnect leads to delayed campaigns, missed nurturing opportunities, and inconsistent customer experiences. Valuable leads sit idle in the CRM while marketing operates on outdated lists.

This automated workflow solves that problem permanently. It acts as a real-time bridge between HubSpot CRM and SALESmanago. Every time a sales representative creates a new contact or a lead submits a form that populates HubSpot, this system instantly and silently adds that individual to your designated SALESmanago contact list. It ensures marketing can begin targeted engagement immediately, turning new leads into nurtured prospects without any manual intervention or waiting.

Pro tip: Use this automation to also sync contact property updates (like lead score or lifecycle stage) from HubSpot to SALESmanago. This keeps marketing segmentation hyper-relevant and dynamic.

How It Works

The automation is built on a trigger-and-action principle, orchestrated by Make.com to work seamlessly in the background.

Step 1: Monitoring HubSpot for New Contacts

The workflow continuously watches your HubSpot CRM for a specific event: the creation of a new contact. It does this by polling the HubSpot API at regular, configurable intervals. When it detects a new entry, it immediately captures all the associated data like name, email, company, phone number, and any custom properties you've defined.

Step 2: Processing and Formatting the Data

Make.com's module takes the raw data from HubSpot and prepares it for SALESmanago. This step can include filtering (e.g., only syncing contacts with a valid email), transforming data formats, or enriching the contact with additional information from other connected apps if needed. This ensures clean, usable data enters your marketing platform.

Step 3: Adding the Contact to SALESmanago

The processed contact data is then sent securely to SALESmanago via its API. The workflow calls the "Add Contact" function, creating a new profile in your SALESmanago account. The contact can be automatically tagged, assigned to a specific list, or triggered into an immediate welcome email sequence, all based on the rules you set up during configuration.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this integration live in under 15 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Get the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the ready-made scenario to your Make.com account.
  2. Connect HubSpot: In the first module, authorize Make.com to access your HubSpot account using your API key.
  3. Connect SALESmanago: In the action module, input your SALESmanago API credentials (Client ID, API Secret) to establish the connection.
  4. Map Your Fields: Review the default field mapping (e.g., email to email, first name to first name). Adjust or add mappings for any custom properties crucial to your marketing.
  5. Test & Activate: Run a single test operation by creating a test contact in HubSpot. Verify it appears correctly in SALESmanago. Once confirmed, turn the scenario on. It will now run automatically.

Pro tip: Start by syncing only a specific HubSpot list (like "Marketing Qualified Leads") to avoid importing all historical contacts at once. You can adjust the trigger filter settings for this.

What This Workflow Does

For marketers and business owners running referral or affiliate programs with UpViral, manually transferring leads from sign-up sheets, partner lists, or webinar registrations is a tedious and error-prone bottleneck. This process often delays participant onboarding, dampening the initial excitement and reducing program effectiveness.

This automation solves that problem by creating a seamless bridge between Google Sheets and UpViral. It acts as a dedicated assistant that periodically checks your designated spreadsheet for new rows. Whenever a new lead is added—whether from a form submission, a team member, or an integrated app—the workflow instantly captures their details (like name and email) and creates a corresponding new contact within your UpViral campaign. This ensures your referral program is always populated with fresh, engaged participants without any manual intervention.

The result is a faster, more reliable, and scalable lead onboarding process. You can collect leads through any method that feeds into Google Sheets, confident that they will automatically join your UpViral ecosystem, ready to start sharing and driving growth.

How It Works

The workflow operates on a simple yet powerful trigger-action principle, ensuring no lead slips through the cracks.

Step 1: Scheduled Check on Google Sheets

The automation is initiated by a schedule module, set by default to run every 15 minutes. At each interval, it connects to your specified Google Sheets spreadsheet and scans for rows that have been added since the last check. It intelligently tracks its progress so it only processes genuinely new entries.

Step 2: Data Retrieval and Parsing

For each new row found, the workflow retrieves the cell values from predefined columns. These typically include essential contact information such as email address, first name, last name, and any custom fields you've set up in your sheet that correspond to UpViral contact properties.

Pro tip: Structure your Google Sheet with clear column headers (e.g., "Email", "FirstName", "LastName"). This makes mapping the data to UpViral fields straightforward and prevents errors during the sync.

Step 3: Contact Creation in UpViral

The core action takes place here. The workflow uses the UpViral module, authenticating with your API key. It takes the parsed data from the sheet and sends a "Create a Contact" API request to your specific UpViral campaign. The contact is created instantly with all the provided details, making them an active participant in your program.

Step 4: Error Handling & Logging (Optional Enhancement)

While the base template focuses on the core sync, you can enhance it with error handling. For instance, you can add a router to check if the email field is empty before attempting the API call, preventing failed operations. You can also log successful creations or errors to a separate sheet for auditing.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to configure the template in your Make.com account:

  1. Clone the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button above. This will open the template in Make.com. Press the "Copy" button to create a copy in your own Make workspace.
  2. Connect Google Sheets: In the first module, click to add a connection. Authenticate with your Google account and select the specific spreadsheet and worksheet that contains your lead list.
  3. Connect UpViral: In the UpViral module, click to add a connection. Enter your UpViral API Key when prompted. This securely links your workflow to your UpViral account.
  4. Map Your Data Fields: In the UpViral "Create a Contact" module, you will see fields like "Email", "First Name", etc. Map each of these to the corresponding column from your Google Sheets row. For example, map "Email" to the column in your sheet that holds email addresses.
  5. Set the Schedule (Optional): The template is set to run every 15 minutes. You can adjust this schedule by clicking on the clock module. Change it to hourly, daily, or a custom interval that matches your lead flow.
  6. Test and Turn On: Run the scenario once manually to test with a single row. Check your UpViral campaign to confirm the contact was created successfully. Once verified, toggle the scenario to "ON" to let it run automatically.

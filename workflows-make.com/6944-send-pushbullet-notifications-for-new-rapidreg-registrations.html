Send Pushbullet Notifications for New RapidReg Registrations

Get instant mobile alerts the moment someone registers for your event, course, or webinar—no manual checking required.

What This Workflow Does

If you manage events, courses, or webinars using RapidReg, you know the frustration of constantly refreshing your dashboard to see new sign-ups. This manual process wastes time and delays your response to potential attendees. Missed registrations can mean lost revenue and a poor first impression.

This automation solves that by creating a direct, real-time bridge between your RapidReg account and your mobile device. Every time a new registration comes in, Make.com detects it instantly and sends a push notification directly to your phone via Pushbullet. You’re informed immediately, anywhere, allowing for instant follow-up and giving your registrants a feeling of VIP treatment from the very first moment.

How It Works

The workflow is a simple, powerful sequence that runs automatically in the background.

Step 1: Monitor for New Registrations

The scenario starts with a RapidReg module set to watch for new registrations. It polls your RapidReg account at regular intervals (e.g., every 5 minutes) or uses a webhook for instant triggering, capturing details like the registrant's name, email, event name, and registration date.

Step 2: Format the Notification Message

Make.com then takes the raw registration data and structures it into a clear, actionable message. It can include key details such as "New registration for [Event Name] by [Registrant Name]". This step ensures the alert is informative at a glance.

Step 3: Send the Push Notification

The formatted message is sent to the Pushbullet module. Pushbullet then delivers it as a native push notification to all your connected devices—your phone, tablet, or desktop. You see the alert just like a text message, without needing to open any app.

Pro tip: Customize the notification message to include the most critical info for your team, like the ticket type or a custom question answer. This helps you prioritize follow-ups immediately.

What You'll Need

  1. A Make.com account (free tier available).
  2. A RapidReg account with an active event or registration form.
  3. A Pushbullet account (free plan works) and the app installed on your mobile device.
  4. Your API keys or login credentials for both RapidReg and Pushbullet to connect them to Make.com.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to open the template on Make.com and create a copy in your own account.
  2. Connect RapidReg: In the first module, authenticate your RapidReg account and select the specific event or form you want to monitor.
  3. Connect Pushbullet: In the notification module, authenticate your Pushbullet account. This will link the scenario to your personal notification channel.
  4. Customize the Message: Edit the text module to include the data points you care about most from the registration (e.g., name, email, event title).
  5. Activate & Test: Turn the scenario on and perform a test registration on your RapidReg form. You should receive a push notification on your phone within moments.

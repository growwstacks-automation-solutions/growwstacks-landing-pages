What This Workflow Does

Every minute counts in sales. When a potential customer fills out a form on your Wishpond landing page, they're at their peak interest. The traditional process of having a team member manually check the Wishpond dashboard or wait for a daily email report introduces dangerous delays. Leads go cold, opportunities are missed, and marketing spend is wasted.

This automated workflow solves that by creating a direct, real-time bridge between your lead generation tool (Wishpond) and your team's communication hub (Slack). It acts as a vigilant assistant that constantly monitors for new leads. The moment one is captured, it instantly formats the lead's key details—like name, email, source, and any custom data—into a clear, actionable Slack message posted directly to your designated sales or channel.

The result is a transformed sales response cycle. Your team is empowered to act immediately, engaging leads while their intent is freshest, which dramatically increases conversion rates and improves the overall customer experience from the very first touchpoint.

How It Works

The automation runs on a reliable, scheduled basis to ensure no lead slips through the cracks. Here’s the step-by-step process:

Step 1: Scheduled Trigger

The workflow is initiated by a Schedule module set to run automatically at intervals you define (e.g., every 5, 10, or 15 minutes). This "polling" method is robust and doesn't require complex webhook setup from Wishpond.

Step 2: Fetch New Leads from Wishpond

Once triggered, the automation connects to your Wishpond account via the Make.com Wishpond module. It queries for leads created since the last time it checked. The system intelligently filters only the newest, unprocessed submissions.

Step 3: Format the Lead Data

The raw lead data is then parsed and structured into a readable format. The workflow maps important fields such as Full Name, Email Address, Phone Number, Lead Source (like which campaign or landing page), and any custom answers from your form.

Pro tip: Customize the message format in this step. Add emojis (👤 for name, 📧 for email) for quick scanning, and include a direct link to the lead's profile in your CRM if you have one, making follow-up a single click for your team.

Step 4: Send Notification to Slack

The formatted message is sent to a specified Slack channel via the Slack "Send a Message" module. The notification appears as if from a bot, clearly announcing a new lead with all necessary details, allowing your sales team to see it instantly among their other conversations.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation live in under 15 minutes. Follow these steps after clicking "Get This Workflow":

  1. Clone the Template: In Make.com, click "Use this template." It will create a copy in your own account for you to edit.
  2. Connect Your Apps: In the first Wishpond module, click to add a connection. Follow the prompts to log into your Wishpond account and authorize Make.com. Repeat the process for the Slack module, authorizing it to post to your chosen workspace.
  3. Configure the Schedule: Click the Schedule module. Set the interval (e.g., every 15 minutes) that makes sense for your business. You can make it as frequent as every minute.
  4. Test the Flow: Use the "Run once" button to execute the scenario manually. Submit a test lead on your Wishpond form and watch the notification appear in your Slack channel.
  5. Activate: Once testing is successful, toggle the scenario from "Off" to "On." It will now run automatically on its schedule, forever.

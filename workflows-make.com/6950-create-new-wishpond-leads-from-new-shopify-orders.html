What This Workflow Does

For e-commerce brands, a sale is just the beginning of the customer relationship. The real challenge is capturing that customer's information and seamlessly integrating them into your marketing ecosystem to drive repeat purchases and loyalty. Manually transferring data from Shopify to Wishpond is a tedious, error-prone task that often gets delayed, causing you to miss critical engagement windows right after a purchase.

This automated workflow solves that by acting as a silent bridge between your store and your marketing platform. Every time a new order is placed in Shopify, this system instantly creates a corresponding lead in Wishpond, complete with the customer's details, purchase information, and order value. It transforms your marketing from a reactive, batch-processed effort into a proactive, real-time channel for growth.

How It Works

The automation runs quietly in the background, ensuring your marketing lists are always up-to-date.

Step 1: Monitor for New Shopify Orders

The workflow is triggered on a schedule (e.g., every 15 minutes) to check your Shopify store for any new orders that have been created since the last check. It connects to the Shopify API to retrieve a list of these fresh transactions.

Step 2: Extract and Format Customer Data

For each new order found, the automation extracts key information: the customer's email address, first and last name, order total, purchased items, and the order date. This data is cleaned and structured into a format that Wishpond can accept for creating a new lead.

Step 3: Create a New Lead in Wishpond

The system then sends the formatted customer data to Wishpond via its API, creating a new lead entry. You can configure it to add leads to specific lists or campaigns automatically, such as a "New Customers" list or a "High-Value Purchasers" segment based on order amount.

Pro tip: Use the "Product Title" data from Shopify to add custom fields or tags in Wishpond. This allows you to segment leads by product interest immediately, enabling hyper-targeted follow-up emails about related accessories or restock reminders.

What You'll Need

To set up this integration, you will need the following accounts and access:

  1. A Make.com account (formerly Integromat). A free plan is sufficient to start.
  2. An active Shopify store with admin access to generate API credentials.
  3. A Wishpond account with access to API keys or OAuth for connection.
  4. (Recommended) A specific Wishpond List or Campaign ID where you want new leads to be added.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation live in under 20 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Get the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button above to copy the template to your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Shopify: In the first module, authenticate your Shopify store by entering your store URL and API credentials (found in Shopify Admin under "Apps and sales channels > Develop apps").
  3. Connect Wishpond: In the Wishpond module, authenticate using your Wishpond API key (found in your Wishpond account settings under "Integrations").
  4. Map the Data: Ensure the customer fields from Shopify (email, name, etc.) are correctly mapped to the corresponding lead fields in Wishpond. The template provides a standard mapping.
  5. Test and Activate: Run the scenario once to test with a recent real order. Confirm a lead appears in your designated Wishpond list. Once verified, activate the schedule.

What This Workflow Does

For businesses using Constant Contact for email marketing and Nutshell for CRM, a common challenge arises: new subscribers captured in your email list don't automatically appear in your sales pipeline. Sales teams must manually check Constant Contact, copy new lead details, and enter them into Nutshell. This process is slow, prone to errors, and leads to missed opportunities as leads sit unnoticed.

This automation solves that disconnect. It acts as a real-time bridge between your marketing and sales systems. Whenever a person joins your Constant Contact list—whether from a newsletter signup, a webinar registration, or a lead magnet download—the workflow triggers instantly. It captures the contact's data (name, email, company, etc.) and creates a new "Person" record in your Nutshell CRM. Your sales team now sees fresh leads in their pipeline immediately, ready for outreach.

The result is a seamless lead flow. Marketing efforts directly fuel sales activity without administrative delay. This not only saves hours of manual work each week but also increases conversion rates by enabling faster, more informed follow-up.

How It Works

Step 1: Trigger on New Constant Contact Contact

The workflow starts with a "Watch Contacts" module in Make.com, connected to your Constant Contact account. It monitors your Constant Contact lists for any new contact additions. This trigger is the starting point—the moment a potential lead enters your marketing ecosystem.

Step 2: Map Contact Data

Make.com receives the new contact's information. The workflow then maps this data to the corresponding fields in Nutshell. For example, the Constant Contact "Email Address" field is mapped to Nutshell's "Email" field, "First Name" to "First Name," and so on. You can also include custom fields like "Company" or "Tags."

Pro tip: Use this mapping step to add valuable context. Map Constant Contact "List Name" to a Nutshell "Note" field, so your sales team knows which campaign the lead came from.

Step 3: Create Person in Nutshell

The mapped data is sent to Nutshell via the "Create a Person" module. Make.com authenticates with your Nutshell account and creates a new person record in the specified pipeline or with a default lead status. The record is created with all the transferred details, forming a complete lead profile.

Step 4: Optional Follow-up Actions

After the person is created, you can chain additional actions. For example, you could automatically assign the lead to a sales rep, send an internal notification via Slack or email, or add a task in Nutshell for initial contact. This extends the automation beyond simple data transfer into actionable sales processes.

Quick Setup Guide

1. Clone the Template: Use the "Get This Workflow" button above to open the template on Make.com and clone it to your own account.

2. Connect Your Apps: In the cloned scenario, connect the Constant Contact module to your account by authenticating with your API key. Similarly, connect the Nutshell module.

Export Wealthbox Events to Google Calendar

Automatically sync client meetings and appointments from your Wealthbox CRM to Google Calendar. Eliminate manual entry, prevent double-booking, and keep your schedule unified.

What This Workflow Does

For financial advisors and wealth managers, managing a client calendar across multiple platforms is a constant source of friction. You log a client meeting in Wealthbox, then have to manually recreate it in Google Calendar for your own daily view and reminders. This double-entry is not just tedious—it’s error-prone, leading to missed appointments, double-bookings, and a fragmented view of your day.

This automation workflow solves that by creating a seamless, one-way sync from Wealthbox to Google Calendar. Whenever a new event is created or updated in your Wealthbox CRM, it is automatically exported as a corresponding event in your Google Calendar. This ensures your schedule is always accurate, unified, and accessible from the tool you use most, saving you significant administrative time each week.

The business value is clear: reduced manual data entry, eliminated scheduling conflicts, and more time to focus on high-value client interactions instead of calendar management. It bridges the gap between your client relationship management and your personal productivity system.

How It Works

The workflow runs on a schedule or triggers instantly, acting as a reliable bridge between Wealthbox and Google.

Step 1: Monitor Wealthbox for New or Updated Events

The scenario starts by checking your Wealthbox account for events. It can be set to run every hour, or trigger based on specific conditions, looking for newly created appointments or changes to existing ones.

Step 2: Filter and Format the Event Data

Not every calendar entry needs to be synced. This step allows you to apply filters—perhaps only syncing events tagged as "Client Meeting" or for clients in a specific segment. The workflow then extracts key details: event title, date/time, duration, description, and associated client name.

Step 3: Create or Update the Event in Google Calendar

Using the formatted data, the automation connects to your Google Calendar via a secure API and creates a new event. It maps the Wealthbox details to the appropriate Google Calendar fields, setting the title, time, and adding useful context in the description. If the event already exists and was merely updated, it can modify the existing calendar entry.

Pro tip: Use the event description field in Google Calendar to auto-populate useful info from Wealthbox, like the client's phone number, meeting agenda link, or associated financial plan ID. This turns your calendar into a powerful briefing tool before each call.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the template into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Your Apps: In the Make scenario, authorize the Wealthbox and Google Calendar modules by logging into your accounts and granting necessary permissions.
  3. Configure the Trigger: Set how often the workflow should check for new events (e.g., every 30 minutes) or configure an instant webhook if available.
  4. Set Your Filters: Adjust the filter module to specify which Wealthbox events should be synced based on your criteria (event type, client category, etc.).
  5. Choose Your Calendar: Select the specific Google Calendar (e.g., "Work," "Client Meetings") where new events should be created.
  6. Test and Activate: Run a test by creating a dummy event in Wealthbox. Verify it appears correctly in Google Calendar, then turn the scenario on.

What This Workflow Does

Webinars are a powerhouse for lead generation, but the manual process of transferring registrant data from platforms like Livestorm into your CRM is a notorious time-sink and prone to errors. Sales teams often waste precious hours copying emails, names, and details, delaying follow-up and letting hot leads go cold.

This automation solves that problem completely. It acts as a seamless bridge between Livestorm and Sellsy. The moment a person registers for your webinar, their information is instantly and accurately sent to Sellsy, creating a fully-formed contact record. This ensures your sales pipeline is always populated with fresh, actionable leads without any manual intervention.

The business value is clear: faster lead response times, elimination of administrative busywork for your team, and a guaranteed, error-free flow of prospect data directly into your central sales hub. You stop managing spreadsheets and start managing relationships.

How It Works

The workflow is triggered by a new event in Livestorm and executes a series of steps in Make.com to create a corresponding record in Sellsy.

Step 1: The Trigger – A New Livestorm Registrant

The automation constantly monitors your Livestorm account. As soon as a new registration is confirmed for any of your webinars, the workflow is instantly activated. It captures all available data points from the registration, such as name, email, company, and answers to custom questions.

Step 2: Data Mapping & Preparation

Make.com processes the raw data from Livestorm. This step formats the information to match the expected field structure in Sellsy. For example, it might split a full name into first and last name fields, or ensure company names are properly capitalized.

Pro tip: Use this step to add valuable context. You can automatically tag the contact in Sellsy with the webinar topic name (e.g., "Registrant-Q2-Product-Launch-Webinar") for easy segmentation later.

Step 3: The Action – Create Contact in Sellsy

The formatted data is sent to Sellsy via its API. The workflow instructs Sellsy to create a new contact in your specified account, populating all the mapped fields. A successful creation confirms the lead is now officially in your CRM system.

Step 4: Optional Notification & Logging

After the contact is created, the workflow can send a notification to your sales Slack channel or via email, alerting the team to a new hot lead. It also logs the action, providing an audit trail for every sync.

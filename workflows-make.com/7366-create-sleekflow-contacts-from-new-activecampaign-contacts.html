What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap between marketing and sales operations. When a new contact is added to ActiveCampaign—through a form submission, webinar registration, or any other campaign—your sales team often doesn't have immediate access to that lead in their daily communication tools. They might need to manually export lists, copy data, or wait for periodic updates, causing delays and missed opportunities.

This Make.com workflow bridges that gap instantly. It monitors ActiveCampaign for new contacts and automatically creates a corresponding contact profile in SleekFlow. SleekFlow is a platform designed for conversational sales via WhatsApp, SMS, and social messaging, making it the perfect tool for quick, personal follow-up. By automating this transfer, you ensure that every new lead captured by marketing is immediately available for sales engagement, creating a seamless, real-time handoff.

The result is a unified customer journey where automated email nurturing (via ActiveCampaign) and human-led conversational sales (via SleekFlow) work in perfect harmony. This eliminates manual data entry, reduces human error, and significantly speeds up your sales response time, directly impacting conversion rates.

How It Works

Step 1: Trigger on New ActiveCampaign Contact

The workflow starts with a Make.com module that watches your ActiveCampaign account. It can be set to trigger instantly whenever a new contact is added to a specific list, tagged with a certain label, or created from a particular source. This ensures you control which leads are deemed ready for sales follow-up.

Step 2: Map Contact Data

The automation then extracts the relevant contact information from ActiveCampaign. This typically includes the contact's full name, email address, phone number, and any custom fields like company name, job title, or lead source. The data is formatted and prepared for SleekFlow's API.

Pro tip: Use ActiveCampaign tags to segment leads. You can configure the workflow to only sync contacts tagged as "Hot Lead" or "Qualified," ensuring your sales team focuses on high-priority opportunities.

Step 3: Create Contact in SleekFlow

Make.com sends the mapped data to SleekFlow using its API. A new contact is created within SleekFlow, instantly populating your sales team's contact list. You can also assign the contact to a specific team member or label them based on the source data from ActiveCampaign.

Step 4: Optional Notification & Logging

The workflow can then send a notification—via email, Slack, or within SleekFlow itself—to alert the assigned sales rep that a new lead is ready for outreach. It also logs the successful sync for auditing purposes, ensuring transparency and allowing you to track the flow of leads from marketing to sales.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Clone the Template: Use the "Get This Workflow" button above to open the template on Make.com and clone it to your own account.
  2. Connect Your Apps: In the Make.com scenario, add your ActiveCampaign and SleekFlow accounts by entering your API keys in the respective modules.
  3. Configure the Trigger: Set the ActiveCampaign module to watch the correct list or define the filter (e.g., "Contact created" and "Tag equals 'Sales Lead'").
  4. Map the Data Fields: In the SleekFlow "Create Contact" module, map the incoming ActiveCampaign fields (name, email, phone, etc.) to the corresponding SleekFlow contact fields.
  5. Test the Flow: Create a test contact in ActiveCampaign and run the scenario once to verify a contact appears correctly in SleekFlow.
  6. Activate & Monitor: Turn on the scenario schedule (e.g., instant/real-time) and monitor the operations log for a few days to ensure smooth, error-free syncing.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manually copying information from emails into spreadsheets. It automatically captures incoming emails and stores key details like sender information, subject lines, message content, and timestamps directly into your Google Sheets or Excel spreadsheet.

Businesses use this to track customer inquiries, log support tickets, monitor order confirmations, or maintain any other email-based records that need structured organization. The workflow eliminates hours of manual data entry while ensuring complete, accurate records.

How It Works

Step 1: Email Trigger

The workflow starts when a new email arrives in your connected email account. It can be configured to watch specific folders or filter for particular senders/subjects.

Step 2: Data Extraction

Key information is extracted from each email including metadata (from, to, date) and content (subject, body text). The system can also handle attachments if needed.

Step 3: Spreadsheet Update

The extracted data is formatted and appended as a new row in your designated Google Sheet with timestamps for complete auditability.

Pro tip: Add a "Status" column to your sheet that the workflow populates as "New" for each entry. You can then build additional automations that update this status as emails get processed.

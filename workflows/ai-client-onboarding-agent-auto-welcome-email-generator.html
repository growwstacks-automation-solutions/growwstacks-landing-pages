AI Client Onboarding Agent: Auto Welcome Email Generator

Transform form responses into personalized welcome emails instantly. Automate your client onboarding with AI-powered personalization.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Manual client onboarding is a time-consuming, error-prone process that often delays your ability to deliver value. When a new client submits an onboarding form, someone must manually transfer their information, draft a welcome email, and coordinate next steps—taking valuable hours away from strategic work.

This AI-powered automation solves that problem completely. It captures new client submissions from Google Forms/Sheets, structures their information, generates a personalized welcome email using Gemini AI, and sends it automatically via Gmail. The entire process happens within minutes of form submission, ensuring every client receives immediate, professional communication with a customized onboarding checklist.

The workflow acts as your 24/7 client success assistant, handling the administrative heavy lifting while maintaining a human touch through AI personalization. It eliminates the risk of missed clients, inconsistent messaging, and delayed responses that can damage client relationships before they even begin.

How It Works

1. Trigger: Capture New Client Data

The workflow activates whenever a new row is added to your Google Sheets "Onboarding" spreadsheet. This typically happens when a client submits your onboarding form through Google Forms, but it can connect to any data source that feeds into Sheets.

2. Structure: Prepare Client Information

The raw form data is cleaned and organized into a standardized format. The workflow extracts the client's name, email, company, requested services, and any additional notes. It also prepares a standard onboarding checklist with items like account setup, welcome call scheduling, and document collection.

3. Generate: AI-Personalized Email Creation

Google Gemini AI (using the 2.0 Flash model for speed) receives the structured client data and generates a professional welcome email. The AI incorporates the client's specific details, references their company and services, and inserts the personalized onboarding checklist—creating an email that feels individually crafted.

4. Deliver: Automated Email Dispatch

The generated email is automatically sent to the client's email address via Gmail with a subject line like "Welcome to Our Service, [Client Name]." The email includes the AI-generated body and maintains your professional branding.

5. Monitor: Error Handling & Success Tracking

Built-in error handling ensures any failures are logged and reported. Success notifications confirm when clients have been onboarded, providing complete transparency into the automation's performance.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with workflow execution capabilities
  2. Google Sheets with your client onboarding data or connected Google Form
  3. Google Gemini API key for AI email generation (available through Google AI Studio)
  4. Gmail account with API access enabled for sending automated emails
  5. Basic understanding of your current onboarding process and desired email structure

Pro tip: Before implementing, map out your ideal client journey from initial contact to first delivered value. This workflow handles the initial welcome, but you can extend it to trigger follow-up tasks, schedule meetings, or create project management tickets for a complete automated onboarding system.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance
  2. Configure the Google Sheets trigger to connect to your specific onboarding spreadsheet
  3. Add your Google Gemini API key in the AI generation node settings
  4. Connect your Gmail account and set the "from" email address
  5. Customize the email prompt to match your brand voice and include your specific onboarding checklist items
  6. Test with a sample client entry to ensure the email generation and sending work correctly
  7. Activate the workflow and begin automating your client onboarding immediately

What This Workflow Does

Manual competitor review analysis is tedious, inconsistent, and prone to missed insights. This automation solves that by turning unstructured Google reviews into actionable intelligence. It watches a Google Sheet where you list competitors, scrapes their latest reviews via Dumpling AI, sends the text to GPT-4o for thematic analysis, and returns a clean summary of common complaints and praises directly back to your spreadsheet.

The result is a systematic, repeatable process that gives your marketing, product, and strategy teams a clear view of competitor weaknesses and strengths without spending hours reading reviews. You get structured data—not just raw comments—ready for decision-making.

How It Works

Step 1: Trigger from Google Sheets

The workflow starts when you add a new competitor name or search query to a designated Google Sheet. This triggers the automation, ensuring analysis happens only for new entries you want to track.

Step 2: Scrape Reviews via Dumpling AI

An HTTP Request node calls Dumpling AI's review scraping agent, fetching up to 20 recent Google reviews for the specified business. This bypasses manual browsing and captures fresh customer feedback.

Step 3: Process and Clean Review Data

A SplitOut node separates the review array into individual items. A Code node extracts and combines the review text, removing irrelevant metadata and preparing a clean corpus for AI analysis.

Step 4: AI Summarization with GPT-4o

The cleaned reviews are sent to GPT-4o with a prompt designed to identify recurring themes, categorize sentiment, and highlight specific pain points and positive mentions. The AI generates a concise, structured summary.

Step 5: Save Insights Back to Sheets

The final node writes the AI-generated summary—including key complaints and praises—back into the same Google Sheet, next to the competitor entry. Your insights are now stored in a format ready for reporting or further analysis.

Who This Is For

This workflow is ideal for marketing teams conducting competitive positioning research, product managers looking for feature gaps, SEO specialists understanding local service weaknesses, and small business owners monitoring nearby competitors. It's especially valuable for agencies, consultants, and any team that needs regular competitor intelligence but lacks the time for manual review reading.

What You'll Need

  1. A Google Sheet with at least a "Business" column for competitor names.
  2. A Dumpling AI account and an agent configured for Google review scraping.
  3. An OpenAI API key with GPT-4o access.
  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with connections to Google Sheets, OpenAI, and HTTP Request nodes set up.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the downloaded JSON template into your n8n workspace.
  2. Configure the Google Sheets trigger node with your sheet ID and OAuth credentials.
  3. Update the HTTP Request node with your Dumpling AI agent endpoint and API key.
  4. Add your OpenAI API key to the GPT-4o node.
  5. Test by adding a competitor name to your Google Sheet and activating the workflow.
  6. Schedule the workflow for regular runs (daily/weekly) or keep it trigger-based.

Pro tip: Extend this workflow by adding a Slack or email notification node to alert your team when a competitor receives a surge of negative reviews—enabling real-time competitive response.

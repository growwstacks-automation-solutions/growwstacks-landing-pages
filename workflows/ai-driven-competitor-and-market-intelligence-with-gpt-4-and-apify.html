What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of staying ahead in competitive markets by providing real-time, AI-powered competitor and market intelligence. Small and medium-sized IT companies often struggle with manual tracking of competitor moves, pricing changes, and emerging trends - this workflow automates that process.

The system continuously collects data from multiple sources, processes it through GPT-4 for strategic analysis, and delivers actionable insights directly to your team via automated reports and notifications. It transforms raw competitive data into structured business intelligence that informs decision-making across sales, marketing, product development and executive strategy.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Data Collection

The workflow automatically triggers at your chosen interval (daily/weekly) to gather fresh competitor data from websites, review platforms, and industry news sources.

2. Multi-Source Aggregation

It combines data from web scraping (via Apify), RSS feeds, and other APIs into a unified dataset for analysis.

3. AI-Powered Analysis

GPT-4 processes the aggregated data to identify competitor strengths/weaknesses, pricing changes, new offerings, customer sentiment, and emerging market trends.

4. Automated Reporting

The system generates professional reports in Google Docs with key findings and recommended actions based on the AI analysis.

5. Team Notifications

Relevant stakeholders receive alerts via Slack or email with the most critical insights and a link to the full report.

6. Historical Tracking

All analyzed data gets stored in your database for long-term trend analysis and performance benchmarking.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (self-hosted or cloud)
  2. OpenAI API key (GPT-4 access recommended)
  3. Apify account (or alternative web scraping service)
  4. Google Workspace account (for Docs integration)
  5. Slack workspace (for notifications)
  6. PostgreSQL/MySQL database (for historical data storage)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Configure all credential connections (OpenAI, Apify, Google, Slack, Database)
  3. Set your competitor URLs and data sources in the HTTP Request nodes
  4. Customize the Google Docs report template with your branding
  5. Adjust the scheduling frequency to match your business needs
  6. Test with manual trigger and verify all steps complete successfully
  7. Activate the workflow for automated execution

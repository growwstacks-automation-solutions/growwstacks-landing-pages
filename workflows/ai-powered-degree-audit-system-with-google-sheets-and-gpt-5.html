What This Workflow Does

Manual degree audits are time-consuming, error-prone, and create bottlenecks in student advising offices. Registrars and academic advisors often spend hours comparing student transcripts against program requirements, leading to inconsistent evaluations and delayed graduation approvals.

This AI-powered degree audit system automates the entire process. It reads student data from Google Sheets, uses GPT-5 to intelligently evaluate completed courses against program requirements, and generates detailed audit summaries. The system identifies missing courses, calculates remaining credit requirements, and provides actionable insights for each student—all in seconds rather than hours.

The workflow transforms how educational institutions handle graduation readiness. Instead of manual spreadsheet checking, advisors receive instant, consistent audits that highlight exactly what each student needs to complete their degree. This improves accuracy, reduces administrative workload, and helps students graduate on time.

How It Works

1. Student Data Collection

The workflow begins by reading student information from a Google Sheets spreadsheet. It processes data including Student ID, Name, Program, Year, and Completed Courses. The system is designed to handle various data formats and can be customized to match your institution's specific spreadsheet structure.

2. AI-Powered Course Evaluation

GPT-5 analyzes each student's completed courses against hard-coded program requirements. The AI understands course equivalencies, prerequisite chains, and credit hour requirements. It evaluates general education requirements, major core courses, electives, and upper-division credit needs based on your institution's specific policies.

3. Missing Requirements Analysis

The system generates a detailed breakdown of missing requirements for each student. This includes specific course codes, credit hour deficits, and program-specific obligations. The analysis considers alternative course substitutions and transfer credit policies that your institution recognizes.

4. Summary Generation & Output

Finally, the workflow creates comprehensive audit summaries and writes them back to Google Sheets. Each student receives a clear, actionable report that advisors can use during counseling sessions. The system can also trigger notifications or generate PDF reports for student files.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Connect Google Sheets by creating OAuth2 credentials in n8n and authorizing access to your student data spreadsheet

  4. Customize program requirements in the workflow to match your institution's specific degree programs
  5. Test with sample data to ensure the system correctly evaluates course completion and generates accurate audits
  6. Schedule automation to run daily, weekly, or on-demand based on your advising cycle needs

What This Workflow Does

Manual product research and SEO content creation is a tedious, time‑consuming bottleneck for e‑commerce teams, SaaS marketers, and content creators. You have to analyze competitor listings, identify trending keywords, craft compelling descriptions, and format everything for your platform—often taking hours per product.

This n8n workflow eliminates that manual grind. It automates the entire process: from a single product title input, it fetches real‑time competitor data via Google Custom Search, extracts key insights, uses AI (Google Gemini via LangChain) to generate SEO‑optimized metadata and descriptions, and organizes the output into a Google Sheet for immediate use.

The result? You get search‑ready titles, descriptions, and keywords in seconds—not hours. This automation scales effortlessly across hundreds of products, ensuring consistency and freeing your team to focus on strategy and creative refinement.

How It Works

Step 1: Input Trigger

The workflow starts when you submit a product title via a simple form or manual trigger. This single input kicks off the entire automation chain.

Step 2: Competitor Research

Using the Google Custom Search API, the workflow searches the web for existing listings of similar products. It extracts titles, snippets, and keyword patterns from top competitor pages to understand market language and SEO trends.

Step 3: AI Content Generation

The gathered research data is sent to a language model (Google Gemini) via LangChain. The AI analyzes the competitive landscape and generates two types of content: SEO‑optimized metadata (title, description, keywords) and a marketing‑friendly product description tailored to your audience.

Step 4: Data Parsing & Organization

The AI output is parsed and structured into clear categories: SEO data and product content. This ensures clean, usable outputs that are ready for your CMS, e‑commerce platform, or marketing campaigns.

Step 5: Output to Google Sheets

The final structured content is automatically written to a Google Sheet. This centralizes all generated content for easy review, collaboration, or integration with other tools like Shopify, WordPress, or social media schedulers.

Pro tip: You can extend this workflow to automatically publish the generated content to your CMS or e‑commerce platform, creating a fully automated product launch pipeline.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self‑hosted) to run the workflow.
  2. Google Custom Search API key and search engine ID (CX) for competitor research.
  3. Google Gemini API access (or another LLM like OpenAI) for content generation.
  4. Google Sheets with pre‑defined columns for SEO metadata and product descriptions.
  5. Basic understanding of n8n node configuration (or follow our setup guide below).

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure API credentials: Add your Google Custom Search API key and CX ID to the HTTP Request node. Set up your Gemini/LangChain credentials in the AI node.
  3. Connect Google Sheets: Authorize n8n to access your Google account and specify the target spreadsheet and sheet name.
  4. Test with a product title: Trigger the workflow manually with a sample product title to verify the research → AI → sheet flow works.
  5. Automate the trigger: Replace the manual trigger with a scheduled trigger, webhook, or form submission to run automatically when new products are added.

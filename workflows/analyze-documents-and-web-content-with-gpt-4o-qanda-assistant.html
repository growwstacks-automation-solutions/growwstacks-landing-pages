What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered document analyzer solves the time-consuming problem of manually reading through documents and web content to find specific information. Instead of spending hours searching through PDFs, Word documents, or lengthy web articles, you can ask natural language questions and get precise answers in seconds.

The workflow automatically processes local files or fetches web content, extracts the relevant text, and uses GPT-4o to analyze the material based on your specific questions. It's like having a research assistant who can instantly read and comprehend any document you provide, delivering formatted answers with citations and context.

Whether you're reviewing contracts, analyzing competitor websites, studying research papers, or extracting key points from technical documentation, this automation transforms days of manual work into minutes of automated insight generation.

How It Works

Step 1: Input Document or URL

The workflow accepts either a local file path or a web URL along with your question in a simple format: path_or_url | your_question. For example, /Users/docs/contract.pdf | What are the termination clauses? or https://competitor.com/pricing | What are their enterprise plan features?

Step 2: Content Extraction

For local files, the system reads PDFs, Word documents, text files, Markdown, JSON, and YAML formats. For web URLs, it fetches and cleans the webpage content, removing navigation and ads to focus on the core article or documentation.

Step 3: AI Analysis with GPT-4o

The extracted content is sent to GPT-4o with your specific question. The AI model comprehends the context, identifies relevant sections, and generates a well-structured answer that includes key points, supporting evidence, and actionable insights.

Step 4: Formatted Output

The workflow delivers the answer in a clean, readable format that you can send to Slack, email to team members, save to a database, or integrate into your existing business systems for further processing.

Pro tip: Use gpt-4o-mini instead of gpt-4o for cost savings on routine document analysis—it's 10x cheaper and still delivers excellent results for most business documents.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (self-hosted or cloud)
  2. OpenAI API key with GPT-4o access
  3. Documents in supported formats: PDF, DOCX, TXT, MD, JSON, YAML
  4. Web access for analyzing online content
  5. Basic n8n knowledge for credential setup

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Add OpenAI credentials in n8n's credential management
  3. Connect the credential to the "OpenAI Document Analyzer" node
  4. Activate the workflow and test with a sample document
  5. Customize as needed: Adjust content length limits, add file types, or change the AI model
  6. Integrate with your systems: Connect to Slack, email, or databases for automated distribution

Pro tip: Set up a webhook trigger to automatically analyze documents uploaded to Google Drive, Dropbox, or your internal file system—creating a fully automated document intelligence pipeline.

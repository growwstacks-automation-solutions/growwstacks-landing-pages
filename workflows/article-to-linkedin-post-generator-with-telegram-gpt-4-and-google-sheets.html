What This Workflow Does

Creating consistent, high-quality LinkedIn content is time-consuming. Most professionals struggle to maintain a posting schedule because analyzing articles, extracting insights, and writing engaging posts takes hours each week. This workflow solves that problem by automating the entire content creation pipeline.

When you find an interesting article, you simply send the link to your Telegram bot. The workflow automatically fetches the content, uses AI to summarize key points and extract insights, stores everything in Google Sheets as your personal content library, and generates polished LinkedIn posts whenever you type "generate." This turns casual reading into organized content production.

The system separates content collection from post creation, allowing you to build a repository of analyzed articles during your normal browsing time, then generate posts on-demand when you need to publish. This eliminates the pressure of creating content on posting day and ensures you always have fresh, relevant material.

How It Works

1. Link Collection via Telegram

You send article links to your private Telegram bot while browsing on any device. The workflow detects URLs, validates them, and prepares the content for processing. This mobile-first approach makes content capture frictionless.

2. AI Analysis & Summarization

The article content is sent to an AI model (GPT-4, Claude, or others) which extracts the main arguments, identifies key insights, captures tone and context, and creates a structured summary. This transforms raw articles into actionable content components.

3. Storage in Google Sheets

Each analyzed article becomes a new row in your Google Sheet with fields for date, title, link, summary, insights, and commentary. This creates a searchable content library you can reference anytime.

4. On-Demand Post Generation

When you type "generate" in Telegram, the workflow pulls from your content library, builds a LinkedIn-ready post using AI, applies your preferred tone and style, and delivers the formatted post back to you for review and publishing.

Pro tip: Build your content library during your normal reading time—send links as you find interesting articles. Then generate posts in batches when you have 15 minutes to schedule content for the week.

What You'll Need

  1. A Telegram account and bot token (free from @BotFather)
  2. Access to an AI provider like OpenAI, Anthropic, or similar (API key)
  3. A Google account with Sheets access
  4. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  5. A LinkedIn account for posting (manual copy-paste or API access)

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in under 30 minutes:

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file.
  2. Create your Telegram bot via @BotFather and add the API token to n8n credentials.
  3. Connect your AI provider by adding the API key to n8n and selecting it in the AI nodes.
  4. Set up Google Sheets by authenticating with Google and creating a sheet with the required columns.
  5. Test the flow by sending a link to your bot, then typing "generate" to receive your first LinkedIn post.

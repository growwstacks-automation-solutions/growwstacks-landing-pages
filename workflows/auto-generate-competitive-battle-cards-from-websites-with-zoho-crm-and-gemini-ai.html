What This Workflow Does

Sales teams spend countless hours manually researching competitors before important deals—visiting websites, comparing features, and compiling notes into battle cards. This process is not only time-consuming but also inconsistent across reps.

This automation solves that problem completely. Whenever a new deal is created in Zoho CRM with a competitor's website URL in the description field, the workflow automatically:

  • Scrapes the competitor's website content
  • Feeds it to an AI model (Gemini, OpenAI, or Claude)
  • Generates a structured battle card with pricing, key features, pros/cons, and a battle summary
  • Updates the Zoho CRM deal with this intelligence
  • Sends an email notification to the sales team

The result? Your sales team gets instant, consistent competitive intelligence exactly when they need it—without lifting a finger.

How It Works

Step 1: Trigger on New Zoho CRM Deals

The workflow runs on a schedule (every 5 minutes) and checks Zoho CRM for newly created deals. It looks for deals created since the last run and filters for those containing competitor information.

Step 2: Extract Competitor Information

Using pattern matching, the workflow extracts the competitor name and website URL from the deal description field. It validates that both pieces of information are present before proceeding.

Step 3: Scrape Competitor Website

An HTTP Request node fetches the competitor's webpage HTML content. This provides the raw data that will be analyzed by the AI in the next step.

Step 4: AI-Powered Analysis

The scraped content is sent to an AI model (configurable for Gemini, OpenAI, or Claude) with a custom prompt designed to extract competitive intelligence. The AI generates a structured JSON output containing pricing overview, key features, advantages, disadvantages, and a concise battle summary.

Step 5: Update CRM & Notify Team

The generated battle card is formatted and written back into the Zoho CRM deal description (or custom fields if configured). Simultaneously, a notification email with the battle card summary is sent to the relevant sales team members via Gmail.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. Zoho CRM OAuth2 credentials with read/write access to deals
  3. Gmail OAuth2 credentials for sending notifications
  4. AI API key (Gemini, OpenAI, or Claude—interchangeable)
  5. Deals in Zoho CRM with competitor names and URLs in the description field

Pro tip: Test the workflow with a single deal first to ensure your AI prompt generates the exact battle card format your team prefers before scaling to all deals.

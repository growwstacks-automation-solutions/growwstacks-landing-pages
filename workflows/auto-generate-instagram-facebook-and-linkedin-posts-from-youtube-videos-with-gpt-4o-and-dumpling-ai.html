What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the massive time drain of manually repurposing YouTube content for social media. Content marketers and creators typically spend 2-3 hours per video creating platform-specific posts, resizing content, and brainstorming captions. This workflow eliminates that manual work entirely.

The system automatically finds relevant YouTube videos based on your topics, extracts transcripts, generates unique posts for Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn using GPT-4o, creates matching AI images with Dumpling AI, and saves everything to Google Sheets for scheduling. It turns what was a multi-hour process into a fully automated pipeline that runs in minutes.

How It Works

Step 1: Topic Selection & YouTube Search

The workflow starts by checking your Google Sheets "YouTube Topics" list for unprocessed topics. It uses Dumpling AI to search YouTube for the most relevant and recent videos matching each topic, then sorts them by relevance and engagement metrics.

Step 2: AI-Powered Video Selection

GPT-4o analyzes the search results and selects the single best video based on relevance, recency, and content quality. This ensures you're always working with the most appropriate source material for your social content.

Step 3: Transcript Extraction & Content Generation

The system extracts the full video transcript using Dumpling AI, then GPT-4o creates three distinct posts: a visually-focused Instagram caption, a community-oriented Facebook post, and a professional LinkedIn article-style post. Each is tailored to platform best practices.

Step 4: AI Image Generation

Dumpling AI generates platform-optimized images for each post. Instagram gets square visuals, Facebook gets landscape images, and LinkedIn receives professional graphics—all based on the video content and generated captions.

Step 5: Output & Scheduling Preparation

All generated content—captions, hashtags, and image URLs—are saved back to Google Sheets in your "Social Media Post" tab, ready for review and scheduling through your preferred social media management tool.

What You'll Need

  1. Dumpling AI API key for video search, transcript extraction, and image generation
  2. OpenAI GPT-4 credentials for content creation and optimization
  3. Google Sheets connection with two sheets: "YouTube Topics" (columns: Topics, Searched?) and "Social Media Post" (columns: Platform, Content, Image)
  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow
  5. Basic understanding of your social media brand voice and content guidelines

Pro tip: Before running the workflow at scale, test with 2-3 topics to fine-tune the AI prompts. Adjust the tone instructions in the GPT nodes to match your brand voice—this small customization dramatically improves output quality.

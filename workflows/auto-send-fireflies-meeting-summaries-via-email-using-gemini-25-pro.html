Auto-send FireFlies Meeting Summaries via Email

Automatically process meeting recaps, extract transcripts, generate AI summaries with Gemini 2.5 Pro, and send professional emails—saving hours weekly.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious, time-consuming task of manually processing meeting recordings and distributing actionable summaries. After a meeting ends, Fireflies.ai sends a recap email with the transcript. Instead of someone needing to open that email, read through the transcript, identify key points, and craft a summary email, this workflow handles everything automatically.

The system extracts the meeting transcript from the Fireflies email, uses Google Gemini 2.5 Pro to analyze the conversation, identifies decisions, action items, and discussion highlights, then generates a professionally formatted email that's immediately sent to designated recipients. This ensures stakeholders receive clear, structured meeting intelligence within minutes of the meeting concluding, not hours or days later.

How It Works

1. Trigger on New Meeting Recap Emails

The workflow monitors your Gmail inbox for new emails from Fireflies.ai with "Your meeting recap" in the subject line. When detected, it captures the full email content including the meeting link and transcript access.

2. Extract Meeting Data with AI

An AI-powered information extractor identifies the precise Fireflies meeting URL from the email body. A custom code node then parses the URL to isolate the unique meeting ID needed for API access.

3. Retrieve Full Transcript & Summary

Using the Fireflies.ai API, the workflow fetches the complete meeting transcript along with AI-generated short summaries and overviews that Fireflies creates automatically.

4. Generate Professional Email with Gemini

Google Gemini 2.5 Pro analyzes the transcript and summary data to create a comprehensive meeting summary email. It structures the content with clear sections: meeting purpose, key decisions, action items (with owners), discussion highlights, and next steps.

5. Format and Send Email

The AI-generated markdown content is converted to HTML for better email client compatibility, then sent via Gmail with an appropriate subject line and professional formatting to all specified recipients.

Pro tip: Customize the Gemini prompt to match your company's communication style. You can instruct it to use specific terminology, include/exclude certain sections, or format action items in a way that integrates with your project management tools.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in under 30 minutes:

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance using the import workflow feature.
  2. Configure credentials for Gmail (OAuth2), Fireflies.ai (API key), and Google Gemini (API key) in n8n's credentials management.
  3. Update email filters in the trigger node if your Fireflies recap emails use different subject lines or sender addresses.
  4. Set recipient email in the final Gmail send node to the person or distribution list that should receive summaries.
  5. Test the workflow by sending a sample Fireflies recap to your connected Gmail account and verifying the summary email arrives correctly.
  6. Activate the workflow and let it run automatically—it will check for new recap emails hourly by default.

Pro tip: Start with a test email address as the recipient during setup. Once you confirm the formatting and content meet your standards, update it to the actual distribution list.

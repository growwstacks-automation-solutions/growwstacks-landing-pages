What This Workflow Does

This automation solution connects your Google Calendar appointments with Retell AI's phone call system to automatically remind clients about upcoming appointments. It eliminates manual reminder calls while significantly reducing no-show rates.

The workflow pulls appointment details from Google Calendar, extracts client contact information, and triggers personalized phone call reminders at your preferred timing before each appointment. It handles confirmation responses and can log call outcomes.

Workflow interface showing the connection between Google Calendar and Retell AI

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow runs daily (or at your preferred frequency) to check Google Calendar for upcoming appointments.

2. Appointment Processing

It extracts client name, phone number, appointment time and details from each calendar event.

3. Retell AI Configuration

The workflow configures Retell with your registered phone number and agent ID for making calls.

4. Call Execution

Retell places the phone call at the scheduled time with a natural-sounding AI agent delivering your customized message.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the workflow template
  2. Connect your Google Calendar account
  3. Add your Retell API credentials
  4. Configure your from_number and agent_id
  5. Adjust the scheduler timing as needed
  6. Activate the workflow

Pro tip: Format your Google Calendar event descriptions to include client phone numbers in E.164 format (e.g., +14155552671) for best results.

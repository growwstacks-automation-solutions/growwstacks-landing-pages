What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-intensive challenge of creating and publishing consistent, high-quality video content for social media. Manual video production for platforms like TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram Reels can consume hours per day—from concept brainstorming to editing and posting across multiple channels.

The workflow completely automates this process: it generates unique ASMR-style glass fruit concepts using AI, creates cinematic video prompts, renders actual videos through Google Veo's AI video generation, converts them to proper vertical formats, and publishes them simultaneously to TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram via Postiz. What normally takes 2-3 hours of creative work happens automatically every 8 hours, producing fresh content while you sleep.

How It Works

1. Concept Generation & Duplicate Prevention

The workflow starts by checking a Google Sheet of previously used fruit concepts to ensure no repetition. An AI "Idea Agent" then selects a new, unique glass fruit theme—like "crystalized watermelon slices" or "frosted grape clusters"—that hasn't been used before.

2. Cinematic Prompt Creation

A second AI "Prompt Agent" transforms the simple fruit concept into a detailed, cinematic description optimized for Google Veo's video generation. This includes specific lighting instructions, camera movements, ASMR sound elements, and visual effects that produce professional-quality results.

3. AI Video Generation with Google Veo

The system sends the crafted prompt to Google Veo 3.0 API, which generates the actual video file. This AI model creates realistic, high-quality short videos with proper lighting, textures, and visual appeal that matches ASMR content standards.

4. Format Conversion & Storage

The generated video is automatically converted to 9:16 vertical format via Shotstack's API—optimized for mobile viewing. The final video is then uploaded to Google Cloud Storage for secure hosting and future reference.

5. Multi-Platform Publishing

Finally, the workflow uses Postiz API to publish the video simultaneously to TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram Reels with appropriate captions, hashtags, and scheduling. The entire process from concept to published content happens without any manual intervention.

Pro tip: Schedule this workflow to run during off-hours (like 2 AM) so fresh content is live by morning in your target time zones, maximizing engagement without disrupting your workday.

What You'll Need

  1. Google Cloud Project with Service Account credentials and a Storage Bucket
  2. Access to Google Veo 3.0 API (through Vertex AI or similar endpoint)
  3. Google Sheets document to track used fruit concepts and prevent duplicates
  4. OpenAI API Key configured in n8n for the AI agents
  5. Shotstack Account with API key for video format conversion
  6. Postiz Account with active integrations for TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram

Quick Setup Guide

1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.

2. Configure credentials for Google Cloud, OpenAI, Shotstack, and Postiz in n8n's credential management.

3. Update the Google Sheet URL in the "Check Used Fruits" node with your tracking document.

4. Modify the AI prompt templates if you want different video styles or themes.

5. Test the workflow manually first, then set the Schedule Trigger to run every 8 hours (or your preferred frequency).

6. Monitor the first few runs to ensure videos meet quality standards before full automation.

Pro tip: Start with a private test Instagram account before connecting your main business profiles to verify content quality and formatting.

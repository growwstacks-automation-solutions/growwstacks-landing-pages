What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the massive time drain of manual ASMR content creation. Instead of spending hours brainstorming ideas, filming setups, editing footage, and uploading to multiple platforms, this workflow handles everything from creative generation to publication.

It connects cutting-edge AI video generation (FalAI) with your social media accounts and content tracking systems. The workflow generates unique ASMR concepts, creates detailed sensory prompts, produces high-quality videos, and distributes them across your channels—all while maintaining a searchable archive of everything created.

The system ensures fresh, non-repetitive content by checking previous creations and incorporates best practices for each platform's specifications (vertical formats for TikTok/Reels, optimal lengths, hashtag strategies).

How It Works

1. Brainstorm Unique ASMR Concepts

The workflow starts by generating creative, unused ASMR object ideas like "Glass Kiwi," "Crystal Banana," or "Frozen Watermelon." It checks your Google Sheets archive to avoid duplicates, ensuring every video feels fresh to your audience.

2. Create Detailed Sensory Prompts

Each object idea is transformed into a rich, descriptive prompt optimized for AI video generation. Example: "Imagine a crystal-clear kiwi sliced open slowly, glistening under soft neon lights with water droplets forming and falling in slow motion." These prompts are designed to trigger specific ASMR responses through visual stimuli.

3. Generate Videos with FalAI

The prompts are sent to FalAI's advanced text-to-video API, which creates hyper-realistic ASMR footage. The workflow automatically selects vertical aspect ratios for social platforms, sets appropriate quality parameters, and waits for processing completion.

4. Track Everything in Google Sheets

Every generated video's details—object idea, prompt used, video URL, creation date, and publishing status—are automatically logged in a Google Sheet. This creates a searchable content library and prevents idea repetition.

5. Publish Across Social Platforms

The final videos are uploaded to Blotato (or your preferred media host) and automatically published to TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and other configured platforms with optimized captions, hashtags, and scheduling.

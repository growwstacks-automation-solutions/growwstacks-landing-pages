What This Workflow Does

Manual B2B lead research is time-consuming and inconsistent. Sales teams spend hours researching companies, identifying pain points, and crafting personalized emails—only to reach a fraction of their target accounts. This workflow automates that entire process, turning days of research into minutes of automated intelligence.

The system reads leads from Google Sheets, enriches each contact with detailed company data using Sona's API, analyzes the information with GPT-4 to identify specific pain points and opportunities, then generates highly personalized outreach emails with one-click Gmail compose links. It processes 20-50 leads per hour completely hands-free, ensuring every prospect receives genuinely relevant communication.

Beyond just email generation, the workflow creates a structured research database in your spreadsheet, tracking pain points, key insights, fit scores, and personalization hooks for every lead. This becomes a valuable asset for your sales team, providing instant context for follow-up conversations and helping prioritize outreach based on AI-calculated fit scores.

How It Works

Step 1: Lead Import & Filtering

The workflow begins by reading your prospect list from Google Sheets. It automatically filters out already-processed leads (marked with a status) and prepares fresh contacts for research. This ensures you never waste API credits re-researching the same companies and can easily track your outreach progress.

Step 2: Company Data Enrichment

Each lead is sent to Sona's API for comprehensive company intelligence. The system retrieves industry classification, estimated revenue, employee count, technology stack, social profiles, and company description. A smart matching algorithm ensures accurate data even with imperfect input, falling back to the closest match when necessary.

Step 3: AI-Powered Analysis

GPT-4 analyzes the enriched company data to identify 3-5 specific pain points, 2-3 growth opportunities, and 3-4 personalization hooks. The AI considers industry trends, company size, tech stack implications, and market positioning to generate insights that would take a human researcher 10-15 minutes to uncover manually.

Step 4: Personalized Email Generation

Using the AI-generated insights, the system crafts 120-150 word emails with curiosity-driven subject lines (max 8 words). Each email opens with a relevant personalization hook, references one specific pain point, focuses on tangible outcomes rather than product features, and includes a natural call-to-action. The tone is professional yet conversational, designed to start meaningful dialogues.

Step 5: Data Sync & Loop

All research insights, generated content, and Gmail compose links are written back to your Google Sheets. The workflow marks each lead as "Completed," waits 2 seconds to respect API rate limits, then processes the next prospect. This creates a complete audit trail and makes it easy to review, edit, or send emails directly from your spreadsheet.

  2. OpenAI API Key – GPT-4 access from platform.openai.com
  3. Google Sheets with OAuth2 – Enable Sheets API in Google Cloud Console
  4. Prospect Spreadsheet – Columns for Website Domain, Company Name, Contact Name, Email, Industry
  5. n8n Instance – Self-hosted or cloud version

Quick Setup Guide

1. Import the downloaded JSON template into your n8n workspace. 2. Add your Sona API key to the HTTP Request node headers (x-api-key). 3. Configure OpenAI credentials in the AI node. 4. Connect Google Sheets with OAuth2 and update the spreadsheet ID in all Sheet nodes. 5. Customize the AI prompts if needed to match your offering. 6. Test with 2-3 leads, review the output, then run your full list.

The workflow includes built-in error handling and rate limiting. If Sona doesn't find data for a domain, it uses the first search result as a fallback. The structured output parser ensures valid JSON from GPT-4, and the system automatically handles API limits with appropriate delays between requests.

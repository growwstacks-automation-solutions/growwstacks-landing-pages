What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious problem of manual business card data entry. When you receive a business card image via LINE messenger, this workflow automatically extracts all contact information using AI, organizes it into structured fields, saves it to Google Sheets, and can even trigger follow-up actions.

Instead of spending 10-15 minutes typing details from each card, the entire process happens in seconds. The system captures company names, contact persons, departments, addresses, email addresses, and phone numbers with remarkable accuracy, eliminating typos and lost contacts.

Beyond simple data extraction, this workflow creates a searchable digital contact database that grows with every networking event. It transforms random card images into organized, actionable business intelligence that integrates seamlessly with your existing tools.

How It Works

The automation follows a logical sequence that mimics how a human would process cards, but with machine speed and accuracy.

1. Trigger via LINE Webhook

When someone sends a business card image to your LINE bot or channel, the workflow activates instantly. The system captures the image file and prepares it for processing, handling various image formats and qualities automatically.

2. AI-Powered Text Extraction

The image is sent to Google Gemini AI, which performs optical character recognition (OCR). The AI doesn't just read text—it understands business card layouts, distinguishes between different information types, and extracts structured data even from complex designs.

3. Data Parsing & Validation

Extracted text is processed through logic nodes that identify and separate different data fields. The system validates email formats, phone number structures, and organizes information into consistent categories regardless of how it appeared on the original card.

4. Google Sheets Integration

Validated contact information is appended to your designated Google Sheets document. Each card creates a new row with timestamp, source image reference, and all extracted fields properly formatted for easy filtering and searching.

5. Automated Follow-up Actions

Optionally, the workflow can trigger thank-you emails via Gmail, create calendar reminders for follow-ups, or notify team members via Slack about new contacts. This turns passive data collection into active relationship building.

Quick Setup Guide

Getting this automation running takes about 30 minutes if you have your accounts ready. Follow these steps:

  1. Download and Import: Download the JSON template above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure LINE Webhook: Create a LINE developer channel, get your Channel Access Token, and update the webhook node in the workflow.
  3. Set Up Google Services: Enable Google Sheets and Drive APIs, create your spreadsheet with the required columns, and note the Sheet ID and folder ID.
  4. Add AI Credentials: Generate a Google Gemini API key and add it to the AI node credentials in n8n.
  5. Test with Sample Image: Send a business card photo to your LINE bot and watch the magic happen—data should appear in your sheet within seconds.

The workflow includes detailed configuration notes within each node. Pay special attention to the AI prompt section—you can customize it for your industry's specific terminology or additional data points you want to capture.

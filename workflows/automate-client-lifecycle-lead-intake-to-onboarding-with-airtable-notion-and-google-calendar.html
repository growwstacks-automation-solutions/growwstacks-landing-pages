What This Workflow Does

For service-based businesses, managing the journey from initial inquiry to fully onboarded client involves countless manual steps: capturing lead details, scheduling calls, sending reminders, updating project trackers, and delivering welcome materials. This disjointed process creates friction, delays, and missed opportunities.

This comprehensive n8n workflow solves that by automating the entire client lifecycle across four critical business flows. It transforms your tech stack into a cohesive system where information flows automatically between your intake form, CRM (Airtable), project management (Notion), calendar (Google Calendar), and communication tools (Gmail/Slack).

The result is a seamless experience for both you and your clients. Leads become scheduled calls automatically, reminders go out without you lifting a finger, and successful payments trigger instant onboarding—all while maintaining perfect synchronization across all your platforms.

How It Works

The workflow operates through four interconnected automation streams that work together as a unified system.

1. Lead Intake & Enrichment

When a potential client submits your contact form, the workflow captures their information via webhook. It validates the data, optionally enriches it with Clearbit for company details, then creates synchronized records in both Airtable (as a lead) and Notion (as a project page). This ensures your sales and delivery teams have immediate access to the same information.

2. Booking Management

Once a lead schedules a call through your calendar link, the workflow receives the booking webhook. It updates the lead status in Airtable, creates the corresponding Google Calendar event with all details, and sends a pre-call survey email to prepare both parties for the conversation.

3. Automated Reminders

A daily scheduled trigger scans Airtable for upcoming calls in the next 24 hours. For each scheduled appointment, it automatically sends personalized reminder emails to reduce no-shows. You can customize timing (24-hour, 1-hour, etc.) and include specific preparation materials.

4. Client Onboarding

When a payment is confirmed via webhook from your payment processor, the workflow marks the lead as "Paid" in Airtable, generates a comprehensive Notion onboarding checklist tailored to the service purchased, and sends a welcome kit via both email and WhatsApp. This instant transition from sale to service delights clients and accelerates time-to-value.

Pro tip: Use the "If" nodes to create different onboarding paths based on service type or package level. For example, premium clients could receive additional resources or faster support channels automatically.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in your business:

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file.
  2. Configure credentials for Airtable, Notion, Google Calendar, and Gmail in n8n's credentials management.
  3. Update node configurations with your specific Airtable base/table names and Notion database IDs.
  4. Customize email templates in the four Email Send nodes with your booking links, survey URLs, and branding.
  5. Connect your webhooks by copying the production URLs from the Lead Intake, Booking, and Payment webhook nodes into your respective tools.
  6. Test the flow with a dummy lead submission to ensure all connections work correctly.
  7. Activate the workflow and start automating your client lifecycle immediately.

Implementation tip: Start with just the lead intake and booking automation, then gradually add the reminder and onboarding components once you're comfortable with the basic flow. This phased approach reduces complexity during initial setup.

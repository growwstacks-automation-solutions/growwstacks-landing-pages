Automate Daily Interview Schedule Delivery from Google Calendar to Gmail

Free n8n workflow that fetches today's interviews and sends personalized email digests to each interviewer—eliminating manual coordination.

What This Workflow Does

Manual interview coordination is a major time sink for HR teams and hiring managers. Recruiters spend hours each week checking calendars, compiling interview details, and sending reminder emails—often with last-minute changes that get missed. This creates scheduling errors, interviewer unpreparedness, and poor candidate experiences.

This n8n automation solves these problems by automatically fetching all interview events scheduled for today from your Google Calendar, organizing them by interviewer, and sending personalized email digests with all necessary details. Each interviewer receives one clean email containing their interview times, candidate information, meeting links, and any preparation notes—all formatted in an easy-to-read HTML table.

The workflow runs daily at 8:00 AM (configurable to your timezone), ensuring every interviewer starts their day fully informed without any manual intervention. It transforms chaotic calendar management into a reliable, zero-friction system that improves interview quality while saving recruiters 5-10 hours weekly.

How It Works

1. Daily Schedule Trigger

A schedule trigger initiates the workflow each morning at your specified time (default 8:00 AM). This ensures interviewers receive their daily digest before their workday begins, giving them time to prepare.

2. Fetch Today's Interview Events

The Google Calendar node connects to your designated "Interviews" calendar and retrieves all events scheduled for the current day. It captures event titles, times, descriptions, attendees, and Google Meet links.

3. Process and Organize Interview Data

A code node processes each calendar event, identifying interviewers from attendee lists and extracting key details. It parses event descriptions for CV links, preparation notes, and candidate information, then organizes everything into a structured format.

4. Group Interviews by Interviewer

The workflow groups all interview events by interviewer email address, compiling each person's daily schedule into a single comprehensive list. This prevents interviewers from receiving multiple fragmented emails.

5. Send Personalized Email Digests

Gmail sends a beautifully formatted HTML email to each interviewer containing their personalized schedule table. The email includes interview times (converted to their timezone), candidate names and positions, meeting links, and any preparation materials.

Pro tip: Add "CV: [link]" and "Notes: [text]" to your Google Calendar event descriptions. The workflow automatically extracts these and includes them in the interviewer's email, eliminating document searching.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and save the JSON file to your computer.
  2. Import into n8n by clicking "Import from File" in your n8n dashboard and selecting the downloaded JSON.
  3. Configure Google Calendar credentials by clicking the Google Calendar node and connecting your account with OAuth.
  4. Set up Gmail credentials in the Gmail node using OAuth or an App Password if you have 2FA enabled.
  5. Adjust the schedule time in the Schedule Trigger node to match your preferred daily delivery time (e.g., 8:00 AM local time).
  6. Test the workflow by manually triggering it once to ensure emails are sent correctly.
  7. Activate the workflow and let it run automatically every morning.

Pro tip: Create a dedicated Google Calendar named "Interviews" for all hiring-related events. This keeps the automation clean and prevents personal events from being included in interview digests.

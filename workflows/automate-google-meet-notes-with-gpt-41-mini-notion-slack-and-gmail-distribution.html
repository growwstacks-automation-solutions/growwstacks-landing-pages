What This Workflow Does

This intelligent automation system transforms Google Meet recordings into structured meeting notes, eliminating manual transcription and summarization. By integrating Fireflies.ai, OpenAI GPT-4.1-mini, Notion, Slack and Gmail, it creates a complete meeting documentation pipeline.

The workflow automatically extracts key information including decisions, action items, risks and questions - then distributes polished notes through multiple channels to ensure all stakeholders receive properly formatted documentation.

Pro tip: This workflow saves approximately 2-3 hours per meeting by eliminating manual note-taking and follow-up distribution.

How It Works

1. Meeting Capture

Fireflies.ai joins your Google Meet and records the session, providing a transcript via webhook trigger.

2. AI Processing

GPT-4.1-mini analyzes the transcript to extract key points, decisions, and action items while maintaining context.

3. Documentation

Structured notes are saved to Notion with proper formatting and tagging for easy reference.

4. Distribution

Summaries are posted to Slack channels and sent via Gmail to all participants with relevant attachments.

What This Workflow Does

Manual lead qualification is slow, inconsistent, and misses opportunities. This end-to-end automation transforms your inbound lead process from a time-consuming chore into a seamless, intelligent system. It captures demo requests from Jotform, instantly filters out personal email addresses (like Gmail, Yahoo) to avoid wasting time on unqualified leads, and routes them to a polite, automated rejection.

For business leads, it scrapes their company website to understand their business, uses OpenAI's GPT to analyze their fit against your ideal customer profile (ICP), identifies potential pain points, and drafts a highly personalized outreach email. Finally, it presents this drafted email to a human for a quick review and approval before sending, ensuring quality and maintaining the human touch at the most critical moment.

How It Works

The workflow is a smart assembly line for your leads, ensuring only the best prospects get your team's attention with maximum context.

1. Lead Capture & Initial Filter

The process starts when a prospect submits your Jotform demo request. The workflow immediately checks the submitted email domain against a list of personal email providers. If it's a personal email, the lead is automatically sent a polite "not a fit" message, saving your team from chasing dead ends.

2. Website Intelligence Gathering

For business emails, the workflow activates a web scraping node. It visits the company website URL provided in the form and extracts key information: company description, services offered, team size hints, and recent news or blog posts. This data provides crucial context that most sales teams would have to manually hunt for.

3. AI-Powered Analysis & Scoring

The scraped website data, along with the form responses, is sent to OpenAI's GPT model. Using a custom prompt, the AI scores the lead based on your ICP criteria, identifies specific pain points your service could solve, and finds unique personalization hooks within the website content to reference in the outreach.

4. Human-in-the-Loop Approval

Instead of blasting automated emails, the drafted personalized email is sent to a designated team member (via Slack, email, or within n8n) for review. This ensures brand voice consistency, catches any AI oddities, and adds that final layer of human judgment before any communication is sent.

5. Personalized Outreach Delivery

Once approved, the workflow sends the tailored email via Gmail. It logs all activity—form submission, website data, AI analysis, and send status—to your CRM or a database for full visibility and follow-up.

Quick Setup Guide

Get this automation running in your business in under 30 minutes.

  1. Download & Import: Click the "Download Template" button above and import the JSON file into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure Credentials: Set up the credential nodes for Jotform (webhook), OpenAI, and Gmail within n8n.
  3. Connect Your Form: In your Jotform settings, find the webhook URL generated by n8n's "Jotform Trigger" node and paste it into Jotform's webhook integration.
  4. Customize the AI Prompt: In the "OpenAI Chat Model" node, edit the system prompt to reflect your company's specific ICP, value proposition, and desired email tone.
  5. Set Approval Method: Decide where approval requests go (Slack, email, n8n interface) and configure the corresponding node.
  6. Test with a Submission: Submit a test lead through your Jotform and watch the magic happen.

Automate Real Estate Open House Follow-ups

Transform open house visitors into clients with zero manual work. Sync SignSnapHome leads to multiple CRMs and execute personalized follow-up sequences automatically.

What This Workflow Does

This automation creates a complete, hands-free lead nurturing system for real estate agents using SignSnap Home for open house visitor management. Every time someone signs in at your open house, this workflow instantly captures all visitor data, intelligently scores each lead based on agent status and property interest, automatically syncs contact information to three CRMs simultaneously (HubSpot, Follow Up Boss, and Monday.com), logs everything to Google Sheets for audit trail, and sends personalized follow-ups over 7 days for qualified leads.

It eliminates the 15-20 minutes per visitor typically spent on manual data entry, CRM updates, and follow-up scheduling. Instead, you get immediate thank you emails, Day 2 SMS check-ins (with 98% open rates), Day 5 market updates, and Day 7 agent call tasks—all automated and compliant with real estate regulations.

How It Works

Step 1: Visitor Sign-in Capture

When a visitor signs in via SignSnap Home at your open house, a webhook instantly sends their data (name, email, phone, property interest, agent status) to the workflow.

Step 2: Intelligent Lead Scoring

The workflow analyzes each visitor's information, applying a scoring algorithm that prioritizes leads without current representation or signed buyer agreements. Hot leads receive full follow-up sequences; represented visitors get respectful basic treatment.

Step 3: Multi-CRM Synchronization

Contact data is simultaneously synced to HubSpot (for marketing automation), Follow Up Boss (for real estate-specific management), and Monday.com (for team collaboration). This ensures every department accesses lead information in their preferred system.

Step 4: Automated Follow-up Sequence

Qualified leads receive a 7-day multi-channel nurturing sequence: immediate thank you email, Day 2 SMS text, Day 5 market update email with consultation offer, and Day 7 task creation for agent follow-up.

Step 5: Complete Activity Tracking

Every touchpoint is logged to Google Sheets across three tabs: Lead Master Log, Follow-up Activity, and Errors. This provides full transparency and audit capability.

What You'll Need

  1. SignSnapHome account with webhook integration enabled
  2. HubSpot account (Free or paid tier) with API access
  3. Follow Up Boss account with API key
  4. Monday.com account with API token
  5. Twilio account with SMS-enabled phone number
  6. SMTP Email service (Gmail, SendGrid, etc.)
  7. Google Sheets with OAuth2 access
  8. Active n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import this workflow JSON into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure authentication for all seven services using their respective API credentials.
  3. Create a Google Sheet with three tabs: "Lead Master Log", "Follow-up Activity", and "Errors" using the provided column structures.
  4. Update placeholder values in the workflow nodes (Google Sheet ID, email address, Twilio phone number, Monday.com board ID).
  5. Activate the workflow in n8n, copy the webhook URL, and configure SignSnap Home integration.
  6. Test with a sample submission before your next open house.

Pro tip: Before going live, test the workflow with a colleague signing in as a "visitor" to verify all CRM syncs, emails, and SMS messages work correctly. This ensures your first real open house runs smoothly.

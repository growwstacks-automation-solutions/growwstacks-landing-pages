What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of unprepared sales meetings. Instead of manually searching through emails and LinkedIn profiles before each call, this workflow automatically compiles everything you need into a concise briefing sent directly to your WhatsApp.

The system checks your calendar for upcoming meetings, analyzes participant information, pulls relevant email history and LinkedIn activity, then uses AI to create a smart summary. You receive this preparation material 30-60 minutes before each meeting, ensuring you're always informed and ready to build stronger relationships.

Sales professionals typically spend 15-30 minutes preparing for important meetings. This automation reduces that to zero while actually improving the quality of preparation through comprehensive data gathering and intelligent summarization.

How It Works

1. Calendar Monitoring

The workflow starts by checking your Google Calendar hourly for upcoming meetings. It identifies meetings scheduled within the next 1-2 hours and extracts participant information including names and email addresses.

2. Data Collection & Analysis

For each meeting participant, the system runs parallel processes to gather relevant information. It searches your Gmail history for recent correspondence and uses LinkedIn data (via APIFY) to pull profile information and recent activity.

3. AI-Powered Summarization

An AI agent analyzes all collected data—email threads, LinkedIn profiles, recent posts—and creates a concise meeting brief. It highlights key discussion points, recent interactions, and relevant background information in an easy-to-scan format.

4. WhatsApp Delivery

The final briefing is sent directly to your WhatsApp Business account, ensuring you receive it on your mobile device where you're most likely to review it before the meeting. The notification includes the meeting time, participants, and key preparation points.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. Google Calendar access for meeting detection
  3. Gmail/Google Workspace account for email history
  4. OpenAI API key for AI summarization
  5. WhatsApp Business account with API access
  6. LinkedIn access (for profile data collection)

Pro tip: Start with personal calendar automation before scaling to team-wide implementation. This allows you to refine the briefing format and information sources based on what actually helps your meetings.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in your n8n instance:

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n workspace
  2. Configure calendar connection to your Google Calendar with appropriate meeting detection settings
  3. Set up email access by connecting your Gmail/Google Workspace account
  4. Add OpenAI credentials for the AI summarization nodes
  5. Connect WhatsApp Business API using your business account credentials
  6. Test with a scheduled meeting to ensure the workflow triggers correctly and delivers useful briefings

What This Workflow Does

Staying ahead in technology requires constant monitoring of industry trends, competitor moves, and emerging tools. Manual research consumes valuable hours each week that could be spent on strategic work. This automation solves that by creating a personal AI research assistant that works while you sleep.

The workflow automatically conducts weekly tech research using AI, organizes findings in a Notion knowledge base, and emails a digest to your team. It transforms scattered information gathering into a systematic, repeatable process that builds institutional knowledge over time.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

Every Monday at 9 AM (configurable), the workflow activates automatically. This time-based trigger ensures consistent research without manual initiation, making insights part of your weekly routine.

2. AI Agent Configuration

A system prompt defines your AI researcher's role, objectives, and behavior. You can customize it to focus on specific technologies, competitors, or market segments relevant to your business.

3. Language Model Processing

The agent uses powerful models (like DeepSeek via OpenRouter or Perplexity AI) to generate relevant insights. It can analyze trends, summarize developments, and identify patterns across thousands of sources in seconds.

4. Data Storage in Notion

Structured findings are saved directly to your connected Notion database. Each entry includes categories, summaries, relevance scores, and source references, creating a searchable knowledge repository.

5. Email Dispatch

A formatted summary report is automatically sent to predefined recipients via Gmail. The email highlights key findings, includes actionable insights, and links to the full Notion database for deeper exploration.

Pro tip: Start with broad research topics, then refine your AI prompts based on the quality of initial results. The system learns from your feedback through prompt adjustments.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with workflow execution capabilities
  2. Notion account with a database ready to receive research entries

  4. OpenRouter API key or access to Perplexity AI/other LLM services
  5. Basic understanding of your research topics and desired output format

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in under 30 minutes:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Configure the Schedule Trigger to match your preferred research timing (weekly, bi-weekly, etc.)
  3. Connect your Notion integration by adding your API token and selecting your target database
  4. Set up your AI provider by adding OpenRouter or Perplexity API credentials to the Agent node
  5. Customize the research prompt to focus on your specific industry, competitors, or topics
  6. Test the workflow with a manual execution to verify all connections work properly
  7. Activate the workflow and let it run automatically according to your schedule

Pro tip: Create a dedicated "Research Archive" view in your Notion database sorted by date. This makes it easy to track how insights evolve over time and identify long-term trends.

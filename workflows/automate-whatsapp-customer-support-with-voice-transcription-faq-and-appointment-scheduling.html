What This Workflow Does

Manual WhatsApp customer support drains hours daily with repetitive questions about services, pricing, and availability. This automation transforms WhatsApp into a 24/7 customer service assistant that handles the complete inquiry-to-booking journey automatically.

The workflow listens for incoming WhatsApp messages, transcribes voice notes using AI, answers FAQs from a knowledge base, provides detailed service information, and guides customers through appointment scheduling—checking real-time calendar availability and sending confirmations. It eliminates manual back-and-forth while maintaining a personalized, conversational experience.

How It Works

1. Message Reception & Voice Transcription

When a customer sends a WhatsApp message or voice note, the workflow triggers immediately. Voice notes are automatically transcribed to text using speech-to-text AI, ensuring voice-first customers receive the same seamless service as those who type.

2. Intent Recognition & Routing

The system analyzes the customer's message to identify their intent: FAQ question, service inquiry, or appointment request. Using natural language processing, it routes the conversation to the appropriate response path without human intervention.

3. FAQ & Service Information Delivery

For common questions, the workflow queries a Google Sheet knowledge base and returns instant, accurate answers. For service inquiries, it pulls current service descriptions, pricing, and availability from your service catalog spreadsheet.

4. Intelligent Appointment Scheduling

When booking is requested, the automation asks for preferred day/time, checks Google Calendar for available slots, offers 3 options, collects customer details, and creates the confirmed appointment—all within the WhatsApp conversation.

Who This Is For

This template is ideal for service-based businesses that receive frequent customer inquiries via WhatsApp: medical clinics, beauty salons, fitness trainers, consultants, repair services, tutors, and professional service providers. If you're currently managing appointments and answering the same questions manually across multiple conversations, this automation will save you 10-15 hours weekly while improving customer satisfaction.

What You'll Need

  1. WhatsApp Business API access or a compatible WhatsApp integration
  2. Google Sheets for your FAQ database and service catalog
  3. Google Calendar for appointment scheduling
  4. Speech-to-Text API (OpenAI Whisper, Google Speech-to-Text, or similar)
  5. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the automation

Pro tip: Start with your 10 most common customer questions in the FAQ sheet. As you identify new recurring questions, add them to the knowledge base—your automation instantly gains new capabilities without code changes.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your accounts for WhatsApp, Google Sheets, Google Calendar, and your chosen speech-to-text service
  3. Prepare your Google Sheets with FAQ columns (id, question, answer) and service columns (id, service_name, description, price)
  4. Customize message templates to match your brand voice and service offerings
  5. Test thoroughly with sample conversations before going live with customers
  6. Monitor and refine based on actual customer interactions for continuous improvement

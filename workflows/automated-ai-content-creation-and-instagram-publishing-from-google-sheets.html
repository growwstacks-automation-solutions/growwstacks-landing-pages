What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of creating and publishing consistent social media content. Instead of manually designing images, writing captions, and scheduling posts, this workflow turns your Google Sheets content calendar into published Instagram posts automatically.

It acts as an execution engine for your content strategy. Your team simply adds post ideas to a Google Sheet with basic details like topic, target audience, and brand voice. The workflow then uses AI to generate a complete social media post—including a unique image and engaging caption—and publishes it directly to your Instagram Business account.

The system ensures you never miss a scheduled post while maintaining brand consistency and creative quality. It's particularly valuable for marketing teams, agencies, and content creators who need to maintain multiple social channels without proportional increases in creative resources.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Content Check

The workflow runs on a schedule you set (daily, hourly, etc.) and checks your Google Sheet for new content ideas marked as "pending." It processes one idea at a time to maintain control and quality.

2. AI Concept Development

Using Google Gemini AI, the system analyzes your topic, audience, and platform to develop a creative concept specifically tailored for a single-image Instagram post. This ensures the content matches both your brand and platform best practices.

3. Visual & Text Creation

The AI generates two distinct image prompt options based on the concept, then creates an engaging caption with relevant hashtags. The first prompt is sent to Replicate's Flux model to generate a unique, brand-appropriate image.

4. Instagram Publishing

The workflow uses Facebook's Graph API to upload the generated image and caption to Instagram, following the proper media container process that ensures reliable publishing to your business account.

5. Status Update

Once published, the workflow marks the row in your Google Sheet as "completed," preventing duplicate posts and giving your team clear visibility into what's been published.

What You'll Need

  1. An active n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. Google account with Sheets API access and OAuth2 credentials
  3. A Google Sheet structured with content ideas and status tracking
  4. Google Cloud Project with Vertex AI API enabled for Gemini access
  5. Replicate account and API token for image generation
  6. Instagram Business Account connected to a Facebook Page
  7. Facebook Developer App with appropriate permissions (instagram_basic, instagram_content_publish)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file.
  2. Configure credentials for Google Sheets, Google Gemini (Vertex AI), Replicate, and Facebook Graph API in n8n's credentials management.
  3. Update spreadsheet details in the Google Sheets nodes with your specific Spreadsheet ID and sheet name.
  4. Set your Instagram Account ID in both Facebook Graph API nodes (replace the placeholder ID).
  5. Adjust the schedule trigger to match your desired posting frequency.
  6. Test with one row in your Google Sheet marked as "pending" (using your status indicator, typically 0).
  7. Activate the workflow and monitor the first execution to ensure all connections work properly.

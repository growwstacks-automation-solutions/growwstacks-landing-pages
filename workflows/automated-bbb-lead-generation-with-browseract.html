What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually researching businesses on BBB, extracting their contact information, and initiating outreach. It combines BrowserAct's powerful web automation with AI data processing to create a turnkey lead generation system.

The workflow automatically searches BBB based on your criteria, extracts business details (name, phone, email, website, rating), cleans and structures the data using AI, checks for duplicates in your Google Sheets database, and initiates personalized email outreach - all while logging every action for transparency.

Workflow structure showing BrowserAct data extraction and AI processing steps

How It Works

1. Automated BBB Data Extraction

BrowserAct navigates BBB search results just like a human, extracting business listings while avoiding detection as a bot. It handles pagination and captures all available contact information.

2. AI Data Processing

Google Gemini AI cleans and structures the scraped data, identifying key information even when it's inconsistently formatted. It extracts business names, websites, phone numbers and ratings into standardized fields.

3. Duplicate Checking

The workflow compares new leads against your existing Google Sheets database using multiple data points to accurately identify duplicates, preventing embarrassing repeat outreach.

4. Email Outreach

Validated leads receive personalized emails through Gmail, with status updates logged back to your spreadsheet. The system respects email sending limits to maintain deliverability.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually sending personalized cold emails to prospects. It automatically fetches uncontacted leads from your Google Sheet, validates their email addresses, sends customized emails on a schedule, and updates your CRM with tracking information.

The workflow handles the entire cold email sequence - from initial outreach to follow-ups - while maintaining personalization and avoiding spam filters. It integrates Gmail or SMTP for sending, Google Sheets as your CRM, and includes smart scheduling to optimize deliverability.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow runs automatically at your configured intervals (default every 6 hours) to process new prospects.

2. Fetch New Leads

Connects to your Google Sheet to identify prospects marked as "Not Contacted" and prepares them for outreach.

3. Email Validation

Checks each email address for validity before sending to maintain high deliverability rates.

4. Personalized Email Dispatch

Sends customized emails using your Gmail or SMTP account with merge fields for personalization.

5. CRM Updates

Marks contacts as "Emailed" in your Google Sheet with timestamps and message IDs for tracking.

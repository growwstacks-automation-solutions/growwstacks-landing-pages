What This Workflow Does

Dirty email lists cost businesses real money. Every bounced email damages your sender reputation, wastes marketing spend, and reduces campaign effectiveness. Manual validation is tedious, error-prone, and doesn't scale.

This automation solves that by creating a self-cleaning email system. It continuously monitors your Google Sheets for new email entries, instantly verifies their deliverability using both Hunter.io and EmailValidation.io APIs, and updates your spreadsheet with validation status. The result: cleaner lists, higher deliverability, and zero manual validation work.

Whether you're importing leads, collecting signups, or maintaining a customer database, this workflow ensures every email address is verified before it enters your marketing sequences or sales outreach.

How It Works

Step 1: Monitor Google Sheets

The workflow starts with a Google Sheets trigger that checks for new rows every minute. When a new email is added to your designated column, the automation immediately captures it for processing.

Step 2: Filter & Format

A filter node removes empty cells and invalid email formats, preventing unnecessary API calls. The email is then standardized into the correct format for validation services.

Step 3: Dual API Validation

The email is sent simultaneously to Hunter.io (for professional verification) and EmailValidation.io (for deliverability checking). This cross-verification provides higher accuracy than single-source validation.

Step 4: Process Results

The workflow analyzes responses from both services, determining overall validity. It categorizes results as "valid," "invalid," "disposable," "catch-all," or "unknown."

Step 5: Update Spreadsheet

Finally, the original Google Sheet is updated with validation status, confidence scores, and timestamps. Your team sees real-time hygiene status without leaving their spreadsheet.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in under 15 minutes:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Connect Google Sheets by setting up OAuth2 credentials in n8n and authorizing access to your spreadsheet.
  3. Configure API keys for Hunter.io and EmailValidation.io in the respective HTTP Request nodes.
  4. Map your columns by updating the Google Sheets nodes to point to your specific email column and desired output columns.
  5. Test with sample data by adding a few test emails to your sheet and activating the workflow.
  6. Monitor results and adjust validation thresholds based on your specific needs.

What This Workflow Does

Every minute a customer waits for a product inquiry response is a minute they might shop elsewhere. This automation solves that problem by creating an intelligent, 24/7 product assistant that handles common questions instantly.

When customers ask about product availability, pricing, sizes, or specifications—whether via Instagram DM, website chat, or messaging apps—the AI understands their natural language question, searches your Google Sheets inventory, and returns a friendly, accurate response with exact product details. No human intervention required.

Perfect for shoe stores, fashion retailers, electronics sellers, or any business with a product catalog. It turns your spreadsheet into a conversational product expert that never sleeps, never makes lookup errors, and always responds immediately.

How It Works

1. Customer Inquiry Trigger

A customer sends a product question through any connected channel—webhook, chat widget, or messaging platform. The workflow triggers instantly upon receiving their message.

2. AI Natural Language Processing

GPT-4 analyzes the customer's question to extract key product attributes: brand, model, size, color, and other specifications. It understands variations like "Nike Air Max 90 size 40" versus "Do you have size 40 Nike Air Max?"

3. Google Sheets Database Search

The extracted attributes become search parameters that query your Google Sheets product database. The system filters through your inventory to find exact matches or close alternatives.

4. AI Response Generation

Using the found product data, GPT-4 crafts a personalized, friendly response that includes availability ("3 pieces in stock"), pricing ("$120"), and any relevant details. It can suggest alternatives if the exact item isn't available.

5. Automated Reply Delivery

The generated response is sent back through the same channel the inquiry came from, completing the conversation loop within seconds—not hours or days.

Quick Setup Guide

Get your AI product assistant running in under an hour:

  1. Import the template into your n8n workspace using the downloaded JSON file
  2. Prepare your Google Sheets with columns: Brand, Model, Size, Color, Price, Quantity, Description
  3. Connect OpenAI by adding your API key to the GPT-4 node configuration
  4. Set up your webhook trigger for your chosen messaging platform
  5. Test with sample inquiries like "Do you have Nike Air Max in size 40?"
  6. Deploy and monitor the workflow, adjusting responses based on real customer interactions

Pro tip: Start with a small subset of popular products to test accuracy before rolling out to your entire catalog. This helps refine the AI's understanding of your specific product naming conventions.

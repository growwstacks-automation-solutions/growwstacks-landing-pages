Turn inbound leads into booked meetings instantly with AI-powered research and personalized email outreach.

What This Workflow Does

Manual lead follow-up is slow, inconsistent, and misses critical context. When a prospect submits your contact form, sales teams often waste precious minutes researching the company before crafting a response—if they respond at all within the crucial first hour.

This automation solves that by acting as your 24/7 AI sales assistant. The moment a lead comes in, it automatically researches their business using Tavily's AI search, generates a highly personalized email with GPT-5 that references specific company details, and sends it through Gmail—all within minutes of submission. This transforms generic "thanks for reaching out" emails into relevant, value-driven conversations that dramatically increase booking rates.

How It Works

1. Lead Capture & Trigger

The workflow activates when a new submission arrives from your website form, CRM, or other lead source. It captures essential details: name, email, company URL, and initial inquiry.

2. AI-Powered Company Research

Using Tavily AI search, the system automatically researches the prospect's company website, recent news, industry positioning, and key challenges. This provides real-time context most sales reps would spend 10-15 minutes gathering manually.

3. Personalized Email Generation

GPT-5 crafts a complete email using the research findings, your company's tone guidelines, and proven sales frameworks. It includes a compelling subject line, personalized opening referencing the company's context, value proposition alignment, and clear call-to-action to schedule a meeting.

4. Automated Delivery & Logging

The finalized email sends instantly via Gmail with proper formatting and tracking. The system can also log the interaction in your CRM or database, creating a complete audit trail of automated touchpoints.

Pro tip: Pair this workflow with your Calendly or scheduling tool to include direct booking links in every email, reducing friction for interested leads.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in your environment:

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Configure credentials for OpenAI, Tavily, and Gmail in n8n's credentials management
  3. Adjust trigger settings to match your lead source (webhook URL, form submission format)
  4. Customize email templates in the GPT node to reflect your company's voice and offerings
  5. Test thoroughly with sample lead data before connecting to live sources
  6. Monitor initial sends and refine prompts based on email quality and response rates

What This Workflow Does

Manual sales proposal creation is a major bottleneck for growing businesses. Sales teams spend hours drafting personalized collateral for each booked lead, copying data between spreadsheets, formatting documents, and sending follow-up emails. This process is repetitive, error-prone, and delays your sales cycle.

This workflow solves that by automating the entire sales collateral pipeline. When a lead is marked as "BOOKED" in your Google Sheets CRM, it triggers an AI-powered system that generates three professional documents: a concise sales summary, a one-page overview with key selling points, and a complete proposal draft. All documents are automatically saved to Google Drive, linked back to your CRM, and a consolidated HTML summary is emailed to your team—completing in minutes what used to take hours.

The system ensures consistency across all client communications while allowing for personalization based on each lead's specific details. It transforms your sales operations from a manual, administrative burden into a strategic, automated advantage.

How It Works

1. Lead Retrieval & Validation

The workflow starts by pulling all lead records from your designated Google Sheets CRM. It validates each entry for correct email format and completeness, logging any invalid data to a separate sheet for cleanup without interrupting the process.

2. AI-Powered Content Generation

For each valid "BOOKED" lead, the system uses GPT-4o (via Azure OpenAI) to generate three tailored documents. The AI analyzes company details, industry, and specific requirements to create a sales summary (80-word follow-up), a one-pager with key selling points and CTA, and a full proposal draft with scope, timeline, and next steps.

3. Document Management & CRM Update

Each generated document is converted to a clean text report and uploaded to a designated Google Drive folder. The system then updates the original Google Sheets CRM row with direct links to each proposal, creating full traceability and easy access for your sales team.

4. Automated Reporting & Notification

Finally, the workflow compiles all processed leads into an HTML-formatted summary, uses GPT-4o to generate insights about the sales activity, and emails this professional report to your designated marketing or sales inbox via Gmail—ready for forwarding to management or team review.

What You'll Need

  1. Google Workspace account with Sheets, Drive, and Gmail access
  2. Azure OpenAI credentials with GPT-4o model access (or alternative AI provider)
  3. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with the necessary nodes installed
  4. Google Sheets CRM with columns for lead details and booking status
  5. Designated Google Drive folder for storing generated proposals

Pro tip: Before full automation, run the workflow with 2-3 test leads to fine-tune the AI prompts. Adjust the tone, length, and structure to match your brand voice and typical proposal requirements.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Configure Google OAuth connections for Sheets, Drive, and Gmail nodes
  3. Set up Azure OpenAI credentials in the AI model configuration node
  4. Update spreadsheet IDs and folder paths to point to your actual Google resources
  5. Test with a single booked lead to verify document generation and email delivery
  6. Schedule or trigger automatically based on your sales process (daily, weekly, or on-demand)

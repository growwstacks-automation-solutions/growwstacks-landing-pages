Automate Job Posting: Extract & Publish Listings with AI

What This Workflow Does

Manual job posting is a time-consuming bottleneck for recruiters, HR teams, and job board managers. Copying details from career pages, formatting them for your website, and ensuring consistency across listings can eat up hours each week. This workflow eliminates that drudgery by creating a fully automated pipeline from discovery to publication.

When you or your team finds a job listing anywhere on the web, you simply send the URL to a Telegram bot. The workflow then springs into action: it scrapes the page content, uses AI to extract and structure the job details, applies your branding and formatting standards, and publishes a polished post directly to your WordPress site. What used to take 20-30 minutes per listing now happens in seconds, with fewer errors and consistent quality.

How It Works

1. Trigger via Telegram

Any team member sends a job listing URL to your dedicated Telegram bot. This simple interface requires no training—just paste and send. The bot immediately forwards the URL to your n8n workflow as a trigger.

2. Intelligent Web Scraping

The workflow uses Firecrawl API (or similar services) to extract clean content from the provided URL. Unlike simple scrapers, it intelligently identifies the main job content while ignoring navigation, ads, and irrelevant page elements.

3. AI-Powered Data Extraction

OpenAI's GPT models parse the scraped content to identify and structure key job details: title, company, location, salary range, description, requirements, and application instructions. The AI also categorizes the job type (full-time, contract, remote) and maps it to your internal classification system.

4. Content Enhancement & Validation

The raw extracted data passes through validation checks to ensure completeness. AI then enhances the description for readability and SEO, maintaining your brand voice. Default values fill any missing fields, and the workflow can even fetch and upload company logos.

5. Automated WordPress Publishing

Using the WordPress REST API, the workflow creates a new post with all structured data, assigns categories, sets featured images, and publishes immediately or schedules for later. Success or error notifications are sent back to Telegram, keeping your team informed.

Who This Is For

This automation delivers the most value for recruitment agencies, HR departments at growing companies, niche job board operators, and content marketers in the employment space. What You'll Need

  1. An active n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. Firecrawl account with API access for web scraping
  3. OpenAI API key for GPT-powered extraction and formatting
  4. WordPress site with the REST API enabled
  5. Telegram bot token (created via @BotFather)
  6. Google Drive account (for optional RAG document storage)
  7. Supabase account (for vector store management)

Pro tip: Start with a single job board or company career page to test your extraction accuracy before expanding to multiple sources. Different sites have varying HTML structures that may require slight adjustments to your scraping configuration.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file.
  2. Configure credentials for all connected services: Firecrawl, OpenAI, WordPress, Telegram, and Supabase.
  3. Customize field mappings to match your WordPress job post structure and category taxonomy.
  4. Test with sample URLs from your target job sources to verify extraction accuracy.
  5. Deploy your Telegram bot and share it with your team for everyday use.

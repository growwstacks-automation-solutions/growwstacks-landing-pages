What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered chatbot workflow transforms how employees access HR information by providing instant, accurate answers to questions about company policies, benefits, and workplace guidelines. It solves the bottleneck of HR teams spending 30-50% of their time answering repetitive questions while employees wait hours or days for responses.

The system integrates with BambooHR to provide real-time answers drawn directly from your official policies and benefits documentation. It intelligently handles routine inquiries while automatically escalating sensitive matters like harassment concerns to appropriate HR personnel, ensuring compliance and employee safety.

The AI chatbot interface providing instant answers to employee policy questions

How It Works

1. Employee submits question

Employees ask questions through your preferred channel (Slack, Teams, web portal, etc.). The system accepts natural language queries like "How much PTO do I have?" or "What's our remote work policy?"

2. AI analyzes and classifies the query

The workflow uses natural language processing to understand the question's intent, classify its topic (benefits, policies, etc.), and determine whether it's routine or requires human escalation.

Example of a routine benefits question being processed automatically

3. Retrieves accurate policy data

For routine questions, the system pulls the most current information from BambooHR, including policy documents, employee-specific benefits data, and handbook content.

4. Generates human-like response

The AI formulates a clear, compliant answer citing relevant policy sections. Responses include disclaimers when appropriate and always offer the option to connect with human HR.

Automatic escalation process for sensitive harassment concerns

5. Handles escalations appropriately

For sensitive topics or when confidence scores are low, the workflow immediately notifies designated HR staff and provides the full interaction history for follow-up.

Pro tip: Train your AI model with actual employee questions from past HR tickets to improve accuracy for your specific workforce's phrasing and concerns.

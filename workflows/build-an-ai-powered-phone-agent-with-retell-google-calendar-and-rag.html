What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered phone agent solution handles two critical business functions automatically: appointment scheduling and customer information retrieval. It eliminates repetitive phone tasks while providing 24/7 availability for your customers.

The system integrates RetellAI's conversational AI with your Google Calendar for real-time booking, while using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to answer customer questions based on your business knowledge base.

The complete automation flow from phone call to calendar booking

How It Works

1. Call Processing

RetellAI handles incoming calls, transcribes the conversation in real-time, and identifies whether the caller wants to book an appointment or ask a question.

2. Appointment Booking

For scheduling requests, the workflow extracts key details (date, time, service type) and creates events directly in Google Calendar, sending confirmation details to the caller.

3. Information Retrieval

For questions, the system searches your knowledge base using RAG technology to provide accurate, up-to-date answers about your business policies, hours, or services.

Who This Is For

What This Workflow Does

Manual prospecting is broken. Sales teams waste hours jumping between databases, copying company domains, hunting for decision-makers, and manually enriching contact data—only to end up with outdated spreadsheets. This workflow fixes that by automating the entire process from discovery to CRM sync.

The automation queries Surfe's database for companies matching your exact Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) criteria—industry, size, revenue, and geography. It then finds the right people inside those companies, enriches their contact data in bulk, and syncs only fully-validated prospects (with both direct email and mobile phone) directly to HubSpot. What used to take days now happens in minutes with zero manual effort.

How It Works

Step 1: ICP Company Discovery

The workflow starts by querying Surfe's company database using your configured filters. You define exact criteria like "Software companies with 10-50 employees in Germany generating $1-10M revenue." Surfe returns matching company domains with firmographic data.

Step 2: People Search & Enrichment

For each discovered company, the workflow searches for relevant decision-makers—typically founders, executives, or department heads. It then batches these contacts and sends them to Surfe's bulk enrichment API, which adds verified direct emails, mobile numbers, and professional details.

Step 3: Quality Filtering

Not all enriched contacts are equally valuable. The workflow applies a critical filter: it keeps only prospects with BOTH a direct email address AND a mobile phone number. This ensures maximum reachability and eliminates incomplete records before they reach your CRM.

Step 4: HubSpot Sync & Notification

Validated contacts are created or updated in your HubSpot CRM with all enriched fields. Finally, you receive a completion email (or Slack notification) with a summary of how many prospects were added. The entire process runs unattended once configured.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n Instance: Self-hosted or cloud version of n8n (free tier works perfectly)
  2. Surfe API Key: From your Surfe dashboard (used for company discovery and contact enrichment)
  3. HubSpot Private App Token: With contact read/write permissions (create in HubSpot Settings → Integrations)
  4. Gmail Account: For completion notifications (configured via OAuth2 in n8n credentials)
  5. ICP Criteria: Clear definition of your target companies (industry, size, geography, revenue)

Pro tip: Start with tighter filters than you think you need. It's better to generate 20 perfectly-matched prospects than 200 mediocre ones. You can always broaden criteria later based on conversion performance.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download & Import: Download the template JSON file and import it into your n8n instance via "Import from File."
  2. Configure Credentials: In n8n Credentials, create three connections: Surfe (Bearer Auth), HubSpot (Private App), and Gmail (OAuth2).
  3. Set ICP Filters: Edit the "Search ICP Companies" HTTP node's JSON body to match your target criteria (industries, employee count, countries, revenue).
  4. Connect Nodes: Link each node to the corresponding credentials you just created.
  5. Test & Schedule: Execute the workflow once manually to verify everything works, then schedule it to run weekly for automatic list refreshes.

Automated Business Lead Generation with Apify & Google Sheets

Scrape business listings from Google Maps for any industry and location. Automatically store structured lead data in Google Sheets for sales teams and marketers.

What This Workflow Does

Manual lead generation is time-consuming and inconsistent. Sales teams spend hours searching directories, copying contact details, and organizing data—often missing opportunities or working with outdated information.

This automation solves that by systematically scraping business listings from Google Maps using Apify’s reliable scraping tools. You define the industry (like “restaurants,” “dentists,” or “contractors”) and location, and the workflow extracts business names, addresses, phone numbers, and other details. The structured data is then automatically saved into a Google Sheets spreadsheet, ready for your sales team to use.

The result is a consistent, scalable lead pipeline that grows without manual effort. Whether you need local service leads, B2B contacts, or market research data, this workflow delivers organized information directly to where your team works.

How It Works

Step 1: Trigger & Configuration

The workflow starts manually or on a schedule. You configure the Apify node with your target location (city, region) and business category search terms. This defines what listings will be scraped.

Step 2: Scraping Execution

Apify executes the Google Maps scraping job, collecting raw listing data. The workflow includes a built-in wait period to ensure scraping completes fully before moving to data processing.

Step 3: Data Processing & Cleaning

Raw scraped data is parsed and structured. The workflow extracts key fields: business name, full address, state/province, neighborhood, phone number, and email addresses when available.

Step 4: Google Sheets Storage

Cleaned lead data is appended row-by-row into your predefined Google Sheets spreadsheet. The sheet maintains columns for each data field, creating an instantly usable lead database.

What You'll Need

  1. Apify Account: Free account to access the Google Maps scraper actor and API tokens.
  2. Google Sheets Document: A spreadsheet with columns for: title (business name), address, state, neighborhood, phone, emails.
  3. Google OAuth Credentials: n8n credentials to connect and write to your Google Sheets.
  4. n8n Instance: Self-hosted n8n or n8n.cloud account to run the workflow.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the Template: Download the JSON file and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure Apify: Sign up for Apify, get your API token, and enter it in the HTTP Request node. Set your location and business type in the JSON body.
  3. Prepare Google Sheets: Create a sheet with the six column headers mentioned above. Note your Sheet ID for the Google Sheets node.
  4. Set Up Google OAuth: In n8n, create a Google Sheets credential using OAuth2, authorizing access to your spreadsheet.
  5. Test the Workflow: Execute manually once to verify scraping runs and data appears correctly in your sheet.
  6. Schedule Automation: Set the trigger to run weekly, monthly, or on-demand based on your lead needs.

Pro tip: Start with a small location and single business category to test accuracy. Then expand to multiple locations or categories once you confirm data quality matches your needs.

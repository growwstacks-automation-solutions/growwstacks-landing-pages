What This Workflow Does

Manual resume screening is one of the most time-consuming tasks in recruitment. HR teams spend 15-20 hours weekly reviewing hundreds of applications, often missing qualified candidates due to fatigue or unconscious bias. This automation solves that problem by creating an intelligent, consistent, and scalable first-pass screening system.

The workflow processes job applications submitted through any form, analyzes CVs/resumes using AI, extracts key qualifications, compares candidates against your job criteria, and automatically routes them to appropriate next steps. Qualified candidates receive interview invitations while others get polite rejections—all without manual intervention.

How It Works

1. Application Submission & File Processing

Candidates submit their information and CV through your preferred form (Google Forms, Typeform, etc.). The system automatically detects file format—processing PDFs, Word documents, or even resume URLs—and converts everything to standardized text for analysis.

2. AI-Powered Information Extraction

Claude AI reads each resume, identifying skills, experience levels, education, certifications, and other relevant qualifications. It structures this information into consistent data points, eliminating the variability of human-written resumes.

3. Criteria Comparison & Classification

The system compares extracted qualifications against your predefined job requirements. You can set must-have criteria (like specific certifications) and nice-to-have attributes (like years of experience). AI determines whether each applicant meets, exceeds, or falls short of requirements.

4. Automated Candidate Routing

Based on classification results, the workflow triggers appropriate actions: qualified candidates receive congratulatory emails with calendar links to schedule interviews; unqualified candidates get personalized rejection messages with encouragement for future opportunities.

5. Documentation & Tracking

All processed applications, AI analysis results, and classification decisions are logged in Google Sheets or your ATS. This creates an audit trail, helps refine your criteria over time, and provides data for hiring analytics.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for: HR teams at growing companies receiving 50+ applications per role; recruitment agencies managing multiple clients and positions; startups needing to scale hiring without expanding HR staff; and any organization wanting to reduce time-to-hire while improving candidate experience. It's particularly valuable for technical roles where specific skills are critical and for high-volume seasonal hiring.

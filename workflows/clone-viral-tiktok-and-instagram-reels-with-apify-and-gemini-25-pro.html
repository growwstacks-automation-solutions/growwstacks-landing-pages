Clone Viral TikTok & Instagram Reels with AI

Automatically analyze successful short-form videos and generate detailed production prompts using Google Gemini AI and Apify.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical problem for content creators and digital marketers: understanding why certain videos go viral. Instead of guessing what makes successful TikTok or Instagram Reels work, this workflow performs forensic analysis using AI to give you a detailed blueprint.

It takes any public Instagram Reel or TikTok URL, downloads the video through Apify's scraping service, converts it to a format AI can understand, and uses Google's Gemini 2.5 Pro vision model to generate what we call a "Generative Manifest." This is an extremely detailed prompt that could be used to recreate similar content with AI video tools like Google Veo or Sora.

The business value is substantial: what would take a human analyst 5-10 hours of frame-by-frame analysis happens automatically in minutes. This gives creators, agencies, and brands a competitive edge in content strategy without proportional increases in labor costs.

How It Works

Step 1: Video URL Input & Platform Detection

The workflow starts with a web form where you input either TikTok or Instagram Reel URLs. It automatically detects which platform the content comes from and routes it through the appropriate scraping path.

Step 2: Automated Video Scraping with Apify

Using Apify's reliable web scraping infrastructure, the workflow downloads the video content while respecting platform terms of service. This happens in the background without manual intervention.

Step 3: Video Processing & AI Preparation

The downloaded video is converted to base64 format and prepared for analysis by Google's Gemini vision model. This technical step ensures the AI receives optimal input for accurate analysis.

Step 4: Comprehensive AI Analysis with Gemini 2.5 Pro

Google Gemini performs forensic analysis including: visual medium identification, color grading breakdown, shot-by-shot deconstruction with timing, camera movement details, subject description, and environmental analysis.

Step 5: Results Delivery & Optional Slack Integration

The generated "Generative Manifest" is output as a detailed text document. Optionally, it can be sent to a Slack channel for team collaboration and immediate action.

What This Workflow Does

Reproducing viral video formats manually is a massive time sink. This workflow solves that by automating the entire process: from discovering a trending TikTok to generating a branded version and distributing it across nine social platforms.

It transforms a single Telegram message containing a TikTok link into a fully produced, AI-generated video posted to Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, Facebook, LinkedIn, Threads, X (Twitter), Pinterest, and Bluesky. The system clones the viral structure, rewrites the script and captions using GPT-4o, creates a new video with your AI avatar via Captions.ai, adds subtitles, and publishes everywhere—without you touching a single upload button.

For content creators, marketers, and agencies, this means scaling short-form content production 5–10x while maintaining brand consistency and capitalizing on proven viral formats.

How It Works

Step 1: Trigger via Telegram

You or your team sends a TikTok URL to a dedicated Telegram bot. This triggers the workflow instantly, kicking off the automated cloning pipeline.

Step 2: Download & Analyze Original Content

The workflow downloads the TikTok video, extracts its audio, thumbnail, and caption, then transcribes the audio. All original data is saved to a Google Sheet for tracking and analysis.

Step 3: AI Content Ideation & Rewriting

Perplexity AI analyzes the video's niche and suggests a new content angle. GPT-4o then rewrites the script, caption, and overlay text to match your brand voice while preserving the viral hooks.

Step 4: AI Avatar Video Generation

Using Captions.ai, the workflow generates a new video with your custom AI avatar speaking the rewritten script. It maintains the original's pacing and visual style but with your branding.

Step 5: Multi-platform Publishing via Blotato

The final video, along with optimized captions, is automatically posted to nine social media platforms through Blotato's API. Each platform receives tailored formatting.

Pro tip: Insert a Telegram approval node before the publishing step if you want manual review. This adds a human-in-the-loop safety check without breaking the automation flow.

What You'll Need

  1. A Telegram bot token (for the trigger).
  2. API keys for OpenAI (GPT-4o), Perplexity AI, and Captions.ai.
  3. A Cloudinary account for media storage.
  4. Blotato API access configured with your social media account IDs.
  5. A Google Sheet with columns for original URL, transcript, new script, and posting status.
  6. Your AI avatar name/ID configured in Captions.ai.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the JSON template into your n8n instance.
  2. Connect your Telegram bot to the trigger node.
  3. Add all required API keys in the respective credential nodes.
  4. Prepare your Google Sheet and update the node with its ID and column mappings.
  5. Replace the default avatar name in the Captions.ai node with your own.
  6. Fill in your social media account IDs in the "Assign Platform IDs" node.
  7. Test by sending a TikTok URL to your Telegram bot—watch the automation run.

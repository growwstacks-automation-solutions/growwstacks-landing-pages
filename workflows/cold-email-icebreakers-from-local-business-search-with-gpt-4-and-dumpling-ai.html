Automate Cold Email Icebreakers with AI

Generate personalized cold email icebreakers for local businesses automatically using GPT-4, Dumpling AI, and Google Sheets.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Cold outreach to local businesses suffers from a fundamental problem: generic emails get ignored. Researching each business manually takes 15-30 minutes per lead, making scalable personalization impossible for sales teams and agencies.

This automation solves that by combining AI-powered research with intelligent writing. It starts with a search keyword (like "dentists in Austin"), finds local businesses through Google Maps data, extracts website information, analyzes their services, and generates genuinely personalized icebreaker messages using GPT-4. The entire process runs automatically, logging results to Google Sheets and optionally pushing qualified leads to your outreach platform.

How It Works

1. Trigger with Search Criteria

The workflow begins with a form or manual trigger where you input your target search criteria—business type, location, and other parameters. This sets the scope for your outreach campaign.

2. Local Business Discovery

Using Dumpling AI's Google Maps API, the system searches for businesses matching your criteria. It extracts key details: business name, address, phone number, and most importantly, website URLs for further research.

3. Website Analysis & Data Extraction

Each business website is analyzed by Dumpling AI to extract a comprehensive summary of services, specialties, and unique selling points. The system also searches for contact email addresses during this stage.

4. AI-Powered Icebreaker Generation

The website summary and business details are sent to GPT-4 with a carefully crafted prompt that generates warm, personalized icebreaker messages. The AI references specific details from the business's actual offerings.

5. Data Validation & Organization

Results are filtered to remove businesses without valid email addresses. All data—business info, website summary, and generated icebreaker—is structured and prepared for output.

6. Output to Your Systems

The final data is logged to Google Sheets for review and management. Optionally, leads can be pushed directly to outreach platforms like Instantly.ai or your CRM for immediate campaign integration.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for digital marketing agencies offering local business services, B2B sales teams targeting specific verticals, freelancers providing lead generation services, and startups building their initial customer base. Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in under 30 minutes:

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file
  2. Configure API credentials for Dumpling AI and OpenAI in their respective nodes
  3. Connect your Google Sheets by authenticating and specifying the target spreadsheet
  4. Test with a single search term like "plumbers in Boston" to verify the flow works end-to-end
  5. Adjust the GPT-4 prompt to match your brand voice and outreach goals
  6. Set up scheduling to run the workflow daily or weekly for consistent lead generation

What This Workflow Does

Lost leads represent missed revenue opportunities. This automation solves the tedious, time-consuming process of manually reviewing old email threads to craft personalized re-engagement messages. Instead of sending generic "checking in" emails that get ignored, this workflow uses AI to analyze each lead's specific conversation history and generate contextually relevant outreach that actually gets responses.

The system automatically pulls cold leads from your Google Sheets tracker, fetches their entire email history from Outlook (or Gmail), summarizes key discussion points using OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini, and creates draft emails with personalized subject lines and body content. You maintain complete control—the emails are created as drafts for your review before sending, ensuring quality while saving 80-90% of the manual work.

How It Works

Step 1: Import Cold Leads from Google Sheets

The workflow begins by reading your cold lead spreadsheet. It extracts contact names, email addresses, and any relevant notes or tags you've added. This serves as your "re-engagement queue" that the automation processes systematically.

Step 2: Fetch Historical Email Conversations

For each lead, the system queries your Outlook account (via Microsoft Graph API) to retrieve all emails exchanged with that contact since a specified date. It gathers subject lines, body content, timestamps, and attachment metadata to build a complete conversation history.

Step 3: AI Analysis and Strategy Generation

The aggregated email data is sent to OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini model with specific instructions to: summarize the previous conversation, identify the lead's expressed interests or pain points, and suggest 2-3 strategic approaches for re-engagement. The AI considers timing, context, and relationship stage.

Step 4: Draft Personalized Email Creation

Using the analysis from step 3, the AI generates a complete email draft including a personalized subject line, opening line referencing past conversations, value proposition aligned with their interests, and clear call-to-action. The tone matches your brand voice and the relationship's history.

Step 5: Save Results and Create Draft

The workflow updates your Google Sheets with the AI's summary, re-engagement strategy, and generated email content. Simultaneously, it creates a draft email in your Outlook account with all fields populated, ready for your final review and sending.

Pro tip: Schedule this workflow to run weekly or bi-weekly. This creates a consistent "lead revival" system that automatically works through your cold pipeline without manual intervention.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Configure credentials for Google Sheets, Microsoft Outlook, and OpenAI in n8n's credentials management
  3. Update the Google Sheets node with your actual spreadsheet ID and worksheet name
  4. Adjust the date filter in the Outlook node to match your desired lookback period (default: emails since Jan 1, 2025)
  5. Test with 2-3 leads first to verify the AI output quality matches your expectations
  6. Schedule execution using n8n's scheduler trigger for automated weekly processing

Important: Always review AI-generated drafts before sending. While the automation handles 95% of the work, human oversight ensures brand voice consistency and prevents any potential AI misinterpretations.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious, time-consuming process of creating fresh visual content for social media, marketing, and education. Instead of manually filming nature scenes or purchasing expensive stock footage, this workflow leverages cutting-edge AI to generate professional, 10-second nature videos on a schedule and deliver them instantly to your Telegram channel.

It connects Google Gemini for intelligent prompt generation, Kie AI's Sora 2 model for high-quality video synthesis, and Telegram for seamless distribution. The result is a fully automated pipeline that produces watermark-free, portrait-format videos with natural ambient audio—ready for commercial use.

Pro tip: This workflow is perfect for content creators who need daily B-roll, marketers requiring themed visuals for campaigns, or educators wanting to deliver randomized nature clips to student groups.

How It Works

Step 1: Schedule Trigger & Prompt Generation

A schedule trigger (e.g., daily at 9 AM) initiates the workflow. Google Gemini then crafts a detailed, cinematography-focused prompt for a nature scene—like "misty forest sunrise with gentle bird sounds"—ensuring each video is unique and professionally described.

Step 2: AI Video Synthesis with Kie AI

The generated prompt is sent to the Kie AI API, which uses the Sora 2 text-to-video model to create a 10-second video. The workflow handles asynchronous processing, polling for completion, and capturing the webhook callback when the video is ready.

Step 3: Download & Delivery to Telegram

Once the video is generated, it's downloaded and sent directly to your specified Telegram chat or group. The entire process runs autonomously, requiring zero manual intervention after setup.

What You'll Need

  1. A running n8n instance with HTTP Request and LangChain nodes enabled.
  2. Google Gemini API access (free tier available) for prompt generation.
  3. Kie AI API account (supports Sora 2 text-to-video) for video creation.
  4. A Telegram Bot token and target chat ID (easily created via @BotFather).

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the downloaded JSON workflow template into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure credentials for Google Gemini, Kie AI (HTTP Header Auth), and Telegram API nodes.
  3. Update the Telegram node's chat ID with your target chat (personal or group).
  4. Adjust the Schedule Trigger interval to match your desired frequency (daily, weekly, etc.).
  5. Activate the workflow and monitor the first execution to ensure video delivery.

Pro tip: For local testing, use ngrok to make your n8n instance publicly accessible for webhook callbacks.

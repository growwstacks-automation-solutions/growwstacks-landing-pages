What This Workflow Does

This automation revolutionizes film location scouting by combining Google Maps data with AI-powered analysis. It automatically identifies potential shooting locations based on your criteria, evaluates their suitability through AI, and organizes all information in a searchable Google Sheets database.

For filmmakers and content creators, this eliminates hours of manual research. The workflow captures key details like location photos, addresses, accessibility notes, and AI-generated commentary about lighting conditions, architectural features, and potential filming challenges.

How It Works

1. Location Search Initiation

The workflow starts by querying Google Maps Places API with your search parameters (e.g., "industrial warehouses in Detroit" or "Victorian homes in San Francisco").

2. Data Collection

For each location found, the system gathers photos, addresses, business hours, Google ratings, and other relevant metadata from Google Maps.

3. AI Analysis

An AI model reviews each location's visual and contextual data, generating a "Director's Commentary" highlighting cinematic potential, lighting conditions, and unique features.

4. Database Population

All collected information is automatically organized in Google Sheets with standardized formatting, making it easy to sort and filter locations.

5. Notification

When the search is complete, you receive a Slack notification with a link to your newly populated location database.

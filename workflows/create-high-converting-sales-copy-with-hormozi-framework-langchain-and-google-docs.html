What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical problem for marketers, founders, and copywriters: creating personalized, high-converting sales copy at scale. Instead of spending days analyzing customer reviews and writing variations manually, this workflow uses AI to do it in minutes.

The system takes your product's Amazon reviews (or any customer feedback stored in Google Sheets), analyzes them using LangChain and AI, applies Alex Hormozi's value-stacking framework and Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs, and generates multiple targeted sales page variants in Google Docs. Each variant addresses different customer personas with tailored messaging, objections, and urgency triggers.

Beyond just generating copy, it creates a reusable customer analysis document that identifies core desires, pain points, and psychological triggers—giving you insights that improve all your marketing, not just one piece of copy.

How It Works

Step 1: Data Collection & Setup

The workflow starts with a form trigger where you input your Google Drive folder IDs, base copy URL, and options. It connects to your Google Sheets containing customer reviews and your Google Docs with product features.

Step 2: Customer Insight Extraction

AI samples and analyzes customer reviews to extract voice-of-customer (VOC) insights. It identifies recurring themes, emotional triggers, specific language used by customers, and maps these to psychological needs (safety, belonging, esteem, etc.).

Step 3: Framework Application

Using LangChain with OpenRouter LLM, the system applies Hormozi's $100M Offers principles: dream outcome, perceived value, proof, and scarcity. It drafts headlines, hooks, and body copy that stack value and overcome specific objections.

Step 4: Persona-Targeted Variant Creation

The workflow generates multiple customer personas based on the review analysis. For each persona, it creates copy with appropriate tone, urgency levels, and objection-handling. A loop then writes one Google Doc per variant in your designated folder.

Step 5: Output & Organization

All generated sales pages are saved as separate Google Docs with clear naming conventions. The customer analysis insights are saved to a second folder for future marketing strategy use.

What You'll Need

  1. Google Sheets with customer reviews (Amazon reviews or other feedback)
  2. Google Docs containing your base sales copy and product features/USPs
  3. Two Google Drive folders (one for customer analysis, one for generated copy)
  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with Google OAuth2 credentials configured
  5. OpenRouter API key or access to another LLM provider via LangChain
  6. Basic understanding of your target customer demographics and key objections

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure credentials for Google Sheets OAuth2 and Google Docs OAuth2 in n8n.
  3. Set up OpenRouter or your preferred LLM provider in the LangChain node.
  4. Share your Google Drive folders and copy the folder IDs (text after "folders/").
  5. Update the workflow variables with your folder IDs, file names, and sheet URL.
  6. Test with a small batch of reviews first (set "Number of reviews to use" to 5-10).
  7. Run the workflow and check the generated Google Docs in your output folder.

Pro tip: Start by analyzing just your 5-star and 1-star reviews first. These contain the strongest emotional language and reveal both what customers love most and what frustrates them—perfect for crafting compelling before/after narratives.

Automate Meeting Minutes with AI & Telegram

Turn post-meeting voice notes or texts into structured minutes, saved to Airtable and shared via Slack & Gmail automatically.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the universal problem of meeting documentation lag. After any meeting—client call, team sync, or project review—you simply send a message or voice note to a Telegram bot. The workflow automatically transcribes voice to text, uses AI to extract key decisions and action items, formats professional minutes, stores them in Airtable for searchable archives, notifies your team in Slack, and sends formal emails via Gmail to stakeholders.

It eliminates the 30-60 minutes typically spent after meetings on manual note-taking, formatting, and distribution. Instead of fragmented notes across different apps, you get consistent, structured documentation delivered instantly to all relevant systems. This ensures everyone works from the same information, action items are tracked, and nothing falls through the cracks between discussion and implementation.

How It Works

The workflow intelligently handles both text and voice inputs, processes them through AI, and distributes the results across your business tools.

1. Capture Meeting Content

Immediately after a meeting, you send either a text summary or a voice recording to your dedicated Telegram bot. The workflow triggers instantly, detecting whether the input is text or audio.

2. Process & Structure with AI

Voice notes are transcribed using OpenAI's speech-to-text. Both text and transcriptions are then sent to GPT models specifically prompted to extract: key decisions, action items with owners and deadlines, discussion highlights, and follow-up requirements. The AI formats this into professional meeting minutes.

3. Store in Central Database

The structured minutes are automatically saved to your Airtable base with consistent fields: meeting date, participants, decisions, action items, and status. This creates a searchable archive of all meetings that integrates with your existing project management and CRM systems.

4. Distribute to Teams & Stakeholders

Instant notifications go to relevant Slack channels with the minutes summary. Simultaneously, formal email versions are sent via Gmail to internal teams and external stakeholders, ensuring everyone receives documentation in their preferred communication channel.

What You'll Need

  1. Telegram account with a bot created via BotFather (free)
  2. OpenAI API key for GPT processing and voice transcription
  3. Airtable base with fields for: Email, Subject, Report, Date, Participants
  4. Slack workspace with appropriate channel access
  5. Gmail account connected via OAuth2 in n8n
  6. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow

Quick Setup Guide

Import and configure this workflow in under 15 minutes with these steps:

  1. Download & Import: Click the download button above, then import the JSON file into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure Telegram: Create a bot via @BotFather, get the token, and add it to the Telegram trigger node.
  3. Connect AI Services: Add your OpenAI API key to the transcription and GPT nodes.
  4. Link Airtable: Connect your Airtable account and select the base and table for storage.
  5. Set Up Notifications: Connect Slack and Gmail nodes, specifying channels and email templates.
  6. Test & Activate: Send a test message to your Telegram bot and verify the complete flow works.

Pro tip: Customize the GPT prompt in the workflow to match your specific meeting formats—add your company's terminology, required sections, or compliance language for consistent documentation.

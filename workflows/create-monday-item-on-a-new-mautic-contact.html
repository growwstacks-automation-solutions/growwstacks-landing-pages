What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common challenge for marketing and sales teams - keeping your CRM and project management tools in sync. When a new contact enters your Mautic marketing automation platform, this workflow automatically creates a corresponding item in your Monday.com sales board.

The template handles the basic field mapping between systems, creating Monday.com items with the contact's name, email, and other key details. You can easily extend it to include custom fields, lead scores, or campaign tags from Mautic to provide richer context for your sales team.

How It Works

1. New Contact Trigger

The workflow starts when Mautic detects a new contact in your database. This could be from a form submission, imported list, or other acquisition channel.

2. Data Preparation

The contact details are extracted and formatted for Monday.com's API. The workflow handles any necessary data transformations to match field formats between the systems.

3. Monday.com Item Creation

The workflow creates a new item in your specified Monday.com board, populating the relevant columns with contact information from Mautic.

Pro tip: Add a Function node to implement business rules - like only creating Monday.com items for leads that meet certain scoring thresholds.

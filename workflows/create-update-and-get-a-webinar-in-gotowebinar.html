What This Workflow Does

Managing webinars manually is time-consuming and error-prone. Every new webinar requires repetitive setup: creating the event, configuring settings, updating details, and tracking registrations across multiple systems. This process can take 2-3 hours per webinar and often leads to inconsistencies in branding, scheduling errors, and missed follow-up opportunities.

This GoToWebinar automation workflow solves these problems by providing a standardized, automated system for webinar management. It allows you to create new webinars with predefined templates, update existing webinar details programmatically, and retrieve webinar information for reporting and integration purposes. The workflow ensures consistency across all your webinars while freeing up your marketing team to focus on content creation and audience engagement rather than administrative tasks.

By automating these core webinar management functions, businesses can scale their webinar programs without proportional increases in staff time, reduce human error in scheduling and configuration, and create a reliable foundation for more advanced automation like attendee segmentation and personalized follow-ups.

How It Works

The workflow follows a logical sequence to manage webinars from creation through updates and reporting.

Step 1: Webinar Creation

The first node connects to your GoToWebinar account and creates a new webinar based on your input parameters. You can define the title, description, date, time, duration, presenters, and registration settings. The workflow can pull this information from a database, spreadsheet, or form submission, ensuring consistent formatting and complete information every time.

Step 2: Webinar Update

After creation, the workflow can update webinar details as needed. This is particularly useful for correcting information, adding late-breaking details, or modifying schedules. The update node ensures all changes are propagated immediately and consistently, eliminating the risk of outdated information being displayed to registrants.

Step 3: Information Retrieval

The final node retrieves comprehensive webinar information, including registration counts, attendee lists, and engagement metrics. This data can be sent to your CRM, analytics dashboard, or reporting tools, providing real-time visibility into your webinar performance without manual data extraction.

Pro tip: Use this workflow as a foundation for more complex automation. Once you have webinar creation automated, you can add nodes to automatically promote new webinars on social media, send personalized invitations to segmented email lists, and trigger post-webinar follow-up sequences based on attendance data.

Quick Setup Guide

Import and configure this workflow in under 15 minutes with these steps:

  1. Download the template using the button above and save the JSON file to your computer.
  2. Import into n8n by navigating to your n8n instance, clicking "Workflows" → "Import from file" and selecting the downloaded JSON.
  3. Configure GoToWebinar credentials by clicking the GoToWebinar nodes and adding your API credentials (organizer key and access token).
  4. Customize webinar parameters by modifying the fields in the first node to match your typical webinar setup (titles, descriptions, dates, etc.).
  5. Test with a dummy webinar by running the workflow once with test data to ensure everything connects properly before automating live webinars.
  6. Schedule or trigger the workflow to run automatically based on your needs (weekly, on form submission, or via API call).

Pro tip: Start by automating just one aspect of your webinar process (like creation), then gradually add more automation as you become comfortable with the workflow. This incremental approach reduces risk and helps your team adapt to the new processes.

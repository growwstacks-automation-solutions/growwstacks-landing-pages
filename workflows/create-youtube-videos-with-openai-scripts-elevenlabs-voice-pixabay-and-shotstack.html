What This Workflow Does

This complete automation system creates YouTube-ready videos from scratch without manual intervention. It combines multiple AI services to handle every step of the video creation process:

The workflow fetches stock footage from Pixabay, generates engaging scripts using OpenAI, converts text to natural-sounding voiceovers with ElevenLabs, assembles everything with professional transitions using Shotstack, and finally uploads the finished video to your Google Drive ready for YouTube publishing.

How It Works

1. Script Generation

OpenAI analyzes your topic and generates a video script optimized for engagement and SEO. You can customize tone, length, and style preferences.

2. Voiceover Creation

ElevenLabs converts the script into natural-sounding narration using AI voices. Multiple voice options are available to match your brand.

3. Visual Assembly

The workflow selects relevant stock footage from Pixabay and combines it with the voiceover using Shotstack's professional video editing API.

4. Final Output

The completed video is saved to your Google Drive in YouTube-ready format, with metadata and thumbnail automatically generated.

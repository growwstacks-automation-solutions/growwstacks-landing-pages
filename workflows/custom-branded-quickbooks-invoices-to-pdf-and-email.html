What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your QuickBooks Online invoices into professionally branded PDF documents and automatically emails them to clients. It solves the problem of generic-looking QuickBooks invoices by applying your custom branding, including logos and signatures, while eliminating manual processing steps.

The workflow triggers whenever a new invoice is created in QuickBooks, fetches all relevant data, generates a multi-page PDF with your branding, and sends it via your preferred email service. This ensures consistent, professional communication with every client while saving your accounting team significant time.

How It Works

1. Invoice Trigger

The workflow starts when QuickBooks creates a new invoice, either through the webhook or on a scheduled check.

2. Data Retrieval

Pulls complete invoice details including line items, client information, and payment terms from QuickBooks API.

3. Branding Application

Your company logo and signature are fetched from specified URLs and incorporated into the invoice design.

4. PDF Generation

Converts the branded HTML template to PDF format using Gotenberg or similar service.

5. Email Delivery

Sends the final PDF to the client's email address with your customized message template.

What You'll Need

  1. QuickBooks Online account with API access
  2. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  3. Gotenberg or similar PDF generation service
  4. Publicly accessible URLs for your logo and signature
  5. Email service credentials (SMTP or API)

Pro tip: Host your branding assets on a CDN for faster loading and reliability.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Configure QuickBooks API credentials
  3. Set up your PDF generation service endpoint
  4. Add your logo and signature image URLs
  5. Connect your email service provider
  6. Test with a sample invoice before going live

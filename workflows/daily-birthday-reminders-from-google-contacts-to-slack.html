Daily Birthday Reminders from Google Contacts to Slack

Never miss a birthday again. Automatically check Google Contacts daily and send personalized reminders to your team's Slack channel.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Forgetting a contact's birthday can mean missing a valuable opportunity to strengthen a professional relationship or celebrate with your team. Manual calendar checking is inconsistent and doesn't scale as your network grows. This automation solves that by creating a reliable, hands-free system that ensures every birthday gets noticed.

The workflow automatically scans your Google Contacts every morning, identifies anyone celebrating a birthday that day, and sends a clean, formatted notification to your designated Slack channel. It turns what was an administrative chore into a seamless process that builds rapport with clients, partners, and colleagues without any ongoing effort from you or your team.

How It Works

The automation follows a logical sequence that mimics what you'd do manually—but with perfect consistency and zero mental overhead.

Step 1: Daily Trigger

A schedule trigger runs the workflow each morning at a time you specify (like 8 AM). This ensures the check happens consistently before your workday begins, giving your team ample time to acknowledge birthdays.

Step 2: Contact Retrieval

The workflow connects to your Google Contacts via OAuth and retrieves your contact list, including birthday fields. It handles pagination automatically, so even thousands of contacts are processed reliably.

Step 3: Date Comparison

A filter node compares each contact's birthday with today's date, isolating only those whose birthday matches. The logic accounts for different date formats and time zones to ensure accuracy.

Step 4: Slack Notification

For each matching contact, a personalized message is sent to your chosen Slack channel. The message can include the contact's name, company, and even custom emojis or @mentions to specific team members.

What You'll Need

  1. Google Account with contacts that have birthday information populated
  2. Slack workspace with permissions to create incoming webhooks or use a Slack app
  3. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow
  4. 5-10 minutes for initial setup and configuration
  5. A designated Slack channel like #birthdays, #team, or #client-relations for notifications

Quick Setup Guide

Getting this automation running takes just a few simple steps. The template handles the complex logic—you just need to connect your accounts.

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Connect Google Contacts by authenticating with OAuth in the Google Contacts node
  3. Set up Slack by creating a webhook or app connection and specifying your channel
  4. Adjust timing in the schedule trigger to match your preferred daily check time
  5. Test and activate by running the workflow once manually, then turning it on

Pro tip: Create separate Slack channels for different contact types—#client-birthdays for business relationships and #team-birthdays for internal celebrations. This keeps notifications organized and appropriate.

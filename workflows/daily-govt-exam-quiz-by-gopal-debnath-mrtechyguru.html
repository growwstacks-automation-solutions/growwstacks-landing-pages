Automated Daily Government Exam Quiz

Deliver automated daily quiz questions to students or subscribers via Email, Telegram, or SMS—straight from your Google Sheets. Perfect for coaching institutes, educators, and study groups.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical problem for educators, coaching centers, and content creators: delivering consistent, engaging study material without daily manual work. Manually sending quizzes is time-consuming, prone to errors, and difficult to scale.

The workflow automatically selects a random question from your curated Google Sheet database every day, formats it professionally, and distributes it through multiple channels simultaneously. It transforms your static question bank into an active, automated teaching assistant that engages your audience daily, reinforcing learning and building a study habit.

Beyond just sending questions, it includes explanations and correct answers, turning simple quizzes into valuable learning moments. This system works 24/7, ensuring your students or subscribers receive their daily dose of preparation exactly when they expect it, increasing trust and perceived value of your service.

How It Works

The automation follows a logical sequence that mimics what a human would do, but with perfect consistency and zero effort.

1. Daily Schedule Trigger

A Cron node triggers the workflow every day at 6:00 AM (configurable to any time). This acts as your reliable digital alarm clock, ensuring the quiz goes out consistently without you having to remember.

2. Fetch Random Question

The workflow connects to your Google Sheets via the Google Sheets node, reads your question database, and uses a Function node to randomly select one entry. This ensures variety and prevents repetition, keeping your content fresh.

3. Format Content

A Code or Function node structures the raw spreadsheet data into a clean, readable format. It organizes the question, multiple-choice options (A-D), identifies the correct answer, and attaches the explanation—creating a complete learning package.

4. Multi-Channel Distribution

The formatted quiz is then sent through parallel channels: via SMTP for email subscribers, through the Telegram Bot API for instant messaging users, and via Twilio for SMS delivery. This ensures maximum reach based on your audience's preferred communication method.

What You'll Need

  1. A Google Sheets document with columns for: Question, Option A, Option B, Option C, Option D, Correct Answer, and Explanation.
  2. Email SMTP credentials (from services like Gmail, SendGrid, or your hosting provider) to send emails.
  3. Telegram Bot Token and Chat ID (created via @BotFather on Telegram) for messaging delivery.
  4. Twilio account with phone number and API credentials if you want SMS delivery.
  5. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow.

Pro tip: Start with just one channel (like Email) to test the workflow, then add Telegram and SMS once you're comfortable. This reduces initial configuration complexity.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 30 minutes with these simple steps:

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance.
  2. Connect your Google Sheets by creating credentials in Google Cloud Console and pasting your Sheet ID into the Google Sheets node.
  3. Configure your email settings in the Email node with your SMTP host, port, and authentication details.
  4. Set up your Telegram Bot by creating a bot via @BotFather and adding the token and chat ID to the Telegram node.
  5. Add Twilio credentials if using SMS, including Account SID, Auth Token, and phone numbers.
  6. Test with a manual trigger before enabling the daily schedule to ensure everything works correctly.
  7. Activate the workflow and let it run automatically every day.

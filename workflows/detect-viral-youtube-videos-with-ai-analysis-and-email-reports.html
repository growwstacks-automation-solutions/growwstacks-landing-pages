What This Workflow Does

Manual YouTube trend research is time-consuming and inconsistent. Content creators, marketers, and analysts spend hours each week scrolling through videos, trying to spot what's gaining traction. This workflow automates that entire process.

It systematically discovers recently published videos in your niche, analyzes their performance against their channel's historical data, calculates a proprietary "Algorithmic Lift Score" to measure true virality potential, and delivers a concise AI-powered report straight to your email. You get data-driven insights without the manual work.

How It Works

1. Keyword-Based YouTube Search

The workflow starts by querying the YouTube Data API using your specified keywords. It filters results by recency (configurable from 1-30 days) and retrieves the top-performing videos based on YouTube's ranking.

2. Data Enrichment & Metric Collection

For each video found, it fetches detailed statistics: view count, likes, comments, and publication date. Crucially, it also retrieves the channel's subscriber count and total views to establish a performance baseline.

3. Virality Score Calculation

A custom "Algorithmic Lift Score" is calculated for each video. This metric emphasizes videos achieving high engagement relative to their channel's subscriber base, helping identify true breakout content rather than just popular channels posting.

4. AI Analysis & Summarization

The top 5 videos by score are sent to an AI model (like OpenAI). The AI analyzes patterns, identifies common themes in successful content, and generates a concise summary highlighting key trends and opportunities.

5. Automated Email Reporting

The final AI analysis is formatted into a clean HTML report and emailed directly to you. You receive a ready-to-read digest of what's trending in your market, delivered on your preferred schedule.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download & Import: Click the download button above to get the JSON file. In your n8n instance, go to Workflows → Import from file and select the downloaded template.
  2. Configure the Setup Node: Open the "Setup" node. Enter your search keyword, Google API key, how many days back to search (e.g., 7), number of videos to analyze (e.g., 20), and your recipient email address.
  3. Connect AI Service: Click the "OpenAI Chat Model" node and add your API credentials. You can adjust the AI instructions to focus on specific aspects of analysis.
  4. Connect Email Service: Click the "Send_Report" node and authenticate your Gmail account (or configure another email node like SMTP).
  5. Test & Schedule: Click "Execute Workflow" to test. Once confirmed, set the Schedule Trigger node to run daily or weekly based on your needs.

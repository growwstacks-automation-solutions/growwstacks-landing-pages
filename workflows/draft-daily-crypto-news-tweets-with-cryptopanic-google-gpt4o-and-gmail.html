What This Workflow Does

The cryptocurrency market moves 24/7, and staying on top of breaking news is essential for social media influence. Manually monitoring dozens of sources, researching stories, and crafting engaging tweets consumes 10-15 hours per week. This automation solves that by creating a "crypto intelligence agent" that works while you sleep.

This n8n workflow continuously scans CryptoPanic for high-impact news in your chosen niche (like DeFi, NFTs, or regulation). When it finds relevant stories, it automatically researches the full articles using Google Search, then uses GPT-4o to draft compelling tweet threads in your brand voice. Everything gets sent to your Gmail for final approval before any posting occurs, giving you complete control while eliminating the grunt work.

How It Works

1. Market Intelligence Gathering

The workflow starts by fetching real-time news from CryptoPanic's API. You can configure filters for specific cryptocurrencies, categories (like "adoption" or "regulation"), and minimum importance scores to avoid noise.

2. Narrative Filtering & Analysis

An AI "Narrative Analyst" examines each headline to determine if it matches your focus areas. This prevents irrelevant stories from progressing and ensures you only create content about topics that matter to your audience.

3. Autonomous Deep Research

For qualifying stories, the workflow uses Google Custom Search to find and extract the full source content. This provides context beyond headlines, allowing the AI to create substantiated tweets with actual data points.

4. AI-Powered Content Creation

GPT-4o acts as your "Tweet Architect," generating multiple tweet variations with appropriate hashtags, emojis, and engagement hooks. The AI follows your brand guidelines and focuses on creating shareable, informative content.

5. Human-in-the-Loop Approval

All draft tweets are sent to a designated Gmail folder with clear subject lines. You review, edit if needed, and approve with a simple reply. Approved tweets are automatically saved to Google Sheets for your content archive.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to run every 2-4 hours during peak crypto trading times (US, EU, and Asia sessions) to catch breaking news as it happens while avoiding information overload.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this crypto intelligence system in under 30 minutes:

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file.
  2. Configure credentials for OpenAI, CryptoPanic, Google Search, and Gmail in n8n's credentials management.
  3. Set your niche filters in the "Narrative Analyst" node—edit the prompt to include your focus areas like "DeFi," "Bitcoin ETF," or "Ethereum upgrades."
  4. Customize the tweet architect by adjusting the GPT-4o prompt to match your brand voice, hashtag preferences, and content style.
  5. Test with a schedule trigger set to manual, run once to verify all connections work, then set to your preferred interval (every 2-4 hours recommended).
  6. Monitor your Gmail folder for draft tweets, approve with simple replies, and watch your Google Sheets archive grow automatically.

Pro tip: Start with broader filters and narrow down based on what generates the best engagement. The Google Sheets archive becomes a valuable dataset showing which news categories perform best with your audience.

What This Workflow Does

Manual, personalized email outreach is a massive time sink for sales teams. Researching each lead, reading past conversations, and crafting a relevant draft can take 15–20 minutes per contact. This bottleneck limits how many quality conversations you can initiate, directly capping sales pipeline growth.

This n8n automation solves that by turning a targeted HubSpot contact list into a queue of pre-drafted, highly personalized outreach emails. It doesn't just insert a first name—it analyzes each contact's recent Gmail conversations to build a lightweight "persona," understanding their communication style, potential pain points, and goals. Then, using AI, it generates a concise sales email that mirrors their tone and aligns your offer with their inferred interests. The final draft is saved to Gmail for your review and send-off.

Essentially, it automates the research and first-draft process, freeing up sales reps to focus on what they do best: having conversations and closing deals.

How It Works

The workflow is a smart sequence that connects your CRM, email history, and AI to produce context-aware drafts.

Step 1: Targeted Contact Selection

A manual trigger initiates the process. The workflow connects to HubSpot and executes a search for a focused list of contacts. You define the filters—for example, hs_buying_role = DECISION_MAKER, specific industry, or a custom lifecycle stage. This ensures you're only spending automation resources on high-potential leads.

Step 2: Historical Context Gathering

For each contact in the list, the workflow uses the Gmail node to fetch up to 20 of their most recent email threads (both sent and received). This raw text forms the basis for understanding who they are and how they communicate.

Step 3: AI-Persona Extraction

This is the core intelligence layer. An Information Extractor node, powered by Google's Gemini AI, analyzes the email history. It scans for and summarizes key attributes: decision-making style, communication preferences, stated goals or motivations, mentioned pain points, work style, and even personality traits. This creates a structured "persona" snapshot.

Step 4: Tailored Draft Generation

Using the extracted persona and your predefined offer (set in a Variables node), a second Gemini AI node crafts the outreach email. The AI is instructed to mirror the contact's tone, address relevant pain points, and present your offer as a solution. It outputs a compelling subject line and a polished HTML email body.

Step 5: Draft Creation & Human Review

The final email subject and body are sent back to Gmail to create a new draft in your account, addressed to the contact. This is a crucial step—it allows a sales rep to quickly skim, make any subtle tweaks, and hit send, maintaining quality control and human oversight.

Pro tip: Start with a very small, hyper-targeted list (5-10 contacts) to test and refine the AI prompts. Tweak the persona attributes and email tone based on the drafts generated before scaling up.

What You'll Need

  1. A HubSpot Account with OAuth2 access to pull contact lists and associated data.
  2. A Gmail Account connected with appropriate permissions to read email threads and create drafts.
  3. A Google Gemini API Key to power the AI analysis and content generation steps. This is easily obtainable via Google AI Studio.
  4. An n8n Instance (cloud or self-hosted) to import and run the workflow.
  5. A Clear Offer & Target Audience: Know what you're selling and which HubSpot contact properties best define your ideal recipient.

Quick Setup Guide

Get this automation running in your environment in under 30 minutes.

  1. Download & Import: Click the "Download Template" button above and import the JSON file into your n8n instance.
  2. Connect HubSpot: On the "Get Contacts" node, authenticate your HubSpot account and update the filter properties to match your target segment (e.g., lifecycle stage, job title).
  3. Connect Gmail: Authenticate the Gmail nodes for both reading emails and creating drafts. Using the same account is recommended for simplicity.
  4. Add Your AI Key: In both Gemini AI nodes (Information Extractor and Chat Model), insert your Google Gemini API key.
  5. Set Your Variables: Locate the Variables node. Update the product_to_sell field with a clear description of your offer. Verify the contact field mappings (first name, last name, email) match your HubSpot property names.
  6. Test & Refine: Execute the workflow manually with a single test contact. Review the generated draft in your Gmail. Adjust the AI prompts in the nodes if you need to change the email length, formality, or call-to-action.
  7. Go Live: Once satisfied, you can run the workflow on a schedule or trigger it manually for new contact lists.

Pro tip: For compliance and privacy, the workflow is designed to process data and create drafts without storing sensitive email content long-term. All processing happens within the workflow execution.

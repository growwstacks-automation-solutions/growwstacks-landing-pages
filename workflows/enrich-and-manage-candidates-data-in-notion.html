What This Workflow Does

This automation streamlines your hiring process by automatically enriching candidate profiles with AI-powered assessments before interviews. When a candidate schedules an interview through Calendly, the workflow:

  • Captures their LinkedIn profile URL from the scheduling form
  • Analyzes their profile using Humantic AI for personality and skill insights
  • Creates a comprehensive candidate profile in your Notion hiring database

Recruiters and hiring managers gain valuable candidate insights before the interview, saving 3-5 hours of manual research per candidate while improving interview quality.

How It Works

1. Calendly Trigger

The workflow starts when a candidate schedules an interview through your Calendly booking page. Make sure to add a LinkedIn URL field to your Calendly invite form.

2. Humantic AI Analysis

The candidate's LinkedIn profile is analyzed by Humantic AI, which provides personality insights, skill assessments, and career trajectory predictions.

3. Notion Database Update

The workflow creates a new page in your Notion hiring database with the candidate's information, interview details, and AI-generated assessment.

Pro tip: Customize the Humantic AI assessment parameters to focus on the traits most important for your open roles.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solution solves the time-consuming process of manually researching LinkedIn profiles to gather professional information about leads. By connecting Google Sheets with Apify's LinkedIn Profile Enrichment actor through n8n, you can automatically enrich spreadsheet data with detailed professional information.

The workflow takes a list of LinkedIn profile URLs from your Google Sheet, processes them through Apify to extract structured professional data, and returns the enriched information to a new sheet. This eliminates hours of manual research while providing more accurate, up-to-date professional data than purchased lead lists.

The workflow interface showing how LinkedIn data flows into Google Sheets

How It Works

Step 1: Input LinkedIn URLs

You provide a Google Sheet containing LinkedIn profile URLs you want to enrich, with one URL per row in a designated worksheet.

Step 2: Apify Processing

The workflow sends each URL to Apify's LinkedIn Profile Enrichment actor, which extracts professional information while complying with LinkedIn's terms of service.

Step 3: Data Transformation

n8n processes the returned data to structure it consistently, handling any variations in how information appears across different profiles.

Step 4: Output to Sheets

The enriched data is written to a new worksheet in your Google Sheet, ready for use in your sales or marketing workflows.

What You'll Need

  1. An Apify account with access to the LinkedIn Profile Enrichment actor
  2. A Google account with Sheets access
  3. n8n installed (cloud or self-hosted)
  4. A list of LinkedIn profile URLs to enrich

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your Google Sheets and Apify credentials in n8n
  3. Configure the Google Sheet URL and worksheet name in the workflow
  4. Run the workflow to begin enriching your LinkedIn leads

Pro tip: Start with a small test batch of 5-10 profiles to verify the workflow works as expected before processing large lists.

What This Workflow Does

Manual invoice processing is a time-consuming, error-prone task that costs businesses hundreds of hours each year. This automation solves that problem by creating a complete system that receives invoice images or PDFs, uses AI to extract key data, validates the information, stores it in Google Sheets, archives the original document in Google Drive, and sends appropriate notifications via Gmail.

The workflow transforms chaotic paper or digital invoices into structured, searchable data automatically. It checks for duplicate entries, validates mandatory fields, and ensures your financial records remain accurate and up-to-date without manual intervention. This eliminates data entry errors, accelerates payment cycles, and provides real-time visibility into your accounts payable.

How It Works

1. Invoice Submission & AI Extraction

The workflow triggers when a user submits an invoice file via chat or upload. Google Gemini Vision AI analyzes the image or PDF to identify and extract key information including invoice number, date, vendor name, line items, quantities, prices, and totals.

2. Data Structuring & Validation

The extracted data is formatted into strict JSON structure. The system validates mandatory fields and checks for completeness. If critical information is missing, the workflow branches to handle exceptions appropriately.

3. Duplicate Detection & Database Check

The automation queries your Google Sheets database to check if the invoice already exists. This prevents duplicate entries and maintains data integrity across your financial records.

4. Data Storage & Document Archiving

New invoice data is appended to your Google Sheets master database while the original invoice file is uploaded to a designated Google Drive folder for permanent archival and audit trail purposes.

5. Notification & Alert System

Based on the outcome, the system sends appropriate Gmail notifications: success confirmations for processed invoices, duplicate alerts for existing entries, or error reports for missing information that requires manual review.

Pro tip: Start with a small set of regular vendors to train the AI model on your specific invoice formats. The system becomes more accurate over time as it learns your common patterns.

Automated Business Card Scanner: Extract Contacts with AI

What This Workflow Does

Manually typing contact details from business cards into your CRM or address book is a tedious, error-prone task that wastes valuable time. This automation eliminates that friction entirely.

This n8n workflow acts as an intelligent business card scanner. You upload a photo or PDF of a business card, and the system automatically extracts all relevant contact information—name, company, job title, email, phone numbers, address, and website—using AI. It then structures this data and saves it directly into a database or your preferred CRM, ready for use.

The process is seamless: from physical card to digital contact in seconds, with no manual typing required. It's perfect for sales teams, event organizers, recruiters, and anyone who networks regularly.

How It Works

The workflow combines optical character recognition (OCR) with large language model (LLM) intelligence to accurately interpret and structure data.

1. Upload & Convert

A web form trigger accepts uploaded business card files (JPG, PNG, PDF). The workflow converts the image into a base64-encoded string, preparing it for the OCR service.

2. Text Extraction with Mistral OCR

The image data is sent to the Mistral AI OCR API, which detects and extracts all visible text from the card, regardless of layout or font.

3. AI-Powered Interpretation

The raw extracted text is passed to OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini model. The AI agent is instructed to identify and categorize each piece of information—distinguishing names from job titles, personal emails from work emails, and office numbers from mobile numbers.

4. Data Structuring & Validation

A Structured Output Parser node ensures the AI's response matches a predefined schema (first name, last name, company, email, etc.). This validates the data format before it moves forward.

5. Database Upsert

The clean, structured contact data is then upserted (updated or inserted) into an n8n Data Table. The email address acts as a unique key to prevent duplicate entries, keeping your contact list organized.

Pro tip: For best results, ensure business card photos are well-lit and straight-on. The AI handles most layouts well, but clear images yield near-perfect accuracy.

Quick Setup Guide

Get this workflow running in your n8n environment in under 10 minutes.

  1. Import the Template: Download the JSON file using the button above and import it into your n8n workspace.
  2. Create Your Data Table: In n8n, create a new Data Table named business_cards with the following columns: firstname, name, company, jobdescription, phone, mobil, email, street, postcode, place, web.
  3. Configure API Credentials: In the workflow, add your Mistral AI API key to the HTTP Request node (Bearer Auth) and your OpenAI API key to the AI Agent node.
  4. Activate the Webhook: Turn on the Webhook node to generate a public URL for uploading cards.
  5. Test the Flow: Upload a sample business card image via the generated URL and verify the contact appears correctly in your Data Table.

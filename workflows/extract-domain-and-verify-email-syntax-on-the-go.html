What This Workflow Does

Email marketing success starts with a clean list. Invalid email addresses waste marketing budget, hurt deliverability rates, and damage sender reputation. Manual validation is tedious, error-prone, and doesn't scale.

This automation solves that by automatically extracting domain names from email addresses while validating their syntax correctness. It processes lists from any source—CSV imports, form submissions, CRM exports—and outputs clean, validated data ready for your marketing campaigns.

The workflow separates valid emails from invalid ones, extracts their domains for segmentation analysis, and provides a clear audit trail of what was cleaned and why. This ensures only properly formatted emails reach your sending platforms.

How It Works

1. Data Input & Preparation

The workflow accepts email data from various sources: manual trigger for one-off lists, scheduled triggers for regular cleaning, or API connections from your CRM. It normalizes the data structure for consistent processing.

2. Syntax Validation

Each email address undergoes pattern matching validation to ensure it follows correct email syntax (user@domain.com format). The workflow checks for missing @ symbols, invalid characters, and proper domain structure.

3. Domain Extraction

Valid emails have their domain portions automatically extracted (everything after the @ symbol). This allows for domain-based segmentation, competitor analysis, or organizational insights from your email lists.

4. Results Organization

The workflow outputs three clear datasets: valid emails with extracted domains, invalid emails with error reasons, and a summary report. These can be sent to different destinations based on your needs.

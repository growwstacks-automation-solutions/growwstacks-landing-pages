What This Workflow Does

Networking events create valuable connections, but manually tracking each person becomes overwhelming. You take LinkedIn screenshots, jot notes, then struggle to organize everything for timely follow-ups. This workflow solves that by automating the entire post-event process.

Simply submit a LinkedIn screenshot and your quick notes through a Google Form. AI vision technology reads the image, extracts profile details (name, title, company, bio), and combines them with your notes. The system then generates a personalized follow-up message draft and logs all data to a Google Sheets spreadsheet automatically.

You move from manual data entry and message drafting to having a complete, organized contact database with ready-to-send follow-ups within minutes after meeting someone.

How It Works

Step 1: Capture & Submit

After meeting someone, take a screenshot of their LinkedIn profile. Open a simple Google Form (added as a shortcut on your phone) and upload the screenshot along with any personal notes about the conversation.

Step 2: AI Vision Processing

The workflow receives the form submission, sends the screenshot to an AI vision model (like OpenAI GPT-4 Vision), which accurately reads and extracts text data from the image—identifying name, job title, company, location, and profile summary.

Step 3: Data Consolidation

The extracted LinkedIn data merges with your submitted notes. The system structures this into clean fields ready for database entry and message generation.

Step 4: Follow-up Generation

Using the consolidated data, AI creates a personalized follow-up message draft. It references the meeting context, the person's role, and suggests relevant next steps based on your notes.

Step 5: Automated Logging

All information—raw screenshot link, extracted data, notes, and generated message—is automatically appended to a Google Sheets spreadsheet. Your contact database grows without manual typing.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for professionals who regularly network: sales teams attending conferences, founders building partnerships, recruiters sourcing candidates, marketers connecting with influencers, and consultants expanding their referral network.

If you meet 10+ new people per month and want to maintain organized, timely follow-ups without administrative overhead, this workflow transforms your networking efficiency. It's especially valuable for teams where multiple members collect contacts that need centralized tracking.

Pro tip: Add the Google Form link as a shortcut on your phone's home screen. This gives you app-like convenience for quick submissions without downloading another app or remembering complex URLs.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow.
  2. Google account with access to Google Drive and Google Sheets for form processing and data storage.
  3. OpenAI API key (or alternative vision AI service) for image analysis and text extraction.
  4. Google Form configured with two fields: file upload for screenshot and text field for notes.
  5. Google Sheets spreadsheet with predefined columns for the extracted data.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template: Download the JSON file and import it into your n8n workspace.
  2. Connect credentials: Set up connections for Google Drive (read), Google Sheets (write), and OpenAI API.
  3. Configure Google resources: Update the workflow variables with your specific Google Form ID and Google Sheets spreadsheet ID/column headers.
  4. Adjust AI settings: Modify the OpenAI node parameters based on your cost/efficiency preferences (model selection, temperature, etc.).
  5. Test with one screenshot: Submit a test LinkedIn screenshot through your form to verify the entire pipeline—extraction, message generation, and sheet logging.
  6. Deploy and shortcut: Activate the workflow and create a phone home screen shortcut to your Google Form for real-world use.

What This Workflow Does

This automation revolutionizes the recruitment process by eliminating manual CV screening. When candidates email their resumes to your hiring inbox, the workflow automatically extracts key information, analyzes their qualifications against your job requirements using AI, and delivers ranked candidates to your team via Slack while updating your Google Sheets applicant tracking system.

The system handles the entire screening pipeline: parsing CVs (PDFs, Word docs), extracting skills/experience, matching against job descriptions, scoring candidates, and routing top applicants for review. This cuts screening time by 80% while improving hiring quality through consistent, bias-free evaluation criteria.

AI-powered CV parsing extracts structured data from resumes

How It Works

1. Email Trigger

The workflow monitors your Gmail inbox for new messages with attachments. When a candidate emails their CV, the system immediately processes the message without manual intervention.

2. Document Processing

Attachments are extracted and converted to text. The workflow handles PDFs, Word docs, and other common resume formats, using OCR when needed to extract text from scanned documents.

Automated document processing pipeline for different file types

3. AI Analysis

The system analyzes the parsed text using natural language processing to identify skills, experience levels, education, and other qualifications. It compares these against your predefined job requirements to generate a match score.

4. Decision Routing

Candidates are automatically categorized based on their match score. Top candidates are posted to Slack for immediate review, while all applicants are logged in Google Sheets with their analysis results for future reference.

Pro tip: Configure different Slack channels for different job openings to keep hiring teams organized.

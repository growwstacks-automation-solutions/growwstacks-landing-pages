What This Workflow Does

This workflow solves the time-consuming process of manually checking multiple job boards by automating the entire collection process. It connects to the JSearch API to fetch the latest postings, checks against your existing Google Sheet to eliminate duplicates, and saves only new opportunities while sending you a Telegram summary.

Recruiters and job seekers waste hours each week repeating this process manually. Our automation reduces this to minutes by handling the data collection, filtering, and organization automatically on a daily schedule.

How It Works

1. Daily Job Posting Fetch

The workflow starts by querying JSearch API with your predefined search parameters (location, job type, keywords etc.). It retrieves all matching postings from the past 24 hours.

2. Duplicate Checking

Each job's unique identifier is checked against your existing Google Sheet. Only jobs not previously recorded proceed to the next steps.

3. Data Formatting

Relevant fields (title, company, location, URL etc.) are extracted and formatted for clean spreadsheet entry.

4. Google Sheets Update

New jobs are appended to your designated Google Sheet with all relevant details in organized columns.

5. Telegram Notification

A summary message is sent to your Telegram with the count of new postings added and direct links to view them.

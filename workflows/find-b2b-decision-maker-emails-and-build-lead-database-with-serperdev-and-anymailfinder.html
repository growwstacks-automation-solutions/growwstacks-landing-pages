What This Workflow Does

Manual B2B lead generation is time-consuming, expensive, and often yields low-quality contacts. Sales teams waste hours searching for company websites, guessing email formats, and dealing with bounced emails. This creates bottlenecks in outbound campaigns and limits revenue growth.

This automation solves these problems by transforming a simple list of company names into a complete, validated prospect database. It automatically discovers official company domains, extracts decision maker emails (CEO, Sales, Marketing), validates email quality, and organizes everything in a structured database ready for outreach. You get targeted contacts with verified deliverability, not generic info@ addresses.

The system handles everything from intelligent web searching to email validation and duplicate prevention. It processes hundreds of companies automatically, saving 20+ hours per week on manual research while providing higher-quality leads than most paid lead generation services.

How It Works

Phase 1: Intelligent Domain Discovery

The workflow starts by retrieving companies from your database. It uses Serper.dev to search for official company websites, then employs AI to analyze search results and identify the correct domain. This ensures you're targeting actual company websites, not irrelevant or outdated pages.

Phase 2: Multi-Role Email Extraction

Once domains are confirmed, the system queries AnyMailFinder for three key decision maker roles: CEO/executive, sales leadership, and marketing leadership. Each email is validated for deliverability and classified as "valid" or "risky" to prioritize outreach efforts.

Phase 3: Database Organization & Backup

Validated contacts are stored in your database with full details including name, position, email status, and LinkedIn URLs when available. For companies where specific decision makers aren't found, the system performs a bulk company email search as a fallback strategy.

Phase 4: Status Tracking & Error Handling

The workflow tracks processing status for each company, includes rate limiting to respect API limits, and continues processing even if individual companies fail. This ensures reliable operation when processing large lists.

Gather Leads in Google Sheet and Mailchimp

Automatically capture leads into Google Sheets and sync them to Mailchimp for automated marketing campaigns.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Manual lead management is a time-consuming bottleneck for growing businesses. When leads come from various sources—website forms, event registrations, partnerships—copying them individually into your marketing platform wastes hours and delays campaign activation.

This automation solves that by creating a seamless pipeline from lead capture to marketing activation. It automatically collects lead data into a central Google Sheet, validates and formats the information, then instantly adds qualified leads to your Mailchimp lists for targeted email sequences. The result is faster response times, cleaner data, and more consistent follow-up.

How It Works

Step 1: Lead Capture Trigger

The workflow starts when new data arrives in your designated Google Sheet. This could be from a connected form, API, or manual entry. The automation detects new rows and prepares them for processing.

Step 2: Data Validation & Cleaning

Before sending leads to Mailchimp, the workflow checks for duplicates, validates email formats, standardizes phone numbers, and ensures required fields are complete. This prevents low-quality data from entering your campaigns.

Step 3: Mailchimp Integration

Cleaned lead records are automatically added to your specified Mailchimp list. The workflow can also assign tags based on lead source or other attributes, enabling segmented campaign targeting right from the start.

Step 4: Confirmation & Logging

The automation logs each successful addition and can send notifications to your team or update a status column in the Sheet. This creates transparency and allows for easy monitoring of the pipeline.

What You'll Need

  1. A Google Sheets spreadsheet configured to receive lead data.
  2. A Mailchimp account with an active list for lead enrollment.
  3. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the automation.
  4. API credentials for Google Sheets and Mailchimp integrations.
  5. Basic understanding of your lead data structure (columns, required fields).

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template JSON file and import it into your n8n workspace.
  2. Configure the Google Sheets node with your spreadsheet ID and sheet name.
  3. Set up the Mailchimp node with your API key and target list ID.
  4. Adjust validation rules to match your data quality requirements.
  5. Test the workflow with sample data to ensure proper functioning.
  6. Activate the workflow and monitor initial runs for any adjustments.

Pro tip: Add a simple webhook trigger to your Google Sheet to make the workflow respond instantly to new entries, rather than running on a schedule.

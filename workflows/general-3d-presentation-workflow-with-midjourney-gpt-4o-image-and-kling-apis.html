What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical problem for businesses that need to showcase 3D models: creating professional spinning videos is time-consuming, expensive, and requires specialized skills. Traditional methods involve hours of manual rendering, animation setup, and video editing for each model.

The workflow automatically generates 360° or 180° spinning videos of high-quality 3D models by connecting Midjourney for initial concept generation, GPT-4o-image for prompt optimization and detail refinement, and Kling APIs for video rendering. What used to take 8-12 hours of manual work now happens automatically in minutes.

For e-commerce stores, this means every product can have professional 3D presentations without hiring animators. For designers, it transforms inspiration into tangible spinning models efficiently. For content creators, it enables engaging visual content that stands out on social media and marketing channels.

How It Works

Step 1: Concept Generation with Midjourney

The workflow starts by sending your product description or design brief to Midjourney's API. It generates initial 3D model concepts based on your specifications, creating visual foundations that can be refined further.

Step 2: Prompt Optimization with GPT-4o-image

GPT-4o-image analyzes the generated concepts and optimizes the prompts for better 3D rendering. It adds technical details about lighting, textures, materials, and camera angles that improve the final output quality.

Step 3: 3D Model Processing

The optimized prompts are sent to 3D rendering services through the PiAPI interface. This stage converts the 2D concepts into proper 3D models with depth, texture, and proper geometry for spinning animation.

Step 4: Video Generation with Kling APIs

Kling APIs take the 3D models and create smooth spinning animations. You can configure rotation speed (360° or 180°), duration, lighting effects, and output format. The system handles all the complex camera work automatically.

Step 5: Delivery and Integration

Finished videos are delivered as MP4 files with URLs that can be integrated directly into e-commerce platforms, social media schedulers, or content management systems. The workflow can also trigger notifications when videos are ready.

Pro tip: Use this workflow in batch mode to process entire product catalogs overnight. Set it up once, and wake up to dozens of professional 3D videos ready for your website.

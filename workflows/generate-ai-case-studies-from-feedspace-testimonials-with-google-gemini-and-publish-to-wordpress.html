What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common bottleneck for marketing and content teams: turning raw customer testimonials into polished, publishable case studies. Instead of manually interviewing customers, writing drafts, and formatting content, this workflow automatically processes feedback collected via Feedspace forms, uses Google Gemini AI to generate complete case studies, and publishes them directly to your WordPress site.

The system listens for new testimonials, extracts key information (customer name, rating, feedback text), then instructs the AI to create structured content with varied angles, tones, and SEO-optimized titles. This ensures each case study feels unique while maintaining brand consistency. The final output is formatted HTML ready for WordPress publication.

Beyond saving time, this automation creates a scalable content engine. Every positive review becomes potential marketing material, strengthening your social proof and search visibility without additional team effort.

How It Works

The workflow follows a logical sequence from data collection to publication:

1. Trigger: Feedspace Webhook Capture

When a customer submits feedback through your Feedspace form, the workflow activates via webhook. It receives the testimonial data including reviewer details, rating, written feedback, and event context.

2. Data Processing & Filtering

The workflow extracts and structures the incoming data. It filters for text-based testimonials (skipping simple star ratings) and prepares the cleaned information for AI processing.

3. AI Case Study Generation

Google Gemini analyzes the testimonial and generates a complete case study following your instructions. The AI varies narrative style, structure, and focus points to avoid repetitive content while highlighting different value propositions.

4. Content Validation & Formatting

The generated content is parsed and validated. The workflow ensures proper HTML formatting, includes metadata like categories and tags, and prepares the final post structure for WordPress.

5. WordPress Publication

The completed case study is automatically published to your WordPress site via REST API. You can configure it to publish immediately, save as drafts for review, or schedule for specific dates.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in under 30 minutes:

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance
  2. Configure Feedspace webhook by copying the production URL from the Webhook node and adding it to your Feedspace automation settings
  3. Add your Google Gemini API credentials to the AI Model node
  4. Connect your WordPress account by entering your site URL and authentication details in the WordPress node
  5. Customize AI prompts in the AI Agent node to match your desired case study format and tone
  6. Test the workflow by submitting a sample testimonial through your Feedspace form
  7. Activate the workflow and monitor the first few publications for quality assurance

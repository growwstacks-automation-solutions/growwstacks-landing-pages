What This Workflow Does

Creating consistent, high-quality LinkedIn content is time-consuming. Between research, drafting, and editing, a single post can take hours. This automation solves that by combining AI efficiency with human judgment.

The workflow acts as your personal content assistant: you request a LinkedIn post topic via Telegram, the AI researches and drafts it, sends it back for your approval, and once approved, saves it to a content library. If you want changes, you provide feedback and the AI revises it—all within the same conversation.

This isn't just AI content generation; it's a complete human-in-the-loop system that ensures every post matches your brand voice while cutting creation time from hours to minutes.

How It Works

1. Request via Telegram

You send a message to your Telegram bot with a topic like "Write a LinkedIn post about AI in marketing." The workflow triggers immediately, capturing your request and initiating the content creation process.

2. AI Research & Drafting

The AI agent uses web search tools to gather current information about your topic, then generates a professional LinkedIn post draft complete with engaging hooks, relevant hashtags, and appropriate structure.

3. Human Approval Loop

The draft gets sent back to you on Telegram with a simple question: "Good to go?" You can approve it, reject it, or provide specific feedback for revisions. This keeps you in complete control of the final output.

4. Revision & Finalization

If you request changes, your feedback goes to a second AI agent that improves the post based on your instructions. The revised version returns for final approval before being saved to your content repository.

5. Content Storage

Approved posts get appended to a Google Sheet (or your preferred database), creating a ready-to-publish content library. You can schedule them directly or use them as inspiration for future content.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. Telegram bot token (free to create via BotFather)
  3. OpenAI or Google Gemini API key for AI text generation
  4. Tavily API key for web research (or alternative search tool)
  5. Google Sheets account for storing approved content
  6. Basic understanding of connecting APIs (we provide setup guide)

Pro tip: Start with a small monthly content budget for AI API calls—typically $10-20 covers dozens of posts. The time savings will far outweigh this minimal cost.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template: Download the JSON file and import it into your n8n workspace.
  2. Configure Telegram: Create a bot via BotFather, get your token, and connect it to the Telegram trigger node.
  3. Set up AI services: Add your OpenAI/Gemini API key and Tavily search credentials to their respective nodes.
  4. Connect Google Sheets: Authenticate with Google and specify the spreadsheet where approved posts should be saved.
  5. Test the flow: Send a test message to your Telegram bot and verify the entire approval loop works correctly.
  6. Customize prompts: Adjust the AI prompts to match your specific brand voice and content preferences.

What This Workflow Does

Creating client proposals manually is slow, repetitive, and often inconsistent. This AI-powered workflow transforms proposal creation from a manual task into a fully automated, professional process that saves hours per week and ensures every proposal meets your quality standards.

The system captures client and project details through a simple webhook, uses GPT-4 to generate a tailored, persuasive proposal, converts it to a professionally formatted PDF, emails it directly to the client, stores a copy in Google Drive for record-keeping, and notifies your team on Slack—all without manual intervention. This eliminates the bottlenecks in your sales process and ensures proposals go out quickly while maintaining consistent branding and messaging.

Whether you're a freelancer sending 5 proposals a week or an agency handling 50, this automation scales with your business. It reduces proposal creation time from hours to minutes, decreases human error, and provides full visibility into your proposal pipeline through automated notifications and centralized storage.

How It Works

1. Capture Client & Project Details

The workflow begins when client information arrives via webhook. This could be from a contact form on your website, your CRM system, or an internal request form. The system captures essential details like client name, email, project scope, budget range, timeline, and specific requirements.

2. AI-Powered Proposal Writing

GPT-4 analyzes the project requirements and generates a structured, client-ready proposal. The AI tailors the language to your brand voice, includes appropriate sections (executive summary, scope, deliverables, timeline, pricing), and creates persuasive content that addresses the client's specific needs and pain points.

3. HTML Proposal Formatting

The raw proposal content is converted into a clean, professional HTML layout with your company branding, logo, colors, and typography. This ensures every proposal maintains consistent visual identity and looks polished regardless of who "wrote" it.

4. PDF Generation & Delivery

The formatted HTML proposal is automatically converted to a high-quality PDF document. The system then emails this PDF directly to the client with a personalized message, and simultaneously saves a copy to a designated Google Drive folder for record-keeping and easy retrieval.

5. Team Notification & Response

Your sales or project team receives an instant Slack notification with key details: which client received the proposal, what it contains, and when it was sent. The workflow then returns a confirmation response to your original system, completing the automation loop.

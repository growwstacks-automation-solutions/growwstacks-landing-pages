What This Workflow Does

This automation harnesses OpenAI's Sora 2 video generation capabilities combined with GPT-5's advanced prompt refinement to create professional-quality videos from simple text descriptions or existing images. The workflow intelligently processes user inputs, enhances prompts for optimal results, and manages the entire video creation process automatically.

Key capabilities include handling both text-to-video and image-to-video generation modes, automatic quality optimization through AI-powered prompt refinement, and seamless integration with content management systems for immediate use of generated videos in your marketing or production workflows.

How It Works

1. Input Processing

The workflow begins by accepting either text prompts or image uploads from your preferred input source (web form, API call, etc.). For text inputs, GPT-5 automatically analyzes and enhances the prompt to maximize Sora 2's output quality.

2. Video Generation

Based on the input type, the workflow routes to the appropriate Sora 2 endpoint (standard or Pro version) through fal.ai's API, specifying duration and aspect ratio parameters as configured.

3. Quality Assurance

The system automatically checks generated videos for completeness and quality standards before making them available in your designated output location (cloud storage, CMS, etc.).

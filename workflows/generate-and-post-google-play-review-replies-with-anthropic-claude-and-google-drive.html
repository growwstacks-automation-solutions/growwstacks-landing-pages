What This Workflow Does

This automation solution empowers app developers and community managers by streamlining Google Play Store review responses. It eliminates manual review management while maintaining human oversight through a structured approval process.

The workflow combines AI-powered response generation with Google Drive organization and Play Store API integration to create a complete review management system. It reduces response time from days to hours while improving consistency and quality.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Review Collection

The workflow runs daily to fetch new reviews from all your connected apps using the Google Play Developer API. It captures the review text, rating, and metadata.

2. AI Response Generation

Anthropic Claude analyzes each review's sentiment and content to generate a draft response that addresses user concerns while matching your brand voice.

3. Human Review Process

Generated responses are organized in Google Sheets within a "ToReview" folder, allowing your team to refine or approve suggestions before posting.

4. Automated Posting

Approved responses are automatically posted back to the Play Store with proper attribution, while all activity is logged for compliance.

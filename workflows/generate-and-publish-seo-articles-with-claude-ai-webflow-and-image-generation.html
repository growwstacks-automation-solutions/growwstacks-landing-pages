What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of consistently producing high-quality, SEO-optimized content at scale. It combines Claude AI's advanced writing capabilities with Webflow's publishing platform to create a complete content production pipeline.

The workflow handles everything from initial content structuring to final publication, including AI-assisted drafting, SEO validation, image generation, and automated publishing. Businesses using this system can maintain a steady stream of optimized content with minimal manual effort.

How It Works

1. Layout Blueprint

Defines content structure before writing begins, including search intent mapping, internal link planning, and call-to-action placement. This ensures consistency and SEO alignment from the start.

2. AI-Assisted Drafting

Claude AI generates initial drafts while maintaining your brand's tone and style. The system pulls insights from your top-performing content to inform new pieces.

3. SEO Validation

Automated checks for keyword coverage, readability scoring, schema markup, and link quality ensure every piece meets SEO standards before publication.

4. Media Production

AI generates relevant images that are automatically added to your CMS library for inclusion in published articles.

5. Automated Publishing

Content is published to Webflow once approved, completing the pipeline from draft to live with minimal manual steps.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical business problem of video content creation being too slow, expensive, and difficult to scale. Traditional video production requires cameras, actors, studios, editors, and weeks of work for just a few minutes of content. This workflow eliminates all those barriers by connecting directly to HeyGen's AI video generation platform.

When you provide a script, the automation instantly sends instructions to HeyGen to create a professional video with your chosen digital avatar and voice. It manages the entire generation process, waits for completion, and delivers the finished video—transforming what used to take days into a process that happens automatically in minutes.

How It Works

Step 1: Script Submission

The workflow receives your video script through various triggers—this could be from a form submission, database update, scheduled run, or API call. The script includes all spoken content and any specific instructions for the video.

Step 2: HeyGen API Request

The automation sends a structured request to HeyGen's API with your script, selected avatar, voice preferences, background settings, and video dimensions. This happens instantly without any manual intervention.

Step 3: Status Monitoring

After initiating video generation, the workflow waits 30 seconds then checks the status. It continues checking at intervals until the video is either completed successfully or fails.

Step 4: Video Delivery

Once the video is ready, the workflow captures the download URL and can distribute it through your preferred channels—saving to cloud storage, sending via email, posting to social media, or integrating with your content management system.

Pro tip: Combine this with customer data from your CRM to create truly personalized video messages. Use merge tags to insert names, company details, or specific offers into the script automatically.

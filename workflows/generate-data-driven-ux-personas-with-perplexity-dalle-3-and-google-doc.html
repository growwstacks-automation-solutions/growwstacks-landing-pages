Automated UX Persona Creation with AI

Generate data-driven user personas using Perplexity AI, DALL·E 3, and Google Docs in minutes—not weeks.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Creating effective UX personas traditionally requires weeks of market research, user interviews, and data analysis. This automation eliminates that manual effort by generating data-driven personas in minutes. The workflow combines AI-powered market research with visual generation to create comprehensive user profiles that actually reflect your target market.

Unlike traditional persona creation that relies on assumptions, this system uses Perplexity AI to analyze real market data, OpenAI to structure findings, and DALL·E 3 to generate representative images. The result is a complete persona document in Google Docs with demographic details, behavioral patterns, pain points, and visual representations—all backed by actual market research.

How It Works

1. Trigger & Input Collection

The workflow starts with a web form where you input your website URL, target region, and industry. This information provides the foundation for market research and ensures personas are relevant to your specific business context.

2. Market Research with Perplexity AI

Perplexity AI conducts comprehensive market analysis based on your inputs. It gathers demographic data, behavioral patterns, psychographic information, and estimates market size for each persona segment. This research forms the data backbone of your personas.

3. Persona Structuring & Documentation

An AI agent transforms the raw research data into detailed persona descriptions with clear narratives. The structured personas include names, backgrounds, goals, frustrations, and behavioral characteristics. These are automatically published to a Google Doc with professional formatting.

4. Visual Generation with DALL·E 3

For each persona, DALL·E 3 generates a realistic image that embodies the demographic and psychographic characteristics. These images are saved to Google Drive and linked in the persona document, creating complete visual representations of your target users.

What You'll Need

  1. Perplexity AI account for market research and data gathering
  2. OpenAI API access for persona structuring and DALL·E 3 image generation
  3. Google Workspace credentials for Google Docs and Google Drive integration
  4. Self-hosted n8n instance (this workflow uses community nodes)
  5. Basic understanding of your target market and industry

Pro tip: Start with broader market segments and use the generated personas to identify which user groups to research further through interviews and testing. The automation gives you hypotheses to validate, not final answers.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious, time-consuming problem of manually processing PDF documents. Businesses in legal, insurance, research, and administrative sectors regularly receive PDFs that need to be read, summarized, and converted into actionable documents. Traditionally, this requires hours of manual work per week—downloading files, extracting key information, drafting summaries, and creating reports.

The workflow automates this entire process end-to-end. When a PDF is uploaded to a designated Google Drive folder, it triggers automatic text extraction, AI-powered summarization using GPT-4, creation of a formatted Google Document, and email notifications with key findings. This eliminates human intervention while ensuring consistent, accurate document processing.

Beyond simple extraction, the workflow can handle multiple PDFs simultaneously, aggregate content for comparative analysis, and customize output formats based on specific business needs. It transforms raw documents into structured, actionable information ready for decision-making.

How It Works

Step 1: PDF Detection & Extraction

The workflow monitors a specific Google Drive folder for new PDF uploads. When detected, it downloads the PDF and extracts all textual content using advanced parsing techniques. If multiple PDFs are present, their content is combined into a unified dataset for comprehensive analysis.

Step 2: AI Information Extraction

Extracted text passes through an AI information extractor that identifies structured data points—names, dates, addresses, amounts, clauses, and other critical information defined by the user. This customizable step ensures relevant details are captured accurately regardless of document format.

Step 3: AI Draft Generation

GPT-4 analyzes the extracted information and generates a professional draft document tailored to the user's requirements. Through prompt engineering, the output can be formatted as formal reports, executive summaries, contract abstracts, claim summaries, or research paper highlights.

Step 4: Document Creation & Notification

The AI-generated draft is automatically saved as a Google Document in the same Drive folder. An email is composed summarizing key points in 3-5 bullet points and sent to designated recipients with a direct link to the document. This completes the cycle from raw PDF to actionable document with notification.

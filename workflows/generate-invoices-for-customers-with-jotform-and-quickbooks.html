What This Workflow Does

Manual invoicing is a time-consuming, error-prone bottleneck for freelancers, consultants, and small businesses. This automation solves that by connecting Jotform (your order collection point) directly to QuickBooks Online (your accounting system).

Whenever a customer submits an order form for your product or service, this workflow automatically checks if they exist in QuickBooks, creates or updates their profile, retrieves the correct product/service item, generates a professional invoice, and emails it to the customer—all without you lifting a finger. It transforms a 15-minute manual task into a 15-second automated process, ensuring accuracy and accelerating your cash flow.

How It Works

The workflow follows a logical, step-by-step process to ensure every invoice is created correctly and delivered promptly.

1. Form Submission Trigger

The automation starts when a customer submits your Jotform order form. The workflow captures all submitted data: customer name, email, billing address, selected product/service, quantity, and any special notes.

2. Customer Lookup & Management

The system searches your QuickBooks customer list using the submitted email address. If the customer exists, it updates their record with any new information. If not, it creates a new customer profile automatically, ensuring your books are always up-to-date.

3. Product/Service Item Retrieval

The workflow fetches the correct product or service item from your QuickBooks item list based on what the customer selected in the form. This ensures pricing and descriptions are always accurate and match your catalog.

4. Invoice Creation

Using the customer and item data, the workflow generates a complete invoice in QuickBooks with all line items, quantities, prices, taxes (if configured), and payment terms. The invoice is saved to your QuickBooks account with a unique number for tracking.

5. Invoice Delivery

Finally, the system emails the invoice directly to the customer from QuickBooks, providing them with a professional PDF and a secure payment link. You can also configure notifications to your team when an invoice is sent.

What You'll Need

  1. A Jotform account with a working order or service request form.
  2. QuickBooks Online subscription with admin or accounting user access.
  3. n8n account (cloud or self-hosted) to run the automation.
  4. Your product/service items already set up in QuickBooks.
  5. Basic understanding of how to connect apps in n8n (guided setup included).

Pro tip: Before setting up, make sure your Jotform fields match the data points QuickBooks needs for invoices: customer name, email, billing address, item description, quantity, and price. This ensures smooth mapping during configuration.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in your business:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n workspace.
  2. Connect your Jotform account in n8n and select the specific form that triggers the workflow.
  3. Connect your QuickBooks Online account using OAuth authentication (n8n will guide you through this).
  4. Map the form fields to the corresponding QuickBooks fields (customer details, items, quantities).
  5. Test with a sample submission using Jotform's test mode to ensure the invoice generates correctly.
  6. Activate the workflow and start processing orders automatically!

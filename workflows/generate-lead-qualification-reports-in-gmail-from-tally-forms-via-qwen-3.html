Automate Lead Qualification with AI: Tally to Gmail via Qwen-3

Transform raw form submissions into actionable lead reports using AI analysis—delivered directly to your sales team's inbox.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Manual lead qualification is slow, inconsistent, and causes sales teams to miss high-potential opportunities. This automated workflow solves that by instantly processing Tally form submissions through Qwen-3 AI analysis and delivering structured qualification reports directly to Gmail.

The system captures comprehensive lead data—including company details, challenges, goals, budget, and urgency—then uses AI to generate executive summaries, identify key pain points, recommend action items, and highlight missing information. Your sales team receives enriched lead profiles in their inbox within seconds of form submission, complete with priority scoring and follow-up suggestions.

Beyond basic notification, this workflow implements security best practices to prevent prompt injections and ensures data privacy throughout the automation chain. It transforms your lead intake process from a manual bottleneck into a competitive advantage.

How It Works

1. Capture Tally Form Submissions

The workflow triggers automatically when a new submission arrives from your Tally lead qualification form. It captures all form fields including contact information, company details, challenges, goals, budget, and any additional notes provided by the prospect.

2. Structure and Prepare Data

Raw form data is parsed and organized into a consistent format. The workflow maps fields, validates essential information, and prepares the structured data for AI analysis while maintaining data integrity.

3. AI Analysis with Qwen-3

The core intelligence layer: Qwen-3 analyzes the lead data against your qualification criteria. It generates concise summaries, identifies primary challenges, assesses urgency, evaluates budget alignment, and provides actionable insights that would take a human reviewer 10-15 minutes to produce.

4. Generate Qualification Report

AI insights are compiled into a professional qualification report. This includes lead scoring, priority classification, recommended next steps, suggested follow-up questions, and any red flags or missing information that needs clarification.

5. Deliver to Gmail

The completed report is formatted into a clear, actionable email and sent directly to your sales team's Gmail inbox. The email includes all essential information at a glance, with clear sections for quick review and decision-making.

What You'll Need

  1. Tally account with an active lead qualification form and webhook integration enabled
  2. Qwen-3 API access through OpenRouter or another AI provider with appropriate credits
  3. Gmail account with app password or OAuth credentials configured in n8n
  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with webhook capabilities
  5. Lead qualification criteria defined for your business (budget ranges, ideal customer profiles, etc.)

Pro tip: Before implementing, document your current lead qualification process. Identify which form fields are most predictive of conversion and ensure they're included in your Tally form. This will maximize the AI's analysis accuracy from day one.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance
  2. Configure the Tally webhook by copying the webhook URL from n8n and adding it to your Tally form settings
  3. Connect your Qwen-3 API by adding your OpenRouter API key to the AI node credentials
  4. Set up Gmail connection using OAuth or app password authentication in the email node
  5. Customize the AI prompt to match your specific qualification criteria and industry terminology
  6. Test the workflow by submitting a test form and verifying the report arrives correctly in Gmail
  7. Activate the workflow and start processing real leads automatically

What This Workflow Does

Manual market research is time-consuming, expensive, and often inconsistent. This automation solves that by systematically extracting business data and customer reviews from Google Maps, processing them with AI (Gemini), and generating comprehensive market intelligence reports.

The workflow transforms unstructured review data into structured insights: competitor strengths/weaknesses, customer sentiment trends, pricing analysis, geographic demand patterns, and actionable recommendations. It delivers a ready-to-use report via email, saving hours of manual analysis each week.

Whether you're evaluating a new location, monitoring competitive moves, or understanding customer preferences, this system provides data-driven intelligence automatically. It's particularly valuable for restaurants, retail chains, service businesses, and investors assessing local market viability.

How It Works

Step 1: Data Collection from Google Maps

The workflow uses SerpAPI (or similar services) to search Google Maps for businesses in your target area. It extracts key details: business names, ratings, review counts, categories, and geographic coordinates. This creates a raw dataset of local competitors and market players.

Step 2: Review Extraction & Aggregation

For each identified business, the system pulls recent customer reviews—both text and star ratings. It batches this data efficiently to handle dozens or hundreds of businesses without hitting API limits. All reviews are compiled into a structured format ready for AI analysis.

Step 3: AI-Powered Sentiment & Theme Analysis

Google Gemini AI processes the aggregated reviews. It identifies common praise points, recurring complaints, sentiment scores, and emerging themes. The AI categorizes feedback into service, quality, price, location, and other business-specific dimensions.

Step 4: Competitive Landscape Mapping

The system compares businesses across metrics: average ratings, review volume, sentiment distribution, and keyword frequency. It highlights market leaders, underperformers, and gaps where customer needs are unmet. This creates a visual competitive positioning map.

Step 5: Report Generation & Delivery

A comprehensive market research report is assembled with executive summary, key findings, strategic recommendations, and 30/90/180-day action plans. The final report is formatted and emailed directly to stakeholders, ready for decision-making.

What You'll Need

  1. SerpAPI account (free tier offers 100 searches/month) for Google Maps data extraction.
  2. Google Gemini API key (free tier available) for AI analysis of review text.
  3. Gmail account or email service to send the final reports.
  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow.
  5. Target location coordinates or city name for the market research focus.
  6. Specific search queries (e.g., "vegan restaurants," "coffee shops") to narrow results.

Quick Setup Guide

1. Download the template JSON file and import it into your n8n workspace.

2. Configure the SerpAPI node with your API key and set your target location/search parameters.

3. Add your Google Gemini API credentials in the AI analysis node.

4. Set up the Gmail node with your sending credentials and recipient email address.

5. Test with a small location (one neighborhood) to verify data extraction and report formatting.

6. Schedule the workflow to run weekly or monthly for ongoing market monitoring.

Pro tip: Start with a specific niche query (like "pet-friendly hotels") rather than generic searches. This yields more focused, actionable insights faster.

What This Workflow Does

Cold emailing is broken. Generic templates get ignored, but personalizing each message manually takes hours. This automation solves that by combining LinkedIn data scraping with GPT-4 AI to generate genuinely personalized icebreakers for your outreach campaigns.

The workflow automatically pulls your lead list from Google Sheets, scrapes their LinkedIn profiles for specific details (job history, skills, posts, education), feeds that data to an AI agent, and outputs conversation-starting icebreakers you can plug directly into your email sequences. It transforms hours of manual research into minutes of automated, scalable personalization.

You get human-sounding, relevant opening lines that reference actual details from the prospect's background—not just their first name. This dramatically increases reply rates while saving your sales team countless hours.

How It Works

The automation follows a logical sequence to transform raw leads into personalized messages.

Step 1: Import & Filter Leads

The workflow starts by reading your lead list from Google Sheets. It looks for entries marked "un-enriched" and filters out any without valid email addresses, ensuring only qualified leads move forward.

Step 2: LinkedIn Data Enrichment

Using the Apify LinkedIn Scraper, the system visits each lead's LinkedIn profile URL and extracts key information: current role, company, past experience, skills, education, and recent activity.

Step 3: AI-Personalization Generation

The scraped data is structured and sent to OpenAI's GPT-4 model with a custom prompt. The AI analyzes the profile and generates a concise, relevant icebreaker that references specific details like career moves, shared interests, or company news.

Step 4: Output & Status Update

The generated icebreaker, along with the enriched lead data, is appended to a separate "Enriched Leads" sheet. The original lead's status is updated to "enriched" to prevent duplicate processing in future runs.

Pro tip: For best results, clean your lead sheet before running. Remove unnecessary columns and ensure LinkedIn URLs are in a consistent format. Process in batches of 50-100 leads to avoid API rate limits.

Quick Setup Guide

Get this automation running in under 15 minutes with these steps:

  1. Import the template: Download the JSON file and import it into your n8n workspace.
  2. Connect Google Sheets: Authenticate n8n with your Google account and point it to your lead sheet.
  3. Configure API credentials: Add your OpenAI and Apify API keys in the respective credential nodes.
  4. Customize the AI prompt: Edit the "Generate Personalized Icebreaker" node to match your industry, tone, and offer.
  5. Test with one lead: Execute the workflow once to verify everything works and the output matches your expectations.
  6. Schedule or trigger: Set the workflow to run daily, or trigger it manually when you have new leads.

Important: Always review AI-generated content before sending. Use the outputs as starting points, and add your own voice and context for maximum authenticity.

AI Product Description Generator for Shopify

Automatically generate SEO-friendly product descriptions, titles, and tags from product images using AI and publish directly to your Shopify store.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Manually writing product descriptions for every item in your Shopify store is time-consuming, inconsistent, and doesn't scale. This automation solves that by turning product images into complete, SEO-optimized listings in minutes.

The workflow receives a product image along with basic details like name and material type. It uploads the image to your hosting service, analyzes it with Google Gemini AI to generate compelling descriptions, tags, bullet points, and SEO metadata, then constructs and publishes a complete product listing to your Shopify store—all without manual intervention.

This transforms a process that typically takes 15-30 minutes per product into one that happens automatically in the background, freeing your team to focus on strategy and growth rather than repetitive data entry.

How It Works

Step 1: Receive Product Data & Image

The workflow starts with a webhook that accepts product information including an uploaded image file, product name, material type, and other key details from your source system (like a form, spreadsheet, or inventory management tool).

Step 2: Host Image & Analyze with AI

The product image is uploaded to your image hosting service (like imgBB) to generate a public URL for Shopify. Simultaneously, the image and product details are sent to Google Gemini AI, which analyzes the visual content and returns structured JSON containing title, description, tags, bullet points, alt text, and SEO metadata.

Step 3: Clean & Structure Data

The AI output is parsed and cleaned, then merged with the hosted image URL and your provided product variants, vendor information, options, and product type to create a complete Shopify API payload.

Step 4: Publish to Shopify

The finalized product data is sent to Shopify's API, creating a new product in your store with all generated content, images, and metadata automatically populated and ready for customers.

What You'll Need

  1. Google Gemini (PaLM) API credentials – For AI content generation
  2. Shopify private access token – To connect to your store
  3. Image hosting API key (imgBB or similar) – To store product images
  4. n8n instance – Self-hosted or cloud version
  5. Source system – Method to send product data and images to the workflow (form, API, spreadsheet, etc.)

Pro tip: Start by testing with a few products in a draft state before enabling automatic publishing. This lets you review AI-generated content and refine prompts without affecting live listings.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance.
  2. Connect credentials for Google Gemini, Shopify, and your image hosting service in the respective nodes.
  3. Configure product defaults like vendor, product type, and variant options in the Set nodes.
  4. Test the webhook by sending a sample product image and details to ensure proper flow.
  5. Connect your source system to trigger the workflow whenever new products need listing.
  6. Monitor initial runs and adjust AI prompts based on output quality for your specific products.

What This Workflow Does

This workflow transforms your n8n instance into a powerful product video generator that operates automatically whenever new products are added to your Google Sheet. It eliminates manual video creation by combining Google Sheets, Gemini AI for image enhancement, and FAL for video generation.

The system handles the entire process from product image to finished video, including downloading source files, applying AI enhancements, generating video clips, uploading final assets, and updating your tracking sheet - all without human intervention.

How It Works

1. Google Sheets Trigger

The workflow activates when a new row is added to your designated Google Sheet with status set to "run". This provides a simple interface for your team to request videos.

2. Image Processing

The system downloads the product image from Google Drive, converts it to base64 format, and sends it to Gemini AI to create a branded, ad-style variant based on your specifications.

Gemini AI transforms raw product images into branded marketing visuals

3. Video Generation

The enhanced image is sent to FAL's image-to-video service, which creates a short promotional clip. The workflow polls FAL's API until the video is ready.

4. Delivery & Tracking

Completed videos are uploaded to your Google Drive videos folder, and the original sheet row is updated with the video link and marked as complete.

Pro tip: Add a notes column in your sheet to provide specific creative direction for each product's video style.

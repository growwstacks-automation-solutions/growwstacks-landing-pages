What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually creating product videos for e-commerce and marketing purposes. It combines BrowserAct's web automation capabilities with AI video generation and Google Drive storage to create a seamless pipeline for product video production.

The workflow significantly reduces the manual effort required to create product videos at scale, enabling businesses to showcase their products more effectively while saving valuable time and resources. By automating the entire process from data collection to final video storage, it eliminates multiple manual steps that would otherwise require different tools and platforms.

How It Works

1. Product Data Collection

BrowserAct navigates product pages and extracts structured data including images, descriptions, and metadata. This eliminates manual screenshotting and data entry.

2. Image Processing

Each product image is downloaded and converted to Base64 format for API compatibility. The workflow handles this conversion automatically.

3. AI Video Generation

The system sends images with predefined prompts to an AI video API, then polls for completion status until videos are ready for download.

Example output showing AI-generated product video from source image

4. Storage & Organization

Final videos along with original images are automatically uploaded to Google Drive in organized folders for easy access and management.

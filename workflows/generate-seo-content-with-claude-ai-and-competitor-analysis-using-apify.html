What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of creating SEO-optimized content at scale. Instead of manually researching competitors and writing meta tags for each page, this workflow automates the entire process. It analyzes top-ranking competitor pages for your target keywords, extracts their heading structures and content approaches, then generates personalized meta titles, descriptions, H1 headings, and comprehensive content briefs using Claude AI.

The system connects Google Sheets (for your keyword list and client information), Apify (for Google search results), Firecrawl (for competitor website scraping), and Anthropic's Claude AI (for content generation). This creates a complete pipeline that transforms raw keywords into ready-to-use SEO content elements, saving hours of manual research and writing per keyword while ensuring data-driven optimization.

How It Works

Step 1: Input and Configuration

The workflow starts by reading your Google Sheets template containing client information, target keywords, and page specifications. It filters for valid keywords that need content generation and prepares batch processing.

Step 2: Competitor Research

For each keyword, the system searches Google via Apify to find the top 10 ranking pages. It then scrapes the first 5 competitor websites using Firecrawl, extracting their heading structures (H1-H6), meta tags, and content organization patterns.

Step 3: AI Analysis and Generation

Claude AI analyzes the competitor data alongside your client's specific information (business description, tone of voice, URL). It generates optimized meta titles (65 characters max), compelling meta descriptions (165 characters max), and user-focused H1 headings (70 characters max) using structured output parsing.

Step 4: Content Brief Creation

The AI develops detailed content briefs including search intent analysis, MECE page structures with H2/H3 sections, rich media suggestions, and writing recommendations with detail level scoring. All content is tailored to your specific business context.

Step 5: Results Integration

All generated content is compiled and written back to your Google Sheets, updating the appropriate columns while preserving existing data. The workflow continues processing remaining keywords in batches.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this SEO content automation:

  1. Copy the template spreadsheet from the provided link and fill in your client information and SEO keywords.
  2. Import the workflow JSON into your n8n instance using the import function.
  3. Configure all API credentials in n8n's credentials section for Google Sheets, Anthropic, Apify, and Firecrawl.
  4. Test the configuration by activating the workflow and sending your Google Sheets URL to the chat trigger.
  5. Review generated content in your spreadsheet and adjust system prompts if needed for better alignment with your brand voice.
  6. Scale up by adding more keywords to your sheet and letting the automation process them in batches.

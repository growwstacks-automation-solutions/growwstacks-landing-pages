What This Workflow Does

Creating high-quality, SEO-friendly blog posts is one of the most time-consuming tasks for content marketers and business owners. This workflow solves that problem by automating the entire process—from research to publication.

The system starts with a research query, uses Perplexity AI to gather current, authoritative information, then leverages OpenAI to generate a complete blog post with proper SEO elements. Finally, it publishes everything directly to your WordPress site as a draft, complete with featured image and metadata.

Instead of spending 6-8 hours per post on research, writing, formatting, and publishing, this automation reduces the process to minutes. You get consistent, well-researched content that's optimized to rank in search results and drive organic traffic to your site.

How It Works

Step 1: Research Trigger

The workflow begins when you submit a research topic or keyword. This can be triggered manually, scheduled regularly, or initiated by other events like trending topics in your industry.

Step 2: AI-Powered Research

Perplexity AI analyzes your query and gathers current information from authoritative sources across the web. This ensures your content is based on up-to-date data and industry insights.

Step 3: Content Generation

OpenAI processes the research data to create a complete blog post. This includes an engaging title, SEO-friendly URL slug, meta description, and properly formatted HTML content with H2/H3 headings.

Step 4: Image Processing

The workflow automatically selects and uploads a relevant featured image to your WordPress media library, ensuring visual appeal and proper optimization.

Step 5: WordPress Publishing

All elements—title, content, featured image, metadata—are published to your WordPress site as a draft. You maintain full control to review and schedule publication.

Step 6: Notification

Once complete, you receive a notification via Telegram (or your preferred channel) with a link to the draft and key details about the generated content.

What You'll Need

  1. A self-hosted or managed WordPress installation with REST API access
  2. OpenAI API credentials (GPT-3.5 or GPT-4)
  3. Perplexity AI API access for research capabilities
  4. Telegram bot token (for notifications, optional but recommended)
  5. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow
  6. Basic understanding of WordPress content structure and SEO principles

Pro tip: Start with a narrow topic focus for your first few automated posts. This helps the AI produce more focused, authoritative content that performs better in search results.

What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms product images into authentic-looking user-generated content (UGC) videos for eCommerce marketing. It combines OpenAI's Sora 2 for video generation with Google Gemini for scriptwriting and persona creation, delivering ready-to-use social media content at scale.

The workflow analyzes your product images, generates influencer personas that would authentically promote your products, creates natural dialogue scripts, and produces multiple video variations for A/B testing. All videos are automatically formatted for social platforms and stored in your preferred cloud storage.

How It Works

1. Product Analysis

OpenAI's vision API examines the uploaded product image to identify key features, benefits, and potential selling points that will resonate with your target audience.

2. Persona Creation

The system generates detailed profiles of ideal influencers/creators who would authentically promote this product, including demographic details and speaking style.

3. Script Generation

Gemini 2.5 Pro creates multiple 12-second UGC video scripts with frame-by-frame breakdowns, natural dialogue, and suggested camera movements.

4. Video Production

Sora 2 API generates the actual videos using the scripts and adapted product images as reference, creating authentic-looking influencer content.

5. Delivery

Completed videos are automatically uploaded to your designated cloud storage with organized naming conventions for easy access.

What This Workflow Does

This workflow transforms Telegram into a powerful dating assistant that leverages Google Gemini's AI capabilities. It acts as a full-featured CRM for your dating life, helping you craft better responses, remember important details about matches, and optimize your dating strategy.

The system analyzes text conversations, profile screenshots, and even voice notes to provide tactical advice. It maintains long-term memory about your matches' preferences and automatically tracks your dating pipeline in Google Sheets.

How It Works

1. Message Processing

The Telegram bot receives your messages, screenshots, or voice notes and routes them to the appropriate processing nodes.

2. Multimodal Analysis

Google Gemini analyzes text for sentiment and intent, while the vision model interprets profile screenshots. Voice notes are transcribed and analyzed for tone.

3. CRM Integration

The workflow checks your Google Sheets CRM to see if this is a new match or existing connection, pulling relevant history.

4. Response Generation

The AI generates three response options tailored to your defined "Rizz Style" (direct, mysterious, funny) which you can send with one tap.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious, time-consuming process of managing meetings and follow-ups manually. Sales teams, consultants, and client managers often waste hours each week copying meeting details from spreadsheets to calendars, sending confirmation messages, and trying to schedule follow-up meetings. This workflow eliminates that manual work entirely.

It operates through two synchronized branches: one automatically creates new calendar events from Google Sheets data and sends confirmations via WhatsApp and email. The second branch intelligently follows up on past meetings by using Gemini AI to analyze calendar availability and suggest optimal times for next meetings, then sends these personalized suggestions through multiple channels. The system ensures no meeting falls through the cracks while saving 5-10 hours per week per team member.

How It Works

1. Automated Meeting Creation

The workflow starts by checking a designated Google Sheet for new meeting entries. When it finds a new meeting request (filtered by status), it automatically creates an event in Google Calendar with all details—title, description, location, time, and color coding. Immediately after creation, it sends confirmation messages via both WhatsApp (using Rapiwa) and Gmail to all participants, then updates the sheet to mark the meeting as "sent" to prevent duplicates.

2. Intelligent Follow-up Scheduling

On a separate schedule, the system retrieves recently completed meetings from Google Calendar. It uses a "Mark as Seen" node to prevent processing the same event multiple times, then filters for meetings that require follow-up. The magic happens when Gemini AI analyzes the meeting context and checks calendar availability to suggest 2-3 optimal times for the next meeting, formatted into a professional, personalized message.

3. Multi-Channel Communication

All communications are sent through both WhatsApp and email simultaneously, ensuring maximum reach and response rates. The templates are customizable for different meeting types and client segments, maintaining brand voice while automating the entire communication cycle from initial confirmation to follow-up scheduling.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure credentials for Google Calendar, Google Sheets, Gmail, Rapiwa, and Gemini AI in n8n's credentials management.
  3. Update the Google Sheet ID in the "Get in sheet" node to point to your meeting data spreadsheet.
  4. Adjust calendar settings in the "Create an event" node to use your preferred Google Calendar.
  5. Customize message templates in the WhatsApp and Gmail nodes to match your brand voice.
  6. Set the schedule triggers to your preferred frequency (every minute for real-time, or less frequently).
  7. Test with one meeting entry in your sheet before full deployment.

Pro tip: Start with a test Google Sheet and calendar to verify the workflow works correctly before connecting to your main business accounts. Adjust the AI prompt in the Meeting Agent node to match your specific scheduling preferences and business rules.

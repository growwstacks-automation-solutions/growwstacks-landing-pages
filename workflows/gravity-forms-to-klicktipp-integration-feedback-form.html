What This Workflow Does

Manually transferring customer feedback, webinar sign-ups, or lead data from Gravity Forms to your email marketing platform is a repetitive, error-prone task. This free n8n workflow automates that entire process, seamlessly connecting Gravity Forms to KlickTipp.

Whenever a new form is submitted, the workflow instantly captures the data, transforms it into the correct format for KlickTipp (handling phone numbers, dates, and ratings), and adds or updates the contact in your subscriber list. It also applies dynamic tags based on the user's responses, enabling immediate segmentation for targeted follow-up campaigns. This eliminates manual data entry, ensures data accuracy, and accelerates your marketing response time.

How It Works

The automation follows a logical sequence to ensure reliable data flow from form submission to marketing activation.

1. Gravity Forms Webhook Trigger

The workflow is initiated by a webhook configured in your Gravity Forms. Every time a form is submitted, Gravity Forms sends the complete entry data to n8n, triggering the automation instantly.

2. Data Processing & Transformation

Raw form data is cleaned and prepared for KlickTipp. This includes converting phone numbers to an international format, transforming dates into UNIX timestamps, and scaling numerical ratings. The "Edit Fields (Set)" node maps each form field to its corresponding KlickTipp custom field.

3. Subscriber Management in KlickTipp

The processed contact data is sent to KlickTipp. The workflow checks if the contact (via email) already exists. If they do, their record is updated; if not, a new subscriber is created in your specified list with all the mapped information.

4. Dynamic Tagging Logic

The workflow fetches all existing tags from your KlickTipp account. It then defines new tags based on the form submission (e.g., "Feedback: Product X", "Webinar: Advanced Course"). It merges these lists, creates any new tags in KlickTipp, and finally applies all relevant tags to the contact for precise segmentation.

Pro tip: Use the tagging feature to trigger automated email sequences in KlickTipp. For example, tag someone as "Webinar_Registered" to automatically enroll them in a confirmation and reminder sequence.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to get your automation running in under 15 minutes.

  1. Import the Template: Download the JSON file above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure the Webhook: In the first node, copy the unique Webhook URL provided by n8n. In your Gravity Forms form settings, add this URL as a Webhook.
  3. Connect KlickTipp: Add your KlickTipp credentials (username and API password) to the KlickTipp nodes in the workflow.
  4. Map Your Fields: In the "Edit Fields (Set)" node, adjust the field mappings to match your specific Gravity Form fields and the custom fields you created in KlickTipp.
  5. Test & Activate: Submit a test form entry. Check your KlickTipp list to see the new contact appear with correct data and tags. Then activate the workflow in n8n.

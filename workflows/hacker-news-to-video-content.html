Hacker News to Video Content Automation

Transform trending tech articles into engaging video content automatically

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually converting trending tech articles from Hacker News into engaging video content. It eliminates hours of repetitive work by automatically processing articles through AI tools to generate summaries, create visuals, and produce finished videos ready for publishing.

The workflow intelligently filters relevant content, extracts key points, generates complementary visuals, and assembles everything into professional video formats. This enables content creators and marketers to scale their video production without proportional increases in time or resources.

How It Works

Step 1: Article Retrieval

The workflow connects to Hacker News API to fetch trending articles based on your specified criteria (upvotes, recency, etc.). It can process multiple articles simultaneously.

Step 2: Content Analysis

Each article is analyzed by AI to extract key themes, generate summaries, and identify opportunities for visual representation. The system assesses relevance to your target topics.

Step 3: Media Generation

AI tools create complementary visuals and animations based on the article content. The workflow maintains consistent branding across all generated assets.

Step 4: Video Assembly

The system combines generated assets into polished video formats, complete with transitions, captions, and optional narration tracks.

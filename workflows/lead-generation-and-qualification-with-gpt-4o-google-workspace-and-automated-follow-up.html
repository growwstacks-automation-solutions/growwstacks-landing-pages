What This Workflow Does

Manual lead processing is a major bottleneck for sales teams. This automation solves that by creating a complete, AI-driven lead management system. It captures leads from any source (web forms, email, etc.), enriches them with company data, uses GPT-4o to intelligently classify them as "Demo-Ready," "Nurture," or "Drop," and then triggers personalized follow-up sequences—all without human intervention.

The system logs every interaction in Google Sheets, schedules demos for hot leads in Google Calendar, and can even answer common questions instantly via an AI assistant. It turns a scattered, time-consuming process into a seamless, scalable pipeline that ensures no lead is ever forgotten and your team focuses only on the most promising opportunities.

How It Works

The workflow is a multi-stage automation that mimics and enhances a top sales rep's decision-making process.

1. Lead Capture & Initial Processing

New lead data arrives (e.g., from a webhook or form). The workflow immediately timestamps the entry and performs basic validation. It then triggers the enrichment and qualification process simultaneously to minimize delay.

2. AI-Powered Qualification & Enrichment

This is the core intelligence layer. The lead data is sent to GPT-4o alongside your predefined Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) criteria. The AI analyzes the lead's company, role, message intent, and any available firmographic data. It outputs a classification and a confidence score. Simultaneously, the system can enrich the lead record with additional company data from external APIs.

3. Automated Routing & Action

Based on the classification, the lead is routed down one of three paths. "Demo-Ready" leads instantly receive a calendar invite and a personalized confirmation email. "Nurture" leads are added to a multi-email educational sequence. "Drop" leads receive a polite, templated response, and the record is marked accordingly. All actions and status changes are logged in your master Google Sheet.

4. Continuous Logging & AI Assistant

Every step is documented. The integrated AI Answer Agent can monitor a shared inbox or form responses, providing instant, accurate answers to common prospect questions based on your knowledge base, and escalating complex queries to a human.

What You'll Need

  1. An active n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted).
  2. An OpenAI API key for GPT-4o access.
  3. A Google Workspace account with access to Gmail, Google Sheets, and Google Calendar.
  4. A Google Sheet pre-formatted with columns for lead data (template provided).
  5. A clear understanding of your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) to configure the qualification criteria.

Pro tip: Start with broad ICP criteria and refine them over time. It's better to nurture a few extra leads initially than to incorrectly drop a potential customer.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to deploy this automation in under 30 minutes.

  1. Download & Import: Click the "Download Template" button and import the JSON file into your n8n canvas.
  2. Set Up Credentials: In n8n, create credentials for OpenAI, Google Sheets, Gmail, and Google Calendar using OAuth2.
  3. Configure Your Sheet: Duplicate the provided Google Sheets template and paste its ID into all relevant "Google Sheets" nodes in the workflow.
  4. Define Your ICP: Open the "Define ICP and Lead Criteria" node. Replace the example rules with your actual target company size, industries, and pain points.
  5. Customize Email Templates: Edit the content in each "Gmail" node. Personalize the messaging for demo confirmations, nurture sequences, and drop responses.
  6. Test & Activate: Use the n8n "Execute Workflow" function with sample lead data. Verify the classification and emails work, then connect your live lead source (e.g., form webhook).

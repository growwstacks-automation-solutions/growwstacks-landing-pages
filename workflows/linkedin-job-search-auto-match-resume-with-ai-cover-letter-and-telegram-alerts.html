What This Workflow Does

Job searching is time-consuming and emotionally draining. Manually scanning LinkedIn, reading dozens of job descriptions, and writing custom cover letters can take 15-20 hours per week. Most professionals miss relevant opportunities because they can't keep up with the volume.

This automation solves that by creating a complete, hands-off job search system. It automatically searches LinkedIn for jobs matching your criteria, uses AI (Gemini or OpenAI) to compare each job description against your resume, generates a compatibility score, writes a tailored cover letter, logs everything in Google Sheets, and sends you daily Telegram alerts with the best matches.

You get a curated list of highly relevant jobs with pre-written application materials—saving time while increasing application quality and response rates.

How It Works

1. Automated Job Discovery

The workflow reads your search criteria from a Google Sheet (keywords, location, remote type, job type). It then scans LinkedIn or job boards via HTTP requests to find matching positions.

2. AI Resume Matching

Your resume (stored in Google Drive as a PDF) is extracted and analyzed alongside each job description. The AI model identifies skill matches, experience alignment, and cultural fit, generating a compatibility score.

3. Smart Cover Letter Generation

For high-scoring matches, the AI creates a personalized cover letter that highlights your relevant experience, addresses key requirements from the job description, and maintains a professional tone.

4. Centralized Tracking

All discovered jobs, their compatibility scores, generated cover letters, and application statuses are logged in a Google Sheet. This creates a searchable database of your job search activity.

5. Instant Telegram Alerts

Daily summaries of new high-match jobs are sent to your Telegram, with direct links and compatibility scores. You can review and decide which ones to pursue immediately.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for active job seekers across all industries who want to systematize their search. It's particularly valuable for tech professionals, marketers, sales executives, and managers who receive numerous relevant postings daily.

Recruitment agencies can adapt this to match candidate pools with client requirements. Career coaches can use it to demonstrate modern job search techniques to clients. Busy professionals making career transitions benefit from the consistent, data-driven approach.

What You'll Need

  1. AI API Key from Google Gemini or OpenAI
  2. Telegram Bot Token (created via @BotFather) and your Chat ID
  3. Google Sheets & Drive OAuth2 credentials from Google Cloud Console
  4. Your resume in PDF format uploaded to Google Drive
  5. Google Sheet template with your job search criteria
  6. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow

Pro tip: Start with broad search criteria, then refine based on the quality of matches you receive. The AI matching improves as it processes more jobs against your resume.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure credentials for Google Sheets, Google Drive, Telegram, and your chosen AI provider in n8n.
  3. Upload your resume to Google Drive and note the file ID.
  4. Duplicate the Google Sheet template and add your search criteria (keywords, locations, job types).
  5. Update the workflow nodes with your specific Google Sheet ID, Drive file ID, and Telegram Chat ID.
  6. Activate the workflow and set it to run daily or weekly based on your preference.

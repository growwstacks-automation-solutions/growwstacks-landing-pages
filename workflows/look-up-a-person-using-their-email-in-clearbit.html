What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually researching contacts when all you have is an email address. The workflow connects to Clearbit's API to automatically retrieve complete professional profiles including name, job title, company, social profiles, and other valuable details.

By automating this lookup process, sales and recruiting teams can instantly enrich lead information without leaving their CRM or other tools. The workflow handles the API connection, data parsing, and output formatting so you get clean, structured contact information ready for your sales pipeline or applicant tracking system.

How It Works

Step 1: Receive Trigger

The workflow activates when a new email address is received, either from a form submission, CRM update, or other trigger source.

Step 2: Clearbit API Lookup

The email is sent to Clearbit's Person API which returns a comprehensive profile including professional and social information.

Step 3: Data Parsing

The workflow extracts key fields like name, job title, company, LinkedIn profile, and other relevant details from Clearbit's response.

Step 4: Output Delivery

The enriched contact information is formatted and delivered to your destination system (CRM, spreadsheet, database, etc.).

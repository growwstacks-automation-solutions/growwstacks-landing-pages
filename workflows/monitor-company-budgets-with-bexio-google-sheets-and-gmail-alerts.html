Monitor Company Budgets with Bexio & Google Sheets

Automatically compare live accounting data against targets, get proactive email alerts, and eliminate manual spreadsheet updates.

What This Workflow Does

Financial overspending often goes unnoticed until it's too late. This workflow transforms reactive accounting into proactive management by automatically calculating monthly actuals and flagging budget overages in real-time. It connects your Bexio accounting software with Google Sheets, where you define budget targets, and sends automated weekly email reports when thresholds are exceeded.

The system exports your financial data from Bexio into a flexible spreadsheet environment, eliminating the need for manual data entry and spreadsheet updates. It provides founders and CFOs with a clear, automated view of budget performance, allowing them to make adjustments before minor overspends become critical issues.

How It Works

Step 1: Data Extraction

The workflow uses a recursive pagination loop to fetch journal entries from the Bexio API in batches of 1,000 records. This ensures complete data retrieval even for large accounting journals.

Step 2: Data Synchronization

It utilizes the "Append or Update" operation in Google Sheets to ensure records stay current without creating duplicates. Your live Bexio data flows directly into a designated spreadsheet tab.

Step 3: Financial Analysis

The system reads target thresholds from a "Budgets" sheet and aggregates costs per account by calculating the delta between Debits and Credits. It automatically organizes spending by category and period.

Step 4: Automated Reporting

A logic engine compares monthly actuals against budgets and generates a summary report sent via Gmail if thresholds are exceeded. You receive clear, actionable alerts without manual intervention.

What You'll Need

  1. Bexio Account with API access enabled.
  2. Google Workspace access to Google Sheets for data storage and Gmail for sending reports.
  3. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow.
  4. Template spreadsheet (provided) to define your budget targets.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Connect credentials for Bexio (Bearer Token), Google Sheets (OAuth2), and Gmail in their respective nodes.
  3. Configure target sheet in the "Update Records" node—select your Google Spreadsheet and target tab for journal entries.
  4. Set budget thresholds in the provided template spreadsheet's "Budgets" sheet for your specific metrics.
  5. Adjust date filtering in the "Get Records" node to match your reporting period (currently set for 2025 fiscal year).
  6. Enter recipient email in the Gmail node's "To" field and activate the schedule trigger.

Pro tip: Clone the template spreadsheet before modifying it. This preserves the original structure while allowing you to customize budgets for your specific accounts and periods.

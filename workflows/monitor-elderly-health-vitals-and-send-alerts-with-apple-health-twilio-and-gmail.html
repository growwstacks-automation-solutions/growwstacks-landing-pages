What This Workflow Does

Elder Watch is an automated health monitoring system that continuously tracks vital signs from Apple Watch or iPhone, including heart rate, blood oxygen levels, and walking symmetry. When metrics fall outside predetermined safe ranges, the system automatically alerts caregivers via email or urgent Twilio voice calls.

This solution is ideal for families with elderly members living independently or healthcare providers managing remote patient monitoring programs. It provides peace of mind through 24/7 automated oversight while reducing the need for constant manual check-ins.

Workflow dashboard displaying tracked health metrics and alert thresholds

How It Works

1. Data Collection

The workflow pulls health metrics from Apple Health at regular intervals, including resting heart rate, SpO2 levels, and walking steadiness scores.

2. Threshold Analysis

Each metric is compared against customizable thresholds. For example, alerts trigger if oxygen saturation drops below 92% or heart rate exceeds 100 bpm at rest.

3. Alert Processing

Minor deviations generate daily summary emails, while critical changes initiate immediate Twilio voice calls to designated caregivers.

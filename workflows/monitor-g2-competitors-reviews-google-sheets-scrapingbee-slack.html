What This Workflow Does

This automation solution solves the time-consuming task of manually checking competitor reviews on G2. Marketing and product teams often spend hours each week monitoring competitor activity, missing critical insights when reviews are published between manual checks.

The workflow automatically scrapes designated G2 review pages at regular intervals. When new reviews are detected, it immediately sends a Slack notification to your team while logging all review details (including ratings, comments, and dates) to a Google Sheet for historical tracking and analysis.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Review Checks

The workflow runs on a schedule you define (daily, hourly, etc.) to check your competitors' G2 review pages for new content.

2. Web Scraping

Using ScrapingBee's API, the workflow extracts review data including ratings, text, dates, and reviewer information while handling anti-scraping measures.

3. New Review Detection

The system compares each scrape against previous results, identifying only genuinely new reviews to avoid duplicate notifications.

4. Slack Alerts

For each new review found, the workflow posts a formatted Slack message to your designated channel with key details and a direct link to the review.

5. Google Sheets Logging

All review data is appended to a Google Sheet, creating a searchable historical record for competitive analysis and reporting.

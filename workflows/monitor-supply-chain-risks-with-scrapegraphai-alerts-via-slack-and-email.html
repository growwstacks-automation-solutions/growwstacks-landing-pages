What This Workflow Does

Supply chain disruptions can cripple businesses overnight. This automated workflow continuously monitors your suppliers for early warning signs of trouble—financial instability, operational issues, regulatory problems, or negative news—before they impact your operations.

Instead of relying on manual checks or quarterly reports, this system uses AI-powered web scraping to gather real-time data from multiple sources, analyzes risk factors, and automatically alerts your procurement team via Slack for immediate issues and detailed email reports for strategic review.

The system doesn't just identify problems—it also suggests alternative suppliers when high-risk situations are detected, giving your team actionable intelligence to maintain business continuity.

How It Works

This workflow transforms scattered supplier data into organized risk intelligence through a structured automation pipeline.

1. Scheduled Data Collection

The workflow triggers daily at 9:00 AM (configurable) to scan your predefined list of supplier websites, investor relations pages, and industry news sources. It uses ScrapeGraphAI to intelligently extract relevant information without manual configuration for each site.

2. AI-Powered Risk Analysis

Extracted data passes through AI analysis that evaluates financial indicators, operational status updates, regulatory compliance mentions, and sentiment from news coverage. The system calculates a composite risk score (1-10) for each supplier based on weighted factors you define.

3. Intelligent Alert Routing

Based on risk thresholds you set, the workflow routes notifications appropriately. High-risk alerts (scores 8-10) trigger immediate Slack messages to your procurement channel with specific details and recommended actions. Medium-risk findings generate scheduled email digests with comprehensive analysis.

4. Backup Supplier Discovery

When critical risks are detected, the system automatically searches for alternative suppliers in your database or through integrated directories, providing your team with contingency options before supply disruptions occur.

5. Report Generation & Storage

All risk assessments, historical trends, and alert logs are compiled into structured reports stored in your preferred database or cloud storage, creating an audit trail and enabling trend analysis over time.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with workflow execution capabilities
  2. ScrapeGraphAI API key for intelligent web scraping
  3. Slack workspace with appropriate channels (#procurement-alerts, #supply-chain-updates)
  4. Email service credentials (SMTP or service like SendGrid/Mailgun) for detailed reports
  5. Supplier URL list of websites, investor pages, and news sources to monitor
  6. Risk threshold definitions for your specific industry and tolerance levels

Pro tip: Start with your 5 most critical suppliers to validate the system before scaling to your entire network. This minimizes initial configuration complexity while proving value quickly.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file
  2. Configure ScrapeGraphAI node with your API credentials in the credentials section
  3. Set up Slack webhook by creating an incoming webhook in your Slack workspace and adding it to the Slack node
  4. Add email service credentials (SMTP or transactional email service) for report delivery
  5. Update supplier URLs in the "Supplier List" node with your actual vendor websites and monitoring sources
  6. Adjust risk scoring parameters in the "Risk Calculation" node based on your industry standards
  7. Test with one supplier by temporarily modifying the schedule trigger to run immediately
  8. Activate the schedule once testing confirms proper alerts are generated

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical problem of workflow data loss in n8n. Many businesses don't realize their automation configurations aren't being backed up until it's too late. This template creates a complete nightly backup of all your workflows to Dropbox, maintaining version history and automatically managing retention.

Each backup includes timestamps and organizes files into current/old folders. The system can optionally purge backups older than your specified retention period (default 30 days). Unlike manual backups, this solution runs consistently without human intervention, protecting your automation investments.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow activates nightly at your specified time using n8n's scheduler. This ensures backups happen consistently without manual initiation.

2. Workflow Retrieval

The system fetches all your current n8n workflows through the API, collecting the complete configuration of each automation.

3. Backup Organization

Previous night's backups are moved to an "old" folder while new backups are stored with clear timestamps. This maintains version history while keeping current backups easily accessible.

4. Retention Management

Optionally enabled, this feature automatically purges backups older than your specified age (default 30 days) to manage storage usage.

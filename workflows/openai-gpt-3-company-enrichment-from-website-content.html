AI-Powered Company Enrichment from Website Content

Automatically extract market insights, industry classification, target audience, and value propositions from company websites using OpenAI GPT-3.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Manually researching companies to understand their business model, target audience, and value proposition is time-consuming and inconsistent. Sales teams waste hours visiting websites, reading "About Us" pages, and trying to categorize companies for targeted outreach.

This AI-powered automation solves that problem by automatically extracting key business intelligence from company websites. It reads website content, analyzes it using OpenAI's GPT-3, and returns structured data including market type (B2B/B2C), industry classification, target audience demographics, and core value propositions.

The workflow transforms raw website URLs into actionable intelligence, enabling sales and marketing teams to personalize outreach at scale, prioritize leads based on fit, and make data-driven decisions about which accounts to target.

How It Works

Step 1: Input Company URLs

The workflow starts with a Google Sheets spreadsheet containing company website URLs. Each URL represents a company you want to research and enrich with AI-generated insights.

Step 2: Extract Website Content

Using HTTP Request nodes, the workflow fetches and extracts textual content from each website. It focuses on key pages like homepage, about us, services, and product pages to gather comprehensive business information.

Step 3: AI Analysis with GPT-3

The extracted content is sent to OpenAI's GPT-3 with carefully crafted prompts designed to extract specific business intelligence. The AI identifies market type, industry, target audience characteristics, and value propositions based on the website content.

Step 4: Structured Data Output

GPT-3 returns structured JSON data that's parsed and formatted. The workflow includes validation steps to ensure data quality and consistency across all analyzed companies.

Step 5: Update Google Sheets

The enriched data—market type, industry, target audience, value proposition—is written back to your Google Sheets spreadsheet, creating a comprehensive company intelligence database ready for sales and marketing use.

What This Workflow Does

In today's information-rich world, we constantly consume valuable content from YouTube videos, articles, and blogs—only to forget 90% of it within days. This creates a "learning leak" where insights disappear before they can be applied.

This n8n workflow solves that problem by creating your personal AI knowledge assistant. It automatically captures, summarizes, and organizes information from any source you specify. When you come across a useful YouTube video or article, you simply send the link to your Telegram bot. The system then extracts the content, generates an AI-powered summary, stores it in Google Sheets for permanent reference, and makes it searchable through natural language queries.

Think of it as having a personal research assistant who never sleeps, remembers everything you've ever learned, and can instantly retrieve any piece of information when you need it—all through the convenience of your messaging app.

How It Works

The automation follows a sophisticated but intuitive process that mimics how a human research assistant would work, but with AI speed and consistency.

1. Content Submission via Telegram

You send a YouTube or article URL to your dedicated Telegram bot. This acts as your "inbox" for content you want to save and summarize. The system immediately recognizes this as a new learning opportunity.

2. Intelligent Content Processing

The workflow detects whether the URL points to a YouTube video or a standard article. For YouTube videos, it uses Apify to extract the transcript. For articles, it fetches the full webpage content through an HTTP request. This ensures all types of content are handled appropriately.

3. AI-Powered Summarization

The raw content (transcript or article text) is sent to Google Gemini AI, which analyzes it and generates a concise, insightful summary. The AI extracts key points, main arguments, and actionable insights—transforming hours of content into digestible paragraphs.

4. Organized Storage in Google Sheets

The summary, along with metadata like title, source URL, and processing date, is automatically appended to your Google Sheets knowledgebase. This creates a searchable, permanent record that's accessible from any device and can be organized with filters, tags, or categories.

5. Natural Language Query System

When you need to retrieve information, you simply ask your Telegram bot in plain English. The system searches your Google Sheets knowledgebase, finds relevant entries, and returns the summaries. If no match is found, it politely suggests alternative queries.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the automation workflow
  2. Google Sheets account with two sheets prepared: one for URL tracking and one for knowledge storage
  3. Telegram account and a bot created through BotFather (free)
  4. Google Gemini API key (available through Google AI Studio) for AI summarization
  5. Apify account (free tier available) for YouTube transcript extraction
  6. Basic understanding of how to import JSON workflows into n8n and configure API credentials

Pro tip: Start with a dedicated Google Sheet just for this knowledgebase. Create clear column headers: Title, URL, Summary, Date Added, Tags. This structure makes your knowledgebase more searchable and organized as it grows.

Quick Setup Guide

Getting your personal AI knowledge assistant running takes about 15 minutes if you have all accounts ready.

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance using the "Import from File" option
  2. Create your Telegram bot by messaging @BotFather on Telegram, then copy the bot token into the Telegram trigger node
  3. Set up your Google Sheets with two sheets as specified, then connect the Google Sheets nodes with your account credentials
  4. Configure the AI node by adding your Google Gemini API key and testing with a simple prompt
  5. Test the workflow by sending a YouTube or article URL to your Telegram bot, then checking if it appears summarized in your Google Sheet
  6. Activate the workflow and start building your personal knowledgebase immediately

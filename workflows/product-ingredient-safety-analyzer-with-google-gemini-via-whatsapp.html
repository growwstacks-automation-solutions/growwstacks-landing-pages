What This Workflow Does

This intelligent automation solves a critical problem for health-conscious consumers, parents, and wellness professionals: the time-consuming and confusing process of checking product ingredient safety. Manually researching chemical compounds, cross-referencing safety databases, and interpreting complex labels can take 15-30 minutes per product.

The workflow creates an AI-powered WhatsApp bot that provides instant safety assessments for food, cosmetics, personal care items, pharmaceuticals, and household products. Users can send either text queries (like "Dove soap" or "Maggi noodles") or images of product labels, and receive comprehensive analysis within seconds. The system identifies harmful ingredients like parabens, sulfates, trans fats, and artificial colors while providing clear safety verdicts and practical alternatives.

How It Works

1. User Interaction via WhatsApp

The workflow begins when a user sends a message to your WhatsApp Business number. The system automatically detects whether the message contains text or an image, routing it through the appropriate processing path. For conversational queries like "Hi" or "Help," it provides immediate guidance on how to use the service.

2. Image Processing & OCR Extraction

When users send product label images, the workflow downloads the image, converts it to Base64 format, and sends it to Google Document AI for optical character recognition. This advanced OCR technology extracts all text from the label regardless of font, background, or language, preparing it for AI analysis.

3. AI-Powered Ingredient Analysis

The extracted text (or direct text queries) is sent to Google Gemini AI, which has been configured with specific safety criteria for different product categories. The AI identifies the product type, scans for known harmful ingredients, evaluates concentration levels, and cross-references with current safety standards.

4. Safety Assessment & Response Generation

Based on the analysis, the AI generates a structured safety verdict: ✅ SAFE (minimal concerns), ⚠️ CAUTION (some problematic ingredients), or ❌ AVOID (significant safety issues). It creates a WhatsApp-friendly response that includes the product category, key findings, specific concerns, and actionable recommendations.

5. Delivery & Conversation Management

The final assessment is sent back through WhatsApp with clear formatting for mobile readability. The system maintains conversation context, allowing follow-up questions and providing educational explanations about why specific ingredients are flagged as concerning.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this powerful automation in your business:

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance using the import workflow feature.
  2. Configure WhatsApp Business API by setting up a business account, obtaining API credentials, and configuring webhooks to point to your n8n instance.
  3. Enable Google services in your Google Cloud project, specifically Document AI and Gemini API, and create service account credentials.
  4. Set up Document AI processor for document text extraction, choosing the appropriate processor type for product labels.
  5. Enter all credentials in the corresponding n8n nodes: WhatsApp webhook URL, Google service account JSON, Document AI processor ID, and Gemini API key.
  6. Test the workflow by sending sample product queries and images to your WhatsApp number, verifying that responses are accurate and timely.
  7. Deploy and monitor the workflow in production, setting up error handling and usage analytics to track performance.

Implementation note: Start with a limited product category (like cosmetics or food) to refine the AI analysis criteria before expanding to all product types. This allows you to perfect the safety assessment logic for one domain before scaling.

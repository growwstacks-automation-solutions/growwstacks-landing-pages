What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical bottleneck for product-based businesses: creating marketing assets at scale. Traditionally, turning a product photo and description into a professional flyer or video ad requires multiple tools, manual design work, and significant time investment. This workflow eliminates that friction entirely.

By connecting Airtable as your content hub with cutting-edge AI tools, you can upload raw product images, add simple descriptions, and receive back polished marketing materials ready for social media, email campaigns, or websites. The system handles the entire creative pipeline—from image enhancement and copy generation to video production—without manual intervention.

The business value is substantial: reduce asset creation time from hours to minutes, ensure brand consistency across all materials, and free your marketing team to focus on strategy rather than repetitive production tasks. How It Works

Step 1: Content Input via Airtable

Your team adds new products to an Airtable base with basic information: product images, descriptions, target audience, and key selling points. This becomes the single source of truth for all marketing content generation.

Step 2: AI-Powered Image Enhancement

The workflow sends product images to OpenAI's image generation models, creating professional-grade flyers, social media graphics, or product showcases based on your brand guidelines and target audience specifications.

Step 3: Video Generation with VEO 3

For products that benefit from motion, the system leverages Google's VEO 3 through integration platforms to create short, engaging video ads. These can include animated text, product zooms, and background effects tailored to your brand.

Step 4: Quality Control & Distribution

Generated assets are automatically reviewed against quality thresholds, then sent back to Airtable for team approval. Once approved, they can be distributed to your marketing channels or asset management system.

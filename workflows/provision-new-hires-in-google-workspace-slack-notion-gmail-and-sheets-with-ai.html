What This Workflow Does

This end-to-end automation transforms your employee onboarding process by automatically provisioning new hires across all necessary systems the moment they're added to your HR platform. It eliminates manual account creation, reduces IT support requests, and ensures every new employee has consistent access to all required tools from day one.

The workflow handles Google Workspace account creation, Slack channel invitations, Notion access provisioning, welcome email generation, and onboarding progress tracking - all while maintaining security controls and providing audit trails for compliance.

How It Works

1. New Hire Data Collection

Triggers when a new employee record is created in your HR system or via manual entry form. Captures all essential details including name, email, department, start date, manager, and role-specific requirements.

2. Account Provisioning

Creates Google Workspace accounts with proper organizational unit assignments, generates Slack invitations with role-appropriate channel access, and sets up Notion pages with department-specific onboarding materials.

3. Welcome Package Generation

Automatically produces personalized welcome emails containing first-day instructions, login credentials, and customized resources based on the employee's role and location.

Pro tip: Add a delay step to send account details 1-2 days before the employee's start date for optimal readiness.

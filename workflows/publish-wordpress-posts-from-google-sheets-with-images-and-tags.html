What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious process of manually copying content from Google Sheets to WordPress. It's designed for content teams, marketers, and solo publishers who draft posts in spreadsheets but want a reliable, automated publishing process.

The workflow handles the entire publishing pipeline - from extracting draft content to processing images and tags, all while maintaining a clear audit trail in your spreadsheet. It eliminates copy-paste errors and ensures consistent formatting across all published content.

How It Works

1. Extract Draft Content

The workflow scans your Google Sheet for rows marked "READY" to publish, ensuring you maintain editorial control over what gets published and when.

2. Process Images

If an image URL is provided, the workflow downloads the image, uploads it to WordPress media library, and sets it as the featured image - all automatically.

3. Manage Tags

The system parses hashtags into clean slugs, checks if they exist in WordPress, creates any missing tags, and associates them with your post.

4. Publish via API

Using WordPress REST API, the workflow publishes the post with all associated metadata while maintaining proper formatting and structure.

5. Update Tracking

Finally, it marks the row as "POSTED" in your Google Sheet and records the publication timestamp and post URL for reference.

Automatically filter, qualify, and schedule only the most relevant meetings—so your team spends time on leads that convert, not screening.

What This Workflow Does

Every business that offers consultations, demos, or sales calls faces the same problem: too many unqualified meeting requests clogging the calendar. Manual screening eats up hours each week, leads slip through the cracks, and your team ends up in meetings that go nowhere.

This n8n workflow solves that by adding an AI-powered qualification layer to your appointment booking process. Instead of a simple "pick a time" form, prospects first explain their needs. An AI model instantly analyzes their request against your criteria—checking for budget, timeline, project scope, or intent. Only leads that match your ideal customer profile proceed to schedule, while others are politely redirected to more appropriate resources.

The result? Your sales or service team only spends time on conversations that have a high chance of converting. You reduce no-shows, improve lead quality, and reclaim dozens of hours each month previously lost to manual vetting.

How It Works

The automation creates a smart funnel that qualifies leads before they ever reach your calendar.

1. Smart Form Capture

The process starts with a custom n8n form embedded on your website or shared via link. Instead of just collecting name and email, it asks the prospect to describe their request in their own words. This open‑ended input provides rich context for the AI to evaluate.

2. AI‑Powered Qualification

Each submission is sent to an AI text classifier (like OpenAI) that you’ve trained on what makes a “good” vs. “bad” lead for your business. The AI scores the request based on relevance, urgency, and fit, then categorizes it as “Qualified,” “Maybe,” or “Not a Fit.”

3. Dynamic Routing & Scheduling

Qualified leads are automatically sent a follow‑up form to pick a meeting time from your connected calendar (Google Calendar, Outlook, etc.). “Maybe” leads can be routed for manual review, while “Not a Fit” leads receive a polite, automated response with alternative resources—keeping the experience positive.

4. Approval & Calendar Sync

For extra control, the workflow can include a “wait for approval” step where a team member gets an email to confirm or decline the meeting before it’s booked. Once approved, the event is created in your calendar and confirmation details are sent to the lead—all without you lifting a finger.

Quick Setup Guide

Get this workflow running in your n8n environment in under 15 minutes.

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n workspace.
  2. Connect your accounts in the n8n credentials panel: OpenAI, Gmail/Email, and Google Calendar.
  3. Customize the AI prompt in the “Text Classifier” node. Define what “qualified” means for your business (e.g., mentions budget, has a timeline, fits your service).
  4. Set up the n8n Form Trigger and embed the generated link on your website or in your email signature.
  5. Test the flow by submitting a sample request. Check that the AI correctly categorizes it and that calendar events are created as expected.
  6. Activate the workflow and share your new smart booking link with your team and marketing channels.

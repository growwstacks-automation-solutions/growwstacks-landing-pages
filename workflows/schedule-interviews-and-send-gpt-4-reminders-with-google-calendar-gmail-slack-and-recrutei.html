AI Interview Scheduling & Reminders

Automate technical and behavioral interview scheduling with Google Calendar, AI-powered email reminders, and Slack notifications.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Manual interview scheduling is a time-consuming, error-prone process that frustrates recruiters and candidates alike. This automation solves that by creating a seamless end-to-end system that captures interview requests, schedules calendar events with video conferencing links, generates personalized reminders using AI, and sends them at the perfect time.

The workflow transforms your hiring process from a administrative burden into a strategic advantage. It ensures candidates receive professional, timely communication while freeing your recruitment team to focus on relationship-building and assessment rather than calendar coordination.

How It Works

1. Interview Data Capture

The workflow begins when your Applicant Tracking System (ATS) or recruitment platform sends interview details via webhook. This includes candidate information, interviewer assignments, preferred time slots, and interview format.

2. Calendar Event Creation

Automatically creates a Google Calendar event with all participants, generates a Google Meet link for virtual interviews, and sets appropriate reminders within the calendar itself. The system checks for scheduling conflicts before finalizing.

3. AI-Powered Reminder Generation

Using GPT-4, the workflow crafts personalized, professional email reminders that include specific interview details, preparation instructions, and a friendly tone tailored to your company's voice.

4. Timed Delivery System

A wait node ensures reminders are sent exactly 24 hours before the interview—the optimal time to reduce no-shows without causing premature anxiety or forgetfulness.

5. Multi-Channel Notification

Sends the AI-generated email via Gmail while simultaneously posting a Slack notification to your recruitment team channel, keeping everyone informed without manual updates.

Pro tip: Configure the wait node for different reminder schedules based on interview type—24 hours for standard interviews, 48 hours for executive panels, or 2 hours for same-day screenings.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for recruitment agencies, in-house talent acquisition teams, tech companies with frequent technical interviews, and any organization conducting 10+ interviews monthly. It's particularly valuable for distributed teams coordinating across timezones and for roles with high interview volume like customer support, sales, and engineering positions.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. Google Workspace account with Calendar and Gmail access
  3. OpenAI API key for GPT-4 reminder generation
  4. Slack workspace with appropriate channel permissions
  5. Recruitment platform capable of sending webhook data (or manual trigger option)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file
  2. Connect your Google account to the Calendar and Gmail nodes
  3. Add your OpenAI API key to the AI node for reminder generation
  4. Configure the webhook URL in your ATS or use the manual trigger for testing
  5. Set up Slack connection to your recruitment team channel
  6. Test with a sample interview and adjust timing/email templates as needed

Pro tip: Start with a small batch of interviews to refine the AI prompt and ensure the email tone matches your employer brand before full deployment.

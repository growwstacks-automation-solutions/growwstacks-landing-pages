What This Workflow Does

Manual lead generation from Google Maps is slow, repetitive, and prone to errors. Sales teams and agencies spend hours copying business details into spreadsheets, only to end up with unstructured data that's hard to action.

This n8n workflow automates the entire process. It scrapes targeted business listings from Google Maps using Apify, enriches each entry with a concise AI‑powered summary generated by GPT‑4o, and saves the polished, structured results directly into a Google Sheet. The result is a ready‑to‑use lead database, created in minutes instead of days.

Beyond simple data extraction, the AI summarization step adds context—transforming raw addresses and phone numbers into actionable business profiles. This enables more personalized outreach and smarter prioritization for your sales or marketing campaigns.

How It Works

The automation follows a logical, multi‑step pipeline to ensure clean, usable output.

1. Trigger & Scrape

The workflow starts manually (perfect for batch runs). It triggers an Apify actor configured to scrape Google Maps based on your search criteria—like "restaurants in Denver" or "IT services in London." The actor collects names, categories, addresses, phone numbers, websites, and Google Maps URLs.

2. Fetch & Deduplicate

Raw results are fetched from Apify's dataset. A deduplication node removes duplicate listings, ensuring your final sheet contains only unique businesses, saving storage and preventing redundant outreach.

3. Loop & Summarize with AI

The workflow loops through each business. For every entry, it sends the scraped data to OpenAI's GPT‑4o with a custom prompt, instructing it to generate a natural‑language summary. This includes the business name, category, location, contact details, and a clickable Google Maps link.

4. Store & Organize

Each enriched business profile—original data plus AI summary—is appended as a new row in your designated Google Sheet. The sheet becomes a dynamic, sortable lead list, automatically formatted for easy import into your CRM or outreach tools.

5. Rate Limit Handling

A built‑in pause between processing each business prevents hitting API rate limits (for OpenAI or Google Sheets), ensuring the workflow runs smoothly even with hundreds of listings.

What You'll Need

  1. Apify Account & Actor: Access to an Apify Google Maps scraping actor (community or paid). You'll need your Actor ID and API token.
  2. OpenAI API Key: An account with access to the GPT‑4o API for generating summaries.
  3. Google Sheets Access: A Google account with a prepared spreadsheet to receive the data.
  4. n8n Instance: A self‑hosted n8n setup or a n8n.cloud account to import and run the workflow.

Pro tip: Start with a small, specific search query (e.g., "coffee shops in Seattle") to test the workflow and understand the data output before scaling to broader searches.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download & Import: Download the JSON template and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure Apify: In the Apify node, enter your Actor ID, API token, and set your search query (location, keywords, category).
  3. Connect OpenAI: Add your OpenAI API key to the "Generate Summary" node. Adjust the prompt if you want different summary styles.
  4. Link Google Sheets: Authenticate with Google and select your target spreadsheet and worksheet.
  5. Test Run: Execute the workflow once manually to verify data flows from Apify → OpenAI → Sheets correctly.
  6. Schedule (Optional): Set up a schedule trigger in n8n to run this automation weekly or monthly for fresh leads.

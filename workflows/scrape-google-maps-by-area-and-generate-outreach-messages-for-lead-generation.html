What This Workflow Does

Manual lead generation is a time-consuming bottleneck for sales and marketing teams. Searching Google Maps, copying business details, and crafting individual outreach messages can eat up hours each week. This workflow automates the entire process.

It systematically scrapes Google Maps for businesses in your chosen location and category, enriches the data with AI to understand each company's context, and generates personalized cold outreach scripts. The enriched leads are then stored in a database like Supabase, ready for your sales team to act upon immediately.

This turns a sporadic, manual task into a consistent, scalable lead-generation engine. You define the target area and keywords once, and the system delivers a polished list of prospects with tailored talking points.

How It Works

The automation follows a logical, multi-step process to ensure quality output.

1. Form Submission & Trigger

The workflow starts when a user submits a simple web form (built with n8n's Form Trigger node). This form collects the essential parameters: the target geographic area (e.g., "San Francisco, CA"), a business keyword (e.g., "plumbing services"), and the country. This submission kicks off the entire automated sequence.

2. Google Maps Scraping via Bright Data

The core data extraction is handled by the Bright Data community node. Using the submitted parameters, it initiates a scraping job on Google Maps. The node monitors the job's progress, waits for completion, and then downloads the resulting dataset—typically containing business names, addresses, phone numbers, websites, and reviews.

3. AI-Powered Lead Enrichment & Script Generation

Raw data isn't enough for effective outreach. This is where AI adds immense value. The workflow passes each scraped lead to an AI model (like OpenAI's GPT or Google Gemini) via Bright Data's MCP client. The AI analyzes the available business information and generates two key outputs: a personalized cold call opening script and a set of relevant talking points tailored to that specific business.

4. Data Storage & Organization

The final step is structuring and storing the enriched data for immediate use. The workflow uses the Merge node to combine the original scraped data with the AI-generated scripts. This complete lead profile is then "upserted" (updated or inserted) into a PostgreSQL database—like Supabase—creating a clean, queryable table of ready-to-contact prospects.

Quick Setup Guide

Importing and configuring this workflow is straightforward. Follow these steps to get it running in your n8n environment.

  1. Download & Import: Click the "Download Template" button above to get the JSON file. In your n8n instance, create a new workflow and use the "Import from File" option to load it.
  2. Configure Credentials: Set up credentials in n8n for Bright Data, your chosen LLM (OpenAI, etc.), and your PostgreSQL/Supabase database connection.
  3. Map Your Data: Review the workflow nodes. Ensure the data mapping between the Bright Data output, the AI node, and your database table matches your desired lead profile structure.
  4. Create the Form Trigger URL: The workflow uses a Form Trigger node. Activate the workflow to generate a unique URL. Use this URL in your internal tools or share it with your team to submit new scraping jobs.
  5. Test & Refine: Run a test with a small area (like a single zip code). Check the data in your database and the quality of the AI-generated scripts. Tweak the AI prompt in the workflow if needed to better suit your industry.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious, time-consuming process of manually searching Google Maps for local business leads and compiling their contact information. Sales teams and marketers often waste hours copying business names, phone numbers, and websites into spreadsheets - only to find much of the data incomplete or outdated.

The workflow automatically extracts structured lead data from Google Maps listings using Apify's specialized scraping tools, enhances the information with GPT-powered analysis to fill gaps and add context, then organizes everything in Airtable for immediate outreach. It transforms what would normally take days of manual work into a fully automated process that runs in minutes.

How It Works

1. Google Maps scraping with Apify

The workflow starts by sending your search parameters (location, business category, etc.) to Apify's Google Maps Scraper. This specialized tool handles all the technical challenges of extracting structured data from Maps listings, including pagination, proxy rotation, and CAPTCHA solving.

2. Data cleaning and validation

Raw scraped data often contains inconsistencies. The workflow standardizes phone numbers, verifies website URLs, and removes duplicate entries automatically. This ensures your lead list is clean before enrichment.

Data transformation steps that standardize phone numbers and website formats

3. GPT-powered enrichment

GPT analyzes each business profile to add valuable context like estimated company size, industry classification, and personalized outreach angles. It can also infer missing emails using common patterns when direct scraping doesn't find them.

4. Airtable organization

The enriched leads flow into Airtable where they're automatically categorized by location, industry, and other filters you specify. This structured database becomes your team's centralized lead repository for outreach campaigns.

Pro tip: Schedule this workflow to run weekly to keep your lead database fresh with new businesses and updated contact information.

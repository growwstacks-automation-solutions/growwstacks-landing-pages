What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the "empty pipeline" problem that plagues sales teams. Instead of manually searching Google Maps, copying business details into spreadsheets, and hoping someone follows up, this workflow creates a systematic lead generation machine. It continuously sources verified local businesses, enriches them with AI-powered insights, prevents duplicate outreach, and delivers hot leads directly to your sales team's fingertips.

The system transforms cold outreach from a time-consuming chore into a predictable, scalable process. By combining data scraping with intelligent qualification and instant assignment, you eliminate the manual bottlenecks that prevent consistent prospecting. Sales teams receive pre-qualified leads with personalized icebreakers, allowing them to focus on what they do best—closing deals rather than finding them.

How It Works

Step 1: Source Fresh Leads from Google Maps

The workflow begins by querying Google Maps via Outscraper for businesses matching your criteria—location, industry, keywords. It extracts verified contact information including phone numbers, addresses, websites, and customer reviews. This data is far superior to purchased lists because it's current, location-specific, and includes valuable context from real customer feedback.

Step 2: Check for Duplicates in Your Notion CRM

Before adding any lead to your pipeline, the system checks your Notion "Active Deals" database for existing entries with matching phone numbers or business names. This prevents embarrassing duplicate outreach and ensures your sales team never wastes time on leads already in the system. Only genuinely new opportunities proceed to the next stage.

Step 3: AI-Powered Qualification & Icebreaker Generation

GPT-4 analyzes the business's customer reviews, website content, and description to identify specific pain points. It generates personalized sales arguments and opening lines based on actual customer complaints or business needs. For example, if reviews mention "slow service," the AI might suggest pitching efficiency solutions with a relevant icebreaker.

Step 4: Deliver to Telegram with One-Click Assignment

Formatted lead cards are sent to your designated Telegram sales channel. Each card includes business details, AI-generated insights, and a prominent "⚡️ Take Lead" button. When a sales agent clicks this button, the system automatically assigns the lead to them in Notion, updates the deal status, and notifies the team—creating a competitive, gamified environment that encourages immediate follow-up.

Step 5: Real-Time CRM Updates & Team Coordination

The workflow maintains synchronization between Telegram interactions and your Notion CRM. When leads are claimed, assigned, or marked as contacted, all systems update simultaneously. This eliminates manual data entry errors and provides complete visibility into who's working which leads, preventing internal conflicts and ensuring seamless handoffs.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Duplicate the Notion template from the provided link in the workflow description. This creates your "Active Deals" and "Agents Configuration" databases with the correct structure.
  2. Import the JSON workflow into your n8n instance using the import function. The template includes all nodes and connections pre-configured.
  3. Configure the settings node with your Outscraper API key, Telegram chat ID, AI tone preferences, and service descriptions.
  4. Add credentials for Notion, OpenAI, Telegram, and Outscraper in n8n's credential management system.
  5. Test with a single query like "coffee shops in downtown" to verify data flows from Google Maps through AI to Telegram correctly.
  6. Add your sales team to the Telegram group and Notion "Agents Configuration" database, mapping their Telegram IDs to Notion user records.
  7. Activate the workflow and monitor the first few leads to ensure assignment and CRM updates work as expected.

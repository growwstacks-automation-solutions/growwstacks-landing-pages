What This Workflow Does

Manually tracking news, forum discussions, and online content across multiple sources is incredibly time-consuming and prone to human error. This automation solves that by creating a fully hands-off intelligence pipeline.

The workflow automatically scrapes content from your specified websites using Decodo, sends the raw text to Google Gemini AI for intelligent structuring, and then logs the clean, organized data—including titles, authors, URLs, dates, and key points—directly into a Google Sheet. It runs on a schedule you set, providing a continuously updated database of market intelligence.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger & Configuration

The workflow begins on a schedule (daily, hourly, etc.). A configuration node holds your target forum URLs, geolocation settings for scraping, and the ID of your destination Google Sheet.

2. Content Scraping with Decodo

For each target URL, the Decodo node executes, bypassing anti-bot measures to reliably extract the raw HTML and text content from the live webpage.

3. AI-Powered Structuring

The messy scraped text is sent to the Google Gemini AI node. A carefully crafted prompt instructs the AI to identify and extract specific entities: article title, author, publication date, source URL, summary, and sentiment. It outputs clean, consistent JSON.

4. Data Validation & Batching

Logic nodes check the AI output for completeness and validity. The "Split in Batches" node then processes multiple scraped items efficiently, preparing them for database insertion.

5. Storage in Google Sheets

Each structured news item is appended as a new row in your designated Google Sheet. The "Edit Fields" node ensures the data matches your column headers perfectly.

6. Logging & Error Handling

The final steps log the execution results—successes, failures, item counts—for monitoring. If the AI fails to parse an item, it's flagged for review without stopping the entire workflow.

Quick Setup Guide

Import this template and be up and running in under 15 minutes.

  1. Download & Import: Click the download button above to get the JSON file. In your n8n dashboard, go to Workflows > Import from File and select it.
  2. Configure Credentials: In the workflow canvas, set up the credentials for Decodo, Google Gemini AI, and Google Sheets in their respective nodes.
  3. Set Your Targets: Open the "Workflow Config" node. Replace the example forum URLs with your own. Paste your destination Google Sheet ID.
  4. Adjust the Schedule: In the "Schedule Trigger" node, set your preferred interval (e.g., every 6 hours, daily at 9 AM).
  5. Test & Activate: Click "Execute Workflow" once to test. Check your Google Sheet for new rows. If successful, toggle the workflow to "Active".

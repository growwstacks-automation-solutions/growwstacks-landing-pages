What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious, time-consuming process of manually researching TikTok influencers and analyzing video content. Marketing teams, agencies, and researchers spend hours each week checking profiles, copying data, and watching videos to understand trends and identify potential partners.

The workflow automatically monitors a Google Sheet for new TikTok video URLs. When a new link is added, it extracts the username, fetches complete profile statistics (followers, following, likes, video count) using Dumpling AI, retrieves the full video transcript, and appends all this structured data back to your spreadsheet. What used to take 15-20 minutes per influencer now happens automatically in seconds.

Beyond basic data collection, this creates a searchable database of TikTok creators and content that grows with your research. You can filter by follower count, analyze transcript themes, track performance changes over time, and share insights across your organization without manual data entry.

How It Works

1. Google Sheets Trigger

The workflow starts when a new row is added to your designated Google Sheet. Typically, you or your team adds TikTok video URLs to a "Video URL" column. The automation detects this new entry and begins processing immediately.

2. Username Extraction

A Set node uses regular expressions to extract the TikTok username from the video URL. This handles various TikTok URL formats and ensures clean, consistent usernames for the next steps. The username becomes the key identifier for all subsequent data collection.

3. Dumpling AI Profile Scraping

The extracted username is sent to Dumpling AI's get-tiktok-profile endpoint via HTTP Request. This returns comprehensive profile data including follower count, following count, total heart (like) count, and video count—all the essential metrics for influencer evaluation.

4. Dumpling AI Transcript Extraction

Simultaneously, the original video URL is sent to Dumpling AI's get-tiktok-transcript endpoint. This extracts the complete spoken content from the video, providing textual data for content analysis, keyword identification, and messaging research.

5. Data Consolidation & Storage

All collected data—username, profile metrics, and transcript—is structured and appended back to your Google Sheet. The automation creates a complete record with timestamp, enabling historical tracking and trend analysis across your entire influencer database.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file.
  2. Connect Google Sheets by authenticating your Google account and selecting your target spreadsheet.
  3. Configure Dumpling AI nodes by adding your API key to both HTTP Request nodes (profile and transcript endpoints).
  4. Test with a sample URL by adding a TikTok video link to your sheet and triggering the workflow manually first.
  5. Activate the workflow and set your preferred trigger schedule (instant, hourly, or daily).
  6. Add your URL list and watch as data populates automatically in your spreadsheet.

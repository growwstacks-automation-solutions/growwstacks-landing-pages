What This Workflow Does

This workflow automates the complete process of monitoring and analyzing Trustpilot reviews for your business. It collects reviews, extracts key data points, performs sentiment analysis using AI, and generates comprehensive reputation reports - all without manual intervention.

The system transforms unstructured customer feedback into structured insights that help you understand customer sentiment, identify improvement opportunities, and track your brand reputation over time. It's particularly valuable for customer experience teams, marketing departments, and business owners who want data-driven insights into their online reputation.

How It Works

1. Automated Review Collection

The workflow scrapes Trustpilot reviews for your specified company, handling pagination to collect all available feedback. It extracts both the review content and metadata like ratings, dates, and reviewer information.

2. AI-Powered Processing

Each review is processed through multiple AI steps: converting HTML to clean text, extracting structured data fields, and classifying sentiment as positive, neutral or negative.

3. Comprehensive Analysis

The system aggregates results to show sentiment distribution, identifies recurring themes in feedback, and highlights potential reputation risks or improvement opportunities.

4. Automated Reporting

Final reports are generated in easy-to-digest formats with visual charts and executive summaries, delivered via email or saved to your preferred storage system.

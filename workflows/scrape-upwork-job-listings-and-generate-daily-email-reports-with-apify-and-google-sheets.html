What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious, manual process of searching Upwork for relevant job listings. Recruiters, freelancers, and businesses spend hours daily scrolling through platforms to find opportunities that match their skills or hiring needs. This workflow eliminates that manual labor by automatically scraping Upwork using Apify's professional scraping tools.

The system collects job titles, descriptions, budgets, client ratings, and posting dates, then stores this structured data in Google Sheets for easy analysis. Finally, it generates a daily email report summarizing the most relevant opportunities, enabling you to make quick decisions without ever visiting the platform manually.

Beyond basic collection, the workflow includes data cleaning and deduplication to ensure high-quality results, keyword-based filtering to target specific niches, and statistical reporting to track market trends over time.

How It Works

Phase 1: Job Scraping & Initial Processing

The workflow begins by reading your search keywords from a Google Sheet. It then loops through each keyword, triggering Apify's scraping actor to collect Upwork job listings matching those terms. After scraping completes, it filters jobs posted within the last 24 hours and formats the data for storage.

Phase 2: Data Cleaning & Deduplication

Raw scraped data often contains duplicates and irrelevant entries. This phase loads all newly collected jobs, removes duplicates based on title and description matching, and saves only unique, clean entries back to the sheet. It also clears old data to maintain a fresh, actionable database.

Phase 3: Daily Summary & Email Report

The system analyzes the cleaned data, counting jobs per keyword and calculating statistics like average budgets and top-performing search terms. It then builds a structured email body with these insights and a link to the full Google Sheet, sending a concise daily report directly to your inbox.

What You'll Need

  1. Apify API credentials and access to an Upwork scraping actor
  2. Google Sheets API access (via Google Cloud Console)
  3. Email service credentials (Gmail, SMTP, or similar)
  4. A list of Upwork search keywords relevant to your niche
  5. Three Google Sheets with specific column structures for keywords, daily jobs, and summaries

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the downloaded JSON template into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure the Apify node with your API token and actor ID.
  3. Set up Google Sheets credentials and connect to your three prepared sheets.
  4. Configure the email node with your SMTP or Gmail service details.
  5. Add your target keywords to the "AI Keywords" sheet and activate the workflow.
  6. Test with a few keywords and verify the email report format matches your needs.

Pro tip: Start with broad keywords to capture market volume, then refine to specific niches once you understand demand patterns. Use the summary sheet data to identify which keywords yield the highest-quality opportunities.

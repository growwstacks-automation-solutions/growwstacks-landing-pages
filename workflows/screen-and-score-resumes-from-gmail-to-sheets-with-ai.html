What This Workflow Does

This intelligent automation solves the time-consuming, error-prone process of manual resume screening that plagues recruiters and hiring managers. When you receive hundreds of applications via email, reviewing each resume individually becomes impossible, leading to missed talent and inconsistent evaluation.

The workflow automatically monitors your Gmail inbox for new applications, extracts resume data using AI, scores candidates based on your criteria, and organizes everything in a Google Sheet. This transforms your chaotic inbox into a structured, filterable candidate database where you can instantly see top applicants ranked by their match to your requirements.

How It Works

1. Gmail Monitoring

The workflow checks your designated hiring inbox at regular intervals (every 6-24 hours) for new emails containing PDF attachments. It identifies application emails based on sender patterns, subject lines, or specific labels you configure.

2. Resume Extraction

When a qualifying email arrives, the automation downloads the attached resume file and converts it to text. Using AI (OpenAI's GPT models), it intelligently parses the unstructured resume content to extract standardized fields: candidate name, email address, phone number, LinkedIn profile, skills, education, and work experience.

3. AI Scoring

The extracted information is evaluated against your predefined scoring criteria. You can configure points for required skills, years of relevant experience, specific certifications, education level, and keyword matches. The AI can also assess the overall quality and relevance of the resume content.

4. Google Sheets Logging

All candidate data—including extracted fields, calculated scores, and the original email timestamp—is automatically appended as a new row in your Google Sheet. This creates a living database where you can sort, filter, and track applicants throughout your hiring pipeline.

5. Notification & Follow-up

Optionally, the workflow can trigger notifications for high-scoring candidates, automatically send acknowledgment emails to applicants, or create tasks in your project management system for interview scheduling.

Quick Setup Guide

1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.

2. Configure Google Cloud by creating a new project, enabling the Gmail and Sheets APIs, and setting up OAuth consent screen and credentials.

3. Connect accounts in n8n by adding your Google OAuth credentials to the Gmail and Sheets nodes, and adding your OpenAI API key to the AI node.

4. Customize the scoring logic in the workflow to match your specific hiring criteria for the role you're filling.

5. Test with sample resumes by sending test emails to your hiring inbox and verifying the data extraction and scoring work correctly.

6. Activate the workflow and set it to run on a schedule that matches your application volume (start with every 12 hours).

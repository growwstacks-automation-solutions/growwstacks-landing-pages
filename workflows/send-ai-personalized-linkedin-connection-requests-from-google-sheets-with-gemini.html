What This Workflow Does

Manual LinkedIn outreach is time-consuming and often ineffective. Sending generic connection requests leads to low acceptance rates, while personalizing each message manually doesn't scale. This automation solves both problems by combining your prospect list in Google Sheets with AI-powered personalization.

The workflow automatically reads pending prospects from your spreadsheet, fetches their complete LinkedIn profile data, uses Google Gemini AI to generate a unique, authentic connection message, and sends the personalized request. It then updates your sheet with the sent message and status, creating a seamless, scalable outreach system that maintains a human touch.

Built for sales teams, recruiters, founders, and growth marketers, this automation turns hours of manual research and messaging into a hands-free process that runs in the background, helping you build valuable professional networks faster.

How It Works

Step 1: Read Prospects from Google Sheets

The workflow starts by checking your Google Sheet for prospects marked as "PENDING." It reads their LinkedIn URL, name, and any available context, then immediately marks the row as "IN PROGRESS" to prevent duplicate processing.

Step 2: Fetch LinkedIn Profile Data

Using the ConnectSafely.ai API, the system retrieves the prospect's complete LinkedIn profile information, including their current position, past experience, education, skills, and recent activity. This rich data becomes the foundation for personalization.

Step 3: Generate AI-Personalized Message

Google Gemini AI analyzes the profile data alongside your custom context (who you are, why you're connecting) to craft a unique connection request. The AI references specific elements from their profile to create authentic, relevant messages under 300 characters.

Step 4: Send Connection Request

The personalized message is sent as a LinkedIn connection request via the ConnectSafely.ai API. A random delay (1-5 minutes) is added between requests to mimic human behavior and maintain platform compliance.

Step 5: Update Tracking Sheet

Finally, the workflow marks the prospect as "DONE" in your Google Sheet and stores the exact message that was sent. This creates a complete audit trail and prevents re-contacting the same person.

Quick Setup Guide

1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance.

2. Create credentials in n8n for Google Sheets (OAuth2), ConnectSafely.ai (Bearer Auth), and Google Gemini API.

3. Prepare your Google Sheet with columns for First Name, LinkedIn URL, Tagline, Status, and Message.

4. Configure the Google Sheets nodes with your document ID and sheet name.

5. Customize the AI prompt in the Gemini node with your personal context and sign-off.

6. Test with one prospect using the manual trigger to ensure everything works before scheduling.

7. Schedule the workflow to run periodically (every 30-60 minutes) for continuous outreach.

Pro tip: Start with 10-20 connection requests per day and gradually increase over 2-3 weeks. Always personalize your AI prompt with specific information about your background and genuine reasons for connecting to maintain authenticity.

What This Workflow Does

Job applications are time-consuming and repetitive. You find a posting, read it carefully, craft a personalized email, attach your resume, and send—often taking 20-45 minutes per application. This automation transforms that entire process into a seamless, AI-powered workflow.

When you screenshot a job posting and send it via Telegram, the system analyzes the content with OpenAI, generates a tailored email draft that matches your skills to the requirements, attaches your resume from Google Drive, and sends the polished application via Gmail—either immediately or on a schedule. It turns hours of manual work into seconds of automated precision.

How It Works

Step 1: Trigger via Telegram

You send a screenshot of a job posting to your Telegram bot. The bot recognizes the image and initiates the automation workflow, sending a "typing" indicator to confirm processing.

Step 2: AI Analysis & Draft Generation

OpenAI's vision capabilities extract key details from the job posting: required skills, company culture, role specifics. It then accesses your resume (uploaded to OpenAI's files) to match your experience with the job requirements, generating a personalized email draft.

Step 3: Resume Attachment & Email Preparation

The system retrieves your resume PDF from Google Drive, attaches it to the email, and formats the complete application package. You receive a preview in Telegram with options to send immediately or schedule.

Step 4: Delivery via Gmail

Upon confirmation, Gmail sends the personalized email with attached resume to the hiring manager or specified recipient. The system logs the sent application and can schedule follow-up reminders.

What You'll Need

  1. A Telegram bot token (created via @BotFather)
  2. OpenAI API key with GPT-4 Vision access
  3. Gmail account with OAuth setup
  4. Google Drive with your resume uploaded
  5. Redis instance (free tier available)
  6. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template JSON file and import into n8n.
  2. Create credentials for Telegram, OpenAI, Gmail, Google Drive, and Redis in n8n.
  3. Upload your resume to OpenAI Files API and Google Drive, noting the file IDs.
  4. Update the workflow nodes with your resume file IDs and credential assignments.
  5. Activate the workflow and test by sending a job posting screenshot to your Telegram bot.

Pro tip: Use Telegram captions to add specific instructions like "make it more casual" or "send to recruiter@company.com" for further customization.

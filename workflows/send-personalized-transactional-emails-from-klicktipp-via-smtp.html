What This Workflow Does

This automation solution bridges KlickTipp with SMTP email services to deliver personalized transactional emails at scale. It solves the challenge of sending customized, brand-consistent emails triggered by customer actions while maintaining deliverability and tracking capabilities.

The workflow automatically generates HTML emails with dynamic content, sends them through your preferred SMTP service, and records delivery status back in KlickTipp. This creates a closed-loop system where you can monitor email performance and customer engagement.

Workflow interface showing the KlickTipp trigger and email generation nodes

How It Works

1. KlickTipp Trigger

The workflow activates when a KlickTipp outbound rule triggers, passing contact data including email address and any custom fields you want to use for personalization.

2. HTML Template Generation

Your email template is dynamically populated with contact-specific information. The system supports variables for names, order details, dates, and other personalized content.

3. SMTP Email Delivery

The completed email is sent through your configured SMTP service (like Gmail, Outlook, or your own mail server) with proper authentication and tracking headers.

4. Status Update

After sending, the workflow records the delivery status in KlickTipp, marking whether the email was successfully sent or failed, creating an audit trail.

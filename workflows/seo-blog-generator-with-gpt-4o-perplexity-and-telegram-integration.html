What This Workflow Does

Creating consistent, high-quality SEO content is one of the biggest challenges for modern businesses. Between research, writing, optimization, and publishing, a single blog post can take days of work. This automation solves that problem by connecting the best AI tools into a seamless content production pipeline.

The workflow automatically researches any topic using Perplexity AI's real-time web search, generates comprehensive blog content with GPT-4o, optimizes it for search engines with proper metadata, and can deliver the finished article to your CMS, email list, or Telegram channel. It turns what was a multi-day process into a button click.

Beyond just writing, this system ensures your content stays relevant and competitive. By leveraging current search data and AI's understanding of ranking factors, it produces content that actually performs in search results while maintaining your brand's unique voice and perspective.

How It Works

1. Topic Research & Analysis

The workflow begins by sending your target keyword or topic to Perplexity AI, which conducts real-time web research. It gathers current information, identifies trending subtopics, analyzes competitor content, and extracts key data points that will make your article comprehensive and authoritative.

2. AI Content Generation

GPT-4o takes the research data and transforms it into a well-structured, engaging blog post. The AI follows your specific guidelines for tone, length, and structure while naturally incorporating target keywords, creating headings, and developing logical arguments that keep readers engaged.

3. SEO Optimization

Before finalizing, the content undergoes SEO enhancement. The system generates optimized meta titles and descriptions, creates URL-friendly slugs, ensures proper keyword density, and structures the content with appropriate H2/H3 headings for both readability and search engine crawling.

4. Delivery & Publishing

The completed blog post, along with all metadata, is formatted and delivered to your chosen destination. This could be your WordPress site via API, a Google Doc for final review, a Telegram channel for immediate sharing, or any other system you've integrated into the workflow.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with workflow execution capabilities
  2. OpenAI API key with access to GPT-4o or GPT-3.5 for content generation
  3. Perplexity AI API key for real-time web research and data gathering

Pro tip: Start with a small set of well-defined topics and gradually expand. The more specific your prompts and guidelines, the better the AI will understand your brand voice and content expectations.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file
  2. Add your credentials for OpenAI (as openAiApi) and Perplexity AI (as httpHeaderAuth) in n8n's credential management
  3. Configure the trigger - use the Form Trigger for manual testing or set up Telegram for automated triggering
  4. Customize the prompts in the "Blog Content Generator" and "Metadata Generator" nodes to match your brand voice
  5. Test with a simple topic like "benefits of remote work" to see the full process from research to output
  6. Connect your publishing destination by adding nodes for your CMS, Google Sheets, or other platforms
  7. Activate the workflow and start generating SEO-optimized content automatically

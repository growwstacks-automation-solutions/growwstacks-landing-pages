Smart Shipping Prioritization with Google Gemini

Automate shipment prioritization using AI analysis of Google Sheets data

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common logistics challenge of efficiently prioritizing shipments when you have limited resources and tight deadlines. Traditional methods often rely solely on shipment dates, missing important context from customer notes and special requests.

The workflow integrates Google Gemini's AI capabilities with your existing Google Sheets shipment data to intelligently prioritize orders based on multiple factors including delivery deadlines, customer notes, and business priorities. It then automatically updates your shipping records and can trigger notifications.

How It Works

1. Webhook Trigger

The workflow activates when your shipping team presses a physical button (connected via webhook) or when scheduled to run at specific intervals.

2. Google Sheets Data Retrieval

It reads your shipping spreadsheet containing order IDs, dates, customer details, notes, and completion status.

3. AI Analysis

Google Gemini processes both the structured data and unstructured customer notes to determine priority based on urgency, special requests, and business rules.

4. Priority Determination

The AI recommends the optimal shipment sequence, which the workflow validates against your business rules.

5. System Updates

The workflow marks completed shipments and can trigger notifications or update other systems.

What You'll Need

  1. A Google Sheet with shipment data (template provided in download)
  2. Google Gemini API access
  3. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  4. Basic understanding of webhooks (optional for button integration)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your Google Sheets and Google Gemini credentials
  3. Configure the sheet ID and tab name in the Google Sheets node
  4. Adjust any business rules in the Function nodes
  5. Test with your sample data
  6. Deploy to production

Pro tip: Start with a copy of your production sheet to test the workflow before connecting it to your live data.

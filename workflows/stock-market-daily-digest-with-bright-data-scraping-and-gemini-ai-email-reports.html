Automate Your Daily Stock Market Digest

Scrape financial data with Bright Data, generate AI-powered summaries with Gemini, and get daily email reports—all automated.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Manually tracking the stock market every day is time-consuming and inconsistent. You might miss key news, spend hours reading reports, or struggle to connect disparate data points. This creates information gaps that can impact investment decisions.

This n8n workflow automates the entire daily market analysis process. It systematically scrapes financial data from trusted sources using Bright Data, processes the information, uses Google's Gemini AI to generate concise summaries highlighting trends and insights, and delivers a polished email digest directly to your inbox. It turns hours of manual research into a fully automated, reliable intelligence system.

How It Works

1. Schedule & Data Preparation

A Schedule Trigger node runs the workflow daily (or at your chosen interval). You provide a list of stock tickers to monitor in JSON format. The workflow cleans and prepares this list for processing.

2. Data Scraping with Bright Data

The workflow sends your stock keywords to the Bright Data Datasets API. This professional scraping service gathers current financial news, price movements, and relevant market data from high-quality sources, bypassing the complexity and instability of building your own scrapers.

3. Progress Monitoring & Data Retrieval

n8n checks the status of the scraping job and, once complete, retrieves the full dataset. This ensures you get all the scraped information reliably.

4. AI Summarization with Gemini

The raw data is passed to the Google Gemini AI Chat Model node. A custom prompt instructs the AI to analyze the information, identify the day's key highlights, trends, and potential implications, and format it into a clear, actionable email summary.

5. Reporting & Storage

The AI-generated digest is sent via Gmail. Simultaneously, the raw scraped data can be appended to a Google Sheet for your records, creating a searchable historical database.

Who This Is For

This workflow is ideal for individual investors who want to stay informed without the daily grind, financial advisors needing consistent market updates for clients, fintech startups building automated research tools, and business executives monitoring market conditions affecting their industry. If you need regular, structured market intelligence but lack the time to compile it manually, this automation is for you.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted).
  2. A Bright Data account with API credentials and a configured dataset for financial news.
  3. A Google Cloud account with the Gemini AI API enabled.
  4. A Gmail account or SMTP credentials for sending emails.
  5. A Google Sheet (optional) for data logging.
  6. Your list of stock tickers to monitor.

Pro tip: Start with a small watchlist of 5-10 stocks to test the workflow and ensure the AI summary quality meets your needs before scaling up.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n workspace.
  2. Configure the Schedule Trigger node with your preferred time (e.g., 6 PM daily).
  3. Replace the sample stock list in the workflow with your actual tickers in JSON format.
  4. Set up your credentials: Add your Bright Data API key to the HTTP Request nodes, your Google Gemini API key to the AI node, and your Gmail credentials to the Email node.
  5. Customize the AI prompt in the Gemini node to tailor the summary's focus, tone, and length.
  6. Test the workflow manually once, then activate the schedule.

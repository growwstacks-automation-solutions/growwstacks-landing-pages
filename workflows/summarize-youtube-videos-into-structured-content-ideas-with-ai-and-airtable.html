What This Workflow Does

Content creators, marketers, and business owners spend hours watching YouTube videos for research, inspiration, and competitive analysis. The valuable insights are often trapped in lengthy videos, requiring manual note-taking that's inconsistent and time-consuming.

This automation solves that problem by systematically turning YouTube videos into structured content ideas. It monitors your target videos, extracts transcripts through an API, uses AI to identify the main idea and key takeaways, then stores everything in Airtable where your team can access, prioritize, and act on the insights.

The result is a continuously growing database of content ideas that fuels your marketing calendar without the manual labor. What used to take hours of watching and note-taking now happens automatically while you focus on creating content.

How It Works

The workflow follows a logical sequence to transform raw video content into actionable insights:

1. Trigger & Source Collection

A scheduled trigger runs every 5 minutes (configurable) to check your Airtable base for new YouTube video URLs that haven't been processed. This ensures your content pipeline stays fresh with minimal delay.

2. Transcript Extraction

For each new video URL, the workflow calls a transcript API (via RapidAPI) to extract the complete spoken content. This converts hours of video into searchable text that AI can analyze efficiently.

3. AI Analysis & Summarization

The transcript is sent to an AI model (OpenAI, Claude, or Gemini) with specific prompts designed to extract the core message, identify 3-5 key takeaways, and note any actionable insights for content creation.

4. Structured Data Storage

The AI-generated insights are formatted and written back to Airtable, updating the original record with the main idea, key takeaways, and marking it as processed. This creates a searchable knowledge base.

5. Error Handling & Logging

If any step fails (invalid URL, API limits, etc.), the workflow logs the error and continues with other videos, ensuring robustness. You receive notifications only for critical issues requiring attention.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to get your YouTube content automation running in under 15 minutes:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n workspace
  2. Configure Airtable credentials in the "Get YouTube Sources" node with your base and table details
  3. Set up RapidAPI by adding your API key to the headers in the transcript extraction node
  4. Connect your AI provider (OpenAI/Claude/Gemini) in the summarization node with your API key
  5. Adjust the schedule trigger to match your content volume (every 5 minutes to daily)
  6. Test with one YouTube URL by adding it to your Airtable base and activating the workflow
  7. Review the results in Airtable and tweak AI prompts if needed for your specific use case

Once configured, the workflow runs autonomously. Simply add YouTube URLs to your Airtable base, and they'll be processed automatically with insights waiting when you check back.

Pro tip: Create an Airtable form for your team to submit YouTube URLs. This creates a simple interface for non-technical team members to contribute to the content research pipeline.

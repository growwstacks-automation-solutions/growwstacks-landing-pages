Sync leads from Webflow to Pipedrive CRM using n8n

What This Workflow Does

Every time a visitor fills out a form on your Webflow website, they become a potential lead. But manually copying that information into your Pipedrive CRM wastes time, introduces errors, and delays sales follow-up. This automation solves that problem by instantly capturing form submissions and syncing them as fully structured leads in Pipedrive.

The workflow ensures each lead is accurately associated with the correct organization (based on email domain), checks for duplicates to keep your CRM clean, and creates a new person record with all submitted details. It transforms a manual, error-prone process into a seamless, automated funnel from marketing capture to sales management.

How It Works

Step 1: Capture the Webflow Form Submission

The workflow triggers whenever a form is submitted on your Webflow site. It receives the lead's name, email, phone, and any custom fields you've set up. This data becomes the foundation for the CRM entry.

Step 2: Extract Company Domain from Email

The lead's email address is analyzed to extract the company domain (e.g., jane@company.com → company.com). This domain is used to search Pipedrive for an existing organization, ensuring leads are grouped correctly under their company.

Step 3: Find or Create the Organization

Pipedrive is queried using the domain. If the organization already exists, the workflow proceeds. If not, a new organization is created automatically, keeping your CRM organized without manual intervention.

Step 4: Check for Existing Person & Create Lead

The workflow searches Pipedrive for a person with the same email. If found, a note is added to their activity log about the new submission. If not, a new person record is created, a note is added, and a new lead is generated in Pipedrive—ready for sales assignment.

Pro tip: You can extend this workflow to instantly notify your sales team via Slack or email when a high-value lead is captured, enabling immediate follow-up while the lead is still warm.

What You'll Need

  1. A Webflow site with form submissions enabled
  2. A Pipedrive CRM account with API access
  3. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the automation
  4. Basic understanding of your Webflow form fields and Pipedrive custom fields
  5. The lead's email address must be captured in the Webflow form (required for domain extraction)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template JSON file and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure the Webflow trigger node with your site's form webhook URL.
  3. Set up the Pipedrive nodes with your API credentials and customize field mappings.
  4. Test the workflow with a sample form submission to verify data flows correctly.
  5. Activate the workflow and monitor initial leads to ensure everything works as expected.

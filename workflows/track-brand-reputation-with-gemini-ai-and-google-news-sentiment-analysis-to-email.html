What This Workflow Does

Manual brand monitoring is time-consuming and inconsistent. Marketing teams spend hours each week scanning news sites and social media, often missing important mentions or failing to understand the true sentiment behind coverage. This creates blind spots where negative perception can grow unchecked, while positive opportunities go unexploited.

This n8n automation solves that by creating a complete, hands-off brand intelligence system. It continuously scans Google News for your brand mentions, uses Google's Gemini AI to perform sophisticated sentiment analysis, and delivers witty, easy-to-read reports directly to your inbox. The AI doesn't just score sentiment—it writes narrative-driven analyses that read like a brand personality assessment, making insights both actionable and engaging.

How It Works

1. Data Collection & Trigger

A Google Sheets spreadsheet serves as your control panel. Each row contains a brand keyword and recipient email. When you add a new brand to monitor, the workflow automatically triggers.

2. News Aggregation

The RSS Read node fetches recent headlines from Google News based on your specified keywords. It captures the latest mentions across thousands of news sources, ensuring comprehensive coverage without manual searching.

3. AI-Powered Analysis

Google's Gemini 2.5 Flash model acts as your brand analyst. It evaluates each article, assessing sentiment on a nuanced scale while writing engaging commentary about your brand's "personality" in the media—highlighting strengths, quirks, and potential challenges.

4. Report Generation

JavaScript code structures the AI's response into well-formatted HTML, creating professional reports with clear sections for sentiment scores, key mentions, and actionable insights.

5. Automated Delivery

Gmail integration sends the complete analysis directly to specified stakeholders. Reports arrive within minutes of news breaking, enabling immediate response to opportunities or issues.

Pro tip: Set up separate monitoring for your top 3 competitors alongside your own brand. The comparative insights will reveal market positioning opportunities you'd otherwise miss.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this brand monitoring system in under 15 minutes:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Create your Google Sheets control panel with "keyword" and "email" columns
  3. Connect your Google Gemini API credentials in the "Message a Model" node
  4. Authenticate your Gmail account in the "Send a Message" node
  5. Add your first brand keyword and recipient email to the Google Sheet
  6. Test the workflow with a known recent news mention to verify everything works
  7. Schedule the workflow to run daily or in real-time based on your needs

Pro tip: Start with 2-3 brand keywords and one recipient email. Once you confirm the system works perfectly, expand to full competitor monitoring and multiple stakeholder reports.

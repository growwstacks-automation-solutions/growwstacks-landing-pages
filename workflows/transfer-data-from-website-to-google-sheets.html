What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of copying data from website forms to spreadsheets. It instantly captures form submissions via webhook and securely transfers the data to your specified Google Sheet, eliminating errors and saving hours of administrative work.

Whether you're collecting leads, customer inquiries, event registrations, or survey responses, this workflow ensures your data is immediately available in Google Sheets for analysis, reporting, or further processing through other automations.

How It Works

1. Webhook Capture

The workflow starts by setting up a webhook endpoint that listens for form submissions from your website. When a visitor submits a form, the data is immediately captured.

2. Data Validation

Before processing, the workflow validates required fields and checks data formats to ensure only clean, complete submissions proceed to the next step.

3. Google Sheets Integration

Validated data is then automatically appended to your specified Google Sheet, with each form submission creating a new row with all the collected information.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the workflow template into your n8n instance
  2. Configure the webhook URL in your website form settings
  3. Connect to your Google Sheets account in the workflow
  4. Map your form fields to the appropriate spreadsheet columns
  5. Test with sample submissions and activate the workflow

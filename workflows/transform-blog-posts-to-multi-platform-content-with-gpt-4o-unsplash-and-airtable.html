Transform Blog Posts to Multi-Platform Content with GPT-4o, Unsplash & Airtable

Automatically repurpose your blog content into LinkedIn posts, Twitter threads, Instagram captions, email newsletters, and video scripts—saving 10+ hours per week.

What This Workflow Does

Content creation is time-consuming, and repurposing a single blog post across multiple platforms can take hours of manual work. This automation solves that problem by transforming your blog content into five different platform-optimized formats automatically.

The workflow monitors your blog's RSS feed for new posts, extracts the full content, uses GPT-4o to create LinkedIn posts, Twitter threads, Instagram captions, email newsletters, and video scripts, suggests relevant images from Unsplash, notifies your team in Slack, and logs everything in Airtable for tracking. What used to take a content team 5-6 hours now happens automatically in minutes.

How It Works

Step 1: Content Monitoring & Extraction

The RSS Read node monitors your blog feed for new publications. When a new post is detected, it extracts the full article content, title, URL, and publication date, cleaning the text for optimal AI processing.

Step 2: AI-Powered Content Transformation

GPT-4o analyzes the blog content and creates five distinct formats: a professional LinkedIn post with hashtags, an engaging Twitter thread, an Instagram caption with visual suggestions, an email newsletter with subject line, and a video script outline.

Step 3: Visual Enhancement & Team Notification

The Unsplash API suggests relevant images for each content piece based on keywords. A Slack notification is sent to your content team with previews of all generated formats for quick review before distribution.

Step 4: Centralized Tracking & Optional Distribution

All content is logged in Airtable with status tracking. Optional nodes can auto-post to LinkedIn and Twitter (disabled by default but easily activated with your OAuth2 credentials).

What You'll Need

  1. OpenAI API key with GPT-4o access for content transformation
  2. Unsplash API key (free tier available) for image suggestions
  3. Slack workspace for team notifications
  4. Airtable account (free plan works) for content tracking
  5. Blog with RSS feed (WordPress, Ghost, Medium, Webflow all supported)
  6. Optional: LinkedIn and Twitter OAuth2 credentials for auto-posting

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Replace the RSS feed URL with your blog's feed (typically /feed, /rss, or /feed.xml)
  3. Add your API credentials: OpenAI, Unsplash, Slack OAuth2, and Airtable token
  4. Create an Airtable base with the 14 specified columns for tracking
  5. Update the Slack channel ID for content team notifications
  6. Test with a recent blog post to verify all formats generate correctly
  7. Optional: Enable LinkedIn/Twitter auto-posting nodes if desired

Pro tip: Start with the free tiers of Unsplash and Airtable to test the workflow. Once you confirm it saves time, consider upgrading based on your volume needs. For best results, provide GPT-4o with 2-3 examples of your brand's tone for each platform format.

What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered automation transforms raw meeting transcripts into polished, multi-format content with minimal manual effort. It solves the common challenge of extracting maximum value from meeting recordings by automatically repurposing them for different platforms and audiences.

The system connects Fathom's accurate meeting transcription with GPT-4's content generation capabilities and DALL-E's image creation to produce video summaries, social media posts, blog content, and visual assets - all from a single meeting recording.

How It Works

1. Meeting Transcript Processing

The workflow automatically retrieves your most recent meeting transcript from Fathom and analyzes it for key insights, action items, and quotable moments using AI.

2. Content Generation

GPT-4 processes the transcript to create written content in appropriate styles for different platforms (concise for social media, detailed for blogs). Simultaneously, it generates prompts for video and image creation.

3. Multi-Format Output

The system distributes content to appropriate platforms - videos to YouTube/Vimeo, images to social media, written content to your CMS or Google Docs - all formatted and ready for review.

What You'll Need

  1. Fathom account with recorded meetings

  3. Google Docs for content storage
  4. Video generation API (Luma AI or Runway ML)
  5. Three subworkflows configured (included in template)

Pro tip: For best results, provide context about your meeting participants and goals in the initial workflow configuration.

AI Creative Director: Transform Product Photos into Ad Campaigns

Turn amateur product snapshots into high-conversion advertising assets automatically. Send a photo via Telegram, get AI-generated ad concepts and visuals in minutes.

What This Workflow Does

This automation acts as your personal AI art director, transforming basic product photos into professional marketing campaigns. Instead of spending hours on design and copywriting, you simply send a product image to a Telegram bot. The system analyzes the photo, critiques its quality, brainstorms multiple marketing angles, and generates polished ad creatives—all within minutes.

The workflow solves the common problem of content creation bottlenecks for e-commerce businesses, digital marketers, and product-based startups. It bridges the gap between amateur photography and professional marketing by leveraging cutting-edge AI models to understand visual content and generate strategic creative concepts.

By automating the creative process, you can test more marketing angles, produce content faster, and maintain consistent quality across all your advertising channels. The system handles everything from initial analysis to final creative delivery, allowing you to focus on strategy and scaling rather than production.

How It Works

The workflow follows a sophisticated multi-step process that mimics how a human creative team would approach ad development, but at machine speed and scale.

Step 1: Photo Submission via Telegram

Users send a product photo with a brief caption to your dedicated Telegram bot. The workflow includes authentication to ensure only authorized users can trigger the process, protecting your API credits from unauthorized use.

Step 2: Visual Analysis with Gemini Vision

The image is sent to Google's Gemini Vision API, which analyzes the product, identifies key features, assesses image quality, and provides detailed observations about composition, lighting, and potential improvements.

Step 3: Creative Concept Generation

A LangChain agent powered by Claude Sonnet (via OpenRouter) acts as your creative director. It takes the visual analysis and generates 3 distinct marketing angles—such as "Cyberpunk Tech," "Luxury Lifestyle," or "Minimalist Professional"—complete with target audience profiles and emotional appeals.

Step 4: AI Image Generation

Using the selected concepts, Gemini 3 Pro creates high-fidelity commercial photography that transforms the original snapshot into professional-grade marketing visuals. The system maintains product accuracy while enhancing composition and style.

Step 5: Delivery Back to Telegram

The final ad creatives are sent back through Telegram, providing the user with ready-to-use marketing assets along with the strategic rationale behind each concept.

Pro tip: Configure the CONFIG node first with your API keys and authorized user ID. This centralizes all sensitive credentials and prevents unauthorized usage of your AI credits.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this AI creative director in your marketing workflow:

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance by downloading the JSON file and importing it via the workflow editor.
  2. Configure the CONFIG node with your Telegram bot token, authorized user ID, Google API key, and OpenRouter credentials.
  3. Test the Telegram trigger by sending a photo to your bot—ensure the authentication check passes before proceeding.
  4. Adjust AI prompts in the LangChain agent node to match your brand voice and preferred marketing angles.
  5. Set up error handling by configuring the IF nodes to catch API failures and notify you via Telegram.
  6. Activate the workflow and start transforming your product photos into ad campaigns instantly.

Pro tip: Start with a small batch of product photos to test different concepts before scaling to your entire catalog. This helps you refine the AI prompts for optimal results specific to your products.

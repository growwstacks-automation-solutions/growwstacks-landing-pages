What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the constant content creation struggle faced by marketers, creators, and business owners. Instead of manually scanning industry news and drafting social media posts, this workflow automatically collects relevant articles from RSS feeds, identifies the most valuable insights, and generates ready-to-publish content for Medium, LinkedIn, and Instagram.

The system acts as your personal content assistant, working 24/7 to transform passive news consumption into active content generation. It maintains your brand voice while adapting content format for each platform—long-form articles for Medium, professional insights for LinkedIn, and engaging visual captions for Instagram.

Beyond just content creation, the workflow includes email confirmation steps so you can review and approve drafts before publishing. This human-in-the-loop approach ensures quality control while still saving you 10-15 hours of weekly content work.

How It Works

1. RSS Feed Collection

The workflow begins by monitoring your configured RSS feeds for new articles. You can add multiple industry publications, competitor blogs, or news aggregators. The system checks at scheduled intervals (daily or hourly) to ensure you never miss important developments in your field.

2. Article Relevance Scoring

Each new article is analyzed for relevance to your specific niche and audience. The system evaluates factors like topic alignment, publication authority, and potential engagement value to select the most promising content for transformation.

3. AI Content Generation

Selected articles are processed through GPT-4o with customized prompts for each platform. The AI extracts key insights, reformats information for different audiences, and applies your brand's tone of voice—professional for LinkedIn, conversational for Instagram, detailed for Medium.

4. Multi-Platform Formatting

The generated content is automatically formatted for each destination: complete Medium articles with proper headings, LinkedIn posts with professional hashtags, and Instagram captions with emoji placement and call-to-actions.

5. Review & Distribution

Before publishing, content drafts are sent to your email for approval. Once reviewed, they can be scheduled or published immediately through your connected platforms, creating a seamless content pipeline from news discovery to publication.

What You'll Need

  2. OpenAI API key with GPT-4o access for content generation

  4. Email account (Gmail or other configured service) to receive content drafts

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this content automation system in under 30 minutes:

  1. Import the workflow into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file.
  2. Configure RSS sources by replacing the sample URLs in the RSS Read node with your preferred industry feeds.
  3. Connect OpenAI by adding your API key to the credentials and adjusting AI prompts to match your brand voice if needed.
  4. Set up email notifications by connecting your Gmail or email service to receive content drafts for review.
  5. Test the workflow manually to ensure content quality meets your standards before enabling scheduled automation.
  6. Activate scheduling to run daily or at your preferred frequency for continuous content generation.

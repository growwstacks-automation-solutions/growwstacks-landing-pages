What This Workflow Does

This advanced automation pipeline solves the time-consuming and expensive problem of video production. Instead of hiring videographers, writing scripts, filming, and editing, you simply describe your idea in text. The workflow intelligently transforms that description into a professional, cinematic video using the latest AI models.

It connects three powerful technologies: Google's Veo 3 model (accessed via Leonardo.ai) for high-quality video generation, Claude 4 for sophisticated prompt engineering, and Google Drive for automatic storage. The result is a hands-off system that turns creative concepts into shareable video content in minutes, not days.

How It Works

1. Input Your Concept

You provide a text description of your video idea in the workflow's "Video Context" node. This could be as simple as "a robot explaining quantum physics in a laboratory" or "a sunny beach scene with relaxing music."

2. AI Prompt Enhancement

Claude 4 Sonnet analyzes your description, accesses Wikipedia for factual context if needed, and crafts an optimal Google Veo 3 prompt. It structures the prompt with eight essential elements: Subject, Context, Action, Style, Camera Motion, Composition, Ambiance, and Audio.

3. Video Generation

The enhanced prompt is sent to Google's Veo 3 model through Leonardo.ai's API. The model generates a 720p resolution video (typically 5-8 seconds) with native audio, following cinematic best practices.

4. Processing & Download

The workflow waits intelligently for 4 minutes to ensure video generation completes, then automatically downloads the finished video file from Leonardo's servers.

5. Cloud Storage

The video is uploaded directly to your specified Google Drive folder, ready for use in marketing campaigns, social media, or presentations.

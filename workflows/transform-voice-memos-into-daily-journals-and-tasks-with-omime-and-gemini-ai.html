What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of valuable insights and tasks getting lost in voice recordings. Professionals using OMI pendants or similar voice transcription devices often accumulate hours of recordings that never get properly processed, organized, or acted upon.

The workflow automatically processes incoming OMI transcripts, uses AI to transform them into well-structured journal entries, extracts actionable tasks, and stores everything in organized systems. It turns raw voice data into a personal knowledge base and task management system without any manual intervention.

Instead of spending 30-60 minutes daily reviewing and typing up notes, this automation delivers polished journals and prioritized tasks directly to your Google Drive and Tasks—saving up to 5 hours per week while ensuring nothing important slips through the cracks.

How It Works

1. Voice Capture & Webhook Trigger

The OMI pendant records conversations, reflections, or meetings and sends the structured transcript data via webhook to your n8n workflow. This includes metadata like category, emotional tone, and key highlights that enrich the processing.

2. AI-Powered Journal Generation

Google Gemini AI analyzes the transcript, understands context and sentiment, and generates a polished journal entry in your preferred style. The AI can be customized to produce minimalist, poetic, or professional outputs based on your preferences.

3. Task Extraction & Creation

The system identifies actionable items within the conversation and creates corresponding tasks in Google Tasks with appropriate due dates and priorities. This ensures commitments are captured immediately rather than being forgotten.

4. Organized Storage & Archiving

Both the raw transcription and the final journal are saved to designated Google Drive folders—one for long-term archival and another for daily processing. This creates a searchable personal knowledge base.

What You'll Need

  1. OMI pendant or compatible voice transcription device that can send data via webhook or API
  2. Google Workspace account with access to Google Drive and Google Tasks
  3. Google Gemini API key or alternative AI service (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.)
  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the automation
  5. Basic familiarity with n8n for configuration and customization

Pro tip: Before implementing, spend a week manually processing a few transcripts to identify your preferred journal structure and task categorization. This insight will help you customize the AI prompts for optimal results.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance
  2. Configure OMI webhook to point to your n8n webhook URL
  3. Connect Google Drive and Tasks credentials in the respective nodes
  4. Add your Google Gemini API key to the AI Agent or Chat node
  5. Update folder IDs in the Google Drive nodes to match your preferred directory structure
  6. Test with a sample transcript and adjust AI prompts if needed
  7. Activate the workflow and begin processing voice memos automatically

What This Workflow Does

Customer Success Managers and Account Managers spend hours manually converting meeting insights into presentation slides for quarterly business reviews. This process is tedious, inconsistent, and often loses the nuance captured in real-time conversations.

This automation solves that by letting you brain dump everything via a quick voice note to a Telegram bot. The workflow transcribes your audio using Groq Whisper (free), structures the content with GPT-5, checks for sensitive information, and generates a Google Slides file in your company's template—pre-populated with categorized insights.

It doesn't create a final, polished presentation. Instead, it gives you a structured first draft with placeholders filled, so you can focus on refining, adding data visuals, and polishing the narrative instead of starting from scratch.

How It Works

Step 1: Voice Capture via Telegram

Record a voice memo directly to your Telegram bot after a client meeting. Speak freely about wins, challenges, recommendations, and next steps—no need to organize your thoughts perfectly.

Step 2: AI Transcription & Security Scan

Groq Whisper converts your audio to text. A GPT-5-nano node then scans for sensitive data (PII, financials, confidential info) and alerts you if detected, ensuring compliance.

Step 3: Content Structuring

GPT-5-mini analyzes the transcription and organizes it into three key sections: Value Realized, Recommendations, and Next Steps. It extracts actionable insights from your unstructured rambling.

Step 4: Human Review & Approval

You receive an email with the extracted content for validation. Add any missing client details or correct misinterpretations before the slides are generated.

Step 5: Template Generation in Google Slides

The workflow copies your predefined Google Slides template into the client's Drive folder and replaces the placeholder text with the structured content from Step 3.

Step 6: Draft Ready for Refinement

A complete slide deck draft is now in the client's folder. You open it, add charts, metrics, screenshots, and polish the language to create the final business review.

Who This Is For

This workflow is ideal for Customer Success Managers, Account Managers, and client-facing professionals who conduct regular business reviews. It's especially valuable for teams managing multiple clients, where manual slide creation becomes a significant time drain.

If you use Google Workspace, have a standard slide template, and want to capture post-meeting insights quickly without losing context, this automation will save you hours per client per quarter.

What You'll Need

  1. A Telegram account and bot created via @BotFather
  2. Groq API key for free Whisper transcription
  3. OpenAI API key (for GPT-5-nano and GPT-5-mini)
  4. Google Workspace access (Gmail, Drive, Slides)
  5. A Google Slides template with three placeholders: value_realized_placeholder, recommendations_placeholder, next_steps_placeholder
  6. Shared access to your clients' Google Drive folders

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template JSON file and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Create credentials for Telegram, Groq, OpenAI, Gmail, Google Drive, and Google Slides in n8n.
  3. Update the "Copy template to customer Folder" node with your Google Slides template ID.
  4. Set your company name in the "Set CSM's company name" node.
  5. Add your email address to all "human in the loop" email nodes.
  6. Test by sending a voice note to your Telegram bot and watch the draft slides appear.

Pro tip: Keep voice notes under 5 minutes for best transcription accuracy and lower API costs. Have your CRM or analytics dashboards open while recording to reference specific metrics.

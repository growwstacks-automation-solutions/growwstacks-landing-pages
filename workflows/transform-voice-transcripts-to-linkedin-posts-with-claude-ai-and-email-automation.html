Transform Voice Transcripts to Polished LinkedIn Posts with AI

Turn your raw voice memos into professional, engaging LinkedIn content automatically. Save hours of editing and maintain a consistent posting schedule.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common problem for busy professionals: you have valuable insights and ideas throughout the day, but turning those scattered voice memos into polished LinkedIn content takes hours of editing. The workflow captures your raw, unpolished voice transcripts and transforms them into well-structured, engaging LinkedIn posts using AI.

Instead of struggling with "ums," incomplete sentences, and disorganized thoughts, you simply email your transcript to the workflow. It combines your content with style examples from your inspiration document, then uses Claude AI to create clean, professional posts that sound like you—just more organized.

How It Works

The automation follows a logical sequence to transform your content while maintaining your authentic voice.

1. Trigger via Email

You send an email containing your raw voice transcript to a dedicated workflow email address. This can come from any transcription service, your phone's voice recorder, or even a quick email you send yourself.

2. Content Extraction & Style Reference

The workflow extracts the transcript text and simultaneously pulls style examples from your Google Doc containing LinkedIn posts you admire. This ensures the AI understands your preferred tone, structure, and formatting.

3. AI Processing with Claude

Claude AI analyzes your raw content alongside the style references. It removes filler words, organizes thoughts logically, adds appropriate formatting (headings, bullet points, hashtags), and creates a coherent 150-300 word post.

4. Delivery & Review

The polished content is emailed back to you with a suggested image description. You review, make any minor tweaks if needed, then copy and paste directly to LinkedIn.

Pro tip: Maintain a "style guide" Google Doc with 5-10 LinkedIn posts you love. Update it monthly to keep your content fresh and aligned with evolving trends.

What You'll Need

  1. Anthropic API key for Claude AI access
  2. Email account (Gmail, Outlook, or any IMAP-compatible service)
  3. Google Doc with LinkedIn post examples for style reference
  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  5. Basic transcription tool (phone recorder, Otter.ai, Rev, etc.)

Quick Setup Guide

Get this automation running in under 30 minutes with these steps:

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance
  2. Configure email trigger with your IMAP credentials and dedicated email address
  3. Connect Claude AI node with your Anthropic API key
  4. Set up Google Docs node with your style reference document ID
  5. Test the workflow by sending a sample voice transcript email
  6. Refine prompts in the AI node based on your first output

Pro tip: Start with 2-3 test runs using different types of content (thought leadership, case study, personal story) to fine-tune the AI prompts before going live.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually converting YouTube videos into social media content. It automatically fetches new videos from your channel, processes them through Vizard AI to extract key moments and metadata, then uses GPT-4.1 to generate engaging captions formatted for different social platforms.

The workflow eliminates hours of manual work by handling the entire process from video analysis to content distribution, while ensuring your social channels stay active with fresh, repurposed content that maintains your brand voice.

How It Works

1. Video Fetching

The workflow monitors your YouTube channel for new uploads via RSS feed, ensuring you never miss an opportunity to repurpose content.

2. AI Processing

Vizard AI analyzes each video to identify viral moments, generate transcripts, and provide editing tools to create short-form clips optimized for social media.

3. Content Generation

GPT-4.1 creates platform-specific captions, hashtags, and summaries based on the video content and your brand guidelines.

4. Distribution

The workflow logs all generated content to Google Sheets for review and can be extended to automatically post to your social platforms.

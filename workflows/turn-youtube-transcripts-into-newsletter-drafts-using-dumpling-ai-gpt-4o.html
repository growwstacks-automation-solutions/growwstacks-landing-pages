What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common problem for content creators, marketers, and businesses: turning video content into written formats efficiently. Instead of manually transcribing YouTube videos and rewriting them as newsletters—a process that can take hours per video—this workflow automatically extracts transcripts, summarizes key points using advanced AI, and generates ready-to-edit newsletter drafts.

The system reads YouTube video URLs from a Google Sheet, processes each video through Dumpling AI for accurate transcript extraction, then uses GPT-4o to transform those transcripts into engaging, email-friendly content. Finished drafts are logged back into the same sheet, and an email notification alerts you when each draft is complete. This creates a scalable content repurposing pipeline that maintains quality while saving significant time.

How It Works

Step 1: Collect YouTube Links

The workflow starts by reading a Google Sheet containing YouTube video URLs in a designated column. It identifies videos that haven't been processed yet (those without corresponding newsletter drafts) and prepares them for transcript extraction.

Step 2: Extract Transcripts with Dumpling AI

Each video URL is sent to Dumpling AI's API, which returns a clean, accurate transcript of the video content. Dumpling AI handles various video lengths, languages, and formats, providing reliable text extraction that serves as the foundation for content generation.

Step 3: Transform Transcripts with GPT-4o

The raw transcript is passed to GPT-4o with specific instructions to create a newsletter-style draft. The AI summarizes key points, maintains the original tone, structures content for email readability, and adds appropriate formatting—producing a draft that's already 80-90% complete.

Step 4: Save Drafts and Notify

The generated newsletter draft is written back to the same row in Google Sheets, creating a clear record of processed content. Simultaneously, a Gmail notification is sent to alert you that the draft is ready for review and editing.

What You'll Need

  1. A Google Sheet with columns for YouTube links and newsletter drafts
  2. Active accounts for Dumpling AI (API access for transcripts)
  3. OpenAI GPT-4 or GPT-4o access
  4. Google Sheets OAuth2 credentials configured in n8n
  5. Gmail OAuth2 credentials for notification emails

Pro tip: Before running the workflow, test Dumpling AI's transcript quality with a sample video to ensure it captures dialogue accurately, especially for technical or nuanced content.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the downloaded JSON template into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure all credentials in n8n's Credential Manager: Google Sheets, Dumpling AI (HTTP Header Auth), OpenAI, and Gmail.
  3. Update the Google Sheets nodes with your specific sheet ID and tab name.
  4. Optional: Customize the GPT-4o prompt in the "Write Newsletter Draft" node to match your brand voice and audience preferences.
  5. Verify the email recipient in the Gmail node and test with a single YouTube link.

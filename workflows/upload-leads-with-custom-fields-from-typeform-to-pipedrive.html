What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between lead capture and sales follow-up. When someone fills out your Typeform—whether it's a contact form, webinar registration, or product inquiry—their submission is instantly transformed into a fully-fledged lead in your Pipedrive CRM.

The workflow goes beyond simple data transfer. It intelligently maps form responses to relevant Pipedrive fields, including custom fields you've defined for your specific sales process. For example, if you ask about company size in your Typeform, that information populates a corresponding custom field in Pipedrive, giving your sales team immediate context about the lead's potential value.

By eliminating the manual copy-paste process, you ensure zero lead leakage, faster response times, and consistent data formatting across your sales pipeline. The automation also adds notes to the lead record based on specific answers, providing your team with ready-made talking points for personalized follow-up.

How It Works

The workflow follows a logical sequence to transform form submissions into actionable leads.

1. Typeform Trigger

The automation starts when someone submits your Typeform. The workflow listens for new submissions in real-time, capturing all form responses including text answers, multiple choice selections, and file uploads.

2. Data Extraction & Cleaning

A Set node processes the raw form data, extracting only the relevant information needed for your CRM. This step filters out unnecessary metadata and structures the data for clean import into Pipedrive.

3. Custom Field Mapping

A Function node handles complex data transformations. For instance, it can convert text-based company size responses ("1-10 employees") into standardized values that match your Pipedrive custom field options, ensuring consistency across your lead database.

4. Pipedrive Lead Creation

The processed data creates a new lead in your specified Pipedrive pipeline. The workflow populates standard fields (name, email, phone) alongside your custom fields, and adds contextual notes based on key form responses to guide your sales team.

Pro tip: Add a delay node before the Pipedrive step during testing to prevent creating duplicate leads while you fine-tune your field mappings.

  2. Pipedrive account with appropriate permissions to create leads and custom fields

  4. API credentials for both Typeform and Pipedrive (available in your account settings)

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in your n8n instance:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n workspace
  2. Configure Typeform trigger by connecting your Typeform account and selecting the specific form to monitor
  3. Map your form fields in the Set node—update the field names to match your Typeform question IDs
  4. Adjust the Function node to match your custom field logic (like company size categorization)
  5. Connect Pipedrive with your API credentials and select the target pipeline and custom fields
  6. Test with a submission using Typeform's preview mode to verify the lead appears correctly in Pipedrive
  7. Activate the workflow and start automatically capturing leads

Pro tip: Create a test lead in Pipedrive first to see all available custom field IDs, then map your Typeform responses to these exact field names in the workflow.

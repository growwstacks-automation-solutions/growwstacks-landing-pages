What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common but time-consuming problem: creating professional-looking videos with custom backgrounds. Instead of spending hours in video editing software manually removing backgrounds and compositing footage, this workflow handles everything automatically.

You provide a foreground video URL and a background image URL (both publicly accessible). The system uses AI-powered segmentation to remove the video background, centers the subject on your custom image while preserving aspect ratio, then uploads the finished video to Google Drive with a shareable link. Perfect for creating consistent branded content, product demos, AI avatars on professional backgrounds, social media content, and presentation videos.

How It Works

1. Input & Trigger

The workflow can be triggered manually for testing or via webhook for automation. You provide two URLs: your foreground video (with the subject to isolate) and your background image (where you want the subject placed).

2. Background Removal Processing

The system sends the video to the VideoBGRemover API, which uses advanced AI segmentation to precisely separate the subject from the background. This happens automatically without any manual masking or rotoscoping.

3. Image Composition

The foreground subject (now with transparent background) is centered onto your custom background image. The workflow maintains the original video's aspect ratio and quality while creating a seamless composite.

4. Google Drive Integration

The finished video is automatically uploaded to your specified Google Drive folder. The workflow generates a shareable link and can notify your team when processing is complete.

Pro tip: Use this workflow to create a library of branded video backgrounds (office scenes, product displays, branded patterns) and process multiple videos against them in batches. Perfect for social media content series or product launch campaigns.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to get your video automation running in under 10 minutes:

  1. Get your API key (2 min): Visit videobgremover.com/api-management, sign up, and copy your API key. Free trial credits are available.
  2. Add API key to n8n (2 min): In n8n Settings → Variables, create a new variable named VIDEOBGREMOVER_KEY with your API key as the value.
  3. Connect Google Drive (2 min): Click the "Upload to Google Drive" node in the workflow, click "Connect," and authorize n8n to access your Drive.
  4. Test with sample URLs (1 min): Use the manual trigger with the sample URLs provided in the "Sample URLs (Edit Here)" node to verify everything works.
  5. Customize for production (2 min): Replace sample URLs with your actual video and image sources, configure your Drive folder structure, and set up webhook triggers if needed.

Note on processing time: The VideoBGRemover API typically processes video at 3-5 minutes per minute of footage. Plan your automation accordingly for longer videos.

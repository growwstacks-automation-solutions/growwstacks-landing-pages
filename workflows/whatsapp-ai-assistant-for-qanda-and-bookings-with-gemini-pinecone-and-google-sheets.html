What This Workflow Does

Manual WhatsApp support is time-consuming and error-prone. Customers wait for answers, bookings get lost in chat history, and staff spend hours on repetitive conversations. This workflow solves that by creating an intelligent AI assistant that handles inquiries 24/7.

The system listens for WhatsApp messages, uses Gemini AI to understand the intent, searches your knowledge base in Pinecone for accurate answers, and when a booking request is detected, automatically creates an entry in Google Sheets. It even sends follow-up reminders if customers don't respond, ensuring no opportunity is missed.

How It Works

1. WhatsApp Message Trigger

When a customer sends a message to your WhatsApp Business number, the workflow is triggered. It captures the message content, sender information, and timestamp.

2. AI Intent Analysis

Gemini AI analyzes the message to determine if it's a general question, service inquiry, or booking request. The system understands natural language, even with spelling variations or informal phrasing.

3. Knowledge Base Search

For questions, the workflow searches your uploaded documents in Pinecone (PDFs, FAQs, service guides) to find the most relevant answer. This ensures responses are accurate and based on your actual business information.

4. Booking Processing

When a booking is detected, the AI extracts details like service type, preferred date/time, and customer information. It then creates a structured entry in your Google Sheets booking log with all necessary fields.

5. Automated Response & Follow-up

The system sends an appropriate WhatsApp reply—either answering the question or confirming the booking. If there's no response within 24 hours, it sends a gentle follow-up message to re-engage the customer.

What You'll Need

  1. WhatsApp Business API access (via providers like Twilio, 360dialog, or directly from Meta)
  2. Google Cloud account with Gemini API enabled
  3. Pinecone account (free tier available) for vector storage
  4. Google Sheets for booking logs
  5. Google Drive with your business documents (FAQs, service menus, policies)
  6. n8n instance (self-hosted or n8n.cloud)

Pro tip: Start with your most frequently asked questions in Google Drive. The AI assistant becomes more valuable as your knowledge base grows. Regularly update it with new services, pricing changes, or policy updates.

  1. Import the template: Download the JSON file and import it into your n8n editor.

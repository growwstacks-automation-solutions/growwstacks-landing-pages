What This Workflow Does

Manual cold email outreach is time-consuming and often ineffective due to lack of personalization. Sales teams and recruiters spend hours researching each lead, finding email addresses, and crafting messages—only to see low reply rates.

This automation solves that. You submit a LinkedIn profile URL through a simple form. The workflow automatically finds the prospect's email and company information using Wiza, researches the individual and their company with Perplexity AI to uncover recent news and pain points, then uses your chosen AI model to generate a personalized icebreaker email with a relevant hook. The finished draft appears in your Gmail inbox, ready for a quick review and send.

It turns a 30-minute manual process into a 30-second automated one, while dramatically increasing the quality and relevance of your outreach.

How It Works

The workflow orchestrates several powerful tools to create a seamless pipeline from lead discovery to email draft.

Step 1: Form Trigger & Data Collection

The process starts when you or a team member submits a LinkedIn profile URL via an n8n form. This triggers the workflow and passes the URL to the next stages.

Step 2: Lead Enrichment with Wiza

The Wiza node takes the LinkedIn URL and retrieves the prospect's verified email address, full name, job title, and company details. This data forms the foundation for personalization.

Step 3: AI-Powered Research

Using the enriched data, the Perplexity AI node conducts real-time research on the prospect's company. It looks for recent funding rounds, product launches, leadership changes, or industry news that can serve as a conversation starter.

Step 4: Personalized Email Generation

An AI model (like OpenAI) receives the prospect's profile, the company research, and your predefined business context and case studies. It synthesizes this information to write a concise, personalized email that references specific, relevant details.

Step 5: Draft Delivery to Gmail

The final email draft, along with the prospect's contact information, is sent to your Gmail account via the Gmail node. It arrives as a draft, allowing you to add final touches or send immediately.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (self-hosted or n8n.cloud).
  2. Wiza API Key for email finding and lead enrichment.
  3. OpenAI API Key (or another compatible AI model) for email generation.
  4. Perplexity API Key for real-time company research.
  5. Gmail OAuth2 credentials configured in n8n to send drafts to your inbox.
  6. Basic business context about your offering to feed into the AI prompt.

Pro tip: Before running at scale, test the workflow with 2-3 LinkedIn profiles to fine-tune the AI prompt. Adjust the tone, length, and structure of the generated emails to match your brand voice.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to get this automation running in your n8n environment.

  1. Import the Template: Download the JSON file using the button above and import it into your n8n instance via the "Import from File" option.
  2. Configure Credentials: In the n8n credentials panel, set up and name your credentials for Wiza, OpenAI, Perplexity, and Gmail.
  3. Update Workflow Nodes: Assign the correct credentials to each corresponding node in the workflow canvas.
  4. Customize Your Context: Locate the "Your Offer" or business context node. Update the text with details about your product/service and relevant case studies.
  5. Test & Activate: Turn on the workflow and use the provided form URL. Submit a test LinkedIn profile URL to see the complete flow from research to Gmail draft.

YouTube Video Summarizer with Gemini AI

Automatically summarize YouTube videos using AI, deliver insights via WhatsApp/Telegram, and log results in Google Sheets.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common business problem: manually watching and summarizing YouTube videos is time-consuming and inconsistent. Whether you're researching competitor content, extracting insights from tutorials, or monitoring industry trends, you need quick, reliable summaries without spending hours viewing.

The workflow automates the entire process. Users submit YouTube links via web forms, WhatsApp, or Telegram. Gemini AI processes the video, generating either a concise English summary or a full transcript. The result is delivered back through the same communication channel and optionally logged in Google Sheets for record-keeping and analysis.

This creates a seamless knowledge extraction pipeline that operates 24/7, handling multiple requests simultaneously while maintaining consistent output quality. It transforms video content into actionable business intelligence instantly.

How It Works

Step 1: Trigger & Input Collection

The workflow starts when a user submits a YouTube video link. This can happen through three channels: a web form (for internal teams), WhatsApp messages (for customer-facing interactions), or Telegram chat (for community or group requests). The system captures the link and user context.

Step 2: AI Processing & Summarization

The YouTube link is passed to Gemini AI with specific instructions. The AI model extracts the video transcript, analyzes the content, and generates a clear English summary or full transcript based on configuration. It handles mixed-language videos, ensuring output is always in English for consistency.

Step 3: Output Delivery & Logging

The generated summary is formatted into a readable structure (bullet points or paragraphs) and sent back to the user via their original channel—WhatsApp, Telegram, or web response. Simultaneously, the result is written to a Google Sheets row, capturing video URL, summary, timestamp, and requester details for future reference.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (self-hosted or cloud) to run the automation workflow.
  2. Gemini/Google AI Platform account with API access for summarization capabilities.
  3. Google Sheets account for logging output (free Google account suffices).
  4. WhatsApp Business API or Telegram Bot API credentials if using those messaging channels.
  5. Public YouTube video links (up to ~30 minutes duration for optimal processing).

  1. Download the template JSON file and import it into your n8n workspace.
  2. Configure credentials for Gemini AI, Google Sheets, and your chosen messaging platforms (WhatsApp/Telegram).
  3. Set up your trigger channels—webhook URL for forms, WhatsApp number, or Telegram bot token.
  4. Test with a sample YouTube link to verify summarization quality and delivery.
  5. Deploy the workflow and share submission methods with your team or users.
